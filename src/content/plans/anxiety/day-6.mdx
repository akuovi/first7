## God is Near

"The Lord is close to the brokenhearted..." - Psalm 34:18

Even in anxiety, God is with you.

You are never alone.

## Reflection
- Do I feel alone?
- Can I sense God's presence?

## Prayer
God, remind me that You are always near.
