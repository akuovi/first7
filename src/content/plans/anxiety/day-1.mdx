## God is in Control

"Trust in the Lord with all your heart..." - Proverbs 3:5

Anxiety often comes from trying to control everything. But God is already in control.

You don't need to carry everything alone.

## Reflection
- What am I trying to control?
- Can I trust God with it?

## Prayer
God, help me trust You with what I cannot control.
