## Focus on Today

"Do not worry about tomorrow..." - Matthew 6:34

Anxiety often lives in the future.

God calls you to live today.

## Reflection
- Am I worrying about the future?
- How can I focus on today?

## Prayer
God, help me live in today and trust You with tomorrow.
