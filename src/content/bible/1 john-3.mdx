## 1 John 3

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Behold, what manner of love the Father hath bestowed upon us, that we should be called the sons of God: therefore the world knoweth us not, because it knew him not.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Beloved, now are we the sons of God, and it doth not yet appear what we shall be: but we know that, when he shall appear, we shall be like him; for we shall see him as he is.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And every man that hath this hope in him purifieth himself, even as he is pure.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Whosoever committeth sin transgresseth also the law: for sin is the transgression of the law.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And ye know that he was manifested to take away our sins; and in him is no sin.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Whosoever abideth in him sinneth not: whosoever sinneth hath not seen him, neither known him.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Little children, let no man deceive you: he that doeth righteousness is righteous, even as he is righteous.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> He that committeth sin is of the devil; for the devil sinneth from the beginning. For this purpose the Son of God was manifested, that he might destroy the works of the devil.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Whosoever is born of God doth not commit sin; for his seed remaineth in him: and he cannot sin, because he is born of God.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> In this the children of God are manifest, and the children of the devil: whosoever doeth not righteousness is not of God, neither he that loveth not his brother.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> For this is the message that ye heard from the beginning, that we should love one another.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Not as Cain, who was of that wicked one, and slew his brother. And wherefore slew he him? Because his own works were evil, and his brother's righteous.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Marvel not, my brethren, if the world hate you.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> We know that we have passed from death unto life, because we love the brethren. He that loveth not his brother abideth in death.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Whosoever hateth his brother is a murderer: and ye know that no murderer hath eternal life abiding in him.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Hereby perceive we the love of God, because he laid down his life for us: and we ought to lay down our lives for the brethren.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> But whoso hath this world's good, and seeth his brother have need, and shutteth up his bowels of compassion from him, how dwelleth the love of God in him?</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> My little children, let us not love in word, neither in tongue; but in deed and in truth.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And hereby we know that we are of the truth, and shall assure our hearts before him.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> For if our heart condemn us, God is greater than our heart, and knoweth all things.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Beloved, if our heart condemn us not, then have we confidence toward God.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And whatsoever we ask, we receive of him, because we keep his commandments, and do those things that are pleasing in his sight.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And this is his commandment, That we should believe on the name of his Son Jesus Christ, and love one another, as he gave us commandment.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And he that keepeth his commandments dwelleth in him, and he in him. And hereby we know that he abideth in us, by the Spirit which he hath given us.</p>
