## Philippians 1

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Paul and Timotheus, the servants of Jesus Christ, to all the saints in Christ Jesus which are at Philippi, with the bishops and deacons:</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Grace be unto you, and peace, from God our Father, and from the Lord Jesus Christ.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> I thank my God upon every remembrance of you,</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Always in every prayer of mine for you all making request with joy,</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> For your fellowship in the gospel from the first day until now;</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Being confident of this very thing, that he which hath begun a good work in you will perform it until the day of Jesus Christ:</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Even as it is meet for me to think this of you all, because I have you in my heart; inasmuch as both in my bonds, and in the defence and confirmation of the gospel, ye all are partakers of my grace.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> For God is my record, how greatly I long after you all in the bowels of Jesus Christ.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And this I pray, that your love may abound yet more and more in knowledge and in all judgment;</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> That ye may approve things that are excellent; that ye may be sincere and without offence till the day of Christ;</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Being filled with the fruits of righteousness, which are by Jesus Christ, unto the glory and praise of God.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> But I would ye should understand, brethren, that the things which happened unto me have fallen out rather unto the furtherance of the gospel;</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> So that my bonds in Christ are manifest in all the palace, and in all other places;</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And many of the brethren in the Lord, waxing confident by my bonds, are much more bold to speak the word without fear.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Some indeed preach Christ even of envy and strife; and some also of good will:</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> The one preach Christ of contention, not sincerely, supposing to add affliction to my bonds:</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> But the other of love, knowing that I am set for the defence of the gospel.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> What then? notwithstanding, every way, whether in pretence, or in truth, Christ is preached; and I therein do rejoice, yea, and will rejoice.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> For I know that this shall turn to my salvation through your prayer, and the supply of the Spirit of Jesus Christ,</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> According to my earnest expectation and my hope, that in nothing I shall be ashamed, but that with all boldness, as always, so now also Christ shall be magnified in my body, whether it be by life, or by death.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> For to me to live is Christ, and to die is gain.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> But if I live in the flesh, this is the fruit of my labour: yet what I shall choose I wot not.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> For I am in a strait betwixt two, having a desire to depart, and to be with Christ; which is far better:</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Nevertheless to abide in the flesh is more needful for you.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And having this confidence, I know that I shall abide and continue with you all for your furtherance and joy of faith;</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> That your rejoicing may be more abundant in Jesus Christ for me by my coming to you again.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Only let your conversation be as it becometh the gospel of Christ: that whether I come and see you, or else be absent, I may hear of your affairs, that ye stand fast in one spirit, with one mind striving together for the faith of the gospel;</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And in nothing terrified by your adversaries: which is to them an evident token of perdition, but to you of salvation, and that of God.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> For unto you it is given in the behalf of Christ, not only to believe on him, but also to suffer for his sake;</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Having the same conflict which ye saw in me, and now hear to be in me.</p>
