## Zechariah 2

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> I lifted up mine eyes again, and looked, and behold a man with a measuring line in his hand.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Then said I, Whither goest thou? And he said unto me, To measure Jerusalem, to see what is the breadth thereof, and what is the length thereof.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And, behold, the angel that talked with me went forth, and another angel went out to meet him,</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And said unto him, Run, speak to this young man, saying, Jerusalem shall be inhabited as towns without walls for the multitude of men and cattle therein:</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> For I, saith the LORD, will be unto her a wall of fire round about, and will be the glory in the midst of her.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Ho, ho, come forth, and flee from the land of the north, saith the LORD: for I have spread you abroad as the four winds of the heaven, saith the LORD.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Deliver thyself, O Zion, that dwellest with the daughter of Babylon.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> For thus saith the LORD of hosts; After the glory hath he sent me unto the nations which spoiled you: for he that toucheth you toucheth the apple of his eye.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> For, behold, I will shake mine hand upon them, and they shall be a spoil to their servants: and ye shall know that the LORD of hosts hath sent me.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Sing and rejoice, O daughter of Zion: for, lo, I come, and I will dwell in the midst of thee, saith the LORD.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And many nations shall be joined to the LORD in that day, and shall be my people: and I will dwell in the midst of thee, and thou shalt know that the LORD of hosts hath sent me unto thee.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And the LORD shall inherit Judah his portion in the holy land, and shall choose Jerusalem again.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Be silent, O all flesh, before the LORD: for he is raised up out of his holy habitation.</p>
