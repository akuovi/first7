## Job 12

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And Job answered and said,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> No doubt but ye are the people, and wisdom shall die with you.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> But I have understanding as well as you; I am not inferior to you: yea, who knoweth not such things as these?</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> I am as one mocked of his neighbour, who calleth upon God, and he answereth him: the just upright man is laughed to scorn.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> He that is ready to slip with his feet is as a lamp despised in the thought of him that is at ease.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> The tabernacles of robbers prosper, and they that provoke God are secure; into whose hand God bringeth abundantly.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> But ask now the beasts, and they shall teach thee; and the fowls of the air, and they shall tell thee:</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Or speak to the earth, and it shall teach thee: and the fishes of the sea shall declare unto thee.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Who knoweth not in all these that the hand of the LORD hath wrought this?</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> In whose hand is the soul of every living thing, and the breath of all mankind.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Doth not the ear try words? and the mouth taste his meat?</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> With the ancient is wisdom; and in length of days understanding.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> With him is wisdom and strength, he hath counsel and understanding.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Behold, he breaketh down, and it cannot be built again: he shutteth up a man, and there can be no opening.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Behold, he withholdeth the waters, and they dry up: also he sendeth them out, and they overturn the earth.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> With him is strength and wisdom: the deceived and the deceiver are his.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> He leadeth counsellors away spoiled, and maketh the judges fools.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> He looseth the bond of kings, and girdeth their loins with a girdle.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> He leadeth princes away spoiled, and overthroweth the mighty.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> He removeth away the speech of the trusty, and taketh away the understanding of the aged.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> He poureth contempt upon princes, and weakeneth the strength of the mighty.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> He discovereth deep things out of darkness, and bringeth out to light the shadow of death.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> He increaseth the nations, and destroyeth them: he enlargeth the nations, and straiteneth them again.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> He taketh away the heart of the chief of the people of the earth, and causeth them to wander in a wilderness where there is no way.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> They grope in the dark without light, and he maketh them to stagger like a drunken man.</p>
