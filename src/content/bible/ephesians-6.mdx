## Ephesians 6

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Children, obey your parents in the Lord: for this is right.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Honour thy father and mother; (which is the first commandment with promise;)</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> That it may be well with thee, and thou mayest live long on the earth.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And, ye fathers, provoke not your children to wrath: but bring them up in the nurture and admonition of the Lord.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Servants, be obedient to them that are your masters according to the flesh, with fear and trembling, in singleness of your heart, as unto Christ;</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Not with eyeservice, as menpleasers; but as the servants of Christ, doing the will of God from the heart;</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> With good will doing service, as to the Lord, and not to men:</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Knowing that whatsoever good thing any man doeth, the same shall he receive of the Lord, whether he be bond or free.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And, ye masters, do the same things unto them, forbearing threatening: knowing that your Master also is in heaven; neither is there respect of persons with him.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Finally, my brethren, be strong in the Lord, and in the power of his might.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Put on the whole armour of God, that ye may be able to stand against the wiles of the devil.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> For we wrestle not against flesh and blood, but against principalities, against powers, against the rulers of the darkness of this world, against spiritual wickedness in high places.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Wherefore take unto you the whole armour of God, that ye may be able to withstand in the evil day, and having done all, to stand.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Stand therefore, having your loins girt about with truth, and having on the breastplate of righteousness;</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And your feet shod with the preparation of the gospel of peace;</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Above all, taking the shield of faith, wherewith ye shall be able to quench all the fiery darts of the wicked.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And take the helmet of salvation, and the sword of the Spirit, which is the word of God:</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Praying always with all prayer and supplication in the Spirit, and watching thereunto with all perseverance and supplication for all saints;</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And for me, that utterance may be given unto me, that I may open my mouth boldly, to make known the mystery of the gospel,</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> For which I am an ambassador in bonds: that therein I may speak boldly, as I ought to speak.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> But that ye also may know my affairs, and how I do, Tychicus, a beloved brother and faithful minister in the Lord, shall make known to you all things:</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Whom I have sent unto you for the same purpose, that ye might know our affairs, and that he might comfort your hearts.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Peace be to the brethren, and love with faith, from God the Father and the Lord Jesus Christ.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Grace be with all them that love our Lord Jesus Christ in sincerity. Amen.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Written from Rome unto the Ephesians by Tychicus.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> EPHESIANS</p>
