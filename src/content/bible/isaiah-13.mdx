## Isaiah 13

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> The burden of Babylon, which Isaiah the son of Amoz did see.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Lift ye up a banner upon the high mountain, exalt the voice unto them, shake the hand, that they may go into the gates of the nobles.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> I have commanded my sanctified ones, I have also called my mighty ones for mine anger, even them that rejoice in my highness.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> The noise of a multitude in the mountains, like as of a great people; a tumultuous noise of the kingdoms of nations gathered together: the LORD of hosts mustereth the host of the battle.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> They come from a far country, from the end of heaven, even the LORD, and the weapons of his indignation, to destroy the whole land.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Howl ye; for the day of the LORD is at hand; it shall come as a destruction from the Almighty.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Therefore shall all hands be faint, and every man's heart shall melt:</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And they shall be afraid: pangs and sorrows shall take hold of them; they shall be in pain as a woman that travaileth: they shall be amazed one at another; their faces shall be as flames.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Behold, the day of the LORD cometh, cruel both with wrath and fierce anger, to lay the land desolate: and he shall destroy the sinners thereof out of it.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> For the stars of heaven and the constellations thereof shall not give their light: the sun shall be darkened in his going forth, and the moon shall not cause her light to shine.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And I will punish the world for their evil, and the wicked for their iniquity; and I will cause the arrogancy of the proud to cease, and will lay low the haughtiness of the terrible.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> I will make a man more precious than fine gold; even a man than the golden wedge of Ophir.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Therefore I will shake the heavens, and the earth shall remove out of her place, in the wrath of the LORD of hosts, and in the day of his fierce anger.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And it shall be as the chased roe, and as a sheep that no man taketh up: they shall every man turn to his own people, and flee every one into his own land.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Every one that is found shall be thrust through; and every one that is joined unto them shall fall by the sword.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Their children also shall be dashed to pieces before their eyes; their houses shall be spoiled, and their wives ravished.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Behold, I will stir up the Medes against them, which shall not regard silver; and as for gold, they shall not delight in it.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Their bows also shall dash the young men to pieces; and they shall have no pity on the fruit of the womb; their eye shall not spare children.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And Babylon, the glory of kingdoms, the beauty of the Chaldees' excellency, shall be as when God overthrew Sodom and Gomorrah.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> It shall never be inhabited, neither shall it be dwelt in from generation to generation: neither shall the Arabian pitch tent there; neither shall the shepherds make their fold there.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> But wild beasts of the desert shall lie there; and their houses shall be full of doleful creatures; and owls shall dwell there, and satyrs shall dance there.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And the wild beasts of the islands shall cry in their desolate houses, and dragons in their pleasant palaces: and her time is near to come, and her days shall not be prolonged.</p>
