## Proverbs 14

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Every wise woman buildeth her house: but the foolish plucketh it down with her hands.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> He that walketh in his uprightness feareth the LORD: but he that is perverse in his ways despiseth him.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> In the mouth of the foolish is a rod of pride: but the lips of the wise shall preserve them.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Where no oxen are, the crib is clean: but much increase is by the strength of the ox.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> A faithful witness will not lie: but a false witness will utter lies.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> A scorner seeketh wisdom, and findeth it not: but knowledge is easy unto him that understandeth.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Go from the presence of a foolish man, when thou perceivest not in him the lips of knowledge.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> The wisdom of the prudent is to understand his way: but the folly of fools is deceit.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Fools make a mock at sin: but among the righteous there is favour.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> The heart knoweth his own bitterness; and a stranger doth not intermeddle with his joy.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> The house of the wicked shall be overthrown: but the tabernacle of the upright shall flourish.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> There is a way which seemeth right unto a man, but the end thereof are the ways of death.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Even in laughter the heart is sorrowful; and the end of that mirth is heaviness.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> The backslider in heart shall be filled with his own ways: and a good man shall be satisfied from himself.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> The simple believeth every word: but the prudent man looketh well to his going.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> A wise man feareth, and departeth from evil: but the fool rageth, and is confident.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> He that is soon angry dealeth foolishly: and a man of wicked devices is hated.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> The simple inherit folly: but the prudent are crowned with knowledge.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> The evil bow before the good; and the wicked at the gates of the righteous.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> The poor is hated even of his own neighbour: but the rich hath many friends.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> He that despiseth his neighbour sinneth: but he that hath mercy on the poor, happy is he.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Do they not err that devise evil? but mercy and truth shall be to them that devise good.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> In all labour there is profit: but the talk of the lips tendeth only to penury.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> The crown of the wise is their riches: but the foolishness of fools is folly.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> A true witness delivereth souls: but a deceitful witness speaketh lies.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> In the fear of the LORD is strong confidence: and his children shall have a place of refuge.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> The fear of the LORD is a fountain of life, to depart from the snares of death.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> In the multitude of people is the king's honour: but in the want of people is the destruction of the prince.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> He that is slow to wrath is of great understanding: but he that is hasty of spirit exalteth folly.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> A sound heart is the life of the flesh: but envy the rottenness of the bones.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> He that oppresseth the poor reproacheth his Maker: but he that honoureth him hath mercy on the poor.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> The wicked is driven away in his wickedness: but the righteous hath hope in his death.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> Wisdom resteth in the heart of him that hath understanding: but that which is in the midst of fools is made known.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> Righteousness exalteth a nation: but sin is a reproach to any people.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> The king's favour is toward a wise servant: but his wrath is against him that causeth shame.</p>
