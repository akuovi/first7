## Luke 24

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Now upon the first day of the week, very early in the morning, they came unto the sepulchre, bringing the spices which they had prepared, and certain others with them.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And they found the stone rolled away from the sepulchre.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And they entered in, and found not the body of the Lord Jesus.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And it came to pass, as they were much perplexed thereabout, behold, two men stood by them in shining garments:</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And as they were afraid, and bowed down their faces to the earth, they said unto them, Why seek ye the living among the dead?</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> He is not here, but is risen: remember how he spake unto you when he was yet in Galilee,</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Saying, The Son of man must be delivered into the hands of sinful men, and be crucified, and the third day rise again.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And they remembered his words,</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And returned from the sepulchre, and told all these things unto the eleven, and to all the rest.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> It was Mary Magdalene, and Joanna, and Mary the mother of James, and other women that were with them, which told these things unto the apostles.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And their words seemed to them as idle tales, and they believed them not.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Then arose Peter, and ran unto the sepulchre; and stooping down, he beheld the linen clothes laid by themselves, and departed, wondering in himself at that which was come to pass.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And, behold, two of them went that same day to a village called Emmaus, which was from Jerusalem about threescore furlongs.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And they talked together of all these things which had happened.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And it came to pass, that, while they communed together and reasoned, Jesus himself drew near, and went with them.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> But their eyes were holden that they should not know him.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And he said unto them, What manner of communications are these that ye have one to another, as ye walk, and are sad?</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And the one of them, whose name was Cleopas, answering said unto him, Art thou only a stranger in Jerusalem, and hast not known the things which are come to pass there in these days?</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And he said unto them, What things? And they said unto him, Concerning Jesus of Nazareth, which was a prophet mighty in deed and word before God and all the people:</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And how the chief priests and our rulers delivered him to be condemned to death, and have crucified him.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> But we trusted that it had been he which should have redeemed Israel: and beside all this, to day is the third day since these things were done.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Yea, and certain women also of our company made us astonished, which were early at the sepulchre;</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And when they found not his body, they came, saying, that they had also seen a vision of angels, which said that he was alive.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And certain of them which were with us went to the sepulchre, and found it even so as the women had said: but him they saw not.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Then he said unto them, O fools, and slow of heart to believe all that the prophets have spoken:</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Ought not Christ to have suffered these things, and to enter into his glory?</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And beginning at Moses and all the prophets, he expounded unto them in all the scriptures the things concerning himself.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And they drew nigh unto the village, whither they went: and he made as though he would have gone further.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> But they constrained him, saying, Abide with us: for it is toward evening, and the day is far spent. And he went in to tarry with them.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And it came to pass, as he sat at meat with them, he took bread, and blessed it, and brake, and gave to them.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> And their eyes were opened, and they knew him; and he vanished out of their sight.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> And they said one to another, Did not our heart burn within us, while he talked with us by the way, and while he opened to us the scriptures?</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> And they rose up the same hour, and returned to Jerusalem, and found the eleven gathered together, and them that were with them,</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> Saying, The Lord is risen indeed, and hath appeared to Simon.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> And they told what things were done in the way, and how he was known of them in breaking of bread.</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> And as they thus spake, Jesus himself stood in the midst of them, and saith unto them, Peace be unto you.</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> But they were terrified and affrighted, and supposed that they had seen a spirit.</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> And he said unto them, Why are ye troubled? and why do thoughts arise in your hearts?</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> Behold my hands and my feet, that it is I myself: handle me, and see; for a spirit hath not flesh and bones, as ye see me have.</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> And when he had thus spoken, he shewed them his hands and his feet.</p>

<p id="v41" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">41</span> And while they yet believed not for joy, and wondered, he said unto them, Have ye here any meat?</p>

<p id="v42" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">42</span> And they gave him a piece of a broiled fish, and of an honeycomb.</p>

<p id="v43" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">43</span> And he took it, and did eat before them.</p>

<p id="v44" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">44</span> And he said unto them, These are the words which I spake unto you, while I was yet with you, that all things must be fulfilled, which were written in the law of Moses, and in the prophets, and in the psalms, concerning me.</p>

<p id="v45" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">45</span> Then opened he their understanding, that they might understand the scriptures,</p>

<p id="v46" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">46</span> And said unto them, Thus it is written, and thus it behoved Christ to suffer, and to rise from the dead the third day:</p>

<p id="v47" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">47</span> And that repentance and remission of sins should be preached in his name among all nations, beginning at Jerusalem.</p>

<p id="v48" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">48</span> And ye are witnesses of these things.</p>

<p id="v49" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">49</span> And, behold, I send the promise of my Father upon you: but tarry ye in the city of Jerusalem, until ye be endued with power from on high.</p>

<p id="v50" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">50</span> And he led them out as far as to Bethany, and he lifted up his hands, and blessed them.</p>

<p id="v51" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">51</span> And it came to pass, while he blessed them, he was parted from them, and carried up into heaven.</p>

<p id="v52" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">52</span> And they worshipped him, and returned to Jerusalem with great joy:</p>

<p id="v53" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">53</span> And were continually in the temple, praising and blessing God. Amen.</p>

<p id="v54" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">54</span> LUKE</p>
