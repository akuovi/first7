## Isaiah 43

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> But now thus saith the LORD that created thee, O Jacob, and he that formed thee, O Israel, Fear not: for I have redeemed thee, I have called thee by thy name; thou art mine.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> When thou passest through the waters, I will be with thee; and through the rivers, they shall not overflow thee: when thou walkest through the fire, thou shalt not be burned; neither shall the flame kindle upon thee.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For I am the LORD thy God, the Holy One of Israel, thy Saviour: I gave Egypt for thy ransom, Ethiopia and Seba for thee.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Since thou wast precious in my sight, thou hast been honourable, and I have loved thee: therefore will I give men for thee, and people for thy life.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Fear not: for I am with thee: I will bring thy seed from the east, and gather thee from the west;</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> I will say to the north, Give up; and to the south, Keep not back: bring my sons from far, and my daughters from the ends of the earth;</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Even every one that is called by my name: for I have created him for my glory, I have formed him; yea, I have made him.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Bring forth the blind people that have eyes, and the deaf that have ears.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Let all the nations be gathered together, and let the people be assembled: who among them can declare this, and shew us former things? let them bring forth their witnesses, that they may be justified: or let them hear, and say, It is truth.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Ye are my witnesses, saith the LORD, and my servant whom I have chosen: that ye may know and believe me, and understand that I am he: before me there was no God formed, neither shall there be after me.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> I, even I, am the LORD; and beside me there is no saviour.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> I have declared, and have saved, and I have shewed, when there was no strange god among you: therefore ye are my witnesses, saith the LORD, that I am God.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Yea, before the day was I am he; and there is none that can deliver out of my hand: I will work, and who shall let it?</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Thus saith the LORD, your redeemer, the Holy One of Israel; For your sake I have sent to Babylon, and have brought down all their nobles, and the Chaldeans, whose cry is in the ships.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> I am the LORD, your Holy One, the creator of Israel, your King.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Thus saith the LORD, which maketh a way in the sea, and a path in the mighty waters;</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Which bringeth forth the chariot and horse, the army and the power; they shall lie down together, they shall not rise: they are extinct, they are quenched as tow.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Remember ye not the former things, neither consider the things of old.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Behold, I will do a new thing; now it shall spring forth; shall ye not know it? I will even make a way in the wilderness, and rivers in the desert.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> The beast of the field shall honour me, the dragons and the owls: because I give waters in the wilderness, and rivers in the desert, to give drink to my people, my chosen.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> This people have I formed for myself; they shall shew forth my praise.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> But thou hast not called upon me, O Jacob; but thou hast been weary of me, O Israel.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Thou hast not brought me the small cattle of thy burnt offerings; neither hast thou honoured me with thy sacrifices. I have not caused thee to serve with an offering, nor wearied thee with incense.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Thou hast bought me no sweet cane with money, neither hast thou filled me with the fat of thy sacrifices: but thou hast made me to serve with thy sins, thou hast wearied me with thine iniquities.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> I, even I, am he that blotteth out thy transgressions for mine own sake, and will not remember thy sins.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Put me in remembrance: let us plead together: declare thou, that thou mayest be justified.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Thy first father hath sinned, and thy teachers have transgressed against me.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Therefore I have profaned the princes of the sanctuary, and have given Jacob to the curse, and Israel to reproaches.</p>
