## Exodus 6

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Then the LORD said unto Moses, Now shalt thou see what I will do to Pharaoh: for with a strong hand shall he let them go, and with a strong hand shall he drive them out of his land.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And God spake unto Moses, and said unto him, I am the LORD:</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And I appeared unto Abraham, unto Isaac, and unto Jacob, by the name of God Almighty, but by my name JEHOVAH was I not known to them.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And I have also established my covenant with them, to give them the land of Canaan, the land of their pilgrimage, wherein they were strangers.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And I have also heard the groaning of the children of Israel, whom the Egyptians keep in bondage; and I have remembered my covenant.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Wherefore say unto the children of Israel, I am the LORD, and I will bring you out from under the burdens of the Egyptians, and I will rid you out of their bondage, and I will redeem you with a stretched out arm, and with great judgments:</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And I will take you to me for a people, and I will be to you a God: and ye shall know that I am the LORD your God, which bringeth you out from under the burdens of the Egyptians.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And I will bring you in unto the land, concerning the which I did swear to give it to Abraham, to Isaac, and to Jacob; and I will give it you for an heritage: I am the LORD.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And Moses spake so unto the children of Israel: but they hearkened not unto Moses for anguish of spirit, and for cruel bondage.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying,</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Go in, speak unto Pharaoh king of Egypt, that he let the children of Israel go out of his land.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And Moses spake before the LORD, saying, Behold, the children of Israel have not hearkened unto me; how then shall Pharaoh hear me, who am of uncircumcised lips?</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And the LORD spake unto Moses and unto Aaron, and gave them a charge unto the children of Israel, and unto Pharaoh king of Egypt, to bring the children of Israel out of the land of Egypt.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> These be the heads of their fathers' houses: The sons of Reuben the firstborn of Israel; Hanoch, and Pallu, Hezron, and Carmi: these be the families of Reuben.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And the sons of Simeon; Jemuel, and Jamin, and Ohad, and Jachin, and Zohar, and Shaul the son of a Canaanitish woman: these are the families of Simeon.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And these are the names of the sons of Levi according to their generations; Gershon, and Kohath, and Merari: and the years of the life of Levi were an hundred thirty and seven years.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> The sons of Gershon; Libni, and Shimi, according to their families.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And the sons of Kohath; Amram, and Izhar, and Hebron, and Uzziel: and the years of the life of Kohath were an hundred thirty and three years.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And the sons of Merari; Mahali and Mushi: these are the families of Levi according to their generations.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And Amram took him Jochebed his father's sister to wife; and she bare him Aaron and Moses: and the years of the life of Amram were an hundred and thirty and seven years.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And the sons of Izhar; Korah, and Nepheg, and Zichri.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And the sons of Uzziel; Mishael, and Elzaphan, and Zithri.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And Aaron took him Elisheba, daughter of Amminadab, sister of Naashon, to wife; and she bare him Nadab, and Abihu, Eleazar, and Ithamar.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And the sons of Korah; Assir, and Elkanah, and Abiasaph: these are the families of the Korhites.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And Eleazar Aaron's son took him one of the daughters of Putiel to wife; and she bare him Phinehas: these are the heads of the fathers of the Levites according to their families.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> These are that Aaron and Moses, to whom the LORD said, Bring out the children of Israel from the land of Egypt according to their armies.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> These are they which spake to Pharaoh king of Egypt, to bring out the children of Israel from Egypt: these are that Moses and Aaron.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And it came to pass on the day when the LORD spake unto Moses in the land of Egypt,</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> That the LORD spake unto Moses, saying, I am the LORD: speak thou unto Pharaoh king of Egypt all that I say unto thee.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And Moses said before the LORD, Behold, I am of uncircumcised lips, and how shall Pharaoh hearken unto me?</p>
