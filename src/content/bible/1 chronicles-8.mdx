## 1 Chronicles 8

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Now Benjamin begat Bela his firstborn, Ashbel the second, and Aharah the third,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Nohah the fourth, and Rapha the fifth.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And the sons of Bela were, Addar, and Gera, and Abihud,</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And Abishua, and Naaman, and Ahoah,</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And Gera, and Shephuphan, and Huram.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And these are the sons of Ehud: these are the heads of the fathers of the inhabitants of Geba, and they removed them to Manahath:</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And Naaman, and Ahiah, and Gera, he removed them, and begat Uzza, and Ahihud.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And Shaharaim begat children in the country of Moab, after he had sent them away; Hushim and Baara were his wives.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And he begat of Hodesh his wife, Jobab, and Zibia, and Mesha, and Malcham,</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And Jeuz, and Shachia, and Mirma. These were his sons, heads of the fathers.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And of Hushim he begat Abitub, and Elpaal.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> The sons of Elpaal; Eber, and Misham, and Shamed, who built Ono, and Lod, with the towns thereof:</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Beriah also, and Shema, who were heads of the fathers of the inhabitants of Aijalon, who drove away the inhabitants of Gath:</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And Ahio, Shashak, and Jeremoth,</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And Zebadiah, and Arad, and Ader,</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And Michael, and Ispah, and Joha, the sons of Beriah;</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And Zebadiah, and Meshullam, and Hezeki, and Heber,</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Ishmerai also, and Jezliah, and Jobab, the sons of Elpaal;</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And Jakim, and Zichri, and Zabdi,</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And Elienai, and Zilthai, and Eliel,</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And Adaiah, and Beraiah, and Shimrath, the sons of Shimhi;</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And Ishpan, and Heber, and Eliel,</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And Abdon, and Zichri, and Hanan,</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And Hananiah, and Elam, and Antothijah,</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And Iphedeiah, and Penuel, the sons of Shashak;</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And Shamsherai, and Shehariah, and Athaliah,</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And Jaresiah, and Eliah, and Zichri, the sons of Jeroham.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> These were heads of the fathers, by their generations, chief men. These dwelt in Jerusalem.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And at Gibeon dwelt the father of Gibeon; whose wife's name was Maachah:</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And his firstborn son Abdon, and Zur, and Kish, and Baal, and Nadab,</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> And Gedor, and Ahio, and Zacher.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> And Mikloth begat Shimeah. And these also dwelt with their brethren in Jerusalem, over against them.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> And Ner begat Kish, and Kish begat Saul, and Saul begat Jonathan, and Malchi-shua, and Abinadab, and Esh-baal.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> And the son of Jonathan was Merib-baal; and Merib-baal begat Micah.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> And the sons of Micah were, Pithon, and Melech, and Tarea, and Ahaz.</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> And Ahaz begat Jehoadah; and Jehoadah begat Alemeth, and Azmaveth, and Zimri; and Zimri begat Moza,</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> And Moza begat Binea: Rapha was his son, Eleasah his son, Azel his son:</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> And Azel had six sons, whose names are these, Azrikam, Bocheru, and Ishmael, and Sheariah, and Obadiah, and Hanan. All these were the sons of Azel.</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> And the sons of Eshek his brother were, Ulam his firstborn, Jehush the second, and Eliphelet the third.</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> And the sons of Ulam were mighty men of valour, archers, and had many sons, and sons' sons, an hundred and fifty. All these are of the sons of Benjamin.</p>
