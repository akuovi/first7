## Exodus 31

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> See, I have called by name Bezaleel the son of Uri, the son of Hur, of the tribe of Judah:</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And I have filled him with the spirit of God, in wisdom, and in understanding, and in knowledge, and in all manner of workmanship,</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> To devise cunning works, to work in gold, and in silver, and in brass,</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And in cutting of stones, to set them, and in carving of timber, to work in all manner of workmanship.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And I, behold, I have given with him Aholiab, the son of Ahisamach, of the tribe of Dan: and in the hearts of all that are wise hearted I have put wisdom, that they may make all that I have commanded thee;</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> The tabernacle of the congregation, and the ark of the testimony, and the mercy seat that is thereupon, and all the furniture of the tabernacle,</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And the table and his furniture, and the pure candlestick with all his furniture, and the altar of incense,</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And the altar of burnt offering with all his furniture, and the laver and his foot,</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And the cloths of service, and the holy garments for Aaron the priest, and the garments of his sons, to minister in the priest's office,</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And the anointing oil, and sweet incense for the holy place: according to all that I have commanded thee shall they do.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying,</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Speak thou also unto the children of Israel, saying, Verily my sabbaths ye shall keep: for it is a sign between me and you throughout your generations; that ye may know that I am the LORD that doth sanctify you.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Ye shall keep the sabbath therefore; for it is holy unto you: every one that defileth it shall surely be put to death: for whosoever doeth any work therein, that soul shall be cut off from among his people.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Six days may work be done; but in the seventh is the sabbath of rest, holy to the LORD: whosoever doeth any work in the sabbath day, he shall surely be put to death.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Wherefore the children of Israel shall keep the sabbath, to observe the sabbath throughout their generations, for a perpetual covenant.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> It is a sign between me and the children of Israel for ever: for in six days the LORD made heaven and earth, and on the seventh day he rested, and was refreshed.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And he gave unto Moses, when he had made an end of communing with him upon mount Sinai, two tables of testimony, tables of stone, written with the finger of God.</p>
