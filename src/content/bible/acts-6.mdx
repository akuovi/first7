## Acts 6

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And in those days, when the number of the disciples was multiplied, there arose a murmuring of the Grecians against the Hebrews, because their widows were neglected in the daily ministration.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Then the twelve called the multitude of the disciples unto them, and said, It is not reason that we should leave the word of God, and serve tables.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Wherefore, brethren, look ye out among you seven men of honest report, full of the Holy Ghost and wisdom, whom we may appoint over this business.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> But we will give ourselves continually to prayer, and to the ministry of the word.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And the saying pleased the whole multitude: and they chose Stephen, a man full of faith and of the Holy Ghost, and Philip, and Prochorus, and Nicanor, and Timon, and Parmenas, and Nicolas a proselyte of Antioch:</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Whom they set before the apostles: and when they had prayed, they laid their hands on them.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And the word of God increased; and the number of the disciples multiplied in Jerusalem greatly; and a great company of the priests were obedient to the faith.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And Stephen, full of faith and power, did great wonders and miracles among the people.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Then there arose certain of the synagogue, which is called the synagogue of the Libertines, and Cyrenians, and Alexandrians, and of them of Cilicia and of Asia, disputing with Stephen.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And they were not able to resist the wisdom and the spirit by which he spake.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Then they suborned men, which said, We have heard him speak blasphemous words against Moses, and against God.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And they stirred up the people, and the elders, and the scribes, and came upon him, and caught him, and brought him to the council,</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And set up false witnesses, which said, This man ceaseth not to speak blasphemous words against this holy place, and the law:</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> For we have heard him say, that this Jesus of Nazareth shall destroy this place, and shall change the customs which Moses delivered us.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And all that sat in the council, looking stedfastly on him, saw his face as it had been the face of an angel.</p>
