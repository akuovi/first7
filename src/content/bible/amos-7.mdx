## Amos 7

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Thus hath the Lord GOD shewed unto me; and, behold, he formed grasshoppers in the beginning of the shooting up of the latter growth; and, lo, it was the latter growth after the king's mowings.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And it came to pass, that when they had made an end of eating the grass of the land, then I said, O Lord GOD, forgive, I beseech thee: by whom shall Jacob arise? for he is small.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> The LORD repented for this: It shall not be, saith the LORD.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Thus hath the Lord GOD shewed unto me: and, behold, the Lord GOD called to contend by fire, and it devoured the great deep, and did eat up a part.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Then said I, O Lord GOD, cease, I beseech thee: by whom shall Jacob arise? for he is small.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> The LORD repented for this: This also shall not be, saith the Lord GOD.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Thus he shewed me: and, behold, the Lord stood upon a wall made by a plumbline, with a plumbline in his hand.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And the LORD said unto me, Amos, what seest thou? And I said, A plumbline. Then said the Lord, Behold, I will set a plumbline in the midst of my people Israel: I will not again pass by them any more:</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And the high places of Isaac shall be desolate, and the sanctuaries of Israel shall be laid waste; and I will rise against the house of Jeroboam with the sword.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Then Amaziah the priest of Beth-el sent to Jeroboam king of Israel, saying, Amos hath conspired against thee in the midst of the house of Israel: the land is not able to bear all his words.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> For thus Amos saith, Jeroboam shall die by the sword, and Israel shall surely be led away captive out of their own land.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Also Amaziah said unto Amos, O thou seer, go, flee thee away into the land of Judah, and there eat bread, and prophesy there:</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> But prophesy not again any more at Beth-el: for it is the king's chapel, and it is the king's court.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Then answered Amos, and said to Amaziah, I was no prophet, neither was I a prophet's son; but I was an herdman, and a gatherer of sycomore fruit:</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And the LORD took me as I followed the flock, and the LORD said unto me, Go, prophesy unto my people Israel.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Now therefore hear thou the word of the LORD: Thou sayest, Prophesy not against Israel, and drop not thy word against the house of Isaac.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Therefore thus saith the LORD; Thy wife shall be an harlot in the city, and thy sons and thy daughters shall fall by the sword, and thy land shall be divided by line; and thou shalt die in a polluted land: and Israel shall surely go into captivity forth of his land.</p>
