## Hosea 5

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Hear ye this, O priests; and hearken, ye house of Israel; and give ye ear, O house of the king; for judgment is toward you, because ye have been a snare on Mizpah, and a net spread upon Tabor.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And the revolters are profound to make slaughter, though I have been a rebuker of them all.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> I know Ephraim, and Israel is not hid from me: for now, O Ephraim, thou committest whoredom, and Israel is defiled.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> They will not frame their doings to turn unto their God: for the spirit of whoredoms is in the midst of them, and they have not known the LORD.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And the pride of Israel doth testify to his face: therefore shall Israel and Ephraim fall in their iniquity; Judah also shall fall with them.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> They shall go with their flocks and with their herds to seek the LORD; but they shall not find him; he hath withdrawn himself from them.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> They have dealt treacherously against the LORD: for they have begotten strange children: now shall a month devour them with their portions.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Blow ye the cornet in Gibeah, and the trumpet in Ramah: cry aloud at Beth-aven, after thee, O Benjamin.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Ephraim shall be desolate in the day of rebuke: among the tribes of Israel have I made known that which shall surely be.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> The princes of Judah were like them that remove the bound: therefore I will pour out my wrath upon them like water.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Ephraim is oppressed and broken in judgment, because he willingly walked after the commandment.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Therefore will I be unto Ephraim as a moth, and to the house of Judah as rottenness.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> When Ephraim saw his sickness, and Judah saw his wound, then went Ephraim to the Assyrian, and sent to king Jareb: yet could he not heal you, nor cure you of your wound.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> For I will be unto Ephraim as a lion, and as a young lion to the house of Judah: I, even I, will tear and go away; I will take away, and none shall rescue him.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> I will go and return to my place, till they acknowledge their offence, and seek my face: in their affliction they will seek me early.</p>
