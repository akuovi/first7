## Ezekiel 21

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And the word of the LORD came unto me, saying,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Son of man, set thy face toward Jerusalem, and drop thy word toward the holy places, and prophesy against the land of Israel,</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And say to the land of Israel, Thus saith the LORD; Behold, I am against thee, and will draw forth my sword out of his sheath, and will cut off from thee the righteous and the wicked.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Seeing then that I will cut off from thee the righteous and the wicked, therefore shall my sword go forth out of his sheath against all flesh from the south to the north:</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> That all flesh may know that I the LORD have drawn forth my sword out of his sheath: it shall not return any more.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Sigh therefore, thou son of man, with the breaking of thy loins; and with bitterness sigh before their eyes.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And it shall be, when they say unto thee, Wherefore sighest thou? that thou shalt answer, For the tidings; because it cometh: and every heart shall melt, and all hands shall be feeble, and every spirit shall faint, and all knees shall be weak as water: behold, it cometh, and shall be brought to pass, saith the Lord GOD.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Again the word of the LORD came unto me, saying,</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Son of man, prophesy, and say, Thus saith the LORD; Say, A sword, a sword is sharpened, and also furbished:</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> It is sharpened to make a sore slaughter; it is furbished that it may glitter: should we then make mirth? it contemneth the rod of my son, as every tree.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And he hath given it to be furbished, that it may be handled: this sword is sharpened, and it is furbished, to give it into the hand of the slayer.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Cry and howl, son of man: for it shall be upon my people, it shall be upon all the princes of Israel: terrors by reason of the sword shall be upon my people: smite therefore upon thy thigh.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Because it is a trial, and what if the sword contemn even the rod? it shall be no more, saith the Lord GOD.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Thou therefore, son of man, prophesy, and smite thine hands together, and let the sword be doubled the third time, the sword of the slain: it is the sword of the great men that are slain, which entereth into their privy chambers.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> I have set the point of the sword against all their gates, that their heart may faint, and their ruins be multiplied: ah! it is made bright, it is wrapped up for the slaughter.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Go thee one way or other, either on the right hand, or on the left, whithersoever thy face is set.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> I will also smite mine hands together, and I will cause my fury to rest: I the LORD have said it.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> The word of the LORD came unto me again, saying,</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Also, thou son of man, appoint thee two ways, that the sword of the king of Babylon may come: both twain shall come forth out of one land: and choose thou a place, choose it at the head of the way to the city.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Appoint a way, that the sword may come to Rabbath of the Ammonites, and to Judah in Jerusalem the defenced.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> For the king of Babylon stood at the parting of the way, at the head of the two ways, to use divination: he made his arrows bright, he consulted with images, he looked in the liver.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> At his right hand was the divination for Jerusalem, to appoint captains, to open the mouth in the slaughter, to lift up the voice with shouting, to appoint battering rams against the gates, to cast a mount, and to build a fort.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And it shall be unto them as a false divination in their sight, to them that have sworn oaths: but he will call to remembrance the iniquity, that they may be taken.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Therefore thus saith the Lord GOD; Because ye have made your iniquity to be remembered, in that your transgressions are discovered, so that in all your doings your sins do appear; because, I say, that ye are come to remembrance, ye shall be taken with the hand.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And thou, profane wicked prince of Israel, whose day is come, when iniquity shall have an end,</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Thus saith the Lord GOD; Remove the diadem, and take off the crown: this shall not be the same: exalt him that is low, and abase him that is high.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> I will overturn, overturn, overturn, it: and it shall be no more, until he come whose right it is; and I will give it him.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And thou, son of man, prophesy and say, Thus saith the Lord GOD concerning the Ammonites, and concerning their reproach; even say thou, The sword, the sword is drawn: for the slaughter it is furbished, to consume because of the glittering:</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Whiles they see vanity unto thee, whiles they divine a lie unto thee, to bring thee upon the necks of them that are slain, of the wicked, whose day is come, when their iniquity shall have an end.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Shall I cause it to return into his sheath? I will judge thee in the place where thou wast created, in the land of thy nativity.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> And I will pour out mine indignation upon thee, I will blow against thee in the fire of my wrath, and deliver thee into the hand of brutish men, and skilful to destroy.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> Thou shalt be for fuel to the fire; thy blood shall be in the midst of the land; thou shalt be no more remembered: for I the LORD have spoken it.</p>
