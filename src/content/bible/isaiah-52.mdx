## Isaiah 52

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Awake, awake; put on thy strength, O Zion; put on thy beautiful garments, O Jerusalem, the holy city: for henceforth there shall no more come into thee the uncircumcised and the unclean.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Shake thyself from the dust; arise, and sit down, O Jerusalem: loose thyself from the bands of thy neck, O captive daughter of Zion.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For thus saith the LORD, Ye have sold yourselves for nought; and ye shall be redeemed without money.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> For thus saith the Lord GOD, My people went down aforetime into Egypt to sojourn there; and the Assyrian oppressed them without cause.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Now therefore, what have I here, saith the LORD, that my people is taken away for nought? they that rule over them make them to howl, saith the LORD; and my name continually every day is blasphemed.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Therefore my people shall know my name: therefore they shall know in that day that I am he that doth speak: behold, it is I.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> How beautiful upon the mountains are the feet of him that bringeth good tidings, that publisheth peace; that bringeth good tidings of good, that publisheth salvation; that saith unto Zion, Thy God reigneth!</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Thy watchmen shall lift up the voice; with the voice together shall they sing: for they shall see eye to eye, when the LORD shall bring again Zion.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Break forth into joy, sing together, ye waste places of Jerusalem: for the LORD hath comforted his people, he hath redeemed Jerusalem.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> The LORD hath made bare his holy arm in the eyes of all the nations; and all the ends of the earth shall see the salvation of our God.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Depart ye, depart ye, go ye out from thence, touch no unclean thing; go ye out of the midst of her; be ye clean, that bear the vessels of the LORD.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> For ye shall not go out with haste, nor go by flight: for the LORD will go before you; and the God of Israel will be your rereward.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Behold, my servant shall deal prudently, he shall be exalted and extolled, and be very high.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> As many were astonied at thee; his visage was so marred more than any man, and his form more than the sons of men:</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> So shall he sprinkle many nations; the kings shall shut their mouths at him: for that which had not been told them shall they see; and that which they had not heard shall they consider.</p>
