## Revelation 2

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Unto the angel of the church of Ephesus write; These things saith he that holdeth the seven stars in his right hand, who walketh in the midst of the seven golden candlesticks;</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> I know thy works, and thy labour, and thy patience, and how thou canst not bear them which are evil: and thou hast tried them which say they are apostles, and are not, and hast found them liars:</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And hast borne, and hast patience, and for my name's sake hast laboured, and hast not fainted.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Nevertheless I have somewhat against thee, because thou hast left thy first love.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Remember therefore from whence thou art fallen, and repent, and do the first works; or else I will come unto thee quickly, and will remove thy candlestick out of his place, except thou repent.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> But this thou hast, that thou hatest the deeds of the Nicolaitans, which I also hate.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> He that hath an ear, let him hear what the Spirit saith unto the churches; To him that overcometh will I give to eat of the tree of life, which is in the midst of the paradise of God.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And unto the angel of the church in Smyrna write; These things saith the first and the last, which was dead, and is alive;</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> I know thy works, and tribulation, and poverty, (but thou art rich) and I know the blasphemy of them which say they are Jews, and are not, but are the synagogue of Satan.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Fear none of those things which thou shalt suffer: behold, the devil shall cast some of you into prison, that ye may be tried; and ye shall have tribulation ten days: be thou faithful unto death, and I will give thee a crown of life.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> He that hath an ear, let him hear what the Spirit saith unto the churches; He that overcometh shall not be hurt of the second death.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And to the angel of the church in Pergamos write; These things saith he which hath the sharp sword with two edges;</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> I know thy works, and where thou dwellest, even where Satan's seat is: and thou holdest fast my name, and hast not denied my faith, even in those days wherein Antipas was my faithful martyr, who was slain among you, where Satan dwelleth.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> But I have a few things against thee, because thou hast there them that hold the doctrine of Balaam, who taught Balac to cast a stumblingblock before the children of Israel, to eat things sacrificed unto idols, and to commit fornication.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> So hast thou also them that hold the doctrine of the Nicolaitans, which thing I hate.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Repent; or else I will come unto thee quickly, and will fight against them with the sword of my mouth.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> He that hath an ear, let him hear what the Spirit saith unto the churches; To him that overcometh will I give to eat of the hidden manna, and will give him a white stone, and in the stone a new name written, which no man knoweth saving he that receiveth it.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And unto the angel of the church in Thyatira write; These things saith the Son of God, who hath his eyes like unto a flame of fire, and his feet are like fine brass;</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> I know thy works, and charity, and service, and faith, and thy patience, and thy works; and the last to be more than the first.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Notwithstanding I have a few things against thee, because thou sufferest that woman Jezebel, which calleth herself a prophetess, to teach and to seduce my servants to commit fornication, and to eat things sacrificed unto idols.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And I gave her space to repent of her fornication; and she repented not.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Behold, I will cast her into a bed, and them that commit adultery with her into great tribulation, except they repent of their deeds.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And I will kill her children with death; and all the churches shall know that I am he which searcheth the reins and hearts: and I will give unto every one of you according to your works.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> But unto you I say, and unto the rest in Thyatira, as many as have not this doctrine, and which have not known the depths of Satan, as they speak; I will put upon you none other burden.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> But that which ye have already hold fast till I come.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And he that overcometh, and keepeth my works unto the end, to him will I give power over the nations:</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And he shall rule them with a rod of iron; as the vessels of a potter shall they be broken to shivers: even as I received of my Father.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And I will give him the morning star.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> He that hath an ear, let him hear what the Spirit saith unto the churches.</p>
