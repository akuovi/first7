## 2 Corinthians 1

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Paul, an apostle of Jesus Christ by the will of God, and Timothy our brother, unto the church of God which is at Corinth, with all the saints which are in all Achaia:</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Grace be to you and peace from God our Father, and from the Lord Jesus Christ.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Blessed be God, even the Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, the Father of mercies, and the God of all comfort;</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Who comforteth us in all our tribulation, that we may be able to comfort them which are in any trouble, by the comfort wherewith we ourselves are comforted of God.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> For as the sufferings of Christ abound in us, so our consolation also aboundeth by Christ.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And whether we be afflicted, it is for your consolation and salvation, which is effectual in the enduring of the same sufferings which we also suffer: or whether we be comforted, it is for your consolation and salvation.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And our hope of you is stedfast, knowing, that as ye are partakers of the sufferings, so shall ye be also of the consolation.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> For we would not, brethren, have you ignorant of our trouble which came to us in Asia, that we were pressed out of measure, above strength, insomuch that we despaired even of life:</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> But we had the sentence of death in ourselves, that we should not trust in ourselves, but in God which raiseth the dead:</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Who delivered us from so great a death, and doth deliver: in whom we trust that he will yet deliver us;</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Ye also helping together by prayer for us, that for the gift bestowed upon us by the means of many persons thanks may be given by many on our behalf.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> For our rejoicing is this, the testimony of our conscience, that in simplicity and godly sincerity, not with fleshly wisdom, but by the grace of God, we have had our conversation in the world, and more abundantly to you-ward.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> For we write none other things unto you, than what ye read or acknowledge; and I trust ye shall acknowledge even to the end;</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> As also ye have acknowledged us in part, that we are your rejoicing, even as ye also are our's in the day of the Lord Jesus.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And in this confidence I was minded to come unto you before, that ye might have a second benefit;</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And to pass by you into Macedonia, and to come again out of Macedonia unto you, and of you to be brought on my way toward Judaea.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> When I therefore was thus minded, did I use lightness? or the things that I purpose, do I purpose according to the flesh, that with me there should be yea yea, and nay nay?</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> But as God is true, our word toward you was not yea and nay.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> For the Son of God, Jesus Christ, who was preached among you by us, even by me and Silvanus and Timotheus, was not yea and nay, but in him was yea.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> For all the promises of God in him are yea, and in him Amen, unto the glory of God by us.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Now he which stablisheth us with you in Christ, and hath anointed us, is God;</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Who hath also sealed us, and given the earnest of the Spirit in our hearts.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Moreover I call God for a record upon my soul, that to spare you I came not as yet unto Corinth.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Not for that we have dominion over your faith, but are helpers of your joy: for by faith ye stand.</p>
