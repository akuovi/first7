## Genesis 11

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And the whole earth was of one language, and of one speech.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And it came to pass, as they journeyed from the east, that they found a plain in the land of Shinar; and they dwelt there.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And they said one to another, Go to, let us make brick, and burn them throughly. And they had brick for stone, and slime had they for morter.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And they said, Go to, let us build us a city and a tower, whose top may reach unto heaven; and let us make us a name, lest we be scattered abroad upon the face of the whole earth.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And the LORD came down to see the city and the tower, which the children of men builded.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And the LORD said, Behold, the people is one, and they have all one language; and this they begin to do: and now nothing will be restrained from them, which they have imagined to do.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Go to, let us go down, and there confound their language, that they may not understand one another's speech.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> So the LORD scattered them abroad from thence upon the face of all the earth: and they left off to build the city.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Therefore is the name of it called Babel; because the LORD did there confound the language of all the earth: and from thence did the LORD scatter them abroad upon the face of all the earth.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> These are the generations of Shem: Shem was an hundred years old, and begat Arphaxad two years after the flood:</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And Shem lived after he begat Arphaxad five hundred years, and begat sons and daughters.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And Arphaxad lived five and thirty years, and begat Salah:</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And Arphaxad lived after he begat Salah four hundred and three years, and begat sons and daughters.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And Salah lived thirty years, and begat Eber:</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And Salah lived after he begat Eber four hundred and three years, and begat sons and daughters.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And Eber lived four and thirty years, and begat Peleg:</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And Eber lived after he begat Peleg four hundred and thirty years, and begat sons and daughters.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And Peleg lived thirty years, and begat Reu:</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And Peleg lived after he begat Reu two hundred and nine years, and begat sons and daughters.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And Reu lived two and thirty years, and begat Serug:</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And Reu lived after he begat Serug two hundred and seven years, and begat sons and daughters.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And Serug lived thirty years, and begat Nahor:</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And Serug lived after he begat Nahor two hundred years, and begat sons and daughters.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And Nahor lived nine and twenty years, and begat Terah:</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And Nahor lived after he begat Terah an hundred and nineteen years, and begat sons and daughters.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And Terah lived seventy years, and begat Abram, Nahor, and Haran.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Now these are the generations of Terah: Terah begat Abram, Nahor, and Haran; and Haran begat Lot.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And Haran died before his father Terah in the land of his nativity, in Ur of the Chaldees.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And Abram and Nahor took them wives: the name of Abram's wife was Sarai; and the name of Nahor's wife, Milcah, the daughter of Haran, the father of Milcah, and the father of Iscah.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> But Sarai was barren; she had no child.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> And Terah took Abram his son, and Lot the son of Haran his son's son, and Sarai his daughter in law, his son Abram's wife; and they went forth with them from Ur of the Chaldees, to go into the land of Canaan; and they came unto Haran, and dwelt there.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> And the days of Terah were two hundred and five years: and Terah died in Haran.</p>
