## 1 Chronicles 26

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Concerning the divisions of the porters: Of the Korhites was Meshelemiah the son of Kore, of the sons of Asaph.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And the sons of Meshelemiah were, Zechariah the firstborn, Jediael the second, Zebadiah the third, Jathniel the fourth,</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Elam the fifth, Jehohanan the sixth, Elioenai the seventh.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Moreover the sons of Obed-edom were, Shemaiah the firstborn, Jehozabad the second, Joah the third, and Sacar the fourth, and Nethaneel the fifth,</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Ammiel the sixth, Issachar the seventh, Peulthai the eighth: for God blessed him.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Also unto Shemaiah his son were sons born, that ruled throughout the house of their father: for they were mighty men of valour.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> The sons of Shemaiah; Othni, and Rephael, and Obed, Elzabad, whose brethren were strong men, Elihu, and Semachiah.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> All these of the sons of Obed-edom: they and their sons and their brethren, able men for strength for the service, were threescore and two of Obed-edom.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And Meshelemiah had sons and brethren, strong men, eighteen.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Also Hosah, of the children of Merari, had sons; Simri the chief, (for though he was not the firstborn, yet his father made him the chief;)</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Hilkiah the second, Tebaliah the third, Zechariah the fourth: all the sons and brethren of Hosah were thirteen.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Among these were the divisions of the porters, even among the chief men, having wards one against another, to minister in the house of the LORD.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And they cast lots, as well the small as the great, according to the house of their fathers, for every gate.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And the lot eastward fell to Shelemiah. Then for Zechariah his son, a wise counsellor, they cast lots; and his lot came out northward.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> To Obed-edom southward; and to his sons the house of Asuppim.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> To Shuppim and Hosah the lot came forth westward, with the gate Shallecheth, by the causeway of the going up, ward against ward.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Eastward were six Levites, northward four a day, southward four a day, and toward Asuppim two and two.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> At Parbar westward, four at the causeway, and two at Parbar.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> These are the divisions of the porters among the sons of Kore, and among the sons of Merari.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And of the Levites, Ahijah was over the treasures of the house of God, and over the treasures of the dedicated things.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> As concerning the sons of Laadan; the sons of the Gershonite Laadan, chief fathers, even of Laadan the Gershonite, were Jehieli.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> The sons of Jehieli; Zetham, and Joel his brother, which were over the treasures of the house of the LORD.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Of the Amramites, and the Izharites, the Hebronites, and the Uzzielites:</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And Shebuel the son of Gershom, the son of Moses, was ruler of the treasures.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And his brethren by Eliezer; Rehabiah his son, and Jeshaiah his son, and Joram his son, and Zichri his son, and Shelomith his son.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Which Shelomith and his brethren were over all the treasures of the dedicated things, which David the king, and the chief fathers, the captains over thousands and hundreds, and the captains of the host, had dedicated.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Out of the spoils won in battles did they dedicate to maintain the house of the LORD.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And all that Samuel the seer, and Saul the son of Kish, and Abner the son of Ner, and Joab the son of Zeruiah, had dedicated; and whosoever had dedicated any thing, it was under the hand of Shelomith, and of his brethren.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Of the Izharites, Chenaniah and his sons were for the outward business over Israel, for officers and judges.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And of the Hebronites, Hashabiah and his brethren, men of valour, a thousand and seven hundred, were officers among them of Israel on this side Jordan westward in all the business of the LORD, and in the service of the king.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Among the Hebronites was Jerijah the chief, even among the Hebronites, according to the generations of his fathers. In the fortieth year of the reign of David they were sought for, and there were found among them mighty men of valour at Jazer of Gilead.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> And his brethren, men of valour, were two thousand and seven hundred chief fathers, whom king David made rulers over the Reubenites, the Gadites, and the half tribe of Manasseh, for every matter pertaining to God, and affairs of the king.</p>
