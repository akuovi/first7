## Job 35

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Elihu spake moreover, and said,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Thinkest thou this to be right, that thou saidst, My righteousness is more than God's?</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For thou saidst, What advantage will it be unto thee? and, What profit shall I have, if I be cleansed from my sin?</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> I will answer thee, and thy companions with thee.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Look unto the heavens, and see; and behold the clouds which are higher than thou.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> If thou sinnest, what doest thou against him? or if thy transgressions be multiplied, what doest thou unto him?</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> If thou be righteous, what givest thou him? or what receiveth he of thine hand?</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Thy wickedness may hurt a man as thou art; and thy righteousness may profit the son of man.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> By reason of the multitude of oppressions they make the oppressed to cry: they cry out by reason of the arm of the mighty.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> But none saith, Where is God my maker, who giveth songs in the night;</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Who teacheth us more than the beasts of the earth, and maketh us wiser than the fowls of heaven?</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> There they cry, but none giveth answer, because of the pride of evil men.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Surely God will not hear vanity, neither will the Almighty regard it.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Although thou sayest thou shalt not see him, yet judgment is before him; therefore trust thou in him.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> But now, because it is not so, he hath visited in his anger; yet he knoweth it not in great extremity:</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Therefore doth Job open his mouth in vain; he multiplieth words without knowledge.</p>
