## Proverbs 1

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> The proverbs of Solomon the son of David, king of Israel;</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> To know wisdom and instruction; to perceive the words of understanding;</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> To receive the instruction of wisdom, justice, and judgment, and equity;</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> To give subtilty to the simple, to the young man knowledge and discretion.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> A wise man will hear, and will increase learning; and a man of understanding shall attain unto wise counsels:</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> To understand a proverb, and the interpretation; the words of the wise, and their dark sayings.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> The fear of the LORD is the beginning of knowledge: but fools despise wisdom and instruction.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> My son, hear the instruction of thy father, and forsake not the law of thy mother:</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> For they shall be an ornament of grace unto thy head, and chains about thy neck.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> My son, if sinners entice thee, consent thou not.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> If they say, Come with us, let us lay wait for blood, let us lurk privily for the innocent without cause:</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Let us swallow them up alive as the grave; and whole, as those that go down into the pit:</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> We shall find all precious substance, we shall fill our houses with spoil:</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Cast in thy lot among us; let us all have one purse:</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> My son, walk not thou in the way with them; refrain thy foot from their path:</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> For their feet run to evil, and make haste to shed blood.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Surely in vain the net is spread in the sight of any bird.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And they lay wait for their own blood; they lurk privily for their own lives.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> So are the ways of every one that is greedy of gain; which taketh away the life of the owners thereof.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Wisdom crieth without; she uttereth her voice in the streets:</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> She crieth in the chief place of concourse, in the openings of the gates: in the city she uttereth her words, saying,</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> How long, ye simple ones, will ye love simplicity? and the scorners delight in their scorning, and fools hate knowledge?</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Turn you at my reproof: behold, I will pour out my spirit unto you, I will make known my words unto you.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Because I have called, and ye refused; I have stretched out my hand, and no man regarded;</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> But ye have set at nought all my counsel, and would none of my reproof:</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> I also will laugh at your calamity; I will mock when your fear cometh;</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> When your fear cometh as desolation, and your destruction cometh as a whirlwind; when distress and anguish cometh upon you.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Then shall they call upon me, but I will not answer; they shall seek me early, but they shall not find me:</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> For that they hated knowledge, and did not choose the fear of the LORD:</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> They would none of my counsel: they despised all my reproof.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Therefore shall they eat of the fruit of their own way, and be filled with their own devices.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> For the turning away of the simple shall slay them, and the prosperity of fools shall destroy them.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> But whoso hearkeneth unto me shall dwell safely, and shall be quiet from fear of evil.</p>
