## Genesis 46

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And Israel took his journey with all that he had, and came to Beer-sheba, and offered sacrifices unto the God of his father Isaac.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And God spake unto Israel in the visions of the night, and said, Jacob, Jacob. And he said, Here am I.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And he said, I am God, the God of thy father: fear not to go down into Egypt; for I will there make of thee a great nation:</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> I will go down with thee into Egypt; and I will also surely bring thee up again: and Joseph shall put his hand upon thine eyes.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And Jacob rose up from Beer-sheba: and the sons of Israel carried Jacob their father, and their little ones, and their wives, in the wagons which Pharaoh had sent to carry him.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And they took their cattle, and their goods, which they had gotten in the land of Canaan, and came into Egypt, Jacob, and all his seed with him:</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> His sons, and his sons' sons with him, his daughters, and his sons' daughters, and all his seed brought he with him into Egypt.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And these are the names of the children of Israel, which came into Egypt, Jacob and his sons: Reuben, Jacob's firstborn.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And the sons of Reuben; Hanoch, and Phallu, and Hezron, and Carmi.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And the sons of Simeon; Jemuel, and Jamin, and Ohad, and Jachin, and Zohar, and Shaul the son of a Canaanitish woman.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And the sons of Levi; Gershon, Kohath, and Merari.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And the sons of Judah; Er, and Onan, and Shelah, and Pharez, and Zerah: but Er and Onan died in the land of Canaan. And the sons of Pharez were Hezron and Hamul.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And the sons of Issachar; Tola, and Phuvah, and Job, and Shimron.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And the sons of Zebulun; Sered, and Elon, and Jahleel.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> These be the sons of Leah, which she bare unto Jacob in Padan-aram, with his daughter Dinah: all the souls of his sons and his daughters were thirty and three.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And the sons of Gad; Ziphion, and Haggi, Shuni, and Ezbon, Eri, and Arodi, and Areli.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And the sons of Asher; Jimnah, and Ishuah, and Isui, and Beriah, and Serah their sister: and the sons of Beriah; Heber, and Malchiel.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> These are the sons of Zilpah, whom Laban gave to Leah his daughter, and these she bare unto Jacob, even sixteen souls.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> The sons of Rachel Jacob's wife; Joseph, and Benjamin.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And unto Joseph in the land of Egypt were born Manasseh and Ephraim, which Asenath the daughter of Poti-pherah priest of On bare unto him.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And the sons of Benjamin were Belah, and Becher, and Ashbel, Gera, and Naaman, Ehi, and Rosh, Muppim, and Huppim, and Ard.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> These are the sons of Rachel, which were born to Jacob: all the souls were fourteen.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And the sons of Dan; Hushim.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And the sons of Naphtali; Jahzeel, and Guni, and Jezer, and Shillem.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> These are the sons of Bilhah, which Laban gave unto Rachel his daughter, and she bare these unto Jacob: all the souls were seven.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> All the souls that came with Jacob into Egypt, which came out of his loins, besides Jacob's sons' wives, all the souls were threescore and six;</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And the sons of Joseph, which were born him in Egypt, were two souls: all the souls of the house of Jacob, which came into Egypt, were threescore and ten.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And he sent Judah before him unto Joseph, to direct his face unto Goshen; and they came into the land of Goshen.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And Joseph made ready his chariot, and went up to meet Israel his father, to Goshen, and presented himself unto him; and he fell on his neck, and wept on his neck a good while.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And Israel said unto Joseph, Now let me die, since I have seen thy face, because thou art yet alive.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> And Joseph said unto his brethren, and unto his father's house, I will go up, and shew Pharaoh, and say unto him, My brethren, and my father's house, which were in the land of Canaan, are come unto me;</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> And the men are shepherds, for their trade hath been to feed cattle; and they have brought their flocks, and their herds, and all that they have.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> And it shall come to pass, when Pharaoh shall call you, and shall say, What is your occupation?</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> That ye shall say, Thy servants' trade hath been about cattle from our youth even until now, both we, and also our fathers: that ye may dwell in the land of Goshen; for every shepherd is an abomination unto the Egyptians.</p>
