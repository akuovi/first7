## 1 Corinthians 12

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Now concerning spiritual gifts, brethren, I would not have you ignorant.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Ye know that ye were Gentiles, carried away unto these dumb idols, even as ye were led.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Wherefore I give you to understand, that no man speaking by the Spirit of God calleth Jesus accursed: and that no man can say that Jesus is the Lord, but by the Holy Ghost.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Now there are diversities of gifts, but the same Spirit.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And there are differences of administrations, but the same Lord.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And there are diversities of operations, but it is the same God which worketh all in all.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> But the manifestation of the Spirit is given to every man to profit withal.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> For to one is given by the Spirit the word of wisdom; to another the word of knowledge by the same Spirit;</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> To another faith by the same Spirit; to another the gifts of healing by the same Spirit;</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> To another the working of miracles; to another prophecy; to another discerning of spirits; to another divers kinds of tongues; to another the interpretation of tongues:</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> But all these worketh that one and the selfsame Spirit, dividing to every man severally as he will.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> For as the body is one, and hath many members, and all the members of that one body, being many, are one body: so also is Christ.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> For by one Spirit are we all baptized into one body, whether we be Jews or Gentiles, whether we be bond or free; and have been all made to drink into one Spirit.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> For the body is not one member, but many.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> If the foot shall say, Because I am not the hand, I am not of the body; is it therefore not of the body?</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And if the ear shall say, Because I am not the eye, I am not of the body; is it therefore not of the body?</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> If the whole body were an eye, where were the hearing? If the whole were hearing, where were the smelling?</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> But now hath God set the members every one of them in the body, as it hath pleased him.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And if they were all one member, where were the body?</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> But now are they many members, yet but one body.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And the eye cannot say unto the hand, I have no need of thee: nor again the head to the feet, I have no need of you.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Nay, much more those members of the body, which seem to be more feeble, are necessary:</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And those members of the body, which we think to be less honourable, upon these we bestow more abundant honour; and our uncomely parts have more abundant comeliness.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> For our comely parts have no need: but God hath tempered the body together, having given more abundant honour to that part which lacked:</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> That there should be no schism in the body; but that the members should have the same care one for another.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And whether one member suffer, all the members suffer with it; or one member be honoured, all the members rejoice with it.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Now ye are the body of Christ, and members in particular.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And God hath set some in the church, first apostles, secondarily prophets, thirdly teachers, after that miracles, then gifts of healings, helps, governments, diversities of tongues.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Are all apostles? are all prophets? are all teachers? are all workers of miracles?</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Have all the gifts of healing? do all speak with tongues? do all interpret?</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> But covet earnestly the best gifts: and yet shew I unto you a more excellent way.</p>
