## Ruth 1

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Now it came to pass in the days when the judges ruled, that there was a famine in the land. And a certain man of Beth-lehem-judah went to sojourn in the country of Moab, he, and his wife, and his two sons.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And the name of the man was Elimelech, and the name of his wife Naomi, and the name of his two sons Mahlon and Chilion, Ephrathites of Beth-lehem-judah. And they came into the country of Moab, and continued there.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And Elimelech Naomi's husband died; and she was left, and her two sons.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And they took them wives of the women of Moab; the name of the one was Orpah, and the name of the other Ruth: and they dwelled there about ten years.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And Mahlon and Chilion died also both of them; and the woman was left of her two sons and her husband.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Then she arose with her daughters in law, that she might return from the country of Moab: for she had heard in the country of Moab how that the LORD had visited his people in giving them bread.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Wherefore she went forth out of the place where she was, and her two daughters in law with her; and they went on the way to return unto the land of Judah.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And Naomi said unto her two daughters in law, Go, return each to her mother's house: the LORD deal kindly with you, as ye have dealt with the dead, and with me.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> The LORD grant you that ye may find rest, each of you in the house of her husband. Then she kissed them; and they lifted up their voice, and wept.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And they said unto her, Surely we will return with thee unto thy people.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And Naomi said, Turn again, my daughters: why will ye go with me? are there yet any more sons in my womb, that they may be your husbands?</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Turn again, my daughters, go your way; for I am too old to have an husband. If I should say, I have hope, if I should have an husband also to night, and should also bear sons;</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Would ye tarry for them till they were grown? would ye stay for them from having husbands? nay, my daughters; for it grieveth me much for your sakes that the hand of the LORD is gone out against me.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And they lifted up their voice, and wept again: and Orpah kissed her mother in law; but Ruth clave unto her.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And she said, Behold, thy sister in law is gone back unto her people, and unto her gods: return thou after thy sister in law.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And Ruth said, Intreat me not to leave thee, or to return from following after thee: for whither thou goest, I will go; and where thou lodgest, I will lodge: thy people shall be my people, and thy God my God:</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Where thou diest, will I die, and there will I be buried: the LORD do so to me, and more also, if ought but death part thee and me.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> When she saw that she was stedfastly minded to go with her, then she left speaking unto her.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> So they two went until they came to Beth-lehem. And it came to pass, when they were come to Beth-lehem, that all the city was moved about them, and they said, Is this Naomi?</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And she said unto them, Call me not Naomi, call me Mara: for the Almighty hath dealt very bitterly with me.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> I went out full, and the LORD hath brought me home again empty: why then call ye me Naomi, seeing the LORD hath testified against me, and the Almighty hath afflicted me?</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> So Naomi returned, and Ruth the Moabitess, her daughter in law, with her, which returned out of the country of Moab: and they came to Beth-lehem in the beginning of barley harvest.</p>
