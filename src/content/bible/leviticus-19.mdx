## Leviticus 19

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Speak unto all the congregation of the children of Israel, and say unto them, Ye shall be holy: for I the LORD your God am holy.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Ye shall fear every man his mother, and his father, and keep my sabbaths: I am the LORD your God.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Turn ye not unto idols, nor make to yourselves molten gods: I am the LORD your God.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And if ye offer a sacrifice of peace offerings unto the LORD, ye shall offer it at your own will.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> It shall be eaten the same day ye offer it, and on the morrow: and if ought remain until the third day, it shall be burnt in the fire.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And if it be eaten at all on the third day, it is abominable; it shall not be accepted.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Therefore every one that eateth it shall bear his iniquity, because he hath profaned the hallowed thing of the LORD: and that soul shall be cut off from among his people.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And when ye reap the harvest of your land, thou shalt not wholly reap the corners of thy field, neither shalt thou gather the gleanings of thy harvest.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And thou shalt not glean thy vineyard, neither shalt thou gather every grape of thy vineyard; thou shalt leave them for the poor and stranger: I am the LORD your God.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Ye shall not steal, neither deal falsely, neither lie one to another.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And ye shall not swear by my name falsely, neither shalt thou profane the name of thy God: I am the LORD.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Thou shalt not defraud thy neighbour, neither rob him: the wages of him that is hired shall not abide with thee all night until the morning.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Thou shalt not curse the deaf, nor put a stumblingblock before the blind, but shalt fear thy God: I am the LORD.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Ye shall do no unrighteousness in judgment: thou shalt not respect the person of the poor, nor honour the person of the mighty: but in righteousness shalt thou judge thy neighbour.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Thou shalt not go up and down as a talebearer among thy people: neither shalt thou stand against the blood of thy neighbour: I am the LORD.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Thou shalt not hate thy brother in thine heart: thou shalt in any wise rebuke thy neighbour, and not suffer sin upon him.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Thou shalt not avenge, nor bear any grudge against the children of thy people, but thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself: I am the LORD.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Ye shall keep my statutes. Thou shalt not let thy cattle gender with a diverse kind: thou shalt not sow thy field with mingled seed: neither shall a garment mingled of linen and woollen come upon thee.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And whosoever lieth carnally with a woman, that is a bondmaid, betrothed to an husband, and not at all redeemed, nor freedom given her; she shall be scourged; they shall not be put to death, because she was not free.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And he shall bring his trespass offering unto the LORD, unto the door of the tabernacle of the congregation, even a ram for a trespass offering.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And the priest shall make an atonement for him with the ram of the trespass offering before the LORD for his sin which he hath done: and the sin which he hath done shall be forgiven him.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And when ye shall come into the land, and shall have planted all manner of trees for food, then ye shall count the fruit thereof as uncircumcised: three years shall it be as uncircumcised unto you: it shall not be eaten of.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> But in the fourth year all the fruit thereof shall be holy to praise the LORD withal.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And in the fifth year shall ye eat of the fruit thereof, that it may yield unto you the increase thereof: I am the LORD your God.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Ye shall not eat any thing with the blood: neither shall ye use enchantment, nor observe times.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Ye shall not round the corners of your heads, neither shalt thou mar the corners of thy beard.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Ye shall not make any cuttings in your flesh for the dead, nor print any marks upon you: I am the LORD.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Do not prostitute thy daughter, to cause her to be a whore; lest the land fall to whoredom, and the land become full of wickedness.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Ye shall keep my sabbaths, and reverence my sanctuary: I am the LORD.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Regard not them that have familiar spirits, neither seek after wizards, to be defiled by them: I am the LORD your God.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> Thou shalt rise up before the hoary head, and honour the face of the old man, and fear thy God: I am the LORD.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> And if a stranger sojourn with thee in your land, ye shall not vex him.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> But the stranger that dwelleth with you shall be unto you as one born among you, and thou shalt love him as thyself; for ye were strangers in the land of Egypt: I am the LORD your God.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> Ye shall do no unrighteousness in judgment, in meteyard, in weight, or in measure.</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> Just balances, just weights, a just ephah, and a just hin, shall ye have: I am the LORD your God, which brought you out of the land of Egypt.</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> Therefore shall ye observe all my statutes, and all my judgments, and do them: I am the LORD.</p>
