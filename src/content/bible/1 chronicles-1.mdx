## 1 Chronicles 1

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Adam, Sheth, Enosh,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Kenan, Mahalaleel, Jered,</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Henoch, Methuselah, Lamech,</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Noah, Shem, Ham, and Japheth.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> The sons of Japheth; Gomer, and Magog, and Madai, and Javan, and Tubal, and Meshech, and Tiras.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And the sons of Gomer; Ashchenaz, and Riphath, and Togarmah.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And the sons of Javan; Elishah, and Tarshish, Kittim, and Dodanim.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> The sons of Ham; Cush, and Mizraim, Put, and Canaan.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And the sons of Cush; Seba, and Havilah, and Sabta, and Raamah, and Sabtecha. And the sons of Raamah; Sheba, and Dedan.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And Cush begat Nimrod: he began to be mighty upon the earth.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And Mizraim begat Ludim, and Anamim, and Lehabim, and Naphtuhim,</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And Pathrusim, and Casluhim, (of whom came the Philistines,) and Caphthorim.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And Canaan begat Zidon his firstborn, and Heth,</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> The Jebusite also, and the Amorite, and the Girgashite,</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And the Hivite, and the Arkite, and the Sinite,</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And the Arvadite, and the Zemarite, and the Hamathite.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> The sons of Shem; Elam, and Asshur, and Arphaxad, and Lud, and Aram, and Uz, and Hul, and Gether, and Meshech.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And Arphaxad begat Shelah, and Shelah begat Eber.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And unto Eber were born two sons: the name of the one was Peleg; because in his days the earth was divided: and his brother's name was Joktan.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And Joktan begat Almodad, and Sheleph, and Hazarmaveth, and Jerah,</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Hadoram also, and Uzal, and Diklah,</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And Ebal, and Abimael, and Sheba,</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And Ophir, and Havilah, and Jobab. All these were the sons of Joktan.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Shem, Arphaxad, Shelah,</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Eber, Peleg, Reu,</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Serug, Nahor, Terah,</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Abram; the same is Abraham.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> The sons of Abraham; Isaac, and Ishmael.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> These are their generations: The firstborn of Ishmael, Nebaioth; then Kedar, and Adbeel, and Mibsam,</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Mishma, and Dumah, Massa, Hadad, and Tema,</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Jetur, Naphish, and Kedemah. These are the sons of Ishmael.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> Now the sons of Keturah, Abraham's concubine: she bare Zimran, and Jokshan, and Medan, and Midian, and Ishbak, and Shuah. And the sons of Jokshan; Sheba, and Dedan.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> And the sons of Midian; Ephah, and Epher, and Henoch, and Abida, and Eldaah. All these are the sons of Keturah.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> And Abraham begat Isaac. The sons of Isaac; Esau and Israel.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> The sons of Esau; Eliphaz, Reuel, and Jeush, and Jaalam, and Korah.</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> The sons of Eliphaz; Teman, and Omar, Zephi, and Gatam, Kenaz, and Timna, and Amalek.</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> The sons of Reuel; Nahath, Zerah, Shammah, and Mizzah.</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> And the sons of Seir; Lotan, and Shobal, and Zibeon, and Anah, and Dishon, and Ezer, and Dishan.</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> And the sons of Lotan; Hori, and Homam: and Timna was Lotan's sister.</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> The sons of Shobal; Alian, and Manahath, and Ebal, Shephi, and Onam. And the sons of Zibeon; Aiah, and Anah.</p>

<p id="v41" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">41</span> The sons of Anah; Dishon. And the sons of Dishon; Amram, and Eshban, and Ithran, and Cheran.</p>

<p id="v42" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">42</span> The sons of Ezer; Bilhan, and Zavan, and Jakan. The sons of Dishan; Uz, and Aran.</p>

<p id="v43" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">43</span> Now these are the kings that reigned in the land of Edom before any king reigned over the children of Israel; Bela the son of Beor: and the name of his city was Dinhabah.</p>

<p id="v44" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">44</span> And when Bela was dead, Jobab the son of Zerah of Bozrah reigned in his stead.</p>

<p id="v45" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">45</span> And when Jobab was dead, Husham of the land of the Temanites reigned in his stead.</p>

<p id="v46" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">46</span> And when Husham was dead, Hadad the son of Bedad, which smote Midian in the field of Moab, reigned in his stead: and the name of his city was Avith.</p>

<p id="v47" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">47</span> And when Hadad was dead, Samlah of Masrekah reigned in his stead.</p>

<p id="v48" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">48</span> And when Samlah was dead, Shaul of Rehoboth by the river reigned in his stead.</p>

<p id="v49" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">49</span> And when Shaul was dead, Baal-hanan the son of Achbor reigned in his stead.</p>

<p id="v50" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">50</span> And when Baal-hanan was dead, Hadad reigned in his stead: and the name of his city was Pai; and his wife's name was Mehetabel, the daughter of Matred, the daughter of Mezahab.</p>

<p id="v51" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">51</span> Hadad died also. And the dukes of Edom were; duke Timnah, duke Aliah, duke Jetheth,</p>

<p id="v52" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">52</span> Duke Aholibamah, duke Elah, duke Pinon,</p>

<p id="v53" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">53</span> Duke Kenaz, duke Teman, duke Mibzar,</p>

<p id="v54" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">54</span> Duke Magdiel, duke Iram. These are the dukes of Edom.</p>
