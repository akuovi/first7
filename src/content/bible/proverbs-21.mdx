## Proverbs 21

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> The king's heart is in the hand of the LORD, as the rivers of water: he turneth it whithersoever he will.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Every way of a man is right in his own eyes: but the LORD pondereth the hearts.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> To do justice and judgment is more acceptable to the LORD than sacrifice.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> An high look, and a proud heart, and the plowing of the wicked, is sin.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> The thoughts of the diligent tend only to plenteousness; but of every one that is hasty only to want.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> The getting of treasures by a lying tongue is a vanity tossed to and fro of them that seek death.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> The robbery of the wicked shall destroy them; because they refuse to do judgment.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> The way of man is froward and strange: but as for the pure, his work is right.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> It is better to dwell in a corner of the housetop, than with a brawling woman in a wide house.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> The soul of the wicked desireth evil: his neighbour findeth no favour in his eyes.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> When the scorner is punished, the simple is made wise: and when the wise is instructed, he receiveth knowledge.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> The righteous man wisely considereth the house of the wicked: but God overthroweth the wicked for their wickedness.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Whoso stoppeth his ears at the cry of the poor, he also shall cry himself, but shall not be heard.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> A gift in secret pacifieth anger: and a reward in the bosom strong wrath.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> It is joy to the just to do judgment: but destruction shall be to the workers of iniquity.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> The man that wandereth out of the way of understanding shall remain in the congregation of the dead.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> He that loveth pleasure shall be a poor man: he that loveth wine and oil shall not be rich.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> The wicked shall be a ransom for the righteous, and the transgressor for the upright.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> It is better to dwell in the wilderness, than with a contentious and an angry woman.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> There is treasure to be desired and oil in the dwelling of the wise; but a foolish man spendeth it up.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> He that followeth after righteousness and mercy findeth life, righteousness, and honour.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> A wise man scaleth the city of the mighty, and casteth down the strength of the confidence thereof.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Whoso keepeth his mouth and his tongue keepeth his soul from troubles.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Proud and haughty scorner is his name, who dealeth in proud wrath.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> The desire of the slothful killeth him; for his hands refuse to labour.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> He coveteth greedily all the day long: but the righteous giveth and spareth not.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> The sacrifice of the wicked is abomination: how much more, when he bringeth it with a wicked mind?</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> A false witness shall perish: but the man that heareth speaketh constantly.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> A wicked man hardeneth his face: but as for the upright, he directeth his way.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> There is no wisdom nor understanding nor counsel against the LORD.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> The horse is prepared against the day of battle: but safety is of the LORD.</p>
