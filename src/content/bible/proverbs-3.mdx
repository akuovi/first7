## Proverbs 3

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> My son, forget not my law; but let thine heart keep my commandments:</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> For length of days, and long life, and peace, shall they add to thee.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Let not mercy and truth forsake thee: bind them about thy neck; write them upon the table of thine heart:</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> So shalt thou find favour and good understanding in the sight of God and man.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Trust in the LORD with all thine heart; and lean not unto thine own understanding.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> In all thy ways acknowledge him, and he shall direct thy paths.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Be not wise in thine own eyes: fear the LORD, and depart from evil.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> It shall be health to thy navel, and marrow to thy bones.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Honour the LORD with thy substance, and with the firstfruits of all thine increase:</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> So shall thy barns be filled with plenty, and thy presses shall burst out with new wine.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> My son, despise not the chastening of the LORD; neither be weary of his correction:</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> For whom the LORD loveth he correcteth; even as a father the son in whom he delighteth.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Happy is the man that findeth wisdom, and the man that getteth understanding.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> For the merchandise of it is better than the merchandise of silver, and the gain thereof than fine gold.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> She is more precious than rubies: and all the things thou canst desire are not to be compared unto her.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Length of days is in her right hand; and in her left hand riches and honour.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Her ways are ways of pleasantness, and all her paths are peace.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> She is a tree of life to them that lay hold upon her: and happy is every one that retaineth her.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> The LORD by wisdom hath founded the earth; by understanding hath he established the heavens.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> By his knowledge the depths are broken up, and the clouds drop down the dew.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> My son, let not them depart from thine eyes: keep sound wisdom and discretion:</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> So shall they be life unto thy soul, and grace to thy neck.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Then shalt thou walk in thy way safely, and thy foot shall not stumble.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> When thou liest down, thou shalt not be afraid: yea, thou shalt lie down, and thy sleep shall be sweet.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Be not afraid of sudden fear, neither of the desolation of the wicked, when it cometh.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> For the LORD shall be thy confidence, and shall keep thy foot from being taken.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Withhold not good from them to whom it is due, when it is in the power of thine hand to do it.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Say not unto thy neighbour, Go, and come again, and to morrow I will give; when thou hast it by thee.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Devise not evil against thy neighbour, seeing he dwelleth securely by thee.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Strive not with a man without cause, if he have done thee no harm.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Envy thou not the oppressor, and choose none of his ways.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> For the froward is abomination to the LORD: but his secret is with the righteous.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> The curse of the LORD is in the house of the wicked: but he blesseth the habitation of the just.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> Surely he scorneth the scorners: but he giveth grace unto the lowly.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> The wise shall inherit glory: but shame shall be the promotion of fools.</p>
