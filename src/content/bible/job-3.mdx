## Job 3

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> After this opened Job his mouth, and cursed his day.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And Job spake, and said,</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Let the day perish wherein I was born, and the night in which it was said, There is a man child conceived.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Let that day be darkness; let not God regard it from above, neither let the light shine upon it.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Let darkness and the shadow of death stain it; let a cloud dwell upon it; let the blackness of the day terrify it.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> As for that night, let darkness seize upon it; let it not be joined unto the days of the year, let it not come into the number of the months.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Lo, let that night be solitary, let no joyful voice come therein.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Let them curse it that curse the day, who are ready to raise up their mourning.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Let the stars of the twilight thereof be dark; let it look for light, but have none; neither let it see the dawning of the day:</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Because it shut not up the doors of my mother's womb, nor hid sorrow from mine eyes.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Why died I not from the womb? why did I not give up the ghost when I came out of the belly?</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Why did the knees prevent me? or why the breasts that I should suck?</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> For now should I have lain still and been quiet, I should have slept: then had I been at rest,</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> With kings and counsellors of the earth, which built desolate places for themselves;</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Or with princes that had gold, who filled their houses with silver:</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Or as an hidden untimely birth I had not been; as infants which never saw light.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> There the wicked cease from troubling; and there the weary be at rest.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> There the prisoners rest together; they hear not the voice of the oppressor.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> The small and great are there; and the servant is free from his master.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Wherefore is light given to him that is in misery, and life unto the bitter in soul;</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Which long for death, but it cometh not; and dig for it more than for hid treasures;</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Which rejoice exceedingly, and are glad, when they can find the grave?</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Why is light given to a man whose way is hid, and whom God hath hedged in?</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> For my sighing cometh before I eat, and my roarings are poured out like the waters.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> For the thing which I greatly feared is come upon me, and that which I was afraid of is come unto me.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> I was not in safety, neither had I rest, neither was I quiet; yet trouble came.</p>
