## Isaiah 33

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Woe to thee that spoilest, and thou wast not spoiled; and dealest treacherously, and they dealt not treacherously with thee! when thou shalt cease to spoil, thou shalt be spoiled; and when thou shalt make an end to deal treacherously, they shall deal treacherously with thee.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> O LORD, be gracious unto us; we have waited for thee: be thou their arm every morning, our salvation also in the time of trouble.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> At the noise of the tumult the people fled; at the lifting up of thyself the nations were scattered.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And your spoil shall be gathered like the gathering of the caterpiller: as the running to and fro of locusts shall he run upon them.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> The LORD is exalted; for he dwelleth on high: he hath filled Zion with judgment and righteousness.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And wisdom and knowledge shall be the stability of thy times, and strength of salvation: the fear of the LORD is his treasure.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Behold, their valiant ones shall cry without: the ambassadors of peace shall weep bitterly.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> The highways lie waste, the wayfaring man ceaseth: he hath broken the covenant, he hath despised the cities, he regardeth no man.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> The earth mourneth and languisheth: Lebanon is ashamed and hewn down: Sharon is like a wilderness; and Bashan and Carmel shake off their fruits.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Now will I rise, saith the LORD; now will I be exalted; now will I lift up myself.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Ye shall conceive chaff, ye shall bring forth stubble: your breath, as fire, shall devour you.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And the people shall be as the burnings of lime: as thorns cut up shall they be burned in the fire.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Hear, ye that are far off, what I have done; and, ye that are near, acknowledge my might.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> The sinners in Zion are afraid; fearfulness hath surprised the hypocrites. Who among us shall dwell with the devouring fire? who among us shall dwell with everlasting burnings?</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> He that walketh righteously, and speaketh uprightly; he that despiseth the gain of oppressions, that shaketh his hands from holding of bribes, that stoppeth his ears from hearing of blood, and shutteth his eyes from seeing evil;</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> He shall dwell on high: his place of defence shall be the munitions of rocks: bread shall be given him; his waters shall be sure.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Thine eyes shall see the king in his beauty: they shall behold the land that is very far off.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Thine heart shall meditate terror. Where is the scribe? where is the receiver? where is he that counted the towers?</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Thou shalt not see a fierce people, a people of a deeper speech than thou canst perceive; of a stammering tongue, that thou canst not understand.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Look upon Zion, the city of our solemnities: thine eyes shall see Jerusalem a quiet habitation, a tabernacle that shall not be taken down; not one of the stakes thereof shall ever be removed, neither shall any of the cords thereof be broken.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> But there the glorious LORD will be unto us a place of broad rivers and streams; wherein shall go no galley with oars, neither shall gallant ship pass thereby.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> For the LORD is our judge, the LORD is our lawgiver, the LORD is our king; he will save us.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Thy tacklings are loosed; they could not well strengthen their mast, they could not spread the sail: then is the prey of a great spoil divided; the lame take the prey.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And the inhabitant shall not say, I am sick: the people that dwell therein shall be forgiven their iniquity.</p>
