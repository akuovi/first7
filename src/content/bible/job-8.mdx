## Job 8

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Then answered Bildad the Shuhite, and said,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> How long wilt thou speak these things? and how long shall the words of thy mouth be like a strong wind?</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Doth God pervert judgment? or doth the Almighty pervert justice?</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> If thy children have sinned against him, and he have cast them away for their transgression;</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> If thou wouldest seek unto God betimes, and make thy supplication to the Almighty;</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> If thou wert pure and upright; surely now he would awake for thee, and make the habitation of thy righteousness prosperous.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Though thy beginning was small, yet thy latter end should greatly increase.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> For enquire, I pray thee, of the former age, and prepare thyself to the search of their fathers:</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> (For we are but of yesterday, and know nothing, because our days upon earth are a shadow:)</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Shall not they teach thee, and tell thee, and utter words out of their heart?</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Can the rush grow up without mire? can the flag grow without water?</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Whilst it is yet in his greenness, and not cut down, it withereth before any other herb.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> So are the paths of all that forget God; and the hypocrite's hope shall perish:</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Whose hope shall be cut off, and whose trust shall be a spider's web.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> He shall lean upon his house, but it shall not stand: he shall hold it fast, but it shall not endure.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> He is green before the sun, and his branch shooteth forth in his garden.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> His roots are wrapped about the heap, and seeth the place of stones.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> If he destroy him from his place, then it shall deny him, saying, I have not seen thee.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Behold, this is the joy of his way, and out of the earth shall others grow.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Behold, God will not cast away a perfect man, neither will he help the evil doers:</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Till he fill thy mouth with laughing, and thy lips with rejoicing.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> They that hate thee shall be clothed with shame; and the dwelling place of the wicked shall come to nought.</p>
