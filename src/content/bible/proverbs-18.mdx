## Proverbs 18

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Through desire a man, having separated himself, seeketh and intermeddleth with all wisdom.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> A fool hath no delight in understanding, but that his heart may discover itself.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> When the wicked cometh, then cometh also contempt, and with ignominy reproach.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> The words of a man's mouth are as deep waters, and the wellspring of wisdom as a flowing brook.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> It is not good to accept the person of the wicked, to overthrow the righteous in judgment.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> A fool's lips enter into contention, and his mouth calleth for strokes.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> A fool's mouth is his destruction, and his lips are the snare of his soul.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> The words of a talebearer are as wounds, and they go down into the innermost parts of the belly.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> He also that is slothful in his work is brother to him that is a great waster.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> The name of the LORD is a strong tower: the righteous runneth into it, and is safe.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> The rich man's wealth is his strong city, and as an high wall in his own conceit.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Before destruction the heart of man is haughty, and before honour is humility.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> He that answereth a matter before he heareth it, it is folly and shame unto him.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> The spirit of a man will sustain his infirmity; but a wounded spirit who can bear?</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> The heart of the prudent getteth knowledge; and the ear of the wise seeketh knowledge.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> A man's gift maketh room for him, and bringeth him before great men.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> He that is first in his own cause seemeth just; but his neighbour cometh and searcheth him.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> The lot causeth contentions to cease, and parteth between the mighty.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> A brother offended is harder to be won than a strong city: and their contentions are like the bars of a castle.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> A man's belly shall be satisfied with the fruit of his mouth; and with the increase of his lips shall he be filled.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Death and life are in the power of the tongue: and they that love it shall eat the fruit thereof.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Whoso findeth a wife findeth a good thing, and obtaineth favour of the LORD.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> The poor useth intreaties; but the rich answereth roughly.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> A man that hath friends must shew himself friendly: and there is a friend that sticketh closer than a brother.</p>
