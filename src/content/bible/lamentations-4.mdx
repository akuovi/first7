## Lamentations 4

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> How is the gold become dim! how is the most fine gold changed! the stones of the sanctuary are poured out in the top of every street.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> The precious sons of Zion, comparable to fine gold, how are they esteemed as earthen pitchers, the work of the hands of the potter!</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Even the sea monsters draw out the breast, they give suck to their young ones: the daughter of my people is become cruel, like the ostriches in the wilderness.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> The tongue of the sucking child cleaveth to the roof of his mouth for thirst: the young children ask bread, and no man breaketh it unto them.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> They that did feed delicately are desolate in the streets: they that were brought up in scarlet embrace dunghills.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> For the punishment of the iniquity of the daughter of my people is greater than the punishment of the sin of Sodom, that was overthrown as in a moment, and no hands stayed on her.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Her Nazarites were purer than snow, they were whiter than milk, they were more ruddy in body than rubies, their polishing was of sapphire:</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Their visage is blacker than a coal; they are not known in the streets: their skin cleaveth to their bones; it is withered, it is become like a stick.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> They that be slain with the sword are better than they that be slain with hunger: for these pine away, stricken through for want of the fruits of the field.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> The hands of the pitiful women have sodden their own children: they were their meat in the destruction of the daughter of my people.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> The LORD hath accomplished his fury; he hath poured out his fierce anger, and hath kindled a fire in Zion, and it hath devoured the foundations thereof.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> The kings of the earth, and all the inhabitants of the world, would not have believed that the adversary and the enemy should have entered into the gates of Jerusalem.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Â¶ For the sins of her prophets, and the iniquities of her priests, that have shed the blood of the just in the midst of her,</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> They have wandered as blind men in the streets, they have polluted themselves with blood, so that men could not touch their garments.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> They cried unto them, Depart ye; it is unclean; depart, depart, touch not: when they fled away and wandered, they said among the heathen, They shall no more sojourn there.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> The anger of the LORD hath divided them; he will no more regard them: they respected not the persons of the priests, they favoured not the elders.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> As for us, our eyes as yet failed for our vain help: in our watching we have watched for a nation that could not save us.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> They hunt our steps, that we cannot go in our streets: our end is near, our days are fulfilled; for our end is come.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Our persecutors are swifter than the eagles of the heaven: they pursued us upon the mountains, they laid wait for us in the wilderness.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> The breath of our nostrils, the anointed of the LORD, was taken in their pits, of whom we said, Under his shadow we shall live among the heathen.</p>
