## Proverbs 20

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Wine is a mocker, strong drink is raging: and whosoever is deceived thereby is not wise.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> The fear of a king is as the roaring of a lion: whoso provoketh him to anger sinneth against his own soul.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> It is an honour for a man to cease from strife: but every fool will be meddling.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> The sluggard will not plow by reason of the cold; therefore shall he beg in harvest, and have nothing.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Counsel in the heart of man is like deep water; but a man of understanding will draw it out.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Most men will proclaim every one his own goodness: but a faithful man who can find?</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> The just man walketh in his integrity: his children are blessed after him.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> A king that sitteth in the throne of judgment scattereth away all evil with his eyes.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Who can say, I have made my heart clean, I am pure from my sin?</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Divers weights, and divers measures, both of them are alike abomination to the LORD.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Even a child is known by his doings, whether his work be pure, and whether it be right.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> The hearing ear, and the seeing eye, the LORD hath made even both of them.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Love not sleep, lest thou come to poverty; open thine eyes, and thou shalt be satisfied with bread.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> It is naught, it is naught, saith the buyer: but when he is gone his way, then he boasteth.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> There is gold, and a multitude of rubies: but the lips of knowledge are a precious jewel.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Take his garment that is surety for a stranger: and take a pledge of him for a strange woman.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Bread of deceit is sweet to a man; but afterwards his mouth shall be filled with gravel.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Every purpose is established by counsel: and with good advice make war.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> He that goeth about as a talebearer revealeth secrets: therefore meddle not with him that flattereth with his lips.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Whoso curseth his father or his mother, his lamp shall be put out in obscure darkness.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> An inheritance may be gotten hastily at the beginning; but the end thereof shall not be blessed.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Say not thou, I will recompense evil; but wait on the LORD, and he shall save thee.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Divers weights are an abomination unto the LORD; and a false balance is not good.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Man's goings are of the LORD; how can a man then understand his own way?</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> It is a snare to the man who devoureth that which is holy, and after vows to make enquiry.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> A wise king scattereth the wicked, and bringeth the wheel over them.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> The spirit of man is the candle of the LORD, searching all the inward parts of the belly.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Mercy and truth preserve the king: and his throne is upholden by mercy.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> The glory of young men is their strength: and the beauty of old men is the gray head.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> The blueness of a wound cleanseth away evil: so do stripes the inward parts of the belly.</p>
