## 1 Chronicles 12

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Now these are they that came to David to Ziklag, while he yet kept himself close because of Saul the son of Kish: and they were among the mighty men, helpers of the war.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> They were armed with bows, and could use both the right hand and the left in hurling stones and shooting arrows out of a bow, even of Saul's brethren of Benjamin.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> The chief was Ahiezer, then Joash, the sons of Shemaah the Gibeathite; and Jeziel, and Pelet, the sons of Azmaveth; and Berachah, and Jehu the Antothite,</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And Ismaiah the Gibeonite, a mighty man among the thirty, and over the thirty; and Jeremiah, and Jahaziel, and Johanan, and Josabad the Gederathite,</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Eluzai, and Jerimoth, and Bealiah, and Shemariah, and Shephatiah the Haruphite,</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Elkanah, and Jesiah, and Azareel, and Joezer, and Jashobeam, the Korhites,</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And Joelah, and Zebadiah, the sons of Jeroham of Gedor.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And of the Gadites there separated themselves unto David into the hold to the wilderness men of might, and men of war fit for the battle, that could handle shield and buckler, whose faces were like the faces of lions, and were as swift as the roes upon the mountains;</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Ezer the first, Obadiah the second, Eliab the third,</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Mishmannah the fourth, Jeremiah the fifth,</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Attai the sixth, Eliel the seventh,</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Johanan the eighth, Elzabad the ninth,</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Jeremiah the tenth, Machbanai the eleventh.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> These were of the sons of Gad, captains of the host: one of the least was over an hundred, and the greatest over a thousand.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> These are they that went over Jordan in the first month, when it had overflown all his banks; and they put to flight all them of the valleys, both toward the east, and toward the west.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And there came of the children of Benjamin and Judah to the hold unto David.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And David went out to meet them, and answered and said unto them, If ye be come peaceably unto me to help me, mine heart shall be knit unto you: but if ye be come to betray me to mine enemies, seeing there is no wrong in mine hands, the God of our fathers look thereon, and rebuke it.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Then the spirit came upon Amasai, who was chief of the captains, and he said, Thine are we, David, and on thy side, thou son of Jesse: peace, peace be unto thee, and peace be to thine helpers; for thy God helpeth thee. Then David received them, and made them captains of the band.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And there fell some of Manasseh to David, when he came with the Philistines against Saul to battle: but they helped them not: for the lords of the Philistines upon advisement sent him away, saying, He will fall to his master Saul to the jeopardy of our heads.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> As he went to Ziklag, there fell to him of Manasseh, Adnah, and Jozabad, and Jediael, and Michael, and Jozabad, and Elihu, and Zilthai, captains of the thousands that were of Manasseh.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And they helped David against the band of the rovers: for they were all mighty men of valour, and were captains in the host.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> For at that time day by day there came to David to help him, until it was a great host, like the host of God.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And these are the numbers of the bands that were ready armed to the war, and came to David to Hebron, to turn the kingdom of Saul to him, according to the word of the LORD.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> The children of Judah that bare shield and spear were six thousand and eight hundred, ready armed to the war.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Of the children of Simeon, mighty men of valour for the war, seven thousand and one hundred.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Of the children of Levi four thousand and six hundred.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And Jehoiada was the leader of the Aaronites, and with him were three thousand and seven hundred;</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And Zadok, a young man mighty of valour, and of his father's house twenty and two captains.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And of the children of Benjamin, the kindred of Saul, three thousand: for hitherto the greatest part of them had kept the ward of the house of Saul.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And of the children of Ephraim twenty thousand and eight hundred, mighty men of valour, famous throughout the house of their fathers.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> And of the half tribe of Manasseh eighteen thousand, which were expressed by name, to come and make David king.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> And of the children of Issachar, which were men that had understanding of the times, to know what Israel ought to do; the heads of them were two hundred; and all their brethren were at their commandment.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> Of Zebulun, such as went forth to battle, expert in war, with all instruments of war, fifty thousand, which could keep rank: they were not of double heart.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> And of Naphtali a thousand captains, and with them with shield and spear thirty and seven thousand.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> And of the Danites expert in war twenty and eight thousand and six hundred.</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> And of Asher, such as went forth to battle, expert in war, forty thousand.</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> And on the other side of Jordan, of the Reubenites, and the Gadites, and of the half tribe of Manasseh, with all manner of instruments of war for the battle, an hundred and twenty thousand.</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> All these men of war, that could keep rank, came with a perfect heart to Hebron, to make David king over all Israel: and all the rest also of Israel were of one heart to make David king.</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> And there they were with David three days, eating and drinking: for their brethren had prepared for them.</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> Moreover they that were nigh them, even unto Issachar and Zebulun and Naphtali, brought bread on asses, and on camels, and on mules, and on oxen, and meat, meal, cakes of figs, and bunches of raisins, and wine, and oil, and oxen, and sheep abundantly: for there was joy in Israel.</p>
