## Job 39

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Knowest thou the time when the wild goats of the rock bring forth? or canst thou mark when the hinds do calve?</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Canst thou number the months that they fulfil? or knowest thou the time when they bring forth?</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> They bow themselves, they bring forth their young ones, they cast out their sorrows.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Their young ones are in good liking, they grow up with corn; they go forth, and return not unto them.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Who hath sent out the wild ass free? or who hath loosed the bands of the wild ass?</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Whose house I have made the wilderness, and the barren land his dwellings.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> He scorneth the multitude of the city, neither regardeth he the crying of the driver.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> The range of the mountains is his pasture, and he searcheth after every green thing.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Will the unicorn be willing to serve thee, or abide by thy crib?</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Canst thou bind the unicorn with his band in the furrow? or will he harrow the valleys after thee?</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Wilt thou trust him, because his strength is great? or wilt thou leave thy labour to him?</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Wilt thou believe him, that he will bring home thy seed, and gather it into thy barn?</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Gavest thou the goodly wings unto the peacocks? or wings and feathers unto the ostrich?</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Which leaveth her eggs in the earth, and warmeth them in dust,</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And forgetteth that the foot may crush them, or that the wild beast may break them.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> She is hardened against her young ones, as though they were not hers: her labour is in vain without fear;</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Because God hath deprived her of wisdom, neither hath he imparted to her understanding.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> What time she lifteth up herself on high, she scorneth the horse and his rider.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Hast thou given the horse strength? hast thou clothed his neck with thunder?</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Canst thou make him afraid as a grasshopper? the glory of his nostrils is terrible.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> He paweth in the valley, and rejoiceth in his strength: he goeth on to meet the armed men.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> He mocketh at fear, and is not affrighted; neither turneth he back from the sword.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> The quiver rattleth against him, the glittering spear and the shield.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> He swalloweth the ground with fierceness and rage: neither believeth he that it is the sound of the trumpet.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> He saith among the trumpets, Ha, ha; and he smelleth the battle afar off, the thunder of the captains, and the shouting.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Doth the hawk fly by thy wisdom, and stretch her wings toward the south?</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Doth the eagle mount up at thy command, and make her nest on high?</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> She dwelleth and abideth on the rock, upon the crag of the rock, and the strong place.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> From thence she seeketh the prey, and her eyes behold afar off.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Her young ones also suck up blood: and where the slain are, there is she.</p>
