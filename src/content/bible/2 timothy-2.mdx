## 2 Timothy 2

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Thou therefore, my son, be strong in the grace that is in Christ Jesus.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And the things that thou hast heard of me among many witnesses, the same commit thou to faithful men, who shall be able to teach others also.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Thou therefore endure hardness, as a good soldier of Jesus Christ.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> No man that warreth entangleth himself with the affairs of this life; that he may please him who hath chosen him to be a soldier.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And if a man also strive for masteries, yet is he not crowned, except he strive lawfully.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> The husbandman that laboureth must be first partaker of the fruits.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Consider what I say; and the Lord give thee understanding in all things.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Remember that Jesus Christ of the seed of David was raised from the dead according to my gospel:</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Wherein I suffer trouble, as an evil doer, even unto bonds; but the word of God is not bound.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Therefore I endure all things for the elect's sakes, that they may also obtain the salvation which is in Christ Jesus with eternal glory.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> It is a faithful saying: For if we be dead with him, we shall also live with him:</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> If we suffer, we shall also reign with him: if we deny him, he also will deny us:</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> If we believe not, yet he abideth faithful: he cannot deny himself.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Of these things put them in remembrance, charging them before the Lord that they strive not about words to no profit, but to the subverting of the hearers.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Study to shew thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> But shun profane and vain babblings: for they will increase unto more ungodliness.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And their word will eat as doth a canker: of whom is Hymenaeus and Philetus;</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Who concerning the truth have erred, saying that the resurrection is past already; and overthrow the faith of some.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Nevertheless the foundation of God standeth sure, having this seal, The Lord knoweth them that are his. And, Let every one that nameth the name of Christ depart from iniquity.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> But in a great house there are not only vessels of gold and of silver, but also of wood and of earth; and some to honour, and some to dishonour.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> If a man therefore purge himself from these, he shall be a vessel unto honour, sanctified, and meet for the master's use, and prepared unto every good work.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Flee also youthful lusts: but follow righteousness, faith, charity, peace, with them that call on the Lord out of a pure heart.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> But foolish and unlearned questions avoid, knowing that they do gender strifes.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And the servant of the Lord must not strive; but be gentle unto all men, apt to teach, patient,</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> In meekness instructing those that oppose themselves; if God peradventure will give them repentance to the acknowledging of the truth;</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And that they may recover themselves out of the snare of the devil, who are taken captive by him at his will.</p>
