## Lamentations 3

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> I am the man that hath seen affliction by the rod of his wrath.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> He hath led me, and brought me into darkness, but not into light.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Surely against me is he turned; he turneth his hand against me all the day.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> My flesh and my skin hath he made old; he hath broken my bones.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> He hath builded against me, and compassed me with gall and travail.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> He hath set me in dark places, as they that be dead of old.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> He hath hedged me about, that I cannot get out: he hath made my chain heavy.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Also when I cry and shout, he shutteth out my prayer.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> He hath inclosed my ways with hewn stone, he hath made my paths crooked.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> He was unto me as a bear lying in wait, and as a lion in secret places.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> He hath turned aside my ways, and pulled me in pieces: he hath made me desolate.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> He hath bent his bow, and set me as a mark for the arrow.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> He hath caused the arrows of his quiver to enter into my reins.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> I was a derision to all my people; and their song all the day.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> He hath filled me with bitterness, he hath made me drunken with wormwood.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> He hath also broken my teeth with gravel stones, he hath covered me with ashes.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And thou hast removed my soul far off from peace: I forgat prosperity.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And I said, My strength and my hope is perished from the LORD:</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Remembering mine affliction and my misery, the wormwood and the gall.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> My soul hath them still in remembrance, and is humbled in me.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> This I recall to my mind, therefore have I hope.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Â¶ It is of the LORD'S mercies that we are not consumed, because his compassions fail not.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> They are new every morning: great is thy faithfulness.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> The LORD is my portion, saith my soul; therefore will I hope in him.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> The LORD is good unto them that wait for him, to the soul that seeketh him.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> It is good that a man should both hope and quietly wait for the salvation of the LORD.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> It is good for a man that he bear the yoke in his youth.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> He sitteth alone and keepeth silence, because he hath borne it upon him.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> He putteth his mouth in the dust; if so be there may be hope.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> He giveth his cheek to him that smiteth him: he is filled full with reproach.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> For the Lord will not cast off for ever:</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> But though he cause grief, yet will he have compassion according to the multitude of his mercies.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> For he doth not afflict willingly nor grieve the children of men.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> To crush under his feet all the prisoners of the earth,</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> To turn aside the right of a man before the face of the most High,</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> To subvert a man in his cause, the Lord approveth not.</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> Â¶ Who is he that saith, and it cometh to pass, when the Lord commandeth it not?</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> Out of the mouth of the most High proceedeth not evil and good?</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> Wherefore doth a living man complain, a man for the punishment of his sins?</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> Let us search and try our ways, and turn again to the LORD.</p>

<p id="v41" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">41</span> Let us lift up our heart with our hands unto God in the heavens.</p>

<p id="v42" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">42</span> We have transgressed and have rebelled: thou hast not pardoned.</p>

<p id="v43" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">43</span> Thou hast covered with anger, and persecuted us: thou hast slain, thou hast not pitied.</p>

<p id="v44" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">44</span> Thou hast covered thyself with a cloud, that our prayer should not pass through.</p>

<p id="v45" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">45</span> Thou hast made us as the offscouring and refuse in the midst of the people.</p>

<p id="v46" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">46</span> All our enemies have opened their mouths against us.</p>

<p id="v47" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">47</span> Fear and a snare is come upon us, desolation and destruction.</p>

<p id="v48" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">48</span> Mine eye runneth down with rivers of water for the destruction of the daughter of my people.</p>

<p id="v49" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">49</span> Mine eye trickleth down, and ceaseth not, without any intermission,</p>

<p id="v50" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">50</span> Till the LORD look down, and behold from heaven.</p>

<p id="v51" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">51</span> Mine eye affecteth mine heart because of all the daughters of my city.</p>

<p id="v52" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">52</span> Mine enemies chased me sore, like a bird, without cause.</p>

<p id="v53" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">53</span> They have cut off my life in the dungeon, and cast a stone upon me.</p>

<p id="v54" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">54</span> Waters flowed over mine head; then I said, I am cut off.</p>

<p id="v55" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">55</span> Â¶ I called upon thy name, O LORD, out of the low dungeon.</p>

<p id="v56" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">56</span> Thou hast heard my voice: hide not thine ear at my breathing, at my cry.</p>

<p id="v57" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">57</span> Thou drewest near in the day that I called upon thee: thou saidst, Fear not.</p>

<p id="v58" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">58</span> O Lord, thou hast pleaded the causes of my soul; thou hast redeemed my life.</p>

<p id="v59" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">59</span> O LORD, thou hast seen my wrong: judge thou my cause.</p>

<p id="v60" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">60</span> Thou hast seen all their vengeance and all their imaginations against me.</p>

<p id="v61" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">61</span> Thou hast heard their reproach, O LORD, and all their imaginations against me;</p>

<p id="v62" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">62</span> The lips of those that rose up against me, and their device against me all the day.</p>

<p id="v63" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">63</span> Behold their sitting down, and their rising up; I am their musick.</p>

<p id="v64" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">64</span> Â¶ Render unto them a recompence, O LORD, according to the work of their hands.</p>

<p id="v65" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">65</span> Give them sorrow of heart, thy curse unto them.</p>

<p id="v66" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">66</span> Persecute and destroy them in anger from under the heavens of the LORD.</p>
