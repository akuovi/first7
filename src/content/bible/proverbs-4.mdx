## Proverbs 4

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Hear, ye children, the instruction of a father, and attend to know understanding.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> For I give you good doctrine, forsake ye not my law.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For I was my father's son, tender and only beloved in the sight of my mother.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> He taught me also, and said unto me, Let thine heart retain my words: keep my commandments, and live.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Get wisdom, get understanding: forget it not; neither decline from the words of my mouth.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Forsake her not, and she shall preserve thee: love her, and she shall keep thee.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Wisdom is the principal thing; therefore get wisdom: and with all thy getting get understanding.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Exalt her, and she shall promote thee: she shall bring thee to honour, when thou dost embrace her.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> She shall give to thine head an ornament of grace: a crown of glory shall she deliver to thee.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Hear, O my son, and receive my sayings; and the years of thy life shall be many.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> I have taught thee in the way of wisdom; I have led thee in right paths.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> When thou goest, thy steps shall not be straitened; and when thou runnest, thou shalt not stumble.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Take fast hold of instruction; let her not go: keep her; for she is thy life.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Enter not into the path of the wicked, and go not in the way of evil men.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Avoid it, pass not by it, turn from it, and pass away.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> For they sleep not, except they have done mischief; and their sleep is taken away, unless they cause some to fall.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> For they eat the bread of wickedness, and drink the wine of violence.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> But the path of the just is as the shining light, that shineth more and more unto the perfect day.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> The way of the wicked is as darkness: they know not at what they stumble.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> My son, attend to my words; incline thine ear unto my sayings.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Let them not depart from thine eyes; keep them in the midst of thine heart.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> For they are life unto those that find them, and health to all their flesh.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Keep thy heart with all diligence; for out of it are the issues of life.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Put away from thee a froward mouth, and perverse lips put far from thee.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Let thine eyes look right on, and let thine eyelids look straight before thee.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Ponder the path of thy feet, and let all thy ways be established.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Turn not to the right hand nor to the left: remove thy foot from evil.</p>
