## Proverbs 5

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> My son, attend unto my wisdom, and bow thine ear to my understanding:</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> That thou mayest regard discretion, and that thy lips may keep knowledge.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For the lips of a strange woman drop as an honeycomb, and her mouth is smoother than oil:</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> But her end is bitter as wormwood, sharp as a twoedged sword.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Her feet go down to death; her steps take hold on hell.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Lest thou shouldest ponder the path of life, her ways are moveable, that thou canst not know them.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Hear me now therefore, O ye children, and depart not from the words of my mouth.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Remove thy way far from her, and come not nigh the door of her house:</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Lest thou give thine honour unto others, and thy years unto the cruel:</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Lest strangers be filled with thy wealth; and thy labours be in the house of a stranger;</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And thou mourn at the last, when thy flesh and thy body are consumed,</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And say, How have I hated instruction, and my heart despised reproof;</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And have not obeyed the voice of my teachers, nor inclined mine ear to them that instructed me!</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> I was almost in all evil in the midst of the congregation and assembly.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Drink waters out of thine own cistern, and running waters out of thine own well.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Let thy fountains be dispersed abroad, and rivers of waters in the streets.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Let them be only thine own, and not strangers' with thee.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Let thy fountain be blessed: and rejoice with the wife of thy youth.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Let her be as the loving hind and pleasant roe; let her breasts satisfy thee at all times; and be thou ravished always with her love.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And why wilt thou, my son, be ravished with a strange woman, and embrace the bosom of a stranger?</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> For the ways of man are before the eyes of the LORD, and he pondereth all his goings.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> His own iniquities shall take the wicked himself, and he shall be holden with the cords of his sins.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> He shall die without instruction; and in the greatness of his folly he shall go astray.</p>
