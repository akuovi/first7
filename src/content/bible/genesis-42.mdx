## Genesis 42

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Now when Jacob saw that there was corn in Egypt, Jacob said unto his sons, Why do ye look one upon another?</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And he said, Behold, I have heard that there is corn in Egypt: get you down thither, and buy for us from thence; that we may live, and not die.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And Joseph's ten brethren went down to buy corn in Egypt.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> But Benjamin, Joseph's brother, Jacob sent not with his brethren; for he said, Lest peradventure mischief befall him.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And the sons of Israel came to buy corn among those that came: for the famine was in the land of Canaan.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And Joseph was the governor over the land, and he it was that sold to all the people of the land: and Joseph's brethren came, and bowed down themselves before him with their faces to the earth.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And Joseph saw his brethren, and he knew them, but made himself strange unto them, and spake roughly unto them; and he said unto them, Whence come ye? And they said, From the land of Canaan to buy food.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And Joseph knew his brethren, but they knew not him.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And Joseph remembered the dreams which he dreamed of them, and said unto them, Ye are spies; to see the nakedness of the land ye are come.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And they said unto him, Nay, my lord, but to buy food are thy servants come.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> We are all one man's sons; we are true men, thy servants are no spies.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And he said unto them, Nay, but to see the nakedness of the land ye are come.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And they said, Thy servants are twelve brethren, the sons of one man in the land of Canaan; and, behold, the youngest is this day with our father, and one is not.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And Joseph said unto them, That is it that I spake unto you, saying, Ye are spies:</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Hereby ye shall be proved: By the life of Pharaoh ye shall not go forth hence, except your youngest brother come hither.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Send one of you, and let him fetch your brother, and ye shall be kept in prison, that your words may be proved, whether there be any truth in you: or else by the life of Pharaoh surely ye are spies.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And he put them all together into ward three days.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And Joseph said unto them the third day, This do, and live; for I fear God:</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> If ye be true men, let one of your brethren be bound in the house of your prison: go ye, carry corn for the famine of your houses:</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> But bring your youngest brother unto me; so shall your words be verified, and ye shall not die. And they did so.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And they said one to another, We are verily guilty concerning our brother, in that we saw the anguish of his soul, when he besought us, and we would not hear; therefore is this distress come upon us.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And Reuben answered them, saying, Spake I not unto you, saying, Do not sin against the child; and ye would not hear? therefore, behold, also his blood is required.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And they knew not that Joseph understood them; for he spake unto them by an interpreter.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And he turned himself about from them, and wept; and returned to them again, and communed with them, and took from them Simeon, and bound him before their eyes.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Then Joseph commanded to fill their sacks with corn, and to restore every man's money into his sack, and to give them provision for the way: and thus did he unto them.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And they laded their asses with the corn, and departed thence.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And as one of them opened his sack to give his ass provender in the inn, he espied his money; for, behold, it was in his sack's mouth.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And he said unto his brethren, My money is restored; and, lo, it is even in my sack: and their heart failed them, and they were afraid, saying one to another, What is this that God hath done unto us?</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And they came unto Jacob their father unto the land of Canaan, and told him all that befell unto them; saying,</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> The man, who is the lord of the land, spake roughly to us, and took us for spies of the country.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> And we said unto him, We are true men; we are no spies:</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> We be twelve brethren, sons of our father; one is not, and the youngest is this day with our father in the land of Canaan.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> And the man, the lord of the country, said unto us, Hereby shall I know that ye are true men; leave one of your brethren here with me, and take food for the famine of your households, and be gone:</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> And bring your youngest brother unto me: then shall I know that ye are no spies, but that ye are true men: so will I deliver you your brother, and ye shall traffick in the land.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> And it came to pass as they emptied their sacks, that, behold, every man's bundle of money was in his sack: and when both they and their father saw the bundles of money, they were afraid.</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> And Jacob their father said unto them, Me have ye bereaved of my children: Joseph is not, and Simeon is not, and ye will take Benjamin away: all these things are against me.</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> And Reuben spake unto his father, saying, Slay my two sons, if I bring him not to thee: deliver him into my hand, and I will bring him to thee again.</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> And he said, My son shall not go down with you; for his brother is dead, and he is left alone: if mischief befall him by the way in the which ye go, then shall ye bring down my gray hairs with sorrow to the grave.</p>
