## Matthew 6

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Take heed that ye do not your alms before men, to be seen of them: otherwise ye have no reward of your Father which is in heaven.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Therefore when thou doest thine alms, do not sound a trumpet before thee, as the hypocrites do in the synagogues and in the streets, that they may have glory of men. Verily I say unto you, They have their reward.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> But when thou doest alms, let not thy left hand know what thy right hand doeth:</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> That thine alms may be in secret: and thy Father which seeth in secret himself shall reward thee openly.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And when thou prayest, thou shalt not be as the hypocrites are: for they love to pray standing in the synagogues and in the corners of the streets, that they may be seen of men. Verily I say unto you, They have their reward.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> But thou, when thou prayest, enter into thy closet, and when thou hast shut thy door, pray to thy Father which is in secret; and thy Father which seeth in secret shall reward thee openly.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> But when ye pray, use not vain repetitions, as the heathen do: for they think that they shall be heard for their much speaking.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Be not ye therefore like unto them: for your Father knoweth what things ye have need of, before ye ask him.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> After this manner therefore pray ye: Our Father which art in heaven, Hallowed be thy name.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Thy kingdom come. Thy will be done in earth, as it is in heaven.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Give us this day our daily bread.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And forgive us our debts, as we forgive our debtors.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil: For thine is the kingdom, and the power, and the glory, for ever. Amen.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> For if ye forgive men their trespasses, your heavenly Father will also forgive you:</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> But if ye forgive not men their trespasses, neither will your Father forgive your trespasses.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Moreover when ye fast, be not, as the hypocrites, of a sad countenance: for they disfigure their faces, that they may appear unto men to fast. Verily I say unto you, They have their reward.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> But thou, when thou fastest, anoint thine head, and wash thy face;</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> That thou appear not unto men to fast, but unto thy Father which is in secret: and thy Father, which seeth in secret, shall reward thee openly.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Lay not up for yourselves treasures upon earth, where moth and rust doth corrupt, and where thieves break through and steal:</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> But lay up for yourselves treasures in heaven, where neither moth nor rust doth corrupt, and where thieves do not break through nor steal:</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> For where your treasure is, there will your heart be also.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> The light of the body is the eye: if therefore thine eye be single, thy whole body shall be full of light.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> But if thine eye be evil, thy whole body shall be full of darkness. If therefore the light that is in thee be darkness, how great is that darkness!</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> No man can serve two masters: for either he will hate the one, and love the other; or else he will hold to the one, and despise the other. Ye cannot serve God and mammon.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Therefore I say unto you, Take no thought for your life, what ye shall eat, or what ye shall drink; nor yet for your body, what ye shall put on. Is not the life more than meat, and the body than raiment?</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Behold the fowls of the air: for they sow not, neither do they reap, nor gather into barns; yet your heavenly Father feedeth them. Are ye not much better than they?</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Which of you by taking thought can add one cubit unto his stature?</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And why take ye thought for raiment? Consider the lilies of the field, how they grow; they toil not, neither do they spin:</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And yet I say unto you, That even Solomon in all his glory was not arrayed like one of these.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Wherefore, if God so clothe the grass of the field, which to day is, and to morrow is cast into the oven, shall he not much more clothe you, O ye of little faith?</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Therefore take no thought, saying, What shall we eat? or, What shall we drink? or, Wherewithal shall we be clothed?</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> (For after all these things do the Gentiles seek:) for your heavenly Father knoweth that ye have need of all these things.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> But seek ye first the kingdom of God, and his righteousness; and all these things shall be added unto you.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> Take therefore no thought for the morrow: for the morrow shall take thought for the things of itself. Sufficient unto the day is the evil thereof.</p>
