## Proverbs 11

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> A false balance is abomination to the LORD: but a just weight is his delight.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> When pride cometh, then cometh shame: but with the lowly is wisdom.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> The integrity of the upright shall guide them: but the perverseness of transgressors shall destroy them.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Riches profit not in the day of wrath: but righteousness delivereth from death.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> The righteousness of the perfect shall direct his way: but the wicked shall fall by his own wickedness.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> The righteousness of the upright shall deliver them: but transgressors shall be taken in their own naughtiness.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> When a wicked man dieth, his expectation shall perish: and the hope of unjust men perisheth.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> The righteous is delivered out of trouble, and the wicked cometh in his stead.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> An hypocrite with his mouth destroyeth his neighbour: but through knowledge shall the just be delivered.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> When it goeth well with the righteous, the city rejoiceth: and when the wicked perish, there is shouting.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> By the blessing of the upright the city is exalted: but it is overthrown by the mouth of the wicked.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> He that is void of wisdom despiseth his neighbour: but a man of understanding holdeth his peace.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> A talebearer revealeth secrets: but he that is of a faithful spirit concealeth the matter.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Where no counsel is, the people fall: but in the multitude of counsellors there is safety.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> He that is surety for a stranger shall smart for it: and he that hateth suretiship is sure.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> A gracious woman retaineth honour: and strong men retain riches.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> The merciful man doeth good to his own soul: but he that is cruel troubleth his own flesh.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> The wicked worketh a deceitful work: but to him that soweth righteousness shall be a sure reward.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> As righteousness tendeth to life: so he that pursueth evil pursueth it to his own death.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> They that are of a froward heart are abomination to the LORD: but such as are upright in their way are his delight.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Though hand join in hand, the wicked shall not be unpunished: but the seed of the righteous shall be delivered.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> As a jewel of gold in a swine's snout, so is a fair woman which is without discretion.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> The desire of the righteous is only good: but the expectation of the wicked is wrath.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> There is that scattereth, and yet increaseth; and there is that withholdeth more than is meet, but it tendeth to poverty.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> The liberal soul shall be made fat: and he that watereth shall be watered also himself.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> He that withholdeth corn, the people shall curse him: but blessing shall be upon the head of him that selleth it.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> He that diligently seeketh good procureth favour: but he that seeketh mischief, it shall come unto him.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> He that trusteth in his riches shall fall: but the righteous shall flourish as a branch.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> He that troubleth his own house shall inherit the wind: and the fool shall be servant to the wise of heart.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> The fruit of the righteous is a tree of life; and he that winneth souls is wise.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Behold, the righteous shall be recompensed in the earth: much more the wicked and the sinner.</p>
