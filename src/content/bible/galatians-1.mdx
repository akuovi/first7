## Galatians 1

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Paul, an apostle, (not of men, neither by man, but by Jesus Christ, and God the Father, who raised him from the dead;)</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And all the brethren which are with me, unto the churches of Galatia:</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Grace be to you and peace from God the Father, and from our Lord Jesus Christ,</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Who gave himself for our sins, that he might deliver us from this present evil world, according to the will of God and our Father:</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> To whom be glory for ever and ever. Amen.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> I marvel that ye are so soon removed from him that called you into the grace of Christ unto another gospel:</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Which is not another; but there be some that trouble you, and would pervert the gospel of Christ.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> But though we, or an angel from heaven, preach any other gospel unto you than that which we have preached unto you, let him be accursed.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> As we said before, so say I now again, If any man preach any other gospel unto you than that ye have received, let him be accursed.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> For do I now persuade men, or God? or do I seek to please men? for if I yet pleased men, I should not be the servant of Christ.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> But I certify you, brethren, that the gospel which was preached of me is not after man.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> For I neither received it of man, neither was I taught it, but by the revelation of Jesus Christ.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> For ye have heard of my conversation in time past in the Jews' religion, how that beyond measure I persecuted the church of God, and wasted it:</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And profited in the Jews' religion above many my equals in mine own nation, being more exceedingly zealous of the traditions of my fathers.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> But when it pleased God, who separated me from my mother's womb, and called me by his grace,</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> To reveal his Son in me, that I might preach him among the heathen; immediately I conferred not with flesh and blood:</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Neither went I up to Jerusalem to them which were apostles before me; but I went into Arabia, and returned again unto Damascus.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Then after three years I went up to Jerusalem to see Peter, and abode with him fifteen days.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> But other of the apostles saw I none, save James the Lord's brother.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Now the things which I write unto you, behold, before God, I lie not.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Afterwards I came into the regions of Syria and Cilicia;</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And was unknown by face unto the churches of Judaea which were in Christ:</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> But they had heard only, That he which persecuted us in times past now preacheth the faith which once he destroyed.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And they glorified God in me.</p>
