## 1 Timothy 4

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Now the Spirit speaketh expressly, that in the latter times some shall depart from the faith, giving heed to seducing spirits, and doctrines of devils;</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Speaking lies in hypocrisy; having their conscience seared with a hot iron;</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Forbidding to marry, and commanding to abstain from meats, which God hath created to be received with thanksgiving of them which believe and know the truth.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> For every creature of God is good, and nothing to be refused, if it be received with thanksgiving:</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> For it is sanctified by the word of God and prayer.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> If thou put the brethren in remembrance of these things, thou shalt be a good minister of Jesus Christ, nourished up in the words of faith and of good doctrine, whereunto thou hast attained.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> But refuse profane and old wives' fables, and exercise thyself rather unto godliness.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> For bodily exercise profiteth little: but godliness is profitable unto all things, having promise of the life that now is, and of that which is to come.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> This is a faithful saying and worthy of all acceptation.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> For therefore we both labour and suffer reproach, because we trust in the living God, who is the Saviour of all men, specially of those that believe.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> These things command and teach.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Let no man despise thy youth; but be thou an example of the believers, in word, in conversation, in charity, in spirit, in faith, in purity.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Till I come, give attendance to reading, to exhortation, to doctrine.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Neglect not the gift that is in thee, which was given thee by prophecy, with the laying on of the hands of the presbytery.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Meditate upon these things; give thyself wholly to them; that thy profiting may appear to all.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Take heed unto thyself, and unto the doctrine; continue in them: for in doing this thou shalt both save thyself, and them that hear thee.</p>
