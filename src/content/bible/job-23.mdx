## Job 23

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Then Job answered and said,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Even to day is my complaint bitter: my stroke is heavier than my groaning.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Oh that I knew where I might find him! that I might come even to his seat!</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> I would order my cause before him, and fill my mouth with arguments.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> I would know the words which he would answer me, and understand what he would say unto me.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Will he plead against me with his great power? No; but he would put strength in me.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> There the righteous might dispute with him; so should I be delivered for ever from my judge.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Behold, I go forward, but he is not there; and backward, but I cannot perceive him:</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> On the left hand, where he doth work, but I cannot behold him: he hideth himself on the right hand, that I cannot see him:</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> But he knoweth the way that I take: when he hath tried me, I shall come forth as gold.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> My foot hath held his steps, his way have I kept, and not declined.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Neither have I gone back from the commandment of his lips; I have esteemed the words of his mouth more than my necessary food.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> But he is in one mind, and who can turn him? and what his soul desireth, even that he doeth.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> For he performeth the thing that is appointed for me: and many such things are with him.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Therefore am I troubled at his presence: when I consider, I am afraid of him.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> For God maketh my heart soft, and the Almighty troubleth me:</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Because I was not cut off before the darkness, neither hath he covered the darkness from my face.</p>
