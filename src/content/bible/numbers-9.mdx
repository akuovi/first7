## Numbers 9

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And the LORD spake unto Moses in the wilderness of Sinai, in the first month of the second year after they were come out of the land of Egypt, saying,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Let the children of Israel also keep the passover at his appointed season.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> In the fourteenth day of this month, at even, ye shall keep it in his appointed season: according to all the rites of it, and according to all the ceremonies thereof, shall ye keep it.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And Moses spake unto the children of Israel, that they should keep the passover.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And they kept the passover on the fourteenth day of the first month at even in the wilderness of Sinai: according to all that the LORD commanded Moses, so did the children of Israel.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And there were certain men, who were defiled by the dead body of a man, that they could not keep the passover on that day: and they came before Moses and before Aaron on that day:</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And those men said unto him, We are defiled by the dead body of a man: wherefore are we kept back, that we may not offer an offering of the LORD in his appointed season among the children of Israel?</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And Moses said unto them, Stand still, and I will hear what the LORD will command concerning you.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying,</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Speak unto the children of Israel, saying, If any man of you or of your posterity shall be unclean by reason of a dead body, or be in a journey afar off, yet he shall keep the passover unto the LORD.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> The fourteenth day of the second month at even they shall keep it, and eat it with unleavened bread and bitter herbs.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> They shall leave none of it unto the morning, nor break any bone of it: according to all the ordinances of the passover they shall keep it.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> But the man that is clean, and is not in a journey, and forbeareth to keep the passover, even the same soul shall be cut off from among his people: because he brought not the offering of the LORD in his appointed season, that man shall bear his sin.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And if a stranger shall sojourn among you, and will keep the passover unto the LORD; according to the ordinance of the passover, and according to the manner thereof, so shall he do: ye shall have one ordinance, both for the stranger, and for him that was born in the land.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And on the day that the tabernacle was reared up the cloud covered the tabernacle, namely, the tent of the testimony: and at even there was upon the tabernacle as it were the appearance of fire, until the morning.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> So it was alway: the cloud covered it by day, and the appearance of fire by night.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And when the cloud was taken up from the tabernacle, then after that the children of Israel journeyed: and in the place where the cloud abode, there the children of Israel pitched their tents.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> At the commandment of the LORD the children of Israel journeyed, and at the commandment of the LORD they pitched: as long as the cloud abode upon the tabernacle they rested in their tents.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And when the cloud tarried long upon the tabernacle many days, then the children of Israel kept the charge of the LORD, and journeyed not.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And so it was, when the cloud was a few days upon the tabernacle; according to the commandment of the LORD they abode in their tents, and according to the commandment of the LORD they journeyed.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And so it was, when the cloud abode from even unto the morning, and that the cloud was taken up in the morning, then they journeyed: whether it was by day or by night that the cloud was taken up, they journeyed.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Or whether it were two days, or a month, or a year, that the cloud tarried upon the tabernacle, remaining thereon, the children of Israel abode in their tents, and journeyed not: but when it was taken up, they journeyed.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> At the commandment of the LORD they rested in the tents, and at the commandment of the LORD they journeyed: they kept the charge of the LORD, at the commandment of the LORD by the hand of Moses.</p>
