## 1 Peter 2

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Wherefore laying aside all malice, and all guile, and hypocrisies, and envies, and all evil speakings,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> As newborn babes, desire the sincere milk of the word, that ye may grow thereby:</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> If so be ye have tasted that the Lord is gracious.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> To whom coming, as unto a living stone, disallowed indeed of men, but chosen of God, and precious,</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Ye also, as lively stones, are built up a spiritual house, an holy priesthood, to offer up spiritual sacrifices, acceptable to God by Jesus Christ.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Wherefore also it is contained in the scripture, Behold, I lay in Sion a chief corner stone, elect, precious: and he that believeth on him shall not be confounded.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Unto you therefore which believe he is precious: but unto them which be disobedient, the stone which the builders disallowed, the same is made the head of the corner,</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And a stone of stumbling, and a rock of offence, even to them which stumble at the word, being disobedient: whereunto also they were appointed.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> But ye are a chosen generation, a royal priesthood, an holy nation, a peculiar people; that ye should shew forth the praises of him who hath called you out of darkness into his marvellous light:</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Which in time past were not a people, but are now the people of God: which had not obtained mercy, but now have obtained mercy.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Dearly beloved, I beseech you as strangers and pilgrims, abstain from fleshly lusts, which war against the soul;</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Having your conversation honest among the Gentiles: that, whereas they speak against you as evildoers, they may by your good works, which they shall behold, glorify God in the day of visitation.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Submit yourselves to every ordinance of man for the Lord's sake: whether it be to the king, as supreme;</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Or unto governors, as unto them that are sent by him for the punishment of evildoers, and for the praise of them that do well.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> For so is the will of God, that with well doing ye may put to silence the ignorance of foolish men:</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> As free, and not using your liberty for a cloke of maliciousness, but as the servants of God.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Honour all men. Love the brotherhood. Fear God. Honour the king.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Servants, be subject to your masters with all fear; not only to the good and gentle, but also to the froward.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> For this is thankworthy, if a man for conscience toward God endure grief, suffering wrongfully.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> For what glory is it, if, when ye be buffeted for your faults, ye shall take it patiently? but if, when ye do well, and suffer for it, ye take it patiently, this is acceptable with God.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> For even hereunto were ye called: because Christ also suffered for us, leaving us an example, that ye should follow his steps:</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Who did no sin, neither was guile found in his mouth:</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Who, when he was reviled, reviled not again; when he suffered, he threatened not; but committed himself to him that judgeth righteously:</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Who his own self bare our sins in his own body on the tree, that we, being dead to sins, should live unto righteousness: by whose stripes ye were healed.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> For ye were as sheep going astray; but are now returned unto the Shepherd and Bishop of your souls.</p>
