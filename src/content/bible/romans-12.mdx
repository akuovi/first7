## Romans 12

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> I beseech you therefore, brethren, by the mercies of God, that ye present your bodies a living sacrifice, holy, acceptable unto God, which is your reasonable service.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And be not conformed to this world: but be ye transformed by the renewing of your mind, that ye may prove what is that good, and acceptable, and perfect, will of God.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For I say, through the grace given unto me, to every man that is among you, not to think of himself more highly than he ought to think; but to think soberly, according as God hath dealt to every man the measure of faith.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> For as we have many members in one body, and all members have not the same office:</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> So we, being many, are one body in Christ, and every one members one of another.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Having then gifts differing according to the grace that is given to us, whether prophecy, let us prophesy according to the proportion of faith;</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Or ministry, let us wait on our ministering: or he that teacheth, on teaching;</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Or he that exhorteth, on exhortation: he that giveth, let him do it with simplicity; he that ruleth, with diligence; he that sheweth mercy, with cheerfulness.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Let love be without dissimulation. Abhor that which is evil; cleave to that which is good.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Be kindly affectioned one to another with brotherly love; in honour preferring one another;</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Not slothful in business; fervent in spirit; serving the Lord;</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Rejoicing in hope; patient in tribulation; continuing instant in prayer;</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Distributing to the necessity of saints; given to hospitality.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Bless them which persecute you: bless, and curse not.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Rejoice with them that do rejoice, and weep with them that weep.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Be of the same mind one toward another. Mind not high things, but condescend to men of low estate. Be not wise in your own conceits.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Recompense to no man evil for evil. Provide things honest in the sight of all men.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> If it be possible, as much as lieth in you, live peaceably with all men.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Dearly beloved, avenge not yourselves, but rather give place unto wrath: for it is written, Vengeance is mine; I will repay, saith the Lord.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Therefore if thine enemy hunger, feed him; if he thirst, give him drink: for in so doing thou shalt heap coals of fire on his head.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Be not overcome of evil, but overcome evil with good.</p>
