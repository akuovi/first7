## Genesis 15

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> After these things the word of the LORD came unto Abram in a vision, saying, Fear not, Abram: I am thy shield, and thy exceeding great reward.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And Abram said, Lord GOD, what wilt thou give me, seeing I go childless, and the steward of my house is this Eliezer of Damascus?</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And Abram said, Behold, to me thou hast given no seed: and, lo, one born in my house is mine heir.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And, behold, the word of the LORD came unto him, saying, This shall not be thine heir; but he that shall come forth out of thine own bowels shall be thine heir.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And he brought him forth abroad, and said, Look now toward heaven, and tell the stars, if thou be able to number them: and he said unto him, So shall thy seed be.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And he believed in the LORD; and he counted it to him for righteousness.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And he said unto him, I am the LORD that brought thee out of Ur of the Chaldees, to give thee this land to inherit it.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And he said, Lord GOD, whereby shall I know that I shall inherit it?</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And he said unto him, Take me an heifer of three years old, and a she goat of three years old, and a ram of three years old, and a turtledove, and a young pigeon.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And he took unto him all these, and divided them in the midst, and laid each piece one against another: but the birds divided he not.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And when the fowls came down upon the carcases, Abram drove them away.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And when the sun was going down, a deep sleep fell upon Abram; and, lo, an horror of great darkness fell upon him.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And he said unto Abram, Know of a surety that thy seed shall be a stranger in a land that is not their's, and shall serve them; and they shall afflict them four hundred years;</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And also that nation, whom they shall serve, will I judge: and afterward shall they come out with great substance.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And thou shalt go to thy fathers in peace; thou shalt be buried in a good old age.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> But in the fourth generation they shall come hither again: for the iniquity of the Amorites is not yet full.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And it came to pass, that, when the sun went down, and it was dark, behold a smoking furnace, and a burning lamp that passed between those pieces.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> In the same day the LORD made a covenant with Abram, saying, Unto thy seed have I given this land, from the river of Egypt unto the great river, the river Euphrates:</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> The Kenites, and the Kenizzites, and the Kadmonites,</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And the Hittites, and the Perizzites, and the Rephaims,</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And the Amorites, and the Canaanites, and the Girgashites, and the Jebusites.</p>
