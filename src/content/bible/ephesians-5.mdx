## Ephesians 5

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Be ye therefore followers of God, as dear children;</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And walk in love, as Christ also hath loved us, and hath given himself for us an offering and a sacrifice to God for a sweetsmelling savour.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> But fornication, and all uncleanness, or covetousness, let it not be once named among you, as becometh saints;</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Neither filthiness, nor foolish talking, nor jesting, which are not convenient: but rather giving of thanks.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> For this ye know, that no whoremonger, nor unclean person, nor covetous man, who is an idolater, hath any inheritance in the kingdom of Christ and of God.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Let no man deceive you with vain words: for because of these things cometh the wrath of God upon the children of disobedience.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Be not ye therefore partakers with them.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> For ye were sometimes darkness, but now are ye light in the Lord: walk as children of light:</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> (For the fruit of the Spirit is in all goodness and righteousness and truth;)</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Proving what is acceptable unto the Lord.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And have no fellowship with the unfruitful works of darkness, but rather reprove them.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> For it is a shame even to speak of those things which are done of them in secret.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> But all things that are reproved are made manifest by the light: for whatsoever doth make manifest is light.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Wherefore he saith, Awake thou that sleepest, and arise from the dead, and Christ shall give thee light.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> See then that ye walk circumspectly, not as fools, but as wise,</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Redeeming the time, because the days are evil.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Wherefore be ye not unwise, but understanding what the will of the Lord is.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And be not drunk with wine, wherein is excess; but be filled with the Spirit;</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Speaking to yourselves in psalms and hymns and spiritual songs, singing and making melody in your heart to the Lord;</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Giving thanks always for all things unto God and the Father in the name of our Lord Jesus Christ;</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Submitting yourselves one to another in the fear of God.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Wives, submit yourselves unto your own husbands, as unto the Lord.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> For the husband is the head of the wife, even as Christ is the head of the church: and he is the saviour of the body.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Therefore as the church is subject unto Christ, so let the wives be to their own husbands in every thing.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Husbands, love your wives, even as Christ also loved the church, and gave himself for it;</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> That he might sanctify and cleanse it with the washing of water by the word,</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> That he might present it to himself a glorious church, not having spot, or wrinkle, or any such thing; but that it should be holy and without blemish.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> So ought men to love their wives as their own bodies. He that loveth his wife loveth himself.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> For no man ever yet hated his own flesh; but nourisheth and cherisheth it, even as the Lord the church:</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> For we are members of his body, of his flesh, and of his bones.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> For this cause shall a man leave his father and mother, and shall be joined unto his wife, and they two shall be one flesh.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> This is a great mystery: but I speak concerning Christ and the church.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> Nevertheless let every one of you in particular so love his wife even as himself; and the wife see that she reverence her husband.</p>
