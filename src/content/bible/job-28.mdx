## Job 28

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Surely there is a vein for the silver, and a place for gold where they fine it.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Iron is taken out of the earth, and brass is molten out of the stone.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> He setteth an end to darkness, and searcheth out all perfection: the stones of darkness, and the shadow of death.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> The flood breaketh out from the inhabitant; even the waters forgotten of the foot: they are dried up, they are gone away from men.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> As for the earth, out of it cometh bread: and under it is turned up as it were fire.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> The stones of it are the place of sapphires: and it hath dust of gold.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> There is a path which no fowl knoweth, and which the vulture's eye hath not seen:</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> The lion's whelps have not trodden it, nor the fierce lion passed by it.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> He putteth forth his hand upon the rock; he overturneth the mountains by the roots.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> He cutteth out rivers among the rocks; and his eye seeth every precious thing.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> He bindeth the floods from overflowing; and the thing that is hid bringeth he forth to light.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> But where shall wisdom be found? and where is the place of understanding?</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Man knoweth not the price thereof; neither is it found in the land of the living.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> The depth saith, It is not in me: and the sea saith, It is not with me.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> It cannot be gotten for gold, neither shall silver be weighed for the price thereof.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> It cannot be valued with the gold of Ophir, with the precious onyx, or the sapphire.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> The gold and the crystal cannot equal it: and the exchange of it shall not be for jewels of fine gold.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> No mention shall be made of coral, or of pearls: for the price of wisdom is above rubies.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> The topaz of Ethiopia shall not equal it, neither shall it be valued with pure gold.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Whence then cometh wisdom? and where is the place of understanding?</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Seeing it is hid from the eyes of all living, and kept close from the fowls of the air.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Destruction and death say, We have heard the fame thereof with our ears.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> God understandeth the way thereof, and he knoweth the place thereof.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> For he looketh to the ends of the earth, and seeth under the whole heaven;</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> To make the weight for the winds; and he weigheth the waters by measure.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> When he made a decree for the rain, and a way for the lightning of the thunder:</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Then did he see it, and declare it; he prepared it, yea, and searched it out.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And unto man he said, Behold, the fear of the Lord, that is wisdom; and to depart from evil is understanding.</p>
