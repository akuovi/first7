## Isaiah 44

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Yet now hear, O Jacob my servant; and Israel, whom I have chosen:</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Thus saith the LORD that made thee, and formed thee from the womb, which will help thee; Fear not, O Jacob, my servant; and thou, Jesurun, whom I have chosen.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For I will pour water upon him that is thirsty, and floods upon the dry ground: I will pour my spirit upon thy seed, and my blessing upon thine offspring:</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And they shall spring up as among the grass, as willows by the water courses.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> One shall say, I am the LORD'S; and another shall call himself by the name of Jacob; and another shall subscribe with his hand unto the LORD, and surname himself by the name of Israel.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Thus saith the LORD the King of Israel, and his redeemer the LORD of hosts; I am the first, and I am the last; and beside me there is no God.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And who, as I, shall call, and shall declare it, and set it in order for me, since I appointed the ancient people? and the things that are coming, and shall come, let them shew unto them.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Fear ye not, neither be afraid: have not I told thee from that time, and have declared it? ye are even my witnesses. Is there a God beside me? yea, there is no God; I know not any.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> They that make a graven image are all of them vanity; and their delectable things shall not profit; and they are their own witnesses; they see not, nor know; that they may be ashamed.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Who hath formed a god, or molten a graven image that is profitable for nothing?</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Behold, all his fellows shall be ashamed: and the workmen, they are of men: let them all be gathered together, let them stand up; yet they shall fear, and they shall be ashamed together.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> The smith with the tongs both worketh in the coals, and fashioneth it with hammers, and worketh it with the strength of his arms: yea, he is hungry, and his strength faileth: he drinketh no water, and is faint.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> The carpenter stretcheth out his rule; he marketh it out with a line; he fitteth it with planes, and he marketh it out with the compass, and maketh it after the figure of a man, according to the beauty of a man; that it may remain in the house.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> He heweth him down cedars, and taketh the cypress and the oak, which he strengtheneth for himself among the trees of the forest: he planteth an ash, and the rain doth nourish it.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Then shall it be for a man to burn: for he will take thereof, and warm himself; yea, he kindleth it, and baketh bread; yea, he maketh a god, and worshippeth it; he maketh it a graven image, and falleth down thereto.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> He burneth part thereof in the fire; with part thereof he eateth flesh; he roasteth roast, and is satisfied: yea, he warmeth himself, and saith, Aha, I am warm, I have seen the fire:</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And the residue thereof he maketh a god, even his graven image: he falleth down unto it, and worshippeth it, and prayeth unto it, and saith, Deliver me; for thou art my god.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> They have not known nor understood: for he hath shut their eyes, that they cannot see; and their hearts, that they cannot understand.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And none considereth in his heart, neither is there knowledge nor understanding to say, I have burned part of it in the fire; yea, also I have baked bread upon the coals thereof; I have roasted flesh, and eaten it: and shall I make the residue thereof an abomination? shall I fall down to the stock of a tree?</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> He feedeth on ashes: a deceived heart hath turned him aside, that he cannot deliver his soul, nor say, Is there not a lie in my right hand?</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Remember these, O Jacob and Israel; for thou art my servant: I have formed thee; thou art my servant: O Israel, thou shalt not be forgotten of me.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> I have blotted out, as a thick cloud, thy transgressions, and, as a cloud, thy sins: return unto me; for I have redeemed thee.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Sing, O ye heavens; for the LORD hath done it: shout, ye lower parts of the earth: break forth into singing, ye mountains, O forest, and every tree therein: for the LORD hath redeemed Jacob, and glorified himself in Israel.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Thus saith the LORD, thy redeemer, and he that formed thee from the womb, I am the LORD that maketh all things; that stretcheth forth the heavens alone; that spreadeth abroad the earth by myself;</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> That frustrateth the tokens of the liars, and maketh diviners mad; that turneth wise men backward, and maketh their knowledge foolish;</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> That confirmeth the word of his servant, and performeth the counsel of his messengers; that saith to Jerusalem, Thou shalt be inhabited; and to the cities of Judah, Ye shall be built, and I will raise up the decayed places thereof:</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> That saith to the deep, Be dry, and I will dry up thy rivers:</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> That saith of Cyrus, He is my shepherd, and shall perform all my pleasure: even saying to Jerusalem, Thou shalt be built; and to the temple, Thy foundation shall be laid.</p>
