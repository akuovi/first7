## Isaiah 62

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> For Zion's sake will I not hold my peace, and for Jerusalem's sake I will not rest, until the righteousness thereof go forth as brightness, and the salvation thereof as a lamp that burneth.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And the Gentiles shall see thy righteousness, and all kings thy glory: and thou shalt be called by a new name, which the mouth of the LORD shall name.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Thou shalt also be a crown of glory in the hand of the LORD, and a royal diadem in the hand of thy God.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Thou shalt no more be termed Forsaken; neither shall thy land any more be termed Desolate: but thou shalt be called Hephzi-bah, and thy land Beulah: for the LORD delighteth in thee, and thy land shall be married.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> For as a young man marrieth a virgin, so shall thy sons marry thee: and as the bridegroom rejoiceth over the bride, so shall thy God rejoice over thee.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> I have set watchmen upon thy walls, O Jerusalem, which shall never hold their peace day nor night: ye that make mention of the LORD, keep not silence,</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And give him no rest, till he establish, and till he make Jerusalem a praise in the earth.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> The LORD hath sworn by his right hand, and by the arm of his strength, Surely I will no more give thy corn to be meat for thine enemies; and the sons of the stranger shall not drink thy wine, for the which thou hast laboured:</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> But they that have gathered it shall eat it, and praise the LORD; and they that have brought it together shall drink it in the courts of my holiness.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Go through, go through the gates; prepare ye the way of the people; cast up, cast up the highway; gather out the stones; lift up a standard for the people.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Behold, the LORD hath proclaimed unto the end of the world, Say ye to the daughter of Zion, Behold, thy salvation cometh; behold, his reward is with him, and his work before him.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And they shall call them, The holy people, The redeemed of the LORD: and thou shalt be called, Sought out, A city not forsaken.</p>
