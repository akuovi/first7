## Numbers 33

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> These are the journeys of the children of Israel, which went forth out of the land of Egypt with their armies under the hand of Moses and Aaron.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And Moses wrote their goings out according to their journeys by the commandment of the LORD: and these are their journeys according to their goings out.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And they departed from Rameses in the first month, on the fifteenth day of the first month; on the morrow after the passover the children of Israel went out with an high hand in the sight of all the Egyptians.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> For the Egyptians buried all their firstborn, which the LORD had smitten among them: upon their gods also the LORD executed judgments.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And the children of Israel removed from Rameses, and pitched in Succoth.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And they departed from Succoth, and pitched in Etham, which is in the edge of the wilderness.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And they removed from Etham, and turned again unto Pi-hahiroth, which is before Baal-zephon: and they pitched before Migdol.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And they departed from before Pi-hahiroth, and passed through the midst of the sea into the wilderness, and went three days' journey in the wilderness of Etham, and pitched in Marah.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And they removed from Marah, and came unto Elim: and in Elim were twelve fountains of water, and threescore and ten palm trees; and they pitched there.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And they removed from Elim, and encamped by the Red sea.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And they removed from the Red sea, and encamped in the wilderness of Sin.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And they took their journey out of the wilderness of Sin, and encamped in Dophkah.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And they departed from Dophkah, and encamped in Alush.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And they removed from Alush, and encamped at Rephidim, where was no water for the people to drink.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And they departed from Rephidim, and pitched in the wilderness of Sinai.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And they removed from the desert of Sinai, and pitched at Kibroth-hattaavah.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And they departed from Kibroth-hattaavah, and encamped at Hazeroth.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And they departed from Hazeroth, and pitched in Rithmah.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And they departed from Rithmah, and pitched at Rimmon-parez.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And they departed from Rimmon-parez, and pitched in Libnah.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And they removed from Libnah, and pitched at Rissah.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And they journeyed from Rissah, and pitched in Kehelathah.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And they went from Kehelathah, and pitched in mount Shapher.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And they removed from mount Shapher, and encamped in Haradah.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And they removed from Haradah, and pitched in Makheloth.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And they removed from Makheloth, and encamped at Tahath.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And they departed from Tahath, and pitched at Tarah.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And they removed from Tarah, and pitched in Mithcah.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And they went from Mithcah, and pitched in Hashmonah.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And they departed from Hashmonah, and encamped at Moseroth.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> And they departed from Moseroth, and pitched in Bene-jaakan.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> And they removed from Bene-jaakan, and encamped at Hor-hagidgad.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> And they went from Hor-hagidgad, and pitched in Jotbathah.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> And they removed from Jotbathah, and encamped at Ebronah.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> And they departed from Ebronah, and encamped at Ezion-gaber.</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> And they removed from Ezion-gaber, and pitched in the wilderness of Zin, which is Kadesh.</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> And they removed from Kadesh, and pitched in mount Hor, in the edge of the land of Edom.</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> And Aaron the priest went up into mount Hor at the commandment of the LORD, and died there, in the fortieth year after the children of Israel were come out of the land of Egypt, in the first day of the fifth month.</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> And Aaron was an hundred and twenty and three years old when he died in mount Hor.</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> And king Arad the Canaanite, which dwelt in the south in the land of Canaan, heard of the coming of the children of Israel.</p>

<p id="v41" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">41</span> And they departed from mount Hor, and pitched in Zalmonah.</p>

<p id="v42" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">42</span> And they departed from Zalmonah, and pitched in Punon.</p>

<p id="v43" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">43</span> And they departed from Punon, and pitched in Oboth.</p>

<p id="v44" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">44</span> And they departed from Oboth, and pitched in Ije-abarim, in the border of Moab.</p>

<p id="v45" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">45</span> And they departed from Iim, and pitched in Dibon-gad.</p>

<p id="v46" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">46</span> And they removed from Dibon-gad, and encamped in Almon-diblathaim.</p>

<p id="v47" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">47</span> And they removed from Almon-diblathaim, and pitched in the mountains of Abarim, before Nebo.</p>

<p id="v48" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">48</span> And they departed from the mountains of Abarim, and pitched in the plains of Moab by Jordan near Jericho.</p>

<p id="v49" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">49</span> And they pitched by Jordan, from Beth-jesimoth even unto Abel-shittim in the plains of Moab.</p>

<p id="v50" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">50</span> And the LORD spake unto Moses in the plains of Moab by Jordan near Jericho, saying,</p>

<p id="v51" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">51</span> Speak unto the children of Israel, and say unto them, When ye are passed over Jordan into the land of Canaan;</p>

<p id="v52" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">52</span> Then ye shall drive out all the inhabitants of the land from before you, and destroy all their pictures, and destroy all their molten images, and quite pluck down all their high places:</p>

<p id="v53" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">53</span> And ye shall dispossess the inhabitants of the land, and dwell therein: for I have given you the land to possess it.</p>

<p id="v54" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">54</span> And ye shall divide the land by lot for an inheritance among your families: and to the more ye shall give the more inheritance, and to the fewer ye shall give the less inheritance: every man's inheritance shall be in the place where his lot falleth; according to the tribes of your fathers ye shall inherit.</p>

<p id="v55" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">55</span> But if ye will not drive out the inhabitants of the land from before you; then it shall come to pass, that those which ye let remain of them shall be pricks in your eyes, and thorns in your sides, and shall vex you in the land wherein ye dwell.</p>

<p id="v56" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">56</span> Moreover it shall come to pass, that I shall do unto you, as I thought to do unto them.</p>
