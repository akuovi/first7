## Mark 11

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And when they came nigh to Jerusalem, unto Bethphage and Bethany, at the mount of Olives, he sendeth forth two of his disciples,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And saith unto them, Go your way into the village over against you: and as soon as ye be entered into it, ye shall find a colt tied, whereon never man sat; loose him, and bring him.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And if any man say unto you, Why do ye this? say ye that the Lord hath need of him; and straightway he will send him hither.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And they went their way, and found the colt tied by the door without in a place where two ways met; and they loose him.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And certain of them that stood there said unto them, What do ye, loosing the colt?</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And they said unto them even as Jesus had commanded: and they let them go.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And they brought the colt to Jesus, and cast their garments on him; and he sat upon him.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And many spread their garments in the way: and others cut down branches off the trees, and strawed them in the way.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And they that went before, and they that followed, cried, saying, Hosanna; Blessed is he that cometh in the name of the Lord:</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Blessed be the kingdom of our father David, that cometh in the name of the Lord: Hosanna in the highest.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And Jesus entered into Jerusalem, and into the temple: and when he had looked round about upon all things, and now the eventide was come, he went out unto Bethany with the twelve.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And on the morrow, when they were come from Bethany, he was hungry:</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And seeing a fig tree afar off having leaves, he came, if haply he might find any thing thereon: and when he came to it, he found nothing but leaves; for the time of figs was not yet.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And Jesus answered and said unto it, No man eat fruit of thee hereafter for ever. And his disciples heard it.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And they come to Jerusalem: and Jesus went into the temple, and began to cast out them that sold and bought in the temple, and overthrew the tables of the moneychangers, and the seats of them that sold doves;</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And would not suffer that any man should carry any vessel through the temple.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And he taught, saying unto them, Is it not written, My house shall be called of all nations the house of prayer? but ye have made it a den of thieves.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And the scribes and chief priests heard it, and sought how they might destroy him: for they feared him, because all the people was astonished at his doctrine.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And when even was come, he went out of the city.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And in the morning, as they passed by, they saw the fig tree dried up from the roots.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And Peter calling to remembrance saith unto him, Master, behold, the fig tree which thou cursedst is withered away.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And Jesus answering saith unto them, Have faith in God.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> For verily I say unto you, That whosoever shall say unto this mountain, Be thou removed, and be thou cast into the sea; and shall not doubt in his heart, but shall believe that those things which he saith shall come to pass; he shall have whatsoever he saith.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Therefore I say unto you, What things soever ye desire, when ye pray, believe that ye receive them, and ye shall have them.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And when ye stand praying, forgive, if ye have ought against any: that your Father also which is in heaven may forgive you your trespasses.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> But if ye do not forgive, neither will your Father which is in heaven forgive your trespasses.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And they come again to Jerusalem: and as he was walking in the temple, there come to him the chief priests, and the scribes, and the elders,</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And say unto him, By what authority doest thou these things? and who gave thee this authority to do these things?</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And Jesus answered and said unto them, I will also ask of you one question, and answer me, and I will tell you by what authority I do these things.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> The baptism of John, was it from heaven, or of men? answer me.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> And they reasoned with themselves, saying, If we shall say, From heaven; he will say, Why then did ye not believe him?</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> But if we shall say, Of men; they feared the people: for all men counted John, that he was a prophet indeed.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> And they answered and said unto Jesus, We cannot tell. And Jesus answering saith unto them, Neither do I tell you by what authority I do these things.</p>
