## Leviticus 9

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And it came to pass on the eighth day, that Moses called Aaron and his sons, and the elders of Israel;</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And he said unto Aaron, Take thee a young calf for a sin offering, and a ram for a burnt offering, without blemish, and offer them before the LORD.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And unto the children of Israel thou shalt speak, saying, Take ye a kid of the goats for a sin offering; and a calf and a lamb, both of the first year, without blemish, for a burnt offering;</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Also a bullock and a ram for peace offerings, to sacrifice before the LORD; and a meat offering mingled with oil: for to day the LORD will appear unto you.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And they brought that which Moses commanded before the tabernacle of the congregation: and all the congregation drew near and stood before the LORD.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And Moses said, This is the thing which the LORD commanded that ye should do: and the glory of the LORD shall appear unto you.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And Moses said unto Aaron, Go unto the altar, and offer thy sin offering, and thy burnt offering, and make an atonement for thyself, and for the people: and offer the offering of the people, and make an atonement for them; as the LORD commanded.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Aaron therefore went unto the altar, and slew the calf of the sin offering, which was for himself.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And the sons of Aaron brought the blood unto him: and he dipped his finger in the blood, and put it upon the horns of the altar, and poured out the blood at the bottom of the altar:</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> But the fat, and the kidneys, and the caul above the liver of the sin offering, he burnt upon the altar; as the LORD commanded Moses.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And the flesh and the hide he burnt with fire without the camp.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And he slew the burnt offering; and Aaron's sons presented unto him the blood, which he sprinkled round about upon the altar.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And they presented the burnt offering unto him, with the pieces thereof, and the head: and he burnt them upon the altar.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And he did wash the inwards and the legs, and burnt them upon the burnt offering on the altar.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And he brought the people's offering, and took the goat, which was the sin offering for the people, and slew it, and offered it for sin, as the first.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And he brought the burnt offering, and offered it according to the manner.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And he brought the meat offering, and took an handful thereof, and burnt it upon the altar, beside the burnt sacrifice of the morning.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> He slew also the bullock and the ram for a sacrifice of peace offerings, which was for the people: and Aaron's sons presented unto him the blood, which he sprinkled upon the altar round about,</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And the fat of the bullock and of the ram, the rump, and that which covereth the inwards, and the kidneys, and the caul above the liver:</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And they put the fat upon the breasts, and he burnt the fat upon the altar:</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And the breasts and the right shoulder Aaron waved for a wave offering before the LORD; as Moses commanded.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And Aaron lifted up his hand toward the people, and blessed them, and came down from offering of the sin offering, and the burnt offering, and peace offerings.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And Moses and Aaron went into the tabernacle of the congregation, and came out, and blessed the people: and the glory of the LORD appeared unto all the people.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And there came a fire out from before the LORD, and consumed upon the altar the burnt offering and the fat: which when all the people saw, they shouted, and fell on their faces.</p>
