## Philippians 2

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> If there be therefore any consolation in Christ, if any comfort of love, if any fellowship of the Spirit, if any bowels and mercies,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Fulfil ye my joy, that ye be likeminded, having the same love, being of one accord, of one mind.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Let nothing be done through strife or vainglory; but in lowliness of mind let each esteem other better than themselves.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Look not every man on his own things, but every man also on the things of others.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Let this mind be in you, which was also in Christ Jesus:</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Who, being in the form of God, thought it not robbery to be equal with God:</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> But made himself of no reputation, and took upon him the form of a servant, and was made in the likeness of men:</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And being found in fashion as a man, he humbled himself, and became obedient unto death, even the death of the cross.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Wherefore God also hath highly exalted him, and given him a name which is above every name:</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> That at the name of Jesus every knee should bow, of things in heaven, and things in earth, and things under the earth;</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And that every tongue should confess that Jesus Christ is Lord, to the glory of God the Father.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Wherefore, my beloved, as ye have always obeyed, not as in my presence only, but now much more in my absence, work out your own salvation with fear and trembling.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> For it is God which worketh in you both to will and to do of his good pleasure.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Do all things without murmurings and disputings:</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> That ye may be blameless and harmless, the sons of God, without rebuke, in the midst of a crooked and perverse nation, among whom ye shine as lights in the world;</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Holding forth the word of life; that I may rejoice in the day of Christ, that I have not run in vain, neither laboured in vain.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Yea, and if I be offered upon the sacrifice and service of your faith, I joy, and rejoice with you all.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> For the same cause also do ye joy, and rejoice with me.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> But I trust in the Lord Jesus to send Timotheus shortly unto you, that I also may be of good comfort, when I know your state.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> For I have no man likeminded, who will naturally care for your state.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> For all seek their own, not the things which are Jesus Christ's.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> But ye know the proof of him, that, as a son with the father, he hath served with me in the gospel.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Him therefore I hope to send presently, so soon as I shall see how it will go with me.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> But I trust in the Lord that I also myself shall come shortly.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Yet I supposed it necessary to send to you Epaphroditus, my brother, and companion in labour, and fellowsoldier, but your messenger, and he that ministered to my wants.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> For he longed after you all, and was full of heaviness, because that ye had heard that he had been sick.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> For indeed he was sick nigh unto death: but God had mercy on him; and not on him only, but on me also, lest I should have sorrow upon sorrow.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> I sent him therefore the more carefully, that, when ye see him again, ye may rejoice, and that I may be the less sorrowful.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Receive him therefore in the Lord with all gladness; and hold such in reputation:</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Because for the work of Christ he was nigh unto death, not regarding his life, to supply your lack of service toward me.</p>
