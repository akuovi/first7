## Ezekiel 7

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Moreover the word of the LORD came unto me, saying,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Also, thou son of man, thus saith the Lord GOD unto the land of Israel; An end, the end is come upon the four corners of the land.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Now is the end come upon thee, and I will send mine anger upon thee, and will judge thee according to thy ways, and will recompense upon thee all thine abominations.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And mine eye shall not spare thee, neither will I have pity: but I will recompense thy ways upon thee, and thine abominations shall be in the midst of thee: and ye shall know that I am the LORD.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Thus saith the Lord GOD; An evil, an only evil, behold, is come.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> An end is come, the end is come: it watcheth for thee; behold, it is come.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> The morning is come unto thee, O thou that dwellest in the land: the time is come, the day of trouble is near, and not the sounding again of the mountains.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Now will I shortly pour out my fury upon thee, and accomplish mine anger upon thee: and I will judge thee according to thy ways, and will recompense thee for all thine abominations.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And mine eye shall not spare, neither will I have pity: I will recompense thee according to thy ways and thine abominations that are in the midst of thee; and ye shall know that I am the LORD that smiteth.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Behold the day, behold, it is come: the morning is gone forth; the rod hath blossomed, pride hath budded.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Violence is risen up into a rod of wickedness: none of them shall remain, nor of their multitude, nor of any of their's: neither shall there be wailing for them.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> The time is come, the day draweth near: let not the buyer rejoice, nor the seller mourn: for wrath is upon all the multitude thereof.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> For the seller shall not return to that which is sold, although they were yet alive: for the vision is touching the whole multitude thereof, which shall not return; neither shall any strengthen himself in the iniquity of his life.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> They have blown the trumpet, even to make all ready; but none goeth to the battle: for my wrath is upon all the multitude thereof.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> The sword is without, and the pestilence and the famine within: he that is in the field shall die with the sword; and he that is in the city, famine and pestilence shall devour him.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> But they that escape of them shall escape, and shall be on the mountains like doves of the valleys, all of them mourning, every one for his iniquity.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> All hands shall be feeble, and all knees shall be weak as water.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> They shall also gird themselves with sackcloth, and horror shall cover them; and shame shall be upon all faces, and baldness upon all their heads.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> They shall cast their silver in the streets, and their gold shall be removed: their silver and their gold shall not be able to deliver them in the day of the wrath of the LORD: they shall not satisfy their souls, neither fill their bowels: because it is the stumblingblock of their iniquity.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> As for the beauty of his ornament, he set it in majesty: but they made the images of their abominations and of their detestable things therein: therefore have I set it far from them.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And I will give it into the hands of the strangers for a prey, and to the wicked of the earth for a spoil; and they shall pollute it.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> My face will I turn also from them, and they shall pollute my secret place: for the robbers shall enter into it, and defile it.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Make a chain: for the land is full of bloody crimes, and the city is full of violence.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Wherefore I will bring the worst of the heathen, and they shall possess their houses: I will also make the pomp of the strong to cease; and their holy places shall be defiled.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Destruction cometh; and they shall seek peace, and there shall be none.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Mischief shall come upon mischief, and rumour shall be upon rumour; then shall they seek a vision of the prophet; but the law shall perish from the priest, and counsel from the ancients.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> The king shall mourn, and the prince shall be clothed with desolation, and the hands of the people of the land shall be troubled: I will do unto them after their way, and according to their deserts will I judge them; and they shall know that I am the LORD.</p>
