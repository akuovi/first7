## Titus 1

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Paul, a servant of God, and an apostle of Jesus Christ, according to the faith of God's elect, and the acknowledging of the truth which is after godliness;</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> In hope of eternal life, which God, that cannot lie, promised before the world began;</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> But hath in due times manifested his word through preaching, which is committed unto me according to the commandment of God our Saviour;</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> To Titus, mine own son after the common faith: Grace, mercy, and peace, from God the Father and the Lord Jesus Christ our Saviour.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> For this cause left I thee in Crete, that thou shouldest set in order the things that are wanting, and ordain elders in every city, as I had appointed thee:</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> If any be blameless, the husband of one wife, having faithful children not accused of riot or unruly.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> For a bishop must be blameless, as the steward of God; not selfwilled, not soon angry, not given to wine, no striker, not given to filthy lucre;</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> But a lover of hospitality, a lover of good men, sober, just, holy, temperate;</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Holding fast the faithful word as he hath been taught, that he may be able by sound doctrine both to exhort and to convince the gainsayers.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> For there are many unruly and vain talkers and deceivers, specially they of the circumcision:</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Whose mouths must be stopped, who subvert whole houses, teaching things which they ought not, for filthy lucre's sake.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> One of themselves, even a prophet of their own, said, The Cretians are alway liars, evil beasts, slow bellies.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> This witness is true. Wherefore rebuke them sharply, that they may be sound in the faith;</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Not giving heed to Jewish fables, and commandments of men, that turn from the truth.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Unto the pure all things are pure: but unto them that are defiled and unbelieving is nothing pure; but even their mind and conscience is defiled.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> They profess that they know God; but in works they deny him, being abominable, and disobedient, and unto every good work reprobate.</p>
