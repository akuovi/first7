## Genesis 35

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And God said unto Jacob, Arise, go up to Beth-el, and dwell there: and make there an altar unto God, that appeared unto thee when thou fleddest from the face of Esau thy brother.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Then Jacob said unto his household, and to all that were with him, Put away the strange gods that are among you, and be clean, and change your garments:</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And let us arise, and go up to Beth-el; and I will make there an altar unto God, who answered me in the day of my distress, and was with me in the way which I went.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And they gave unto Jacob all the strange gods which were in their hand, and all their earrings which were in their ears; and Jacob hid them under the oak which was by Shechem.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And they journeyed: and the terror of God was upon the cities that were round about them, and they did not pursue after the sons of Jacob.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> So Jacob came to Luz, which is in the land of Canaan, that is, Beth-el, he and all the people that were with him.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And he built there an altar, and called the place El-beth-el: because there God appeared unto him, when he fled from the face of his brother.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> But Deborah Rebekah's nurse died, and she was buried beneath Beth-el under an oak: and the name of it was called Allon-bachuth.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And God appeared unto Jacob again, when he came out of Padan-aram, and blessed him.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And God said unto him, Thy name is Jacob: thy name shall not be called any more Jacob, but Israel shall be thy name: and he called his name Israel.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And God said unto him, I am God Almighty: be fruitful and multiply; a nation and a company of nations shall be of thee, and kings shall come out of thy loins;</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And the land which I gave Abraham and Isaac, to thee I will give it, and to thy seed after thee will I give the land.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And God went up from him in the place where he talked with him.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And Jacob set up a pillar in the place where he talked with him, even a pillar of stone: and he poured a drink offering thereon, and he poured oil thereon.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And Jacob called the name of the place where God spake with him, Beth-el.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And they journeyed from Beth-el; and there was but a little way to come to Ephrath: and Rachel travailed, and she had hard labour.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And it came to pass, when she was in hard labour, that the midwife said unto her, Fear not; thou shalt have this son also.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And it came to pass, as her soul was in departing, (for she died) that she called his name Ben-oni: but his father called him Benjamin.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And Rachel died, and was buried in the way to Ephrath, which is Beth-lehem.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And Jacob set a pillar upon her grave: that is the pillar of Rachel's grave unto this day.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And Israel journeyed, and spread his tent beyond the tower of Edar.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And it came to pass, when Israel dwelt in that land, that Reuben went and lay with Bilhah his father's concubine: and Israel heard it. Now the sons of Jacob were twelve:</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> The sons of Leah; Reuben, Jacob's firstborn, and Simeon, and Levi, and Judah, and Issachar, and Zebulun:</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> The sons of Rachel; Joseph, and Benjamin:</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And the sons of Bilhah, Rachel's handmaid; Dan, and Naphtali:</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And the sons of Zilpah, Leah's handmaid; Gad, and Asher: these are the sons of Jacob, which were born to him in Padan-aram.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And Jacob came unto Isaac his father unto Mamre, unto the city of Arbah, which is Hebron, where Abraham and Isaac sojourned.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And the days of Isaac were an hundred and fourscore years.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And Isaac gave up the ghost, and died, and was gathered unto his people, being old and full of days: and his sons Esau and Jacob buried him.</p>
