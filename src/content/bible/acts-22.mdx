## Acts 22

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Men, brethren, and fathers, hear ye my defence which I make now unto you.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> (And when they heard that he spake in the Hebrew tongue to them, they kept the more silence: and he saith,)</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> I am verily a man which am a Jew, born in Tarsus, a city in Cilicia, yet brought up in this city at the feet of Gamaliel, and taught according to the perfect manner of the law of the fathers, and was zealous toward God, as ye all are this day.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And I persecuted this way unto the death, binding and delivering into prisons both men and women.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> As also the high priest doth bear me witness, and all the estate of the elders: from whom also I received letters unto the brethren, and went to Damascus, to bring them which were there bound unto Jerusalem, for to be punished.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And it came to pass, that, as I made my journey, and was come nigh unto Damascus about noon, suddenly there shone from heaven a great light round about me.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And I fell unto the ground, and heard a voice saying unto me, Saul, Saul, why persecutest thou me?</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And I answered, Who art thou, Lord? And he said unto me, I am Jesus of Nazareth, whom thou persecutest.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And they that were with me saw indeed the light, and were afraid; but they heard not the voice of him that spake to me.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And I said, What shall I do, Lord? And the Lord said unto me, Arise, and go into Damascus; and there it shall be told thee of all things which are appointed for thee to do.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And when I could not see for the glory of that light, being led by the hand of them that were with me, I came into Damascus.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And one Ananias, a devout man according to the law, having a good report of all the Jews which dwelt there,</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Came unto me, and stood, and said unto me, Brother Saul, receive thy sight. And the same hour I looked up upon him.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And he said, The God of our fathers hath chosen thee, that thou shouldest know his will, and see that Just One, and shouldest hear the voice of his mouth.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> For thou shalt be his witness unto all men of what thou hast seen and heard.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And now why tarriest thou? arise, and be baptized, and wash away thy sins, calling on the name of the Lord.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And it came to pass, that, when I was come again to Jerusalem, even while I prayed in the temple, I was in a trance;</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And saw him saying unto me, Make haste, and get thee quickly out of Jerusalem: for they will not receive thy testimony concerning me.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And I said, Lord, they know that I imprisoned and beat in every synagogue them that believed on thee:</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And when the blood of thy martyr Stephen was shed, I also was standing by, and consenting unto his death, and kept the raiment of them that slew him.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And he said unto me, Depart: for I will send thee far hence unto the Gentiles.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And they gave him audience unto this word, and then lifted up their voices, and said, Away with such a fellow from the earth: for it is not fit that he should live.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And as they cried out, and cast off their clothes, and threw dust into the air,</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> The chief captain commanded him to be brought into the castle, and bade that he should be examined by scourging; that he might know wherefore they cried so against him.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And as they bound him with thongs, Paul said unto the centurion that stood by, Is it lawful for you to scourge a man that is a Roman, and uncondemned?</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> When the centurion heard that, he went and told the chief captain, saying, Take heed what thou doest: for this man is a Roman.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Then the chief captain came, and said unto him, Tell me, art thou a Roman? He said, Yea.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And the chief captain answered, With a great sum obtained I this freedom. And Paul said, But I was free born.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Then straightway they departed from him which should have examined him: and the chief captain also was afraid, after he knew that he was a Roman, and because he had bound him.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> On the morrow, because he would have known the certainty wherefore he was accused of the Jews, he loosed him from his bands, and commanded the chief priests and all their council to appear, and brought Paul down, and set him before them.</p>
