## Job 20

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Then answered Zophar the Naamathite, and said,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Therefore do my thoughts cause me to answer, and for this I make haste.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> I have heard the check of my reproach, and the spirit of my understanding causeth me to answer.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Knowest thou not this of old, since man was placed upon earth,</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> That the triumphing of the wicked is short, and the joy of the hypocrite but for a moment?</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Though his excellency mount up to the heavens, and his head reach unto the clouds;</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Yet he shall perish for ever like his own dung: they which have seen him shall say, Where is he?</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> He shall fly away as a dream, and shall not be found: yea, he shall be chased away as a vision of the night.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> The eye also which saw him shall see him no more; neither shall his place any more behold him.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> His children shall seek to please the poor, and his hands shall restore their goods.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> His bones are full of the sin of his youth, which shall lie down with him in the dust.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Though wickedness be sweet in his mouth, though he hide it under his tongue;</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Though he spare it, and forsake it not; but keep it still within his mouth:</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Yet his meat in his bowels is turned, it is the gall of asps within him.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> He hath swallowed down riches, and he shall vomit them up again: God shall cast them out of his belly.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> He shall suck the poison of asps: the viper's tongue shall slay him.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> He shall not see the rivers, the floods, the brooks of honey and butter.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> That which he laboured for shall he restore, and shall not swallow it down: according to his substance shall the restitution be, and he shall not rejoice therein.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Because he hath oppressed and hath forsaken the poor; because he hath violently taken away an house which he builded not;</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Surely he shall not feel quietness in his belly, he shall not save of that which he desired.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> There shall none of his meat be left; therefore shall no man look for his goods.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> In the fulness of his sufficiency he shall be in straits: every hand of the wicked shall come upon him.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> When he is about to fill his belly, God shall cast the fury of his wrath upon him, and shall rain it upon him while he is eating.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> He shall flee from the iron weapon, and the bow of steel shall strike him through.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> It is drawn, and cometh out of the body; yea, the glittering sword cometh out of his gall: terrors are upon him.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> All darkness shall be hid in his secret places: a fire not blown shall consume him; it shall go ill with him that is left in his tabernacle.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> The heaven shall reveal his iniquity; and the earth shall rise up against him.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> The increase of his house shall depart, and his goods shall flow away in the day of his wrath.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> This is the portion of a wicked man from God, and the heritage appointed unto him by God.</p>
