## Job 37

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> At this also my heart trembleth, and is moved out of his place.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Hear attentively the noise of his voice, and the sound that goeth out of his mouth.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> He directeth it under the whole heaven, and his lightning unto the ends of the earth.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> After it a voice roareth: he thundereth with the voice of his excellency; and he will not stay them when his voice is heard.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> God thundereth marvellously with his voice; great things doeth he, which we cannot comprehend.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> For he saith to the snow, Be thou on the earth; likewise to the small rain, and to the great rain of his strength.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> He sealeth up the hand of every man; that all men may know his work.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Then the beasts go into dens, and remain in their places.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Out of the south cometh the whirlwind: and cold out of the north.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> By the breath of God frost is given: and the breadth of the waters is straitened.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Also by watering he wearieth the thick cloud: he scattereth his bright cloud:</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And it is turned round about by his counsels: that they may do whatsoever he commandeth them upon the face of the world in the earth.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> He causeth it to come, whether for correction, or for his land, or for mercy.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Hearken unto this, O Job: stand still, and consider the wondrous works of God.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Dost thou know when God disposed them, and caused the light of his cloud to shine?</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Dost thou know the balancings of the clouds, the wondrous works of him which is perfect in knowledge?</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> How thy garments are warm, when he quieteth the earth by the south wind?</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Hast thou with him spread out the sky, which is strong, and as a molten looking glass?</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Teach us what we shall say unto him; for we cannot order our speech by reason of darkness.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Shall it be told him that I speak? if a man speak, surely he shall be swallowed up.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And now men see not the bright light which is in the clouds: but the wind passeth, and cleanseth them.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Fair weather cometh out of the north: with God is terrible majesty.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Touching the Almighty, we cannot find him out: he is excellent in power, and in judgment, and in plenty of justice: he will not afflict.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Men do therefore fear him: he respecteth not any that are wise of heart.</p>
