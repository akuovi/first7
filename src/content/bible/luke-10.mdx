## Luke 10

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> After these things the Lord appointed other seventy also, and sent them two and two before his face into every city and place, whither he himself would come.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Therefore said he unto them, The harvest truly is great, but the labourers are few: pray ye therefore the Lord of the harvest, that he would send forth labourers into his harvest.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Go your ways: behold, I send you forth as lambs among wolves.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Carry neither purse, nor scrip, nor shoes: and salute no man by the way.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And into whatsoever house ye enter, first say, Peace be to this house.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And if the son of peace be there, your peace shall rest upon it: if not, it shall turn to you again.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And in the same house remain, eating and drinking such things as they give: for the labourer is worthy of his hire. Go not from house to house.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And into whatsoever city ye enter, and they receive you, eat such things as are set before you:</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And heal the sick that are therein, and say unto them, The kingdom of God is come nigh unto you.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> But into whatsoever city ye enter, and they receive you not, go your ways out into the streets of the same, and say,</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Even the very dust of your city, which cleaveth on us, we do wipe off against you: notwithstanding be ye sure of this, that the kingdom of God is come nigh unto you.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> But I say unto you, that it shall be more tolerable in that day for Sodom, than for that city.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Woe unto thee, Chorazin! woe unto thee, Bethsaida! for if the mighty works had been done in Tyre and Sidon, which have been done in you, they had a great while ago repented, sitting in sackcloth and ashes.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> But it shall be more tolerable for Tyre and Sidon at the judgment, than for you.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And thou, Capernaum, which art exalted to heaven, shalt be thrust down to hell.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> He that heareth you heareth me; and he that despiseth you despiseth me; and he that despiseth me despiseth him that sent me.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And the seventy returned again with joy, saying, Lord, even the devils are subject unto us through thy name.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And he said unto them, I beheld Satan as lightning fall from heaven.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Behold, I give unto you power to tread on serpents and scorpions, and over all the power of the enemy: and nothing shall by any means hurt you.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Notwithstanding in this rejoice not, that the spirits are subject unto you; but rather rejoice, because your names are written in heaven.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> In that hour Jesus rejoiced in spirit, and said, I thank thee, O Father, Lord of heaven and earth, that thou hast hid these things from the wise and prudent, and hast revealed them unto babes: even so, Father; for so it seemed good in thy sight.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> All things are delivered to me of my Father: and no man knoweth who the Son is, but the Father; and who the Father is, but the Son, and he to whom the Son will reveal him.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And he turned him unto his disciples, and said privately, Blessed are the eyes which see the things that ye see:</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> For I tell you, that many prophets and kings have desired to see those things which ye see, and have not seen them; and to hear those things which ye hear, and have not heard them.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And, behold, a certain lawyer stood up, and tempted him, saying, Master, what shall I do to inherit eternal life?</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> He said unto him, What is written in the law? how readest thou?</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And he answering said, Thou shalt love the Lord thy God with all thy heart, and with all thy soul, and with all thy strength, and with all thy mind; and thy neighbour as thyself.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And he said unto him, Thou hast answered right: this do, and thou shalt live.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> But he, willing to justify himself, said unto Jesus, And who is my neighbour?</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And Jesus answering said, A certain man went down from Jerusalem to Jericho, and fell among thieves, which stripped him of his raiment, and wounded him, and departed, leaving him half dead.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> And by chance there came down a certain priest that way: and when he saw him, he passed by on the other side.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> And likewise a Levite, when he was at the place, came and looked on him, and passed by on the other side.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> But a certain Samaritan, as he journeyed, came where he was: and when he saw him, he had compassion on him,</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> And went to him, and bound up his wounds, pouring in oil and wine, and set him on his own beast, and brought him to an inn, and took care of him.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> And on the morrow when he departed, he took out two pence, and gave them to the host, and said unto him, Take care of him; and whatsoever thou spendest more, when I come again, I will repay thee.</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> Which now of these three, thinkest thou, was neighbour unto him that fell among the thieves?</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> And he said, He that shewed mercy on him. Then said Jesus unto him, Go, and do thou likewise.</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> Now it came to pass, as they went, that he entered into a certain village: and a certain woman named Martha received him into her house.</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> And she had a sister called Mary, which also sat at Jesus' feet, and heard his word.</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> But Martha was cumbered about much serving, and came to him, and said, Lord, dost thou not care that my sister hath left me to serve alone? bid her therefore that she help me.</p>

<p id="v41" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">41</span> And Jesus answered and said unto her, Martha, Martha, thou art careful and troubled about many things:</p>

<p id="v42" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">42</span> But one thing is needful: and Mary hath chosen that good part, which shall not be taken away from her.</p>
