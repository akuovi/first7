## 1 Chronicles 17

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Now it came to pass, as David sat in his house, that David said to Nathan the prophet, Lo, I dwell in an house of cedars, but the ark of the covenant of the LORD remaineth under curtains.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Then Nathan said unto David, Do all that is in thine heart; for God is with thee.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And it came to pass the same night, that the word of God came to Nathan, saying,</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Go and tell David my servant, Thus saith the LORD, Thou shalt not build me an house to dwell in:</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> For I have not dwelt in an house since the day that I brought up Israel unto this day; but have gone from tent to tent, and from one tabernacle to another.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Wheresoever I have walked with all Israel, spake I a word to any of the judges of Israel, whom I commanded to feed my people, saying, Why have ye not built me an house of cedars?</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Now therefore thus shalt thou say unto my servant David, Thus saith the LORD of hosts, I took thee from the sheepcote, even from following the sheep, that thou shouldest be ruler over my people Israel:</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And I have been with thee whithersoever thou hast walked, and have cut off all thine enemies from before thee, and have made thee a name like the name of the great men that are in the earth.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Also I will ordain a place for my people Israel, and will plant them, and they shall dwell in their place, and shall be moved no more; neither shall the children of wickedness waste them any more, as at the beginning,</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And since the time that I commanded judges to be over my people Israel. Moreover I will subdue all thine enemies. Furthermore I tell thee that the LORD will build thee an house.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And it shall come to pass, when thy days be expired that thou must go to be with thy fathers, that I will raise up thy seed after thee, which shall be of thy sons; and I will establish his kingdom.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> He shall build me an house, and I will stablish his throne for ever.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> I will be his father, and he shall be my son: and I will not take my mercy away from him, as I took it from him that was before thee:</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> But I will settle him in mine house and in my kingdom for ever: and his throne shall be established for evermore.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> According to all these words, and according to all this vision, so did Nathan speak unto David.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And David the king came and sat before the LORD, and said, Who am I, O LORD God, and what is mine house, that thou hast brought me hitherto?</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And yet this was a small thing in thine eyes, O God; for thou hast also spoken of thy servant's house for a great while to come, and hast regarded me according to the estate of a man of high degree, O LORD God.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> What can David speak more to thee for the honour of thy servant? for thou knowest thy servant.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> O LORD, for thy servant's sake, and according to thine own heart, hast thou done all this greatness, in making known all these great things.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> O LORD, there is none like thee, neither is there any God beside thee, according to all that we have heard with our ears.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And what one nation in the earth is like thy people Israel, whom God went to redeem to be his own people, to make thee a name of greatness and terribleness, by driving out nations from before thy people, whom thou hast redeemed out of Egypt?</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> For thy people Israel didst thou make thine own people for ever; and thou, LORD, becamest their God.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Therefore now, LORD, let the thing that thou hast spoken concerning thy servant and concerning his house be established for ever, and do as thou hast said.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Let it even be established, that thy name may be magnified for ever, saying, The LORD of hosts is the God of Israel, even a God to Israel: and let the house of David thy servant be established before thee.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> For thou, O my God, hast told thy servant that thou wilt build him an house: therefore thy servant hath found in his heart to pray before thee.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And now, LORD, thou art God, and hast promised this goodness unto thy servant:</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Now therefore let it please thee to bless the house of thy servant, that it may be before thee for ever: for thou blessest, O LORD, and it shall be blessed for ever.</p>
