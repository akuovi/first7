## 2 Chronicles 27

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Jotham was twenty and five years old when he began to reign, and he reigned sixteen years in Jerusalem. His mother's name also was Jerushah, the daughter of Zadok.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And he did that which was right in the sight of the LORD, according to all that his father Uzziah did: howbeit he entered not into the temple of the LORD. And the people did yet corruptly.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> He built the high gate of the house of the LORD, and on the wall of Ophel he built much.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Moreover he built cities in the mountains of Judah, and in the forests he built castles and towers.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> He fought also with the king of the Ammonites, and prevailed against them. And the children of Ammon gave him the same year an hundred talents of silver, and ten thousand measures of wheat, and ten thousand of barley. So much did the children of Ammon pay unto him, both the second year, and the third.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> So Jotham became mighty, because he prepared his ways before the LORD his God.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Now the rest of the acts of Jotham, and all his wars, and his ways, lo, they are written in the book of the kings of Israel and Judah.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> He was five and twenty years old when he began to reign, and reigned sixteen years in Jerusalem.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And Jotham slept with his fathers, and they buried him in the city of David: and Ahaz his son reigned in his stead.</p>
