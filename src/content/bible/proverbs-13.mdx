## Proverbs 13

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> A wise son heareth his father's instruction: but a scorner heareth not rebuke.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> A man shall eat good by the fruit of his mouth: but the soul of the transgressors shall eat violence.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> He that keepeth his mouth keepeth his life: but he that openeth wide his lips shall have destruction.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> The soul of the sluggard desireth, and hath nothing: but the soul of the diligent shall be made fat.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> A righteous man hateth lying: but a wicked man is loathsome, and cometh to shame.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Righteousness keepeth him that is upright in the way: but wickedness overthroweth the sinner.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> There is that maketh himself rich, yet hath nothing: there is that maketh himself poor, yet hath great riches.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> The ransom of a man's life are his riches: but the poor heareth not rebuke.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> The light of the righteous rejoiceth: but the lamp of the wicked shall be put out.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Only by pride cometh contention: but with the well advised is wisdom.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Wealth gotten by vanity shall be diminished: but he that gathereth by labour shall increase.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Hope deferred maketh the heart sick: but when the desire cometh, it is a tree of life.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Whoso despiseth the word shall be destroyed: but he that feareth the commandment shall be rewarded.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> The law of the wise is a fountain of life, to depart from the snares of death.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Good understanding giveth favour: but the way of transgressors is hard.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Every prudent man dealeth with knowledge: but a fool layeth open his folly.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> A wicked messenger falleth into mischief: but a faithful ambassador is health.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Poverty and shame shall be to him that refuseth instruction: but he that regardeth reproof shall be honoured.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> The desire accomplished is sweet to the soul: but it is abomination to fools to depart from evil.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> He that walketh with wise men shall be wise: but a companion of fools shall be destroyed.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Evil pursueth sinners: but to the righteous good shall be repayed.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> A good man leaveth an inheritance to his children's children: and the wealth of the sinner is laid up for the just.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Much food is in the tillage of the poor: but there is that is destroyed for want of judgment.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> He that spareth his rod hateth his son: but he that loveth him chasteneth him betimes.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> The righteous eateth to the satisfying of his soul: but the belly of the wicked shall want.</p>
