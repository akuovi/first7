## Zechariah 13

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> In that day there shall be a fountain opened to the house of David and to the inhabitants of Jerusalem for sin and for uncleanness.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And it shall come to pass in that day, saith the LORD of hosts, that I will cut off the names of the idols out of the land, and they shall no more be remembered: and also I will cause the prophets and the unclean spirit to pass out of the land.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And it shall come to pass, that when any shall yet prophesy, then his father and his mother that begat him shall say unto him, Thou shalt not live; for thou speakest lies in the name of the LORD: and his father and his mother that begat him shall thrust him through when he prophesieth.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And it shall come to pass in that day, that the prophets shall be ashamed every one of his vision, when he hath prophesied; neither shall they wear a rough garment to deceive:</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> But he shall say, I am no prophet, I am an husbandman; for man taught me to keep cattle from my youth.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And one shall say unto him, What are these wounds in thine hands? Then he shall answer, Those with which I was wounded in the house of my friends.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Awake, O sword, against my shepherd, and against the man that is my fellow, saith the LORD of hosts: smite the shepherd, and the sheep shall be scattered: and I will turn mine hand upon the little ones.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And it shall come to pass, that in all the land, saith the LORD, two parts therein shall be cut off and die; but the third shall be left therein.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And I will bring the third part through the fire, and will refine them as silver is refined, and will try them as gold is tried: they shall call on my name, and I will hear them: I will say, It is my people: and they shall say, The LORD is my God.</p>
