## Proverbs 8

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Doth not wisdom cry? and understanding put forth her voice?</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> She standeth in the top of high places, by the way in the places of the paths.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> She crieth at the gates, at the entry of the city, at the coming in at the doors.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Unto you, O men, I call; and my voice is to the sons of man.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> O ye simple, understand wisdom: and, ye fools, be ye of an understanding heart.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Hear; for I will speak of excellent things; and the opening of my lips shall be right things.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> For my mouth shall speak truth; and wickedness is an abomination to my lips.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> All the words of my mouth are in righteousness; there is nothing froward or perverse in them.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> They are all plain to him that understandeth, and right to them that find knowledge.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Receive my instruction, and not silver; and knowledge rather than choice gold.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> For wisdom is better than rubies; and all the things that may be desired are not to be compared to it.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> I wisdom dwell with prudence, and find out knowledge of witty inventions.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> The fear of the LORD is to hate evil: pride, and arrogancy, and the evil way, and the froward mouth, do I hate.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Counsel is mine, and sound wisdom: I am understanding; I have strength.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> By me kings reign, and princes decree justice.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> By me princes rule, and nobles, even all the judges of the earth.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> I love them that love me; and those that seek me early shall find me.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Riches and honour are with me; yea, durable riches and righteousness.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> My fruit is better than gold, yea, than fine gold; and my revenue than choice silver.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> I lead in the way of righteousness, in the midst of the paths of judgment:</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> That I may cause those that love me to inherit substance; and I will fill their treasures.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> The LORD possessed me in the beginning of his way, before his works of old.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> I was set up from everlasting, from the beginning, or ever the earth was.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> When there were no depths, I was brought forth; when there were no fountains abounding with water.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Before the mountains were settled, before the hills was I brought forth:</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> While as yet he had not made the earth, nor the fields, nor the highest part of the dust of the world.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> When he prepared the heavens, I was there: when he set a compass upon the face of the depth:</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> When he established the clouds above: when he strengthened the fountains of the deep:</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> When he gave to the sea his decree, that the waters should not pass his commandment: when he appointed the foundations of the earth:</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Then I was by him, as one brought up with him: and I was daily his delight, rejoicing always before him;</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Rejoicing in the habitable part of his earth; and my delights were with the sons of men.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> Now therefore hearken unto me, O ye children: for blessed are they that keep my ways.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> Hear instruction, and be wise, and refuse it not.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> Blessed is the man that heareth me, watching daily at my gates, waiting at the posts of my doors.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> For whoso findeth me findeth life, and shall obtain favour of the LORD.</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> But he that sinneth against me wrongeth his own soul: all they that hate me love death.</p>
