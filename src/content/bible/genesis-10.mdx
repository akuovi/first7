## Genesis 10

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Now these are the generations of the sons of Noah, Shem, Ham, and Japheth: and unto them were sons born after the flood.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> The sons of Japheth; Gomer, and Magog, and Madai, and Javan, and Tubal, and Meshech, and Tiras.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And the sons of Gomer; Ashkenaz, and Riphath, and Togarmah.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And the sons of Javan; Elishah, and Tarshish, Kittim, and Dodanim.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> By these were the isles of the Gentiles divided in their lands; every one after his tongue, after their families, in their nations.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And the sons of Ham; Cush, and Mizraim, and Phut, and Canaan.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And the sons of Cush; Seba, and Havilah, and Sabtah, and Raamah, and Sabtecha: and the sons of Raamah; Sheba, and Dedan.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And Cush begat Nimrod: he began to be a mighty one in the earth.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> He was a mighty hunter before the LORD: wherefore it is said, Even as Nimrod the mighty hunter before the LORD.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And the beginning of his kingdom was Babel, and Erech, and Accad, and Calneh, in the land of Shinar.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Out of that land went forth Asshur, and builded Nineveh, and the city Rehoboth, and Calah,</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And Resen between Nineveh and Calah: the same is a great city.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And Mizraim begat Ludim, and Anamim, and Lehabim, and Naphtuhim,</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And Pathrusim, and Casluhim, (out of whom came Philistim,) and Caphtorim.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And Canaan begat Sidon his firstborn, and Heth,</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And the Jebusite, and the Amorite, and the Girgasite,</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And the Hivite, and the Arkite, and the Sinite,</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And the Arvadite, and the Zemarite, and the Hamathite: and afterward were the families of the Canaanites spread abroad.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And the border of the Canaanites was from Sidon, as thou comest to Gerar, unto Gaza; as thou goest, unto Sodom, and Gomorrah, and Admah, and Zeboim, even unto Lasha.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> These are the sons of Ham, after their families, after their tongues, in their countries, and in their nations.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Unto Shem also, the father of all the children of Eber, the brother of Japheth the elder, even to him were children born.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> The children of Shem; Elam, and Asshur, and Arphaxad, and Lud, and Aram.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And the children of Aram; Uz, and Hul, and Gether, and Mash.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And Arphaxad begat Salah; and Salah begat Eber.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And unto Eber were born two sons: the name of one was Peleg; for in his days was the earth divided; and his brother's name was Joktan.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And Joktan begat Almodad, and Sheleph, and Hazarmaveth, and Jerah,</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And Hadoram, and Uzal, and Diklah,</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And Obal, and Abimael, and Sheba,</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And Ophir, and Havilah, and Jobab: all these were the sons of Joktan.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And their dwelling was from Mesha, as thou goest unto Sephar a mount of the east.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> These are the sons of Shem, after their families, after their tongues, in their lands, after their nations.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> These are the families of the sons of Noah, after their generations, in their nations: and by these were the nations divided in the earth after the flood.</p>
