## Matthew 17

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And after six days Jesus taketh Peter, James, and John his brother, and bringeth them up into an high mountain apart,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And was transfigured before them: and his face did shine as the sun, and his raiment was white as the light.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And, behold, there appeared unto them Moses and Elias talking with him.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Then answered Peter, and said unto Jesus, Lord, it is good for us to be here: if thou wilt, let us make here three tabernacles; one for thee, and one for Moses, and one for Elias.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> While he yet spake, behold, a bright cloud overshadowed them: and behold a voice out of the cloud, which said, This is my beloved Son, in whom I am well pleased; hear ye him.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And when the disciples heard it, they fell on their face, and were sore afraid.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And Jesus came and touched them, and said, Arise, and be not afraid.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And when they had lifted up their eyes, they saw no man, save Jesus only.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And as they came down from the mountain, Jesus charged them, saying, Tell the vision to no man, until the Son of man be risen again from the dead.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And his disciples asked him, saying, Why then say the scribes that Elias must first come?</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And Jesus answered and said unto them, Elias truly shall first come, and restore all things.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> But I say unto you, That Elias is come already, and they knew him not, but have done unto him whatsoever they listed. Likewise shall also the Son of man suffer of them.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Then the disciples understood that he spake unto them of John the Baptist.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And when they were come to the multitude, there came to him a certain man, kneeling down to him, and saying,</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Lord, have mercy on my son: for he is lunatick, and sore vexed: for ofttimes he falleth into the fire, and oft into the water.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And I brought him to thy disciples, and they could not cure him.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Then Jesus answered and said, O faithless and perverse generation, how long shall I be with you? how long shall I suffer you? bring him hither to me.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And Jesus rebuked the devil; and he departed out of him: and the child was cured from that very hour.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Then came the disciples to Jesus apart, and said, Why could not we cast him out?</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And Jesus said unto them, Because of your unbelief: for verily I say unto you, If ye have faith as a grain of mustard seed, ye shall say unto this mountain, Remove hence to yonder place; and it shall remove; and nothing shall be impossible unto you.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Howbeit this kind goeth not out but by prayer and fasting.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And while they abode in Galilee, Jesus said unto them, The Son of man shall be betrayed into the hands of men:</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And they shall kill him, and the third day he shall be raised again. And they were exceeding sorry.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And when they were come to Capernaum, they that received tribute money came to Peter, and said, Doth not your master pay tribute?</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> He saith, Yes. And when he was come into the house, Jesus prevented him, saying, What thinkest thou, Simon? of whom do the kings of the earth take custom or tribute? of their own children, or of strangers?</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Peter saith unto him, Of strangers. Jesus saith unto him, Then are the children free.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Notwithstanding, lest we should offend them, go thou to the sea, and cast an hook, and take up the fish that first cometh up; and when thou hast opened his mouth, thou shalt find a piece of money: that take, and give unto them for me and thee.</p>
