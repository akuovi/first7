## Numbers 34

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Command the children of Israel, and say unto them, When ye come into the land of Canaan; (this is the land that shall fall unto you for an inheritance, even the land of Canaan with the coasts thereof:)</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Then your south quarter shall be from the wilderness of Zin along by the coast of Edom, and your south border shall be the outmost coast of the salt sea eastward:</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And your border shall turn from the south to the ascent of Akrabbim, and pass on to Zin: and the going forth thereof shall be from the south to Kadesh-barnea, and shall go on to Hazar-addar, and pass on to Azmon:</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And the border shall fetch a compass from Azmon unto the river of Egypt, and the goings out of it shall be at the sea.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And as for the western border, ye shall even have the great sea for a border: this shall be your west border.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And this shall be your north border: from the great sea ye shall point out for you mount Hor:</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> From mount Hor ye shall point out your border unto the entrance of Hamath; and the goings forth of the border shall be to Zedad:</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And the border shall go on to Ziphron, and the goings out of it shall be at Hazar-enan: this shall be your north border.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And ye shall point out your east border from Hazar-enan to Shepham:</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And the coast shall go down from Shepham to Riblah, on the east side of Ain; and the border shall descend, and shall reach unto the side of the sea of Chinnereth eastward:</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And the border shall go down to Jordan, and the goings out of it shall be at the salt sea: this shall be your land with the coasts thereof round about.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And Moses commanded the children of Israel, saying, This is the land which ye shall inherit by lot, which the LORD commanded to give unto the nine tribes, and to the half tribe:</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> For the tribe of the children of Reuben according to the house of their fathers, and the tribe of the children of Gad according to the house of their fathers, have received their inheritance; and half the tribe of Manasseh have received their inheritance:</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> The two tribes and the half tribe have received their inheritance on this side Jordan near Jericho eastward, toward the sunrising.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying,</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> These are the names of the men which shall divide the land unto you: Eleazar the priest, and Joshua the son of Nun.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And ye shall take one prince of every tribe, to divide the land by inheritance.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And the names of the men are these: Of the tribe of Judah, Caleb the son of Jephunneh.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And of the tribe of the children of Simeon, Shemuel the son of Ammihud.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Of the tribe of Benjamin, Elidad the son of Chislon.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And the prince of the tribe of the children of Dan, Bukki the son of Jogli.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> The prince of the children of Joseph, for the tribe of the children of Manasseh, Hanniel the son of Ephod.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And the prince of the tribe of the children of Ephraim, Kemuel the son of Shiphtan.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And the prince of the tribe of the children of Zebulun, Elizaphan the son of Parnach.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And the prince of the tribe of the children of Issachar, Paltiel the son of Azzan.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And the prince of the tribe of the children of Asher, Ahihud the son of Shelomi.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And the prince of the tribe of the children of Naphtali, Pedahel the son of Ammihud.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> These are they whom the LORD commanded to divide the inheritance unto the children of Israel in the land of Canaan.</p>
