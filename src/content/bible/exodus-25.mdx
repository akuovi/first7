## Exodus 25

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Speak unto the children of Israel, that they bring me an offering: of every man that giveth it willingly with his heart ye shall take my offering.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And this is the offering which ye shall take of them; gold, and silver, and brass,</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And blue, and purple, and scarlet, and fine linen, and goats' hair,</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And rams' skins dyed red, and badgers' skins, and shittim wood,</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Oil for the light, spices for anointing oil, and for sweet incense,</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Onyx stones, and stones to be set in the ephod, and in the breastplate.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And let them make me a sanctuary; that I may dwell among them.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> According to all that I shew thee, after the pattern of the tabernacle, and the pattern of all the instruments thereof, even so shall ye make it.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And they shall make an ark of shittim wood: two cubits and a half shall be the length thereof, and a cubit and a half the breadth thereof, and a cubit and a half the height thereof.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And thou shalt overlay it with pure gold, within and without shalt thou overlay it, and shalt make upon it a crown of gold round about.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And thou shalt cast four rings of gold for it, and put them in the four corners thereof; and two rings shall be in the one side of it, and two rings in the other side of it.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And thou shalt make staves of shittim wood, and overlay them with gold.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And thou shalt put the staves into the rings by the sides of the ark, that the ark may be borne with them.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> The staves shall be in the rings of the ark: they shall not be taken from it.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And thou shalt put into the ark the testimony which I shall give thee.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And thou shalt make a mercy seat of pure gold: two cubits and a half shall be the length thereof, and a cubit and a half the breadth thereof.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And thou shalt make two cherubims of gold, of beaten work shalt thou make them, in the two ends of the mercy seat.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And make one cherub on the one end, and the other cherub on the other end: even of the mercy seat shall ye make the cherubims on the two ends thereof.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And the cherubims shall stretch forth their wings on high, covering the mercy seat with their wings, and their faces shall look one to another; toward the mercy seat shall the faces of the cherubims be.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And thou shalt put the mercy seat above upon the ark; and in the ark thou shalt put the testimony that I shall give thee.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And there I will meet with thee, and I will commune with thee from above the mercy seat, from between the two cherubims which are upon the ark of the testimony, of all things which I will give thee in commandment unto the children of Israel.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Thou shalt also make a table of shittim wood: two cubits shall be the length thereof, and a cubit the breadth thereof, and a cubit and a half the height thereof.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And thou shalt overlay it with pure gold, and make thereto a crown of gold round about.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And thou shalt make unto it a border of an hand breadth round about, and thou shalt make a golden crown to the border thereof round about.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And thou shalt make for it four rings of gold, and put the rings in the four corners that are on the four feet thereof.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Over against the border shall the rings be for places of the staves to bear the table.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And thou shalt make the staves of shittim wood, and overlay them with gold, that the table may be borne with them.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And thou shalt make the dishes thereof, and spoons thereof, and covers thereof, and bowls thereof, to cover withal: of pure gold shalt thou make them.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And thou shalt set upon the table shewbread before me alway.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> And thou shalt make a candlestick of pure gold: of beaten work shall the candlestick be made: his shaft, and his branches, his bowls, his knops, and his flowers, shall be of the same.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> And six branches shall come out of the sides of it; three branches of the candlestick out of the one side, and three branches of the candlestick out of the other side:</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> Three bowls made like unto almonds, with a knop and a flower in one branch; and three bowls made like almonds in the other branch, with a knop and a flower: so in the six branches that come out of the candlestick.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> And in the candlestick shall be four bowls made like unto almonds, with their knops and their flowers.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> And there shall be a knop under two branches of the same, and a knop under two branches of the same, and a knop under two branches of the same, according to the six branches that proceed out of the candlestick.</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> Their knops and their branches shall be of the same: all it shall be one beaten work of pure gold.</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> And thou shalt make the seven lamps thereof: and they shall light the lamps thereof, that they may give light over against it.</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> And the tongs thereof, and the snuffdishes thereof, shall be of pure gold.</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> Of a talent of pure gold shall he make it, with all these vessels.</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> And look that thou make them after their pattern, which was shewed thee in the mount.</p>
