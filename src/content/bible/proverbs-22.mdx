## Proverbs 22

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> A good name is rather to be chosen than great riches, and loving favour rather than silver and gold.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> The rich and poor meet together: the LORD is the maker of them all.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> A prudent man foreseeth the evil, and hideth himself: but the simple pass on, and are punished.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> By humility and the fear of the LORD are riches, and honour, and life.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Thorns and snares are in the way of the froward: he that doth keep his soul shall be far from them.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Train up a child in the way he should go: and when he is old, he will not depart from it.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> The rich ruleth over the poor, and the borrower is servant to the lender.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> He that soweth iniquity shall reap vanity: and the rod of his anger shall fail.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> He that hath a bountiful eye shall be blessed; for he giveth of his bread to the poor.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Cast out the scorner, and contention shall go out; yea, strife and reproach shall cease.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> He that loveth pureness of heart, for the grace of his lips the king shall be his friend.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> The eyes of the LORD preserve knowledge, and he overthroweth the words of the transgressor.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> The slothful man saith, There is a lion without, I shall be slain in the streets.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> The mouth of strange women is a deep pit: he that is abhorred of the LORD shall fall therein.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Foolishness is bound in the heart of a child; but the rod of correction shall drive it far from him.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> He that oppresseth the poor to increase his riches, and he that giveth to the rich, shall surely come to want.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Bow down thine ear, and hear the words of the wise, and apply thine heart unto my knowledge.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> For it is a pleasant thing if thou keep them within thee; they shall withal be fitted in thy lips.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> That thy trust may be in the LORD, I have made known to thee this day, even to thee.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Have not I written to thee excellent things in counsels and knowledge,</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> That I might make thee know the certainty of the words of truth; that thou mightest answer the words of truth to them that send unto thee?</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Rob not the poor, because he is poor: neither oppress the afflicted in the gate:</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> For the LORD will plead their cause, and spoil the soul of those that spoiled them.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Make no friendship with an angry man; and with a furious man thou shalt not go:</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Lest thou learn his ways, and get a snare to thy soul.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Be not thou one of them that strike hands, or of them that are sureties for debts.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> If thou hast nothing to pay, why should he take away thy bed from under thee?</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Remove not the ancient landmark, which thy fathers have set.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Seest thou a man diligent in his business? he shall stand before kings; he shall not stand before mean men.</p>
