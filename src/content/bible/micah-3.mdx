## Micah 3

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And I said, Hear, I pray you, O heads of Jacob, and ye princes of the house of Israel; Is it not for you to know judgment?</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Who hate the good, and love the evil; who pluck off their skin from off them, and their flesh from off their bones;</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Who also eat the flesh of my people, and flay their skin from off them; and they break their bones, and chop them in pieces, as for the pot, and as flesh within the caldron.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Then shall they cry unto the LORD, but he will not hear them: he will even hide his face from them at that time, as they have behaved themselves ill in their doings.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Thus saith the LORD concerning the prophets that make my people err, that bite with their teeth, and cry, Peace; and he that putteth not into their mouths, they even prepare war against him.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Therefore night shall be unto you, that ye shall not have a vision; and it shall be dark unto you, that ye shall not divine; and the sun shall go down over the prophets, and the day shall be dark over them.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Then shall the seers be ashamed, and the diviners confounded: yea, they shall all cover their lips; for there is no answer of God.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> But truly I am full of power by the spirit of the LORD, and of judgment, and of might, to declare unto Jacob his transgression, and to Israel his sin.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Hear this, I pray you, ye heads of the house of Jacob, and princes of the house of Israel, that abhor judgment, and pervert all equity.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> They build up Zion with blood, and Jerusalem with iniquity.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> The heads thereof judge for reward, and the priests thereof teach for hire, and the prophets thereof divine for money: yet will they lean upon the LORD, and say, Is not the LORD among us? none evil can come upon us.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Therefore shall Zion for your sake be plowed as a field, and Jerusalem shall become heaps, and the mountain of the house as the high places of the forest.</p>
