## 1 Chronicles 3

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Now these were the sons of David, which were born unto him in Hebron; the firstborn Amnon, of Ahinoam the Jezreelitess; the second Daniel, of Abigail the Carmelitess:</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> The third, Absalom the son of Maachah the daughter of Talmai king of Geshur: the fourth, Adonijah the son of Haggith:</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> The fifth, Shephatiah of Abital: the sixth, Ithream by Eglah his wife.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> These six were born unto him in Hebron; and there he reigned seven years and six months: and in Jerusalem he reigned thirty and three years.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And these were born unto him in Jerusalem; Shimea, and Shobab, and Nathan, and Solomon, four, of Bath-shua the daughter of Ammiel:</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Ibhar also, and Elishama, and Eliphelet,</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And Nogah, and Nepheg, and Japhia,</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And Elishama, and Eliada, and Eliphelet, nine.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> These were all the sons of David, beside the sons of the concubines, and Tamar their sister.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And Solomon's son was Rehoboam, Abia his son, Asa his son, Jehoshaphat his son,</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Joram his son, Ahaziah his son, Joash his son,</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Amaziah his son, Azariah his son, Jotham his son,</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Ahaz his son, Hezekiah his son, Manasseh his son,</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Amon his son, Josiah his son.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And the sons of Josiah were, the firstborn Johanan, the second Jehoiakim, the third Zedekiah, the fourth Shallum.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And the sons of Jehoiakim: Jeconiah his son, Zedekiah his son.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And the sons of Jeconiah; Assir, Salathiel his son,</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Malchiram also, and Pedaiah, and Shenazar, Jecamiah, Hoshama, and Nedabiah.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And the sons of Pedaiah were, Zerubbabel, and Shimei: and the sons of Zerubbabel; Meshullam, and Hananiah, and Shelomith their sister:</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And Hashubah, and Ohel, and Berechiah, and Hasadiah, Jushab-hesed, five.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And the sons of Hananiah; Pelatiah, and Jesaiah: the sons of Rephaiah, the sons of Arnan, the sons of Obadiah, the sons of Shechaniah.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And the sons of Shechaniah; Shemaiah: and the sons of Shemaiah; Hattush, and Igeal, and Bariah, and Neariah, and Shaphat, six.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And the sons of Neariah; Elioenai, and Hezekiah, and Azrikam, three.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And the sons of Elioenai were, Hodaiah, and Eliashib, and Pelaiah, and Akkub, and Johanan, and Dalaiah, and Anani, seven.</p>
