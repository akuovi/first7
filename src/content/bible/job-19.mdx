## Job 19

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Then Job answered and said,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> How long will ye vex my soul, and break me in pieces with words?</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> These ten times have ye reproached me: ye are not ashamed that ye make yourselves strange to me.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And be it indeed that I have erred, mine error remaineth with myself.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> If indeed ye will magnify yourselves against me, and plead against me my reproach:</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Know now that God hath overthrown me, and hath compassed me with his net.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Behold, I cry out of wrong, but I am not heard: I cry aloud, but there is no judgment.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> He hath fenced up my way that I cannot pass, and he hath set darkness in my paths.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> He hath stripped me of my glory, and taken the crown from my head.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> He hath destroyed me on every side, and I am gone: and mine hope hath he removed like a tree.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> He hath also kindled his wrath against me, and he counteth me unto him as one of his enemies.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> His troops come together, and raise up their way against me, and encamp round about my tabernacle.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> He hath put my brethren far from me, and mine acquaintance are verily estranged from me.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> My kinsfolk have failed, and my familiar friends have forgotten me.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> They that dwell in mine house, and my maids, count me for a stranger: I am an alien in their sight.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> I called my servant, and he gave me no answer; I intreated him with my mouth.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> My breath is strange to my wife, though I intreated for the children's sake of mine own body.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Yea, young children despised me; I arose, and they spake against me.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> All my inward friends abhorred me: and they whom I loved are turned against me.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> My bone cleaveth to my skin and to my flesh, and I am escaped with the skin of my teeth.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Have pity upon me, have pity upon me, O ye my friends; for the hand of God hath touched me.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Why do ye persecute me as God, and are not satisfied with my flesh?</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Oh that my words were now written! oh that they were printed in a book!</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> That they were graven with an iron pen and lead in the rock for ever!</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> For I know that my redeemer liveth, and that he shall stand at the latter day upon the earth:</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And though after my skin worms destroy this body, yet in my flesh shall I see God:</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Whom I shall see for myself, and mine eyes shall behold, and not another; though my reins be consumed within me.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> But ye should say, Why persecute we him, seeing the root of the matter is found in me?</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Be ye afraid of the sword: for wrath bringeth the punishments of the sword, that ye may know there is a judgment.</p>
