## Job 36

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Elihu also proceeded, and said,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Suffer me a little, and I will shew thee that I have yet to speak on God's behalf.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> I will fetch my knowledge from afar, and will ascribe righteousness to my Maker.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> For truly my words shall not be false: he that is perfect in knowledge is with thee.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Behold, God is mighty, and despiseth not any: he is mighty in strength and wisdom.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> He preserveth not the life of the wicked: but giveth right to the poor.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> He withdraweth not his eyes from the righteous: but with kings are they on the throne; yea, he doth establish them for ever, and they are exalted.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And if they be bound in fetters, and be holden in cords of affliction;</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Then he sheweth them their work, and their transgressions that they have exceeded.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> He openeth also their ear to discipline, and commandeth that they return from iniquity.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> If they obey and serve him, they shall spend their days in prosperity, and their years in pleasures.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> But if they obey not, they shall perish by the sword, and they shall die without knowledge.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> But the hypocrites in heart heap up wrath: they cry not when he bindeth them.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> They die in youth, and their life is among the unclean.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> He delivereth the poor in his affliction, and openeth their ears in oppression.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Even so would he have removed thee out of the strait into a broad place, where there is no straitness; and that which should be set on thy table should be full of fatness.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> But thou hast fulfilled the judgment of the wicked: judgment and justice take hold on thee.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Because there is wrath, beware lest he take thee away with his stroke: then a great ransom cannot deliver thee.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Will he esteem thy riches? no, not gold, nor all the forces of strength.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Desire not the night, when people are cut off in their place.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Take heed, regard not iniquity: for this hast thou chosen rather than affliction.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Behold, God exalteth by his power: who teacheth like him?</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Who hath enjoined him his way? or who can say, Thou hast wrought iniquity?</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Remember that thou magnify his work, which men behold.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Every man may see it; man may behold it afar off.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Behold, God is great, and we know him not, neither can the number of his years be searched out.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> For he maketh small the drops of water: they pour down rain according to the vapour thereof:</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Which the clouds do drop and distil upon man abundantly.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Also can any understand the spreadings of the clouds, or the noise of his tabernacle?</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Behold, he spreadeth his light upon it, and covereth the bottom of the sea.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> For by them judgeth he the people; he giveth meat in abundance.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> With clouds he covereth the light; and commandeth it not to shine by the cloud that cometh betwixt.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> The noise thereof sheweth concerning it, the cattle also concerning the vapour.</p>
