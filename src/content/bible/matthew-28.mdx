## Matthew 28

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> In the end of the sabbath, as it began to dawn toward the first day of the week, came Mary Magdalene and the other Mary to see the sepulchre.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And, behold, there was a great earthquake: for the angel of the Lord descended from heaven, and came and rolled back the stone from the door, and sat upon it.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> His countenance was like lightning, and his raiment white as snow:</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And for fear of him the keepers did shake, and became as dead men.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And the angel answered and said unto the women, Fear not ye: for I know that ye seek Jesus, which was crucified.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> He is not here: for he is risen, as he said. Come, see the place where the Lord lay.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And go quickly, and tell his disciples that he is risen from the dead; and, behold, he goeth before you into Galilee; there shall ye see him: lo, I have told you.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And they departed quickly from the sepulchre with fear and great joy; and did run to bring his disciples word.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And as they went to tell his disciples, behold, Jesus met them, saying, All hail. And they came and held him by the feet, and worshipped him.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Then said Jesus unto them, Be not afraid: go tell my brethren that they go into Galilee, and there shall they see me.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Now when they were going, behold, some of the watch came into the city, and shewed unto the chief priests all the things that were done.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And when they were assembled with the elders, and had taken counsel, they gave large money unto the soldiers,</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Saying, Say ye, His disciples came by night, and stole him away while we slept.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And if this come to the governor's ears, we will persuade him, and secure you.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> So they took the money, and did as they were taught: and this saying is commonly reported among the Jews until this day.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Then the eleven disciples went away into Galilee, into a mountain where Jesus had appointed them.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And when they saw him, they worshipped him: but some doubted.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And Jesus came and spake unto them, saying, All power is given unto me in heaven and in earth.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Go ye therefore, and teach all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost:</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Teaching them to observe all things whatsoever I have commanded you: and, lo, I am with you alway, even unto the end of the world. Amen.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> MATTHEW</p>
