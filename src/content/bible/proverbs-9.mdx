## Proverbs 9

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Wisdom hath builded her house, she hath hewn out her seven pillars:</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> She hath killed her beasts; she hath mingled her wine; she hath also furnished her table.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> She hath sent forth her maidens: she crieth upon the highest places of the city,</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Whoso is simple, let him turn in hither: as for him that wanteth understanding, she saith to him,</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Come, eat of my bread, and drink of the wine which I have mingled.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Forsake the foolish, and live; and go in the way of understanding.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> He that reproveth a scorner getteth to himself shame: and he that rebuketh a wicked man getteth himself a blot.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Reprove not a scorner, lest he hate thee: rebuke a wise man, and he will love thee.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Give instruction to a wise man, and he will be yet wiser: teach a just man, and he will increase in learning.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> The fear of the LORD is the beginning of wisdom: and the knowledge of the holy is understanding.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> For by me thy days shall be multiplied, and the years of thy life shall be increased.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> If thou be wise, thou shalt be wise for thyself: but if thou scornest, thou alone shalt bear it.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> A foolish woman is clamorous: she is simple, and knoweth nothing.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> For she sitteth at the door of her house, on a seat in the high places of the city,</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> To call passengers who go right on their ways:</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Whoso is simple, let him turn in hither: and as for him that wanteth understanding, she saith to him,</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Stolen waters are sweet, and bread eaten in secret is pleasant.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> But he knoweth not that the dead are there; and that her guests are in the depths of hell.</p>
