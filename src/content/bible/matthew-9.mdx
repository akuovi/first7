## Matthew 9

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And he entered into a ship, and passed over, and came into his own city.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And, behold, they brought to him a man sick of the palsy, lying on a bed: and Jesus seeing their faith said unto the sick of the palsy; Son, be of good cheer; thy sins be forgiven thee.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And, behold, certain of the scribes said within themselves, This man blasphemeth.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And Jesus knowing their thoughts said, Wherefore think ye evil in your hearts?</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> For whether is easier, to say, Thy sins be forgiven thee; or to say, Arise, and walk?</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> But that ye may know that the Son of man hath power on earth to forgive sins, (then saith he to the sick of the palsy,) Arise, take up thy bed, and go unto thine house.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And he arose, and departed to his house.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> But when the multitudes saw it, they marvelled, and glorified God, which had given such power unto men.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And as Jesus passed forth from thence, he saw a man, named Matthew, sitting at the receipt of custom: and he saith unto him, Follow me. And he arose, and followed him.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And it came to pass, as Jesus sat at meat in the house, behold, many publicans and sinners came and sat down with him and his disciples.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And when the Pharisees saw it, they said unto his disciples, Why eateth your Master with publicans and sinners?</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> But when Jesus heard that, he said unto them, They that be whole need not a physician, but they that are sick.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> But go ye and learn what that meaneth, I will have mercy, and not sacrifice: for I am not come to call the righteous, but sinners to repentance.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Then came to him the disciples of John, saying, Why do we and the Pharisees fast oft, but thy disciples fast not?</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And Jesus said unto them, Can the children of the bridechamber mourn, as long as the bridegroom is with them? but the days will come, when the bridegroom shall be taken from them, and then shall they fast.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> No man putteth a piece of new cloth unto an old garment, for that which is put in to fill it up taketh from the garment, and the rent is made worse.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Neither do men put new wine into old bottles: else the bottles break, and the wine runneth out, and the bottles perish: but they put new wine into new bottles, and both are preserved.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> While he spake these things unto them, behold, there came a certain ruler, and worshipped him, saying, My daughter is even now dead: but come and lay thy hand upon her, and she shall live.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And Jesus arose, and followed him, and so did his disciples.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And, behold, a woman, which was diseased with an issue of blood twelve years, came behind him, and touched the hem of his garment:</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> For she said within herself, If I may but touch his garment, I shall be whole.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> But Jesus turned him about, and when he saw her, he said, Daughter, be of good comfort; thy faith hath made thee whole. And the woman was made whole from that hour.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And when Jesus came into the ruler's house, and saw the minstrels and the people making a noise,</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> He said unto them, Give place: for the maid is not dead, but sleepeth. And they laughed him to scorn.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> But when the people were put forth, he went in, and took her by the hand, and the maid arose.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And the fame hereof went abroad into all that land.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And when Jesus departed thence, two blind men followed him, crying, and saying, Thou Son of David, have mercy on us.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And when he was come into the house, the blind men came to him: and Jesus saith unto them, Believe ye that I am able to do this? They said unto him, Yea, Lord.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Then touched he their eyes, saying, According to your faith be it unto you.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And their eyes were opened; and Jesus straitly charged them, saying, See that no man know it.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> But they, when they were departed, spread abroad his fame in all that country.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> As they went out, behold, they brought to him a dumb man possessed with a devil.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> And when the devil was cast out, the dumb spake: and the multitudes marvelled, saying, It was never so seen in Israel.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> But the Pharisees said, He casteth out devils through the prince of the devils.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> And Jesus went about all the cities and villages, teaching in their synagogues, and preaching the gospel of the kingdom, and healing every sickness and every disease among the people.</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> But when he saw the multitudes, he was moved with compassion on them, because they fainted, and were scattered abroad, as sheep having no shepherd.</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> Then saith he unto his disciples, The harvest truly is plenteous, but the labourers are few;</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> Pray ye therefore the Lord of the harvest, that he will send forth labourers into his harvest.</p>
