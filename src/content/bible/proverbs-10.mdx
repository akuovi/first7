## Proverbs 10

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> The proverbs of Solomon. A wise son maketh a glad father: but a foolish son is the heaviness of his mother.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Treasures of wickedness profit nothing: but righteousness delivereth from death.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> The LORD will not suffer the soul of the righteous to famish: but he casteth away the substance of the wicked.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> He becometh poor that dealeth with a slack hand: but the hand of the diligent maketh rich.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> He that gathereth in summer is a wise son: but he that sleepeth in harvest is a son that causeth shame.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Blessings are upon the head of the just: but violence covereth the mouth of the wicked.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> The memory of the just is blessed: but the name of the wicked shall rot.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> The wise in heart will receive commandments: but a prating fool shall fall.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> He that walketh uprightly walketh surely: but he that perverteth his ways shall be known.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> He that winketh with the eye causeth sorrow: but a prating fool shall fall.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> The mouth of a righteous man is a well of life: but violence covereth the mouth of the wicked.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Hatred stirreth up strifes: but love covereth all sins.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> In the lips of him that hath understanding wisdom is found: but a rod is for the back of him that is void of understanding.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Wise men lay up knowledge: but the mouth of the foolish is near destruction.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> The rich man's wealth is his strong city: the destruction of the poor is their poverty.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> The labour of the righteous tendeth to life: the fruit of the wicked to sin.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> He is in the way of life that keepeth instruction: but he that refuseth reproof erreth.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> He that hideth hatred with lying lips, and he that uttereth a slander, is a fool.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> In the multitude of words there wanteth not sin: but he that refraineth his lips is wise.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> The tongue of the just is as choice silver: the heart of the wicked is little worth.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> The lips of the righteous feed many: but fools die for want of wisdom.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> The blessing of the LORD, it maketh rich, and he addeth no sorrow with it.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> It is as sport to a fool to do mischief: but a man of understanding hath wisdom.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> The fear of the wicked, it shall come upon him: but the desire of the righteous shall be granted.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> As the whirlwind passeth, so is the wicked no more: but the righteous is an everlasting foundation.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> As vinegar to the teeth, and as smoke to the eyes, so is the sluggard to them that send him.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> The fear of the LORD prolongeth days: but the years of the wicked shall be shortened.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> The hope of the righteous shall be gladness: but the expectation of the wicked shall perish.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> The way of the LORD is strength to the upright: but destruction shall be to the workers of iniquity.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> The righteous shall never be removed: but the wicked shall not inhabit the earth.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> The mouth of the just bringeth forth wisdom: but the froward tongue shall be cut out.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> The lips of the righteous know what is acceptable: but the mouth of the wicked speaketh frowardness.</p>
