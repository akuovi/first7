## Job 4

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Then Eliphaz the Temanite answered and said,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> If we assay to commune with thee, wilt thou be grieved? but who can withhold himself from speaking?</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Behold, thou hast instructed many, and thou hast strengthened the weak hands.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Thy words have upholden him that was falling, and thou hast strengthened the feeble knees.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> But now it is come upon thee, and thou faintest; it toucheth thee, and thou art troubled.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Is not this thy fear, thy confidence, thy hope, and the uprightness of thy ways?</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Remember, I pray thee, who ever perished, being innocent? or where were the righteous cut off?</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Even as I have seen, they that plow iniquity, and sow wickedness, reap the same.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> By the blast of God they perish, and by the breath of his nostrils are they consumed.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> The roaring of the lion, and the voice of the fierce lion, and the teeth of the young lions, are broken.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> The old lion perisheth for lack of prey, and the stout lion's whelps are scattered abroad.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Now a thing was secretly brought to me, and mine ear received a little thereof.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> In thoughts from the visions of the night, when deep sleep falleth on men,</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Fear came upon me, and trembling, which made all my bones to shake.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Then a spirit passed before my face; the hair of my flesh stood up:</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> It stood still, but I could not discern the form thereof: an image was before mine eyes, there was silence, and I heard a voice, saying,</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Shall mortal man be more just than God? shall a man be more pure than his maker?</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Behold, he put no trust in his servants; and his angels he charged with folly:</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> How much less in them that dwell in houses of clay, whose foundation is in the dust, which are crushed before the moth?</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> They are destroyed from morning to evening: they perish for ever without any regarding it.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Doth not their excellency which is in them go away? they die, even without wisdom.</p>
