## Exodus 2

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And there went a man of the house of Levi, and took to wife a daughter of Levi.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And the woman conceived, and bare a son: and when she saw him that he was a goodly child, she hid him three months.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And when she could not longer hide him, she took for him an ark of bulrushes, and daubed it with slime and with pitch, and put the child therein; and she laid it in the flags by the river's brink.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And his sister stood afar off, to wit what would be done to him.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And the daughter of Pharaoh came down to wash herself at the river; and her maidens walked along by the river's side; and when she saw the ark among the flags, she sent her maid to fetch it.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And when she had opened it, she saw the child: and, behold, the babe wept. And she had compassion on him, and said, This is one of the Hebrews' children.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Then said his sister to Pharaoh's daughter, Shall I go and call to thee a nurse of the Hebrew women, that she may nurse the child for thee?</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And Pharaoh's daughter said to her, Go. And the maid went and called the child's mother.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And Pharaoh's daughter said unto her, Take this child away, and nurse it for me, and I will give thee thy wages. And the woman took the child, and nursed it.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And the child grew, and she brought him unto Pharaoh's daughter, and he became her son. And she called his name Moses: and she said, Because I drew him out of the water.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And it came to pass in those days, when Moses was grown, that he went out unto his brethren, and looked on their burdens: and he spied an Egyptian smiting an Hebrew, one of his brethren.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And he looked this way and that way, and when he saw that there was no man, he slew the Egyptian, and hid him in the sand.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And when he went out the second day, behold, two men of the Hebrews strove together: and he said to him that did the wrong, Wherefore smitest thou thy fellow?</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And he said, Who made thee a prince and a judge over us? intendest thou to kill me, as thou killedst the Egyptian? And Moses feared, and said, Surely this thing is known.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Now when Pharaoh heard this thing, he sought to slay Moses. But Moses fled from the face of Pharaoh, and dwelt in the land of Midian: and he sat down by a well.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Now the priest of Midian had seven daughters: and they came and drew water, and filled the troughs to water their father's flock.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And the shepherds came and drove them away: but Moses stood up and helped them, and watered their flock.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And when they came to Reuel their father, he said, How is it that ye are come so soon to day?</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And they said, An Egyptian delivered us out of the hand of the shepherds, and also drew water enough for us, and watered the flock.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And he said unto his daughters, And where is he? why is it that ye have left the man? call him, that he may eat bread.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And Moses was content to dwell with the man: and he gave Moses Zipporah his daughter.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And she bare him a son, and he called his name Gershom: for he said, I have been a stranger in a strange land.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And it came to pass in process of time, that the king of Egypt died: and the children of Israel sighed by reason of the bondage, and they cried, and their cry came up unto God by reason of the bondage.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And God heard their groaning, and God remembered his covenant with Abraham, with Isaac, and with Jacob.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And God looked upon the children of Israel, and God had respect unto them.</p>
