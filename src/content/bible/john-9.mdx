## John 9

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And as Jesus passed by, he saw a man which was blind from his birth.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And his disciples asked him, saying, Master, who did sin, this man, or his parents, that he was born blind?</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Jesus answered, Neither hath this man sinned, nor his parents: but that the works of God should be made manifest in him.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> I must work the works of him that sent me, while it is day: the night cometh, when no man can work.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> As long as I am in the world, I am the light of the world.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> When he had thus spoken, he spat on the ground, and made clay of the spittle, and he anointed the eyes of the blind man with the clay,</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And said unto him, Go, wash in the pool of Siloam, (which is by interpretation, Sent.) He went his way therefore, and washed, and came seeing.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> The neighbours therefore, and they which before had seen him that he was blind, said, Is not this he that sat and begged?</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Some said, This is he: others said, He is like him: but he said, I am he.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Therefore said they unto him, How were thine eyes opened?</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> He answered and said, A man that is called Jesus made clay, and anointed mine eyes, and said unto me, Go to the pool of Siloam, and wash: and I went and washed, and I received sight.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Then said they unto him, Where is he? He said, I know not.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> They brought to the Pharisees him that aforetime was blind.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And it was the sabbath day when Jesus made the clay, and opened his eyes.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Then again the Pharisees also asked him how he had received his sight. He said unto them, He put clay upon mine eyes, and I washed, and do see.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Therefore said some of the Pharisees, This man is not of God, because he keepeth not the sabbath day. Others said, How can a man that is a sinner do such miracles? And there was a division among them.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> They say unto the blind man again, What sayest thou of him, that he hath opened thine eyes? He said, He is a prophet.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> But the Jews did not believe concerning him, that he had been blind, and received his sight, until they called the parents of him that had received his sight.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And they asked them, saying, Is this your son, who ye say was born blind? how then doth he now see?</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> His parents answered them and said, We know that this is our son, and that he was born blind:</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> But by what means he now seeth, we know not; or who hath opened his eyes, we know not: he is of age; ask him: he shall speak for himself.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> These words spake his parents, because they feared the Jews: for the Jews had agreed already, that if any man did confess that he was Christ, he should be put out of the synagogue.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Therefore said his parents, He is of age; ask him.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Then again called they the man that was blind, and said unto him, Give God the praise: we know that this man is a sinner.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> He answered and said, Whether he be a sinner or no, I know not: one thing I know, that, whereas I was blind, now I see.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Then said they to him again, What did he to thee? how opened he thine eyes?</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> He answered them, I have told you already, and ye did not hear: wherefore would ye hear it again? will ye also be his disciples?</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Then they reviled him, and said, Thou art his disciple; but we are Moses' disciples.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> We know that God spake unto Moses: as for this fellow, we know not from whence he is.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> The man answered and said unto them, Why herein is a marvellous thing, that ye know not from whence he is, and yet he hath opened mine eyes.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Now we know that God heareth not sinners: but if any man be a worshipper of God, and doeth his will, him he heareth.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> Since the world began was it not heard that any man opened the eyes of one that was born blind.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> If this man were not of God, he could do nothing.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> They answered and said unto him, Thou wast altogether born in sins, and dost thou teach us? And they cast him out.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> Jesus heard that they had cast him out; and when he had found him, he said unto him, Dost thou believe on the Son of God?</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> He answered and said, Who is he, Lord, that I might believe on him?</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> And Jesus said unto him, Thou hast both seen him, and it is he that talketh with thee.</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> And he said, Lord, I believe. And he worshipped him.</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> And Jesus said, For judgment I am come into this world, that they which see not might see; and that they which see might be made blind.</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> And some of the Pharisees which were with him heard these words, and said unto him, Are we blind also?</p>

<p id="v41" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">41</span> Jesus said unto them, If ye were blind, ye should have no sin: but now ye say, We see; therefore your sin remaineth.</p>
