## John 14

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Let not your heart be troubled: ye believe in God, believe also in me.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> In my Father's house are many mansions: if it were not so, I would have told you. I go to prepare a place for you.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And if I go and prepare a place for you, I will come again, and receive you unto myself; that where I am, there ye may be also.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And whither I go ye know, and the way ye know.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Thomas saith unto him, Lord, we know not whither thou goest; and how can we know the way?</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Jesus saith unto him, I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by me.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> If ye had known me, ye should have known my Father also: and from henceforth ye know him, and have seen him.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Philip saith unto him, Lord, shew us the Father, and it sufficeth us.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Jesus saith unto him, Have I been so long time with you, and yet hast thou not known me, Philip? he that hath seen me hath seen the Father; and how sayest thou then, Shew us the Father?</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Believest thou not that I am in the Father, and the Father in me? the words that I speak unto you I speak not of myself: but the Father that dwelleth in me, he doeth the works.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Believe me that I am in the Father, and the Father in me: or else believe me for the very works' sake.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Verily, verily, I say unto you, He that believeth on me, the works that I do shall he do also; and greater works than these shall he do; because I go unto my Father.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And whatsoever ye shall ask in my name, that will I do, that the Father may be glorified in the Son.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> If ye shall ask any thing in my name, I will do it.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> If ye love me, keep my commandments.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And I will pray the Father, and he shall give you another Comforter, that he may abide with you for ever;</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Even the Spirit of truth; whom the world cannot receive, because it seeth him not, neither knoweth him: but ye know him; for he dwelleth with you, and shall be in you.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> I will not leave you comfortless: I will come to you.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Yet a little while, and the world seeth me no more; but ye see me: because I live, ye shall live also.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> At that day ye shall know that I am in my Father, and ye in me, and I in you.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> He that hath my commandments, and keepeth them, he it is that loveth me: and he that loveth me shall be loved of my Father, and I will love him, and will manifest myself to him.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Judas saith unto him, not Iscariot, Lord, how is it that thou wilt manifest thyself unto us, and not unto the world?</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Jesus answered and said unto him, If a man love me, he will keep my words: and my Father will love him, and we will come unto him, and make our abode with him.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> He that loveth me not keepeth not my sayings: and the word which ye hear is not mine, but the Father's which sent me.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> These things have I spoken unto you, being yet present with you.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> But the Comforter, which is the Holy Ghost, whom the Father will send in my name, he shall teach you all things, and bring all things to your remembrance, whatsoever I have said unto you.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Peace I leave with you, my peace I give unto you: not as the world giveth, give I unto you. Let not your heart be troubled, neither let it be afraid.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Ye have heard how I said unto you, I go away, and come again unto you. If ye loved me, ye would rejoice, because I said, I go unto the Father: for my Father is greater than I.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And now I have told you before it come to pass, that, when it is come to pass, ye might believe.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Hereafter I will not talk much with you: for the prince of this world cometh, and hath nothing in me.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> But that the world may know that I love the Father; and as the Father gave me commandment, even so I do. Arise, let us go hence.</p>
