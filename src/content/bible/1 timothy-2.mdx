## 1 Timothy 2

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> I exhort therefore, that, first of all, supplications, prayers, intercessions, and giving of thanks, be made for all men;</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> For kings, and for all that are in authority; that we may lead a quiet and peaceable life in all godliness and honesty.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For this is good and acceptable in the sight of God our Saviour;</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Who will have all men to be saved, and to come unto the knowledge of the truth.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> For there is one God, and one mediator between God and men, the man Christ Jesus;</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Who gave himself a ransom for all, to be testified in due time.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Whereunto I am ordained a preacher, and an apostle, (I speak the truth in Christ, and lie not;) a teacher of the Gentiles in faith and verity.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> I will therefore that men pray every where, lifting up holy hands, without wrath and doubting.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> In like manner also, that women adorn themselves in modest apparel, with shamefacedness and sobriety; not with broided hair, or gold, or pearls, or costly array;</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> But (which becometh women professing godliness) with good works.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Let the woman learn in silence with all subjection.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> But I suffer not a woman to teach, nor to usurp authority over the man, but to be in silence.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> For Adam was first formed, then Eve.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And Adam was not deceived, but the woman being deceived was in the transgression.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Notwithstanding she shall be saved in childbearing, if they continue in faith and charity and holiness with sobriety.</p>
