## Job 38

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Then the LORD answered Job out of the whirlwind, and said,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Who is this that darkeneth counsel by words without knowledge?</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Gird up now thy loins like a man; for I will demand of thee, and answer thou me.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Where wast thou when I laid the foundations of the earth? declare, if thou hast understanding.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Who hath laid the measures thereof, if thou knowest? or who hath stretched the line upon it?</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Whereupon are the foundations thereof fastened? or who laid the corner stone thereof;</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> When the morning stars sang together, and all the sons of God shouted for joy?</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Or who shut up the sea with doors, when it brake forth, as if it had issued out of the womb?</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> When I made the cloud the garment thereof, and thick darkness a swaddlingband for it,</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And brake up for it my decreed place, and set bars and doors,</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And said, Hitherto shalt thou come, but no further: and here shall thy proud waves be stayed?</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Hast thou commanded the morning since thy days; and caused the dayspring to know his place;</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> That it might take hold of the ends of the earth, that the wicked might be shaken out of it?</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> It is turned as clay to the seal; and they stand as a garment.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And from the wicked their light is withholden, and the high arm shall be broken.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Hast thou entered into the springs of the sea? or hast thou walked in the search of the depth?</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Have the gates of death been opened unto thee? or hast thou seen the doors of the shadow of death?</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Hast thou perceived the breadth of the earth? declare if thou knowest it all.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Where is the way where light dwelleth? and as for darkness, where is the place thereof,</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> That thou shouldest take it to the bound thereof, and that thou shouldest know the paths to the house thereof?</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Knowest thou it, because thou wast then born? or because the number of thy days is great?</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Hast thou entered into the treasures of the snow? or hast thou seen the treasures of the hail,</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Which I have reserved against the time of trouble, against the day of battle and war?</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> By what way is the light parted, which scattereth the east wind upon the earth?</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Who hath divided a watercourse for the overflowing of waters, or a way for the lightning of thunder;</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> To cause it to rain on the earth, where no man is; on the wilderness, wherein there is no man;</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> To satisfy the desolate and waste ground; and to cause the bud of the tender herb to spring forth?</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Hath the rain a father? or who hath begotten the drops of dew?</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Out of whose womb came the ice? and the hoary frost of heaven, who hath gendered it?</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> The waters are hid as with a stone, and the face of the deep is frozen.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Canst thou bind the sweet influences of Pleiades, or loose the bands of Orion?</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> Canst thou bring forth Mazzaroth in his season? or canst thou guide Arcturus with his sons?</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> Knowest thou the ordinances of heaven? canst thou set the dominion thereof in the earth?</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> Canst thou lift up thy voice to the clouds, that abundance of waters may cover thee?</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> Canst thou send lightnings, that they may go, and say unto thee, Here we are?</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> Who hath put wisdom in the inward parts? or who hath given understanding to the heart?</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> Who can number the clouds in wisdom? or who can stay the bottles of heaven,</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> When the dust groweth into hardness, and the clods cleave fast together?</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> Wilt thou hunt the prey for the lion? or fill the appetite of the young lions,</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> When they couch in their dens, and abide in the covert to lie in wait?</p>

<p id="v41" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">41</span> Who provideth for the raven his food? when his young ones cry unto God, they wander for lack of meat.</p>
