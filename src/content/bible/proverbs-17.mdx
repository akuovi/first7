## Proverbs 17

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Better is a dry morsel, and quietness therewith, than an house full of sacrifices with strife.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> A wise servant shall have rule over a son that causeth shame, and shall have part of the inheritance among the brethren.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> The fining pot is for silver, and the furnace for gold: but the LORD trieth the hearts.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> A wicked doer giveth heed to false lips; and a liar giveth ear to a naughty tongue.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Whoso mocketh the poor reproacheth his Maker: and he that is glad at calamities shall not be unpunished.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Children's children are the crown of old men; and the glory of children are their fathers.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Excellent speech becometh not a fool: much less do lying lips a prince.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> A gift is as a precious stone in the eyes of him that hath it: whithersoever it turneth, it prospereth.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> He that covereth a transgression seeketh love; but he that repeateth a matter separateth very friends.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> A reproof entereth more into a wise man than an hundred stripes into a fool.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> An evil man seeketh only rebellion: therefore a cruel messenger shall be sent against him.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Let a bear robbed of her whelps meet a man, rather than a fool in his folly.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Whoso rewardeth evil for good, evil shall not depart from his house.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> The beginning of strife is as when one letteth out water: therefore leave off contention, before it be meddled with.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> He that justifieth the wicked, and he that condemneth the just, even they both are abomination to the LORD.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Wherefore is there a price in the hand of a fool to get wisdom, seeing he hath no heart to it?</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> A friend loveth at all times, and a brother is born for adversity.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> A man void of understanding striketh hands, and becometh surety in the presence of his friend.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> He loveth transgression that loveth strife: and he that exalteth his gate seeketh destruction.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> He that hath a froward heart findeth no good: and he that hath a perverse tongue falleth into mischief.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> He that begetteth a fool doeth it to his sorrow: and the father of a fool hath no joy.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> A merry heart doeth good like a medicine: but a broken spirit drieth the bones.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> A wicked man taketh a gift out of the bosom to pervert the ways of judgment.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Wisdom is before him that hath understanding; but the eyes of a fool are in the ends of the earth.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> A foolish son is a grief to his father, and bitterness to her that bare him.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Also to punish the just is not good, nor to strike princes for equity.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> He that hath knowledge spareth his words: and a man of understanding is of an excellent spirit.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Even a fool, when he holdeth his peace, is counted wise: and he that shutteth his lips is esteemed a man of understanding.</p>
