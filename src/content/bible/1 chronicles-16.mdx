## 1 Chronicles 16

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> So they brought the ark of God, and set it in the midst of the tent that David had pitched for it: and they offered burnt sacrifices and peace offerings before God.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And when David had made an end of offering the burnt offerings and the peace offerings, he blessed the people in the name of the LORD.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And he dealt to every one of Israel, both man and woman, to every one a loaf of bread, and a good piece of flesh, and a flagon of wine.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And he appointed certain of the Levites to minister before the ark of the LORD, and to record, and to thank and praise the LORD God of Israel:</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Asaph the chief, and next to him Zechariah, Jeiel, and Shemiramoth, and Jehiel, and Mattithiah, and Eliab, and Benaiah, and Obed-edom: and Jeiel with psalteries and with harps; but Asaph made a sound with cymbals;</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Benaiah also and Jahaziel the priests with trumpets continually before the ark of the covenant of God.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Then on that day David delivered first this psalm to thank the LORD into the hand of Asaph and his brethren.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Give thanks unto the LORD, call upon his name, make known his deeds among the people.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Sing unto him, sing psalms unto him, talk ye of all his wondrous works.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Glory ye in his holy name: let the heart of them rejoice that seek the LORD.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Seek the LORD and his strength, seek his face continually.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Remember his marvellous works that he hath done, his wonders, and the judgments of his mouth;</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> O ye seed of Israel his servant, ye children of Jacob, his chosen ones.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> He is the LORD our God; his judgments are in all the earth.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Be ye mindful always of his covenant; the word which he commanded to a thousand generations;</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Even of the covenant which he made with Abraham, and of his oath unto Isaac;</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And hath confirmed the same to Jacob for a law, and to Israel for an everlasting covenant,</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Saying, Unto thee will I give the land of Canaan, the lot of your inheritance;</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> When ye were but few, even a few, and strangers in it.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And when they went from nation to nation, and from one kingdom to another people;</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> He suffered no man to do them wrong: yea, he reproved kings for their sakes,</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Saying, Touch not mine anointed, and do my prophets no harm.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Sing unto the LORD, all the earth; shew forth from day to day his salvation.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Declare his glory among the heathen; his marvellous works among all nations.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> For great is the LORD, and greatly to be praised: he also is to be feared above all gods.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> For all the gods of the people are idols: but the LORD made the heavens.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Glory and honour are in his presence; strength and gladness are in his place.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Give unto the LORD, ye kindreds of the people, give unto the LORD glory and strength.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Give unto the LORD the glory due unto his name: bring an offering, and come before him: worship the LORD in the beauty of holiness.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Fear before him, all the earth: the world also shall be stable, that it be not moved.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Let the heavens be glad, and let the earth rejoice: and let men say among the nations, The LORD reigneth.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> Let the sea roar, and the fulness thereof: let the fields rejoice, and all that is therein.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> Then shall the trees of the wood sing out at the presence of the LORD, because he cometh to judge the earth.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> O give thanks unto the LORD; for he is good; for his mercy endureth for ever.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> And say ye, Save us, O God of our salvation, and gather us together, and deliver us from the heathen, that we may give thanks to thy holy name, and glory in thy praise.</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> Blessed be the LORD God of Israel for ever and ever. And all the people said, Amen, and praised the LORD.</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> So he left there before the ark of the covenant of the LORD Asaph and his brethren, to minister before the ark continually, as every day's work required:</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> And Obed-edom with their brethren, threescore and eight; Obed-edom also the son of Jeduthun and Hosah to be porters:</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> And Zadok the priest, and his brethren the priests, before the tabernacle of the LORD in the high place that was at Gibeon,</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> To offer burnt offerings unto the LORD upon the altar of the burnt offering continually morning and evening, and to do according to all that is written in the law of the LORD, which he commanded Israel;</p>

<p id="v41" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">41</span> And with them Heman and Jeduthun, and the rest that were chosen, who were expressed by name, to give thanks to the LORD, because his mercy endureth for ever;</p>

<p id="v42" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">42</span> And with them Heman and Jeduthun with trumpets and cymbals for those that should make a sound, and with musical instruments of God. And the sons of Jeduthun were porters.</p>

<p id="v43" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">43</span> And all the people departed every man to his house: and David returned to bless his house.</p>
