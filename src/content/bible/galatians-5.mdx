## Galatians 5

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Stand fast therefore in the liberty wherewith Christ hath made us free, and be not entangled again with the yoke of bondage.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Behold, I Paul say unto you, that if ye be circumcised, Christ shall profit you nothing.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For I testify again to every man that is circumcised, that he is a debtor to do the whole law.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Christ is become of no effect unto you, whosoever of you are justified by the law; ye are fallen from grace.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> For we through the Spirit wait for the hope of righteousness by faith.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> For in Jesus Christ neither circumcision availeth any thing, nor uncircumcision; but faith which worketh by love.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Ye did run well; who did hinder you that ye should not obey the truth?</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> This persuasion cometh not of him that calleth you.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> A little leaven leaveneth the whole lump.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> I have confidence in you through the Lord, that ye will be none otherwise minded: but he that troubleth you shall bear his judgment, whosoever he be.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And I, brethren, if I yet preach circumcision, why do I yet suffer persecution? then is the offence of the cross ceased.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> I would they were even cut off which trouble you.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> For, brethren, ye have been called unto liberty; only use not liberty for an occasion to the flesh, but by love serve one another.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> For all the law is fulfilled in one word, even in this; Thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> But if ye bite and devour one another, take heed that ye be not consumed one of another.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> This I say then, Walk in the Spirit, and ye shall not fulfil the lust of the flesh.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> For the flesh lusteth against the Spirit, and the Spirit against the flesh: and these are contrary the one to the other: so that ye cannot do the things that ye would.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> But if ye be led of the Spirit, ye are not under the law.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Now the works of the flesh are manifest, which are these; Adultery, fornication, uncleanness, lasciviousness,</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Idolatry, witchcraft, hatred, variance, emulations, wrath, strife, seditions, heresies,</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Envyings, murders, drunkenness, revellings, and such like: of the which I tell you before, as I have also told you in time past, that they which do such things shall not inherit the kingdom of God.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, longsuffering, gentleness, goodness, faith,</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Meekness, temperance: against such there is no law.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And they that are Christ's have crucified the flesh with the affections and lusts.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> If we live in the Spirit, let us also walk in the Spirit.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Let us not be desirous of vain glory, provoking one another, envying one another.</p>
