## Ezekiel 15

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And the word of the LORD came unto me, saying,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Son of man, What is the vine tree more than any tree, or than a branch which is among the trees of the forest?</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Shall wood be taken thereof to do any work? or will men take a pin of it to hang any vessel thereon?</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Behold, it is cast into the fire for fuel; the fire devoureth both the ends of it, and the midst of it is burned. Is it meet for any work?</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Behold, when it was whole, it was meet for no work: how much less shall it be meet yet for any work, when the fire hath devoured it, and it is burned?</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Therefore thus saith the Lord GOD; As the vine tree among the trees of the forest, which I have given to the fire for fuel, so will I give the inhabitants of Jerusalem.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And I will set my face against them; they shall go out from one fire, and another fire shall devour them; and ye shall know that I am the LORD, when I set my face against them.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And I will make the land desolate, because they have committed a trespass, saith the Lord GOD.</p>
