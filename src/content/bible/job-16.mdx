## Job 16

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Then Job answered and said,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> I have heard many such things: miserable comforters are ye all.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Shall vain words have an end? or what emboldeneth thee that thou answerest?</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> I also could speak as ye do: if your soul were in my soul's stead, I could heap up words against you, and shake mine head at you.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> But I would strengthen you with my mouth, and the moving of my lips should asswage your grief.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Though I speak, my grief is not asswaged: and though I forbear, what am I eased?</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> But now he hath made me weary: thou hast made desolate all my company.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And thou hast filled me with wrinkles, which is a witness against me: and my leanness rising up in me beareth witness to my face.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> He teareth me in his wrath, who hateth me: he gnasheth upon me with his teeth; mine enemy sharpeneth his eyes upon me.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> They have gaped upon me with their mouth; they have smitten me upon the cheek reproachfully; they have gathered themselves together against me.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> God hath delivered me to the ungodly, and turned me over into the hands of the wicked.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> I was at ease, but he hath broken me asunder: he hath also taken me by my neck, and shaken me to pieces, and set me up for his mark.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> His archers compass me round about, he cleaveth my reins asunder, and doth not spare; he poureth out my gall upon the ground.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> He breaketh me with breach upon breach, he runneth upon me like a giant.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> I have sewed sackcloth upon my skin, and defiled my horn in the dust.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> My face is foul with weeping, and on my eyelids is the shadow of death;</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Not for any injustice in mine hands: also my prayer is pure.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> O earth, cover not thou my blood, and let my cry have no place.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Also now, behold, my witness is in heaven, and my record is on high.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> My friends scorn me: but mine eye poureth out tears unto God.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> O that one might plead for a man with God, as a man pleadeth for his neighbour!</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> When a few years are come, then I shall go the way whence I shall not return.</p>
