## Job 24

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Why, seeing times are not hidden from the Almighty, do they that know him not see his days?</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Some remove the landmarks; they violently take away flocks, and feed thereof.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> They drive away the ass of the fatherless, they take the widow's ox for a pledge.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> They turn the needy out of the way: the poor of the earth hide themselves together.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Behold, as wild asses in the desert, go they forth to their work; rising betimes for a prey: the wilderness yieldeth food for them and for their children.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> They reap every one his corn in the field: and they gather the vintage of the wicked.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> They cause the naked to lodge without clothing, that they have no covering in the cold.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> They are wet with the showers of the mountains, and embrace the rock for want of a shelter.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> They pluck the fatherless from the breast, and take a pledge of the poor.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> They cause him to go naked without clothing, and they take away the sheaf from the hungry;</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Which make oil within their walls, and tread their winepresses, and suffer thirst.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Men groan from out of the city, and the soul of the wounded crieth out: yet God layeth not folly to them.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> They are of those that rebel against the light; they know not the ways thereof, nor abide in the paths thereof.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> The murderer rising with the light killeth the poor and needy, and in the night is as a thief.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> The eye also of the adulterer waiteth for the twilight, saying, No eye shall see me: and disguiseth his face.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> In the dark they dig through houses, which they had marked for themselves in the daytime: they know not the light.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> For the morning is to them even as the shadow of death: if one know them, they are in the terrors of the shadow of death.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> He is swift as the waters; their portion is cursed in the earth: he beholdeth not the way of the vineyards.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Drought and heat consume the snow waters: so doth the grave those which have sinned.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> The womb shall forget him; the worm shall feed sweetly on him; he shall be no more remembered; and wickedness shall be broken as a tree.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> He evil entreateth the barren that beareth not: and doeth not good to the widow.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> He draweth also the mighty with his power: he riseth up, and no man is sure of life.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Though it be given him to be in safety, whereon he resteth; yet his eyes are upon their ways.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> They are exalted for a little while, but are gone and brought low; they are taken out of the way as all other, and cut off as the tops of the ears of corn.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And if it be not so now, who will make me a liar, and make my speech nothing worth?</p>
