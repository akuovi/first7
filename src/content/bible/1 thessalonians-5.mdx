## 1 Thessalonians 5

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> But of the times and the seasons, brethren, ye have no need that I write unto you.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> For yourselves know perfectly that the day of the Lord so cometh as a thief in the night.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For when they shall say, Peace and safety; then sudden destruction cometh upon them, as travail upon a woman with child; and they shall not escape.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> But ye, brethren, are not in darkness, that that day should overtake you as a thief.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Ye are all the children of light, and the children of the day: we are not of the night, nor of darkness.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Therefore let us not sleep, as do others; but let us watch and be sober.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> For they that sleep sleep in the night; and they that be drunken are drunken in the night.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> But let us, who are of the day, be sober, putting on the breastplate of faith and love; and for an helmet, the hope of salvation.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> For God hath not appointed us to wrath, but to obtain salvation by our Lord Jesus Christ,</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Who died for us, that, whether we wake or sleep, we should live together with him.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Wherefore comfort yourselves together, and edify one another, even as also ye do.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And we beseech you, brethren, to know them which labour among you, and are over you in the Lord, and admonish you;</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And to esteem them very highly in love for their work's sake. And be at peace among yourselves.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Now we exhort you, brethren, warn them that are unruly, comfort the feebleminded, support the weak, be patient toward all men.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> See that none render evil for evil unto any man; but ever follow that which is good, both among yourselves, and to all men.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Rejoice evermore.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Pray without ceasing.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> In every thing give thanks: for this is the will of God in Christ Jesus concerning you.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Quench not the Spirit.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Despise not prophesyings.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Prove all things; hold fast that which is good.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Abstain from all appearance of evil.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And the very God of peace sanctify you wholly; and I pray God your whole spirit and soul and body be preserved blameless unto the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Faithful is he that calleth you, who also will do it.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Brethren, pray for us.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Greet all the brethren with an holy kiss.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> I charge you by the Lord that this epistle be read unto all the holy brethren.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you. Amen.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> The first epistle unto the Thessalonians was written from Athens.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> THESSALONIANS</p>
