## Jeremiah 5

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Run ye to and fro through the streets of Jerusalem, and see now, and know, and seek in the broad places thereof, if ye can find a man, if there be any that executeth judgment, that seeketh the truth; and I will pardon it.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And though they say, The LORD liveth; surely they swear falsely.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> O LORD, are not thine eyes upon the truth? thou hast stricken them, but they have not grieved; thou hast consumed them, but they have refused to receive correction: they have made their faces harder than a rock; they have refused to return.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Therefore I said, Surely these are poor; they are foolish: for they know not the way of the LORD, nor the judgment of their God.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> I will get me unto the great men, and will speak unto them; for they have known the way of the LORD, and the judgment of their God: but these have altogether broken the yoke, and burst the bonds.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Wherefore a lion out of the forest shall slay them, and a wolf of the evenings shall spoil them, a leopard shall watch over their cities: every one that goeth out thence shall be torn in pieces: because their transgressions are many, and their backslidings are increased.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Â¶ How shall I pardon thee for this? thy children have forsaken me, and sworn by them that are no gods: when I had fed them to the full, they then committed adultery, and assembled themselves by troops in the harlots' houses.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> They were as fed horses in the morning: every one neighed after his neighbour's wife.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Shall I not visit for these things? saith the LORD: and shall not my soul be avenged on such a nation as this?</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Â¶ Go ye up upon her walls, and destroy; but make not a full end: take away her battlements; for they are not the LORD'S.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> For the house of Israel and the house of Judah have dealt very treacherously against me, saith the LORD.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> They have belied the LORD, and said, It is not he; neither shall evil come upon us; neither shall we see sword nor famine:</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And the prophets shall become wind, and the word is not in them: thus shall it be done unto them.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Wherefore thus saith the LORD God of hosts, Because ye speak this word, behold, I will make my words in thy mouth fire, and this people wood, and it shall devour them.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Lo, I will bring a nation upon you from far, O house of Israel, saith the LORD: it is a mighty nation, it is an ancient nation, a nation whose language thou knowest not, neither understandest what they say.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Their quiver is as an open sepulchre, they are all mighty men.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And they shall eat up thine harvest, and thy bread, which thy sons and thy daughters should eat: they shall eat up thy flocks and thine herds: they shall eat up thy vines and thy fig trees: they shall impoverish thy fenced cities, wherein thou trustedst, with the sword.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Nevertheless in those days, saith the LORD, I will not make a full end with you.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Â¶ And it shall come to pass, when ye shall say, Wherefore doeth the LORD our God all these things unto us? then shalt thou answer them, Like as ye have forsaken me, and served strange gods in your land, so shall ye serve strangers in a land that is not your's.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Declare this in the house of Jacob, and publish it in Judah, saying,</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Hear now this, O foolish people, and without understanding; which have eyes, and see not; which have ears, and hear not:</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Fear ye not me? saith the LORD: will ye not tremble at my presence, which have placed the sand for the bound of the sea by a perpetual decree, that it cannot pass it: and though the waves thereof toss themselves, yet can they not prevail; though they roar, yet can they not pass over it?</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> But this people hath a revolting and a rebellious heart; they are revolted and gone.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Neither say they in their heart, Let us now fear the LORD our God, that giveth rain, both the former and the latter, in his season: he reserveth unto us the appointed weeks of the harvest.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Â¶ Your iniquities have turned away these things, and your sins have withholden good things from you.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> For among my people are found wicked men: they lay wait, as he that setteth snares; they set a trap, they catch men.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> As a cage is full of birds, so are their houses full of deceit: therefore they are become great, and waxen rich.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> They are waxen fat, they shine: yea, they overpass the deeds of the wicked: they judge not the cause, the cause of the fatherless, yet they prosper; and the right of the needy do they not judge.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Shall I not visit for these things? saith the LORD: shall not my soul be avenged on such a nation as this?</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Â¶ A wonderful and horrible thing is committed in the land;</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> The prophets prophesy falsely, and the priests bear rule by their means; and my people love to have it so: and what will ye do in the end thereof?</p>
