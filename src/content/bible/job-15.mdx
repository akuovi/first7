## Job 15

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Then answered Eliphaz the Temanite, and said,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Should a wise man utter vain knowledge, and fill his belly with the east wind?</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Should he reason with unprofitable talk? or with speeches wherewith he can do no good?</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Yea, thou castest off fear, and restrainest prayer before God.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> For thy mouth uttereth thine iniquity, and thou choosest the tongue of the crafty.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Thine own mouth condemneth thee, and not I: yea, thine own lips testify against thee.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Art thou the first man that was born? or wast thou made before the hills?</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Hast thou heard the secret of God? and dost thou restrain wisdom to thyself?</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> What knowest thou, that we know not? what understandest thou, which is not in us?</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> With us are both the grayheaded and very aged men, much elder than thy father.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Are the consolations of God small with thee? is there any secret thing with thee?</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Why doth thine heart carry thee away? and what do thy eyes wink at,</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> That thou turnest thy spirit against God, and lettest such words go out of thy mouth?</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> What is man, that he should be clean? and he which is born of a woman, that he should be righteous?</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Behold, he putteth no trust in his saints; yea, the heavens are not clean in his sight.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> How much more abominable and filthy is man, which drinketh iniquity like water?</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> I will shew thee, hear me; and that which I have seen I will declare;</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Which wise men have told from their fathers, and have not hid it:</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Unto whom alone the earth was given, and no stranger passed among them.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> The wicked man travaileth with pain all his days, and the number of years is hidden to the oppressor.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> A dreadful sound is in his ears: in prosperity the destroyer shall come upon him.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> He believeth not that he shall return out of darkness, and he is waited for of the sword.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> He wandereth abroad for bread, saying, Where is it? he knoweth that the day of darkness is ready at his hand.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Trouble and anguish shall make him afraid; they shall prevail against him, as a king ready to the battle.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> For he stretcheth out his hand against God, and strengtheneth himself against the Almighty.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> He runneth upon him, even on his neck, upon the thick bosses of his bucklers:</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Because he covereth his face with his fatness, and maketh collops of fat on his flanks.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And he dwelleth in desolate cities, and in houses which no man inhabiteth, which are ready to become heaps.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> He shall not be rich, neither shall his substance continue, neither shall he prolong the perfection thereof upon the earth.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> He shall not depart out of darkness; the flame shall dry up his branches, and by the breath of his mouth shall he go away.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Let not him that is deceived trust in vanity: for vanity shall be his recompence.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> It shall be accomplished before his time, and his branch shall not be green.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> He shall shake off his unripe grape as the vine, and shall cast off his flower as the olive.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> For the congregation of hypocrites shall be desolate, and fire shall consume the tabernacles of bribery.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> They conceive mischief, and bring forth vanity, and their belly prepareth deceit.</p>
