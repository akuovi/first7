## Isaiah 6

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> In the year that king Uzziah died I saw also the Lord sitting upon a throne, high and lifted up, and his train filled the temple.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Above it stood the seraphims: each one had six wings; with twain he covered his face, and with twain he covered his feet, and with twain he did fly.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And one cried unto another, and said, Holy, holy, holy, is the LORD of hosts: the whole earth is full of his glory.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And the posts of the door moved at the voice of him that cried, and the house was filled with smoke.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Then said I, Woe is me! for I am undone; because I am a man of unclean lips, and I dwell in the midst of a people of unclean lips: for mine eyes have seen the King, the LORD of hosts.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Then flew one of the seraphims unto me, having a live coal in his hand, which he had taken with the tongs from off the altar:</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And he laid it upon my mouth, and said, Lo, this hath touched thy lips; and thine iniquity is taken away, and thy sin purged.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Also I heard the voice of the Lord, saying, Whom shall I send, and who will go for us? Then said I, Here am I; send me.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And he said, Go, and tell this people, Hear ye indeed, but understand not; and see ye indeed, but perceive not.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Make the heart of this people fat, and make their ears heavy, and shut their eyes; lest they see with their eyes, and hear with their ears, and understand with their heart, and convert, and be healed.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Then said I, Lord, how long? And he answered, Until the cities be wasted without inhabitant, and the houses without man, and the land be utterly desolate,</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And the LORD have removed men far away, and there be a great forsaking in the midst of the land.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> But yet in it shall be a tenth, and it shall return, and shall be eaten: as a teil tree, and as an oak, whose substance is in them, when they cast their leaves: so the holy seed shall be the substance thereof.</p>
