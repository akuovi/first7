## Genesis 2

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Thus the heavens and the earth were finished, and all the host of them.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And on the seventh day God ended his work which he had made; and he rested on the seventh day from all his work which he had made.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And God blessed the seventh day, and sanctified it: because that in it he had rested from all his work which God created and made.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> These are the generations of the heavens and of the earth when they were created, in the day that the LORD God made the earth and the heavens,</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And every plant of the field before it was in the earth, and every herb of the field before it grew: for the LORD God had not caused it to rain upon the earth, and there was not a man to till the ground.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> But there went up a mist from the earth, and watered the whole face of the ground.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And the LORD God formed man of the dust of the ground, and breathed into his nostrils the breath of life; and man became a living soul.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And the LORD God planted a garden eastward in Eden; and there he put the man whom he had formed.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And out of the ground made the LORD God to grow every tree that is pleasant to the sight, and good for food; the tree of life also in the midst of the garden, and the tree of knowledge of good and evil.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And a river went out of Eden to water the garden; and from thence it was parted, and became into four heads.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> The name of the first is Pison: that is it which compasseth the whole land of Havilah, where there is gold;</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And the gold of that land is good: there is bdellium and the onyx stone.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And the name of the second river is Gihon: the same is it that compasseth the whole land of Ethiopia.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And the name of the third river is Hiddekel: that is it which goeth toward the east of Assyria. And the fourth river is Euphrates.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And the LORD God took the man, and put him into the garden of Eden to dress it and to keep it.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And the LORD God commanded the man, saying, Of every tree of the garden thou mayest freely eat:</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> But of the tree of the knowledge of good and evil, thou shalt not eat of it: for in the day that thou eatest thereof thou shalt surely die.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And the LORD God said, It is not good that the man should be alone; I will make him an help meet for him.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And out of the ground the LORD God formed every beast of the field, and every fowl of the air; and brought them unto Adam to see what he would call them: and whatsoever Adam called every living creature, that was the name thereof.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And Adam gave names to all cattle, and to the fowl of the air, and to every beast of the field; but for Adam there was not found an help meet for him.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And the LORD God caused a deep sleep to fall upon Adam, and he slept: and he took one of his ribs, and closed up the flesh instead thereof;</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And the rib, which the LORD God had taken from man, made he a woman, and brought her unto the man.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And Adam said, This is now bone of my bones, and flesh of my flesh: she shall be called Woman, because she was taken out of Man.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Therefore shall a man leave his father and his mother, and shall cleave unto his wife: and they shall be one flesh.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And they were both naked, the man and his wife, and were not ashamed.</p>
