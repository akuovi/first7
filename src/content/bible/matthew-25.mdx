## Matthew 25

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Then shall the kingdom of heaven be likened unto ten virgins, which took their lamps, and went forth to meet the bridegroom.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And five of them were wise, and five were foolish.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> They that were foolish took their lamps, and took no oil with them:</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> But the wise took oil in their vessels with their lamps.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> While the bridegroom tarried, they all slumbered and slept.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And at midnight there was a cry made, Behold, the bridegroom cometh; go ye out to meet him.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Then all those virgins arose, and trimmed their lamps.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And the foolish said unto the wise, Give us of your oil; for our lamps are gone out.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> But the wise answered, saying, Not so; lest there be not enough for us and you: but go ye rather to them that sell, and buy for yourselves.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And while they went to buy, the bridegroom came; and they that were ready went in with him to the marriage: and the door was shut.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Afterward came also the other virgins, saying, Lord, Lord, open to us.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> But he answered and said, Verily I say unto you, I know you not.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Watch therefore, for ye know neither the day nor the hour wherein the Son of man cometh.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> For the kingdom of heaven is as a man travelling into a far country, who called his own servants, and delivered unto them his goods.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And unto one he gave five talents, to another two, and to another one; to every man according to his several ability; and straightway took his journey.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Then he that had received the five talents went and traded with the same, and made them other five talents.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And likewise he that had received two, he also gained other two.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> But he that had received one went and digged in the earth, and hid his lord's money.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> After a long time the lord of those servants cometh, and reckoneth with them.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And so he that had received five talents came and brought other five talents, saying, Lord, thou deliveredst unto me five talents: behold, I have gained beside them five talents more.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> His lord said unto him, Well done, thou good and faithful servant: thou hast been faithful over a few things, I will make thee ruler over many things: enter thou into the joy of thy lord.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> He also that had received two talents came and said, Lord, thou deliveredst unto me two talents: behold, I have gained two other talents beside them.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> His lord said unto him, Well done, good and faithful servant; thou hast been faithful over a few things, I will make thee ruler over many things: enter thou into the joy of thy lord.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Then he which had received the one talent came and said, Lord, I knew thee that thou art an hard man, reaping where thou hast not sown, and gathering where thou hast not strawed:</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And I was afraid, and went and hid thy talent in the earth: lo, there thou hast that is thine.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> His lord answered and said unto him, Thou wicked and slothful servant, thou knewest that I reap where I sowed not, and gather where I have not strawed:</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Thou oughtest therefore to have put my money to the exchangers, and then at my coming I should have received mine own with usury.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Take therefore the talent from him, and give it unto him which hath ten talents.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> For unto every one that hath shall be given, and he shall have abundance: but from him that hath not shall be taken away even that which he hath.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And cast ye the unprofitable servant into outer darkness: there shall be weeping and gnashing of teeth.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> When the Son of man shall come in his glory, and all the holy angels with him, then shall he sit upon the throne of his glory:</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> And before him shall be gathered all nations: and he shall separate them one from another, as a shepherd divideth his sheep from the goats:</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> And he shall set the sheep on his right hand, but the goats on the left.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> Then shall the King say unto them on his right hand, Come, ye blessed of my Father, inherit the kingdom prepared for you from the foundation of the world:</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> For I was an hungred, and ye gave me meat: I was thirsty, and ye gave me drink: I was a stranger, and ye took me in:</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> Naked, and ye clothed me: I was sick, and ye visited me: I was in prison, and ye came unto me.</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> Then shall the righteous answer him, saying, Lord, when saw we thee an hungred, and fed thee? or thirsty, and gave thee drink?</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> When saw we thee a stranger, and took thee in? or naked, and clothed thee?</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> Or when saw we thee sick, or in prison, and came unto thee?</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> And the King shall answer and say unto them, Verily I say unto you, Inasmuch as ye have done it unto one of the least of these my brethren, ye have done it unto me.</p>

<p id="v41" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">41</span> Then shall he say also unto them on the left hand, Depart from me, ye cursed, into everlasting fire, prepared for the devil and his angels:</p>

<p id="v42" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">42</span> For I was an hungred, and ye gave me no meat: I was thirsty, and ye gave me no drink:</p>

<p id="v43" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">43</span> I was a stranger, and ye took me not in: naked, and ye clothed me not: sick, and in prison, and ye visited me not.</p>

<p id="v44" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">44</span> Then shall they also answer him, saying, Lord, when saw we thee an hungred, or athirst, or a stranger, or naked, or sick, or in prison, and did not minister unto thee?</p>

<p id="v45" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">45</span> Then shall he answer them, saying, Verily I say unto you, Inasmuch as ye did it not to one of the least of these, ye did it not to me.</p>

<p id="v46" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">46</span> And these shall go away into everlasting punishment: but the righteous into life eternal.</p>
