## Hebrews 10

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> For the law having a shadow of good things to come, and not the very image of the things, can never with those sacrifices which they offered year by year continually make the comers thereunto perfect.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> For then would they not have ceased to be offered? because that the worshippers once purged should have had no more conscience of sins.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> But in those sacrifices there is a remembrance again made of sins every year.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> For it is not possible that the blood of bulls and of goats should take away sins.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Wherefore when he cometh into the world, he saith, Sacrifice and offering thou wouldest not, but a body hast thou prepared me:</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> In burnt offerings and sacrifices for sin thou hast had no pleasure.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Then said I, Lo, I come (in the volume of the book it is written of me,) to do thy will, O God.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Above when he said, Sacrifice and offering and burnt offerings and offering for sin thou wouldest not, neither hadst pleasure therein; which are offered by the law;</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Then said he, Lo, I come to do thy will, O God. He taketh away the first, that he may establish the second.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> By the which will we are sanctified through the offering of the body of Jesus Christ once for all.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And every priest standeth daily ministering and offering oftentimes the same sacrifices, which can never take away sins:</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> But this man, after he had offered one sacrifice for sins for ever, sat down on the right hand of God;</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> From henceforth expecting till his enemies be made his footstool.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> For by one offering he hath perfected for ever them that are sanctified.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Whereof the Holy Ghost also is a witness to us: for after that he had said before,</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> This is the covenant that I will make with them after those days, saith the Lord, I will put my laws into their hearts, and in their minds will I write them;</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And their sins and iniquities will I remember no more.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Now where remission of these is, there is no more offering for sin.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Having therefore, brethren, boldness to enter into the holiest by the blood of Jesus,</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> By a new and living way, which he hath consecrated for us, through the veil, that is to say, his flesh;</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And having an high priest over the house of God;</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Let us draw near with a true heart in full assurance of faith, having our hearts sprinkled from an evil conscience, and our bodies washed with pure water.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Let us hold fast the profession of our faith without wavering; (for he is faithful that promised;)</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And let us consider one another to provoke unto love and to good works:</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Not forsaking the assembling of ourselves together, as the manner of some is; but exhorting one another: and so much the more, as ye see the day approaching.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> For if we sin wilfully after that we have received the knowledge of the truth, there remaineth no more sacrifice for sins,</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> But a certain fearful looking for of judgment and fiery indignation, which shall devour the adversaries.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> He that despised Moses' law died without mercy under two or three witnesses:</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Of how much sorer punishment, suppose ye, shall he be thought worthy, who hath trodden under foot the Son of God, and hath counted the blood of the covenant, wherewith he was sanctified, an unholy thing, and hath done despite unto the Spirit of grace?</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> For we know him that hath said, Vengeance belongeth unto me, I will recompense, saith the Lord. And again, The Lord shall judge his people.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> It is a fearful thing to fall into the hands of the living God.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> But call to remembrance the former days, in which, after ye were illuminated, ye endured a great fight of afflictions;</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> Partly, whilst ye were made a gazingstock both by reproaches and afflictions; and partly, whilst ye became companions of them that were so used.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> For ye had compassion of me in my bonds, and took joyfully the spoiling of your goods, knowing in yourselves that ye have in heaven a better and an enduring substance.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> Cast not away therefore your confidence, which hath great recompence of reward.</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> For ye have need of patience, that, after ye have done the will of God, ye might receive the promise.</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> For yet a little while, and he that shall come will come, and will not tarry.</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> Now the just shall live by faith: but if any man draw back, my soul shall have no pleasure in him.</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> But we are not of them who draw back unto perdition; but of them that believe to the saving of the soul.</p>
