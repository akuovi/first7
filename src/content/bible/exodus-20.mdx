## Exodus 20

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And God spake all these words, saying,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> I am the LORD thy God, which have brought thee out of the land of Egypt, out of the house of bondage.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Thou shalt have no other gods before me.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Thou shalt not make unto thee any graven image, or any likeness of any thing that is in heaven above, or that is in the earth beneath, or that is in the water under the earth:</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Thou shalt not bow down thyself to them, nor serve them: for I the LORD thy God am a jealous God, visiting the iniquity of the fathers upon the children unto the third and fourth generation of them that hate me;</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And shewing mercy unto thousands of them that love me, and keep my commandments.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Thou shalt not take the name of the LORD thy God in vain; for the LORD will not hold him guiltless that taketh his name in vain.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Remember the sabbath day, to keep it holy.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Six days shalt thou labour, and do all thy work:</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> But the seventh day is the sabbath of the LORD thy God: in it thou shalt not do any work, thou, nor thy son, nor thy daughter, thy manservant, nor thy maidservant, nor thy cattle, nor thy stranger that is within thy gates:</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> For in six days the LORD made heaven and earth, the sea, and all that in them is, and rested the seventh day: wherefore the LORD blessed the sabbath day, and hallowed it.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Honour thy father and thy mother: that thy days may be long upon the land which the LORD thy God giveth thee.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Thou shalt not kill.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Thou shalt not commit adultery.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Thou shalt not steal.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Thou shalt not bear false witness against thy neighbour.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Thou shalt not covet thy neighbour's house, thou shalt not covet thy neighbour's wife, nor his manservant, nor his maidservant, nor his ox, nor his ass, nor any thing that is thy neighbour's.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And all the people saw the thunderings, and the lightnings, and the noise of the trumpet, and the mountain smoking: and when the people saw it, they removed, and stood afar off.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And they said unto Moses, Speak thou with us, and we will hear: but let not God speak with us, lest we die.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And Moses said unto the people, Fear not: for God is come to prove you, and that his fear may be before your faces, that ye sin not.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And the people stood afar off, and Moses drew near unto the thick darkness where God was.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And the LORD said unto Moses, Thus thou shalt say unto the children of Israel, Ye have seen that I have talked with you from heaven.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Ye shall not make with me gods of silver, neither shall ye make unto you gods of gold.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> An altar of earth thou shalt make unto me, and shalt sacrifice thereon thy burnt offerings, and thy peace offerings, thy sheep, and thine oxen: in all places where I record my name I will come unto thee, and I will bless thee.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And if thou wilt make me an altar of stone, thou shalt not build it of hewn stone: for if thou lift up thy tool upon it, thou hast polluted it.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Neither shalt thou go up by steps unto mine altar, that thy nakedness be not discovered thereon.</p>
