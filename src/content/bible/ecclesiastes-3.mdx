## Ecclesiastes 3

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> To every thing there is a season, and a time to every purpose under the heaven:</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> A time to be born, and a time to die; a time to plant, and a time to pluck up that which is planted;</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> A time to kill, and a time to heal; a time to break down, and a time to build up;</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> A time to weep, and a time to laugh; a time to mourn, and a time to dance;</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> A time to cast away stones, and a time to gather stones together; a time to embrace, and a time to refrain from embracing;</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> A time to get, and a time to lose; a time to keep, and a time to cast away;</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> A time to rend, and a time to sew; a time to keep silence, and a time to speak;</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> A time to love, and a time to hate; a time of war, and a time of peace.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> What profit hath he that worketh in that wherein he laboureth?</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> I have seen the travail, which God hath given to the sons of men to be exercised in it.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> He hath made every thing beautiful in his time: also he hath set the world in their heart, so that no man can find out the work that God maketh from the beginning to the end.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> I know that there is no good in them, but for a man to rejoice, and to do good in his life.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And also that every man should eat and drink, and enjoy the good of all his labour, it is the gift of God.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> I know that, whatsoever God doeth, it shall be for ever: nothing can be put to it, nor any thing taken from it: and God doeth it, that men should fear before him.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> That which hath been is now; and that which is to be hath already been; and God requireth that which is past.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And moreover I saw under the sun the place of judgment, that wickedness was there; and the place of righteousness, that iniquity was there.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> I said in mine heart, God shall judge the righteous and the wicked: for there is a time there for every purpose and for every work.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> I said in mine heart concerning the estate of the sons of men, that God might manifest them, and that they might see that they themselves are beasts.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> For that which befalleth the sons of men befalleth beasts; even one thing befalleth them: as the one dieth, so dieth the other; yea, they have all one breath; so that a man hath no preeminence above a beast: for all is vanity.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> All go unto one place; all are of the dust, and all turn to dust again.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Who knoweth the spirit of man that goeth upward, and the spirit of the beast that goeth downward to the earth?</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Wherefore I perceive that there is nothing better, than that a man should rejoice in his own works; for that is his portion: for who shall bring him to see what shall be after him?</p>
