## 1 Kings 4

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> So king Solomon was king over all Israel.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And these were the princes which he had; Azariah the son of Zadok the priest,</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Elihoreph and Ahiah, the sons of Shisha, scribes; Jehoshaphat the son of Ahilud, the recorder.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And Benaiah the son of Jehoiada was over the host: and Zadok and Abiathar were the priests:</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And Azariah the son of Nathan was over the officers: and Zabud the son of Nathan was principal officer, and the king's friend:</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And Ahishar was over the household: and Adoniram the son of Abda was over the tribute.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And Solomon had twelve officers over all Israel, which provided victuals for the king and his household: each man his month in a year made provision.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And these are their names: The son of Hur, in mount Ephraim:</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> The son of Dekar, in Makaz, and in Shaalbim, and Beth-shemesh, and Elon-beth-hanan:</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> The son of Hesed, in Aruboth; to him pertained Sochoh, and all the land of Hepher:</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> The son of Abinadab, in all the region of Dor; which had Taphath the daughter of Solomon to wife:</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Baana the son of Ahilud; to him pertained Taanach and Megiddo, and all Beth-shean, which is by Zartanah beneath Jezreel, from Beth-shean to Abel-meholah, even unto the place that is beyond Jokneam:</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> The son of Geber, in Ramoth-gilead; to him pertained the towns of Jair the son of Manasseh, which are in Gilead; to him also pertained the region of Argob, which is in Bashan, threescore great cities with walls and brasen bars:</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Ahinadab the son of Iddo had Mahanaim:</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Ahimaaz was in Naphtali; he also took Basmath the daughter of Solomon to wife:</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Baanah the son of Hushai was in Asher and in Aloth:</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Jehoshaphat the son of Paruah, in Issachar:</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Shimei the son of Elah, in Benjamin:</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Geber the son of Uri was in the country of Gilead, in the country of Sihon king of the Amorites, and of Og king of Bashan; and he was the only officer which was in the land.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Judah and Israel were many, as the sand which is by the sea in multitude, eating and drinking, and making merry.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And Solomon reigned over all kingdoms from the river unto the land of the Philistines, and unto the border of Egypt: they brought presents, and served Solomon all the days of his life.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And Solomon's provision for one day was thirty measures of fine flour, and threescore measures of meal,</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Ten fat oxen, and twenty oxen out of the pastures, and an hundred sheep, beside harts, and roebucks, and fallowdeer, and fatted fowl.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> For he had dominion over all the region on this side the river, from Tiphsah even to Azzah, over all the kings on this side the river: and he had peace on all sides round about him.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And Judah and Israel dwelt safely, every man under his vine and under his fig tree, from Dan even to Beer-sheba, all the days of Solomon.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And Solomon had forty thousand stalls of horses for his chariots, and twelve thousand horsemen.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And those officers provided victual for king Solomon, and for all that came unto king Solomon's table, every man in his month: they lacked nothing.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Barley also and straw for the horses and dromedaries brought they unto the place where the officers were, every man according to his charge.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And God gave Solomon wisdom and understanding exceeding much, and largeness of heart, even as the sand that is on the sea shore.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And Solomon's wisdom excelled the wisdom of all the children of the east country, and all the wisdom of Egypt.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> For he was wiser than all men; than Ethan the Ezrahite, and Heman, and Chalcol, and Darda, the sons of Mahol: and his fame was in all nations round about.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> And he spake three thousand proverbs: and his songs were a thousand and five.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> And he spake of trees, from the cedar tree that is in Lebanon even unto the hyssop that springeth out of the wall: he spake also of beasts, and of fowl, and of creeping things, and of fishes.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> And there came of all people to hear the wisdom of Solomon, from all kings of the earth, which had heard of his wisdom.</p>
