## Proverbs 27

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Boast not thyself of to morrow; for thou knowest not what a day may bring forth.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Let another man praise thee, and not thine own mouth; a stranger, and not thine own lips.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> A stone is heavy, and the sand weighty; but a fool's wrath is heavier than them both.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Wrath is cruel, and anger is outrageous; but who is able to stand before envy?</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Open rebuke is better than secret love.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Faithful are the wounds of a friend; but the kisses of an enemy are deceitful.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> The full soul loatheth an honeycomb; but to the hungry soul every bitter thing is sweet.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> As a bird that wandereth from her nest, so is a man that wandereth from his place.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Ointment and perfume rejoice the heart: so doth the sweetness of a man's friend by hearty counsel.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Thine own friend, and thy father's friend, forsake not; neither go into thy brother's house in the day of thy calamity: for better is a neighbour that is near than a brother far off.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> My son, be wise, and make my heart glad, that I may answer him that reproacheth me.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> A prudent man foreseeth the evil, and hideth himself; but the simple pass on, and are punished.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Take his garment that is surety for a stranger, and take a pledge of him for a strange woman.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> He that blesseth his friend with a loud voice, rising early in the morning, it shall be counted a curse to him.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> A continual dropping in a very rainy day and a contentious woman are alike.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Whosoever hideth her hideth the wind, and the ointment of his right hand, which bewrayeth itself.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Iron sharpeneth iron; so a man sharpeneth the countenance of his friend.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Whoso keepeth the fig tree shall eat the fruit thereof: so he that waiteth on his master shall be honoured.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> As in water face answereth to face, so the heart of man to man.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Hell and destruction are never full; so the eyes of man are never satisfied.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> As the fining pot for silver, and the furnace for gold; so is a man to his praise.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Though thou shouldest bray a fool in a mortar among wheat with a pestle, yet will not his foolishness depart from him.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Be thou diligent to know the state of thy flocks, and look well to thy herds.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> For riches are not for ever: and doth the crown endure to every generation?</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> The hay appeareth, and the tender grass sheweth itself, and herbs of the mountains are gathered.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> The lambs are for thy clothing, and the goats are the price of the field.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And thou shalt have goats' milk enough for thy food, for the food of thy household, and for the maintenance for thy maidens.</p>
