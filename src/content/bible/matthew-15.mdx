## Matthew 15

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Then came to Jesus scribes and Pharisees, which were of Jerusalem, saying,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Why do thy disciples transgress the tradition of the elders? for they wash not their hands when they eat bread.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> But he answered and said unto them, Why do ye also transgress the commandment of God by your tradition?</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> For God commanded, saying, Honour thy father and mother: and, He that curseth father or mother, let him die the death.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> But ye say, Whosoever shall say to his father or his mother, It is a gift, by whatsoever thou mightest be profited by me;</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And honour not his father or his mother, he shall be free. Thus have ye made the commandment of God of none effect by your tradition.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Ye hypocrites, well did Esaias prophesy of you, saying,</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> This people draweth nigh unto me with their mouth, and honoureth me with their lips; but their heart is far from me.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> But in vain they do worship me, teaching for doctrines the commandments of men.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And he called the multitude, and said unto them, Hear, and understand:</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Not that which goeth into the mouth defileth a man; but that which cometh out of the mouth, this defileth a man.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Then came his disciples, and said unto him, Knowest thou that the Pharisees were offended, after they heard this saying?</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> But he answered and said, Every plant, which my heavenly Father hath not planted, shall be rooted up.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Let them alone: they be blind leaders of the blind. And if the blind lead the blind, both shall fall into the ditch.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Then answered Peter and said unto him, Declare unto us this parable.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And Jesus said, Are ye also yet without understanding?</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Do not ye yet understand, that whatsoever entereth in at the mouth goeth into the belly, and is cast out into the draught?</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> But those things which proceed out of the mouth come forth from the heart; and they defile the man.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> For out of the heart proceed evil thoughts, murders, adulteries, fornications, thefts, false witness, blasphemies:</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> These are the things which defile a man: but to eat with unwashen hands defileth not a man.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Then Jesus went thence, and departed into the coasts of Tyre and Sidon.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And, behold, a woman of Canaan came out of the same coasts, and cried unto him, saying, Have mercy on me, O Lord, thou Son of David; my daughter is grievously vexed with a devil.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> But he answered her not a word. And his disciples came and besought him, saying, Send her away; for she crieth after us.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> But he answered and said, I am not sent but unto the lost sheep of the house of Israel.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Then came she and worshipped him, saying, Lord, help me.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> But he answered and said, It is not meet to take the children's bread, and to cast it to dogs.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And she said, Truth, Lord: yet the dogs eat of the crumbs which fall from their masters' table.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Then Jesus answered and said unto her, O woman, great is thy faith: be it unto thee even as thou wilt. And her daughter was made whole from that very hour.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And Jesus departed from thence, and came nigh unto the sea of Galilee; and went up into a mountain, and sat down there.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And great multitudes came unto him, having with them those that were lame, blind, dumb, maimed, and many others, and cast them down at Jesus' feet; and he healed them:</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Insomuch that the multitude wondered, when they saw the dumb to speak, the maimed to be whole, the lame to walk, and the blind to see: and they glorified the God of Israel.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> Then Jesus called his disciples unto him, and said, I have compassion on the multitude, because they continue with me now three days, and have nothing to eat: and I will not send them away fasting, lest they faint in the way.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> And his disciples say unto him, Whence should we have so much bread in the wilderness, as to fill so great a multitude?</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> And Jesus saith unto them, How many loaves have ye? And they said, Seven, and a few little fishes.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> And he commanded the multitude to sit down on the ground.</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> And he took the seven loaves and the fishes, and gave thanks, and brake them, and gave to his disciples, and the disciples to the multitude.</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> And they did all eat, and were filled: and they took up of the broken meat that was left seven baskets full.</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> And they that did eat were four thousand men, beside women and children.</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> And he sent away the multitude, and took ship, and came into the coasts of Magdala.</p>
