## Job 18

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Then answered Bildad the Shuhite, and said,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> How long will it be ere ye make an end of words? mark, and afterwards we will speak.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Wherefore are we counted as beasts, and reputed vile in your sight?</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> He teareth himself in his anger: shall the earth be forsaken for thee? and shall the rock be removed out of his place?</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Yea, the light of the wicked shall be put out, and the spark of his fire shall not shine.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> The light shall be dark in his tabernacle, and his candle shall be put out with him.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> The steps of his strength shall be straitened, and his own counsel shall cast him down.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> For he is cast into a net by his own feet, and he walketh upon a snare.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> The gin shall take him by the heel, and the robber shall prevail against him.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> The snare is laid for him in the ground, and a trap for him in the way.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Terrors shall make him afraid on every side, and shall drive him to his feet.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> His strength shall be hungerbitten, and destruction shall be ready at his side.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> It shall devour the strength of his skin: even the firstborn of death shall devour his strength.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> His confidence shall be rooted out of his tabernacle, and it shall bring him to the king of terrors.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> It shall dwell in his tabernacle, because it is none of his: brimstone shall be scattered upon his habitation.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> His roots shall be dried up beneath, and above shall his branch be cut off.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> His remembrance shall perish from the earth, and he shall have no name in the street.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> He shall be driven from light into darkness, and chased out of the world.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> He shall neither have son nor nephew among his people, nor any remaining in his dwellings.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> They that come after him shall be astonied at his day, as they that went before were affrighted.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Surely such are the dwellings of the wicked, and this is the place of him that knoweth not God.</p>
