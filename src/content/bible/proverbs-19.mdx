## Proverbs 19

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Better is the poor that walketh in his integrity, than he that is perverse in his lips, and is a fool.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Also, that the soul be without knowledge, it is not good; and he that hasteth with his feet sinneth.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> The foolishness of man perverteth his way: and his heart fretteth against the LORD.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Wealth maketh many friends; but the poor is separated from his neighbour.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> A false witness shall not be unpunished, and he that speaketh lies shall not escape.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Many will intreat the favour of the prince: and every man is a friend to him that giveth gifts.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> All the brethren of the poor do hate him: how much more do his friends go far from him? he pursueth them with words, yet they are wanting to him.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> He that getteth wisdom loveth his own soul: he that keepeth understanding shall find good.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> A false witness shall not be unpunished, and he that speaketh lies shall perish.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Delight is not seemly for a fool; much less for a servant to have rule over princes.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> The discretion of a man deferreth his anger; and it is his glory to pass over a transgression.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> The king's wrath is as the roaring of a lion; but his favour is as dew upon the grass.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> A foolish son is the calamity of his father: and the contentions of a wife are a continual dropping.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> House and riches are the inheritance of fathers: and a prudent wife is from the LORD.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Slothfulness casteth into a deep sleep; and an idle soul shall suffer hunger.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> He that keepeth the commandment keepeth his own soul; but he that despiseth his ways shall die.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> He that hath pity upon the poor lendeth unto the LORD; and that which he hath given will he pay him again.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Chasten thy son while there is hope, and let not thy soul spare for his crying.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> A man of great wrath shall suffer punishment: for if thou deliver him, yet thou must do it again.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Hear counsel, and receive instruction, that thou mayest be wise in thy latter end.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> There are many devices in a man's heart; nevertheless the counsel of the LORD, that shall stand.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> The desire of a man is his kindness: and a poor man is better than a liar.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> The fear of the LORD tendeth to life: and he that hath it shall abide satisfied; he shall not be visited with evil.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> A slothful man hideth his hand in his bosom, and will not so much as bring it to his mouth again.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Smite a scorner, and the simple will beware: and reprove one that hath understanding, and he will understand knowledge.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> He that wasteth his father, and chaseth away his mother, is a son that causeth shame, and bringeth reproach.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Cease, my son, to hear the instruction that causeth to err from the words of knowledge.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> An ungodly witness scorneth judgment: and the mouth of the wicked devoureth iniquity.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Judgments are prepared for scorners, and stripes for the back of fools.</p>
