## Hosea 14

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> O Israel, return unto the LORD thy God; for thou hast fallen by thine iniquity.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Take with you words, and turn to the LORD: say unto him, Take away all iniquity, and receive us graciously: so will we render the calves of our lips.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Asshur shall not save us; we will not ride upon horses: neither will we say any more to the work of our hands, Ye are our gods: for in thee the fatherless findeth mercy.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> I will heal their backsliding, I will love them freely: for mine anger is turned away from him.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> I will be as the dew unto Israel: he shall grow as the lily, and cast forth his roots as Lebanon.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> His branches shall spread, and his beauty shall be as the olive tree, and his smell as Lebanon.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> They that dwell under his shadow shall return; they shall revive as the corn, and grow as the vine: the scent thereof shall be as the wine of Lebanon.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Ephraim shall say, What have I to do any more with idols? I have heard him, and observed him: I am like a green fir tree. From me is thy fruit found.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Who is wise, and he shall understand these things? prudent, and he shall know them? for the ways of the LORD are right, and the just shall walk in them: but the transgressors shall fall therein.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> HOSEA</p>
