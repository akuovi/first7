## Job 11

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Then answered Zophar the Naamathite, and said,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Should not the multitude of words be answered? and should a man full of talk be justified?</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Should thy lies make men hold their peace? and when thou mockest, shall no man make thee ashamed?</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> For thou hast said, My doctrine is pure, and I am clean in thine eyes.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> But oh that God would speak, and open his lips against thee;</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And that he would shew thee the secrets of wisdom, that they are double to that which is! Know therefore that God exacteth of thee less than thine iniquity deserveth.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Canst thou by searching find out God? canst thou find out the Almighty unto perfection?</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> It is as high as heaven; what canst thou do? deeper than hell; what canst thou know?</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> The measure thereof is longer than the earth, and broader than the sea.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> If he cut off, and shut up, or gather together, then who can hinder him?</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> For he knoweth vain men: he seeth wickedness also; will he not then consider it?</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> For vain man would be wise, though man be born like a wild ass's colt.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> If thou prepare thine heart, and stretch out thine hands toward him;</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> If iniquity be in thine hand, put it far away, and let not wickedness dwell in thy tabernacles.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> For then shalt thou lift up thy face without spot; yea, thou shalt be stedfast, and shalt not fear:</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Because thou shalt forget thy misery, and remember it as waters that pass away:</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And thine age shall be clearer than the noonday; thou shalt shine forth, thou shalt be as the morning.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And thou shalt be secure, because there is hope; yea, thou shalt dig about thee, and thou shalt take thy rest in safety.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Also thou shalt lie down, and none shall make thee afraid; yea, many shall make suit unto thee.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> But the eyes of the wicked shall fail, and they shall not escape, and their hope shall be as the giving up of the ghost.</p>
