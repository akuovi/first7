## Job 33

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Wherefore, Job, I pray thee, hear my speeches, and hearken to all my words.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Behold, now I have opened my mouth, my tongue hath spoken in my mouth.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> My words shall be of the uprightness of my heart: and my lips shall utter knowledge clearly.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> The Spirit of God hath made me, and the breath of the Almighty hath given me life.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> If thou canst answer me, set thy words in order before me, stand up.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Behold, I am according to thy wish in God's stead: I also am formed out of the clay.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Behold, my terror shall not make thee afraid, neither shall my hand be heavy upon thee.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Surely thou hast spoken in mine hearing, and I have heard the voice of thy words, saying,</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> I am clean without transgression, I am innocent; neither is there iniquity in me.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Behold, he findeth occasions against me, he counteth me for his enemy,</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> He putteth my feet in the stocks, he marketh all my paths.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Behold, in this thou art not just: I will answer thee, that God is greater than man.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Why dost thou strive against him? for he giveth not account of any of his matters.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> For God speaketh once, yea twice, yet man perceiveth it not.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> In a dream, in a vision of the night, when deep sleep falleth upon men, in slumberings upon the bed;</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Then he openeth the ears of men, and sealeth their instruction,</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> That he may withdraw man from his purpose, and hide pride from man.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> He keepeth back his soul from the pit, and his life from perishing by the sword.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> He is chastened also with pain upon his bed, and the multitude of his bones with strong pain:</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> So that his life abhorreth bread, and his soul dainty meat.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> His flesh is consumed away, that it cannot be seen; and his bones that were not seen stick out.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Yea, his soul draweth near unto the grave, and his life to the destroyers.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> If there be a messenger with him, an interpreter, one among a thousand, to shew unto man his uprightness:</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Then he is gracious unto him, and saith, Deliver him from going down to the pit: I have found a ransom.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> His flesh shall be fresher than a child's: he shall return to the days of his youth:</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> He shall pray unto God, and he will be favourable unto him: and he shall see his face with joy: for he will render unto man his righteousness.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> He looketh upon men, and if any say, I have sinned, and perverted that which was right, and it profited me not;</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> He will deliver his soul from going into the pit, and his life shall see the light.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Lo, all these things worketh God oftentimes with man,</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> To bring back his soul from the pit, to be enlightened with the light of the living.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Mark well, O Job, hearken unto me: hold thy peace, and I will speak.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> If thou hast any thing to say, answer me: speak, for I desire to justify thee.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> If not, hearken unto me: hold thy peace, and I shall teach thee wisdom.</p>
