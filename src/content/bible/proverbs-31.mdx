## Proverbs 31

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> The words of king Lemuel, the prophecy that his mother taught him.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> What, my son? and what, the son of my womb? and what, the son of my vows?</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Give not thy strength unto women, nor thy ways to that which destroyeth kings.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> It is not for kings, O Lemuel, it is not for kings to drink wine; nor for princes strong drink:</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Lest they drink, and forget the law, and pervert the judgment of any of the afflicted.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Give strong drink unto him that is ready to perish, and wine unto those that be of heavy hearts.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Let him drink, and forget his poverty, and remember his misery no more.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Open thy mouth for the dumb in the cause of all such as are appointed to destruction.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Open thy mouth, judge righteously, and plead the cause of the poor and needy.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Who can find a virtuous woman? for her price is far above rubies.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> The heart of her husband doth safely trust in her, so that he shall have no need of spoil.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> She will do him good and not evil all the days of her life.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> She seeketh wool, and flax, and worketh willingly with her hands.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> She is like the merchants' ships; she bringeth her food from afar.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> She riseth also while it is yet night, and giveth meat to her household, and a portion to her maidens.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> She considereth a field, and buyeth it: with the fruit of her hands she planteth a vineyard.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> She girdeth her loins with strength, and strengtheneth her arms.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> She perceiveth that her merchandise is good: her candle goeth not out by night.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> She layeth her hands to the spindle, and her hands hold the distaff.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> She stretcheth out her hand to the poor; yea, she reacheth forth her hands to the needy.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> She is not afraid of the snow for her household: for all her household are clothed with scarlet.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> She maketh herself coverings of tapestry; her clothing is silk and purple.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Her husband is known in the gates, when he sitteth among the elders of the land.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> She maketh fine linen, and selleth it; and delivereth girdles unto the merchant.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Strength and honour are her clothing; and she shall rejoice in time to come.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> She openeth her mouth with wisdom; and in her tongue is the law of kindness.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> She looketh well to the ways of her household, and eateth not the bread of idleness.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Her children arise up, and call her blessed; her husband also, and he praiseth her.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Many daughters have done virtuously, but thou excellest them all.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Favour is deceitful, and beauty is vain: but a woman that feareth the LORD, she shall be praised.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Give her of the fruit of her hands; and let her own works praise her in the gates.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> PROVERBS</p>
