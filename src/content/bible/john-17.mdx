## John 17

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> These words spake Jesus, and lifted up his eyes to heaven, and said, Father, the hour is come; glorify thy Son, that thy Son also may glorify thee:</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> As thou hast given him power over all flesh, that he should give eternal life to as many as thou hast given him.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And this is life eternal, that they might know thee the only true God, and Jesus Christ, whom thou hast sent.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> I have glorified thee on the earth: I have finished the work which thou gavest me to do.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And now, O Father, glorify thou me with thine own self with the glory which I had with thee before the world was.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> I have manifested thy name unto the men which thou gavest me out of the world: thine they were, and thou gavest them me; and they have kept thy word.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Now they have known that all things whatsoever thou hast given me are of thee.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> For I have given unto them the words which thou gavest me; and they have received them, and have known surely that I came out from thee, and they have believed that thou didst send me.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> I pray for them: I pray not for the world, but for them which thou hast given me; for they are thine.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And all mine are thine, and thine are mine; and I am glorified in them.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And now I am no more in the world, but these are in the world, and I come to thee. Holy Father, keep through thine own name those whom thou hast given me, that they may be one, as we are.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> While I was with them in the world, I kept them in thy name: those that thou gavest me I have kept, and none of them is lost, but the son of perdition; that the scripture might be fulfilled.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And now come I to thee; and these things I speak in the world, that they might have my joy fulfilled in themselves.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> I have given them thy word; and the world hath hated them, because they are not of the world, even as I am not of the world.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> I pray not that thou shouldest take them out of the world, but that thou shouldest keep them from the evil.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> They are not of the world, even as I am not of the world.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Sanctify them through thy truth: thy word is truth.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> As thou hast sent me into the world, even so have I also sent them into the world.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And for their sakes I sanctify myself, that they also might be sanctified through the truth.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Neither pray I for these alone, but for them also which shall believe on me through their word;</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> That they all may be one; as thou, Father, art in me, and I in thee, that they also may be one in us: that the world may believe that thou hast sent me.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And the glory which thou gavest me I have given them; that they may be one, even as we are one:</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> I in them, and thou in me, that they may be made perfect in one; and that the world may know that thou hast sent me, and hast loved them, as thou hast loved me.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Father, I will that they also, whom thou hast given me, be with me where I am; that they may behold my glory, which thou hast given me: for thou lovedst me before the foundation of the world.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> O righteous Father, the world hath not known thee: but I have known thee, and these have known that thou hast sent me.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And I have declared unto them thy name, and will declare it: that the love wherewith thou hast loved me may be in them, and I in them.</p>
