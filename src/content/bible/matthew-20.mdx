## Matthew 20

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> For the kingdom of heaven is like unto a man that is an householder, which went out early in the morning to hire labourers into his vineyard.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And when he had agreed with the labourers for a penny a day, he sent them into his vineyard.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And he went out about the third hour, and saw others standing idle in the marketplace,</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And said unto them; Go ye also into the vineyard, and whatsoever is right I will give you. And they went their way.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Again he went out about the sixth and ninth hour, and did likewise.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And about the eleventh hour he went out, and found others standing idle, and saith unto them, Why stand ye here all the day idle?</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> They say unto him, Because no man hath hired us. He saith unto them, Go ye also into the vineyard; and whatsoever is right, that shall ye receive.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> So when even was come, the lord of the vineyard saith unto his steward, Call the labourers, and give them their hire, beginning from the last unto the first.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And when they came that were hired about the eleventh hour, they received every man a penny.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> But when the first came, they supposed that they should have received more; and they likewise received every man a penny.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And when they had received it, they murmured against the goodman of the house,</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Saying, These last have wrought but one hour, and thou hast made them equal unto us, which have borne the burden and heat of the day.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> But he answered one of them, and said, Friend, I do thee no wrong: didst not thou agree with me for a penny?</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Take that thine is, and go thy way: I will give unto this last, even as unto thee.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Is it not lawful for me to do what I will with mine own? Is thine eye evil, because I am good?</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> So the last shall be first, and the first last: for many be called, but few chosen.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And Jesus going up to Jerusalem took the twelve disciples apart in the way, and said unto them,</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Behold, we go up to Jerusalem; and the Son of man shall be betrayed unto the chief priests and unto the scribes, and they shall condemn him to death,</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And shall deliver him to the Gentiles to mock, and to scourge, and to crucify him: and the third day he shall rise again.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Then came to him the mother of Zebedee's children with her sons, worshipping him, and desiring a certain thing of him.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And he said unto her, What wilt thou? She saith unto him, Grant that these my two sons may sit, the one on thy right hand, and the other on the left, in thy kingdom.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> But Jesus answered and said, Ye know not what ye ask. Are ye able to drink of the cup that I shall drink of, and to be baptized with the baptism that I am baptized with? They say unto him, We are able.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And he saith unto them, Ye shall drink indeed of my cup, and be baptized with the baptism that I am baptized with: but to sit on my right hand, and on my left, is not mine to give, but it shall be given to them for whom it is prepared of my Father.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And when the ten heard it, they were moved with indignation against the two brethren.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> But Jesus called them unto him, and said, Ye know that the princes of the Gentiles exercise dominion over them, and they that are great exercise authority upon them.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> But it shall not be so among you: but whosoever will be great among you, let him be your minister;</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And whosoever will be chief among you, let him be your servant:</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Even as the Son of man came not to be ministered unto, but to minister, and to give his life a ransom for many.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And as they departed from Jericho, a great multitude followed him.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And, behold, two blind men sitting by the way side, when they heard that Jesus passed by, cried out, saying, Have mercy on us, O Lord, thou Son of David.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> And the multitude rebuked them, because they should hold their peace: but they cried the more, saying, Have mercy on us, O Lord, thou Son of David.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> And Jesus stood still, and called them, and said, What will ye that I shall do unto you?</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> They say unto him, Lord, that our eyes may be opened.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> So Jesus had compassion on them, and touched their eyes: and immediately their eyes received sight, and they followed him.</p>
