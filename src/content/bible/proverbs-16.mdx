## Proverbs 16

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> The preparations of the heart in man, and the answer of the tongue, is from the LORD.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> All the ways of a man are clean in his own eyes; but the LORD weigheth the spirits.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Commit thy works unto the LORD, and thy thoughts shall be established.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> The LORD hath made all things for himself: yea, even the wicked for the day of evil.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Every one that is proud in heart is an abomination to the LORD: though hand join in hand, he shall not be unpunished.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> By mercy and truth iniquity is purged: and by the fear of the LORD men depart from evil.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> When a man's ways please the LORD, he maketh even his enemies to be at peace with him.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Better is a little with righteousness than great revenues without right.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> A man's heart deviseth his way: but the LORD directeth his steps.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> A divine sentence is in the lips of the king: his mouth transgresseth not in judgment.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> A just weight and balance are the LORD'S: all the weights of the bag are his work.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> It is an abomination to kings to commit wickedness: for the throne is established by righteousness.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Righteous lips are the delight of kings; and they love him that speaketh right.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> The wrath of a king is as messengers of death: but a wise man will pacify it.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> In the light of the king's countenance is life; and his favour is as a cloud of the latter rain.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> How much better is it to get wisdom than gold! and to get understanding rather to be chosen than silver!</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> The highway of the upright is to depart from evil: he that keepeth his way preserveth his soul.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Pride goeth before destruction, and an haughty spirit before a fall.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Better it is to be of an humble spirit with the lowly, than to divide the spoil with the proud.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> He that handleth a matter wisely shall find good: and whoso trusteth in the LORD, happy is he.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> The wise in heart shall be called prudent: and the sweetness of the lips increaseth learning.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Understanding is a wellspring of life unto him that hath it: but the instruction of fools is folly.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> The heart of the wise teacheth his mouth, and addeth learning to his lips.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Pleasant words are as an honeycomb, sweet to the soul, and health to the bones.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> There is a way that seemeth right unto a man, but the end thereof are the ways of death.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> He that laboureth laboureth for himself; for his mouth craveth it of him.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> An ungodly man diggeth up evil: and in his lips there is as a burning fire.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> A froward man soweth strife: and a whisperer separateth chief friends.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> A violent man enticeth his neighbour, and leadeth him into the way that is not good.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> He shutteth his eyes to devise froward things: moving his lips he bringeth evil to pass.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> The hoary head is a crown of glory, if it be found in the way of righteousness.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> He that is slow to anger is better than the mighty; and he that ruleth his spirit than he that taketh a city.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> The lot is cast into the lap; but the whole disposing thereof is of the LORD.</p>
