## 2 Corinthians 6

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> We then, as workers together with him, beseech you also that ye receive not the grace of God in vain.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> (For he saith, I have heard thee in a time accepted, and in the day of salvation have I succoured thee: behold, now is the accepted time; behold, now is the day of salvation.)</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Giving no offence in any thing, that the ministry be not blamed:</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> But in all things approving ourselves as the ministers of God, in much patience, in afflictions, in necessities, in distresses,</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> In stripes, in imprisonments, in tumults, in labours, in watchings, in fastings;</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> By pureness, by knowledge, by longsuffering, by kindness, by the Holy Ghost, by love unfeigned,</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> By the word of truth, by the power of God, by the armour of righteousness on the right hand and on the left,</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> By honour and dishonour, by evil report and good report: as deceivers, and yet true;</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> As unknown, and yet well known; as dying, and, behold, we live; as chastened, and not killed;</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> As sorrowful, yet alway rejoicing; as poor, yet making many rich; as having nothing, and yet possessing all things.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> O ye Corinthians, our mouth is open unto you, our heart is enlarged.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Ye are not straitened in us, but ye are straitened in your own bowels.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Now for a recompence in the same, (I speak as unto my children,) be ye also enlarged.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Be ye not unequally yoked together with unbelievers: for what fellowship hath righteousness with unrighteousness? and what communion hath light with darkness?</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And what concord hath Christ with Belial? or what part hath he that believeth with an infidel?</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And what agreement hath the temple of God with idols? for ye are the temple of the living God; as God hath said, I will dwell in them, and walk in them; and I will be their God, and they shall be my people.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Wherefore come out from among them, and be ye separate, saith the Lord, and touch not the unclean thing; and I will receive you,</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And will be a Father unto you, and ye shall be my sons and daughters, saith the Lord Almighty.</p>
