## 2 Timothy 1

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Paul, an apostle of Jesus Christ by the will of God, according to the promise of life which is in Christ Jesus,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> To Timothy, my dearly beloved son: Grace, mercy, and peace, from God the Father and Christ Jesus our Lord.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> I thank God, whom I serve from my forefathers with pure conscience, that without ceasing I have remembrance of thee in my prayers night and day;</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Greatly desiring to see thee, being mindful of thy tears, that I may be filled with joy;</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> When I call to remembrance the unfeigned faith that is in thee, which dwelt first in thy grandmother Lois, and thy mother Eunice; and I am persuaded that in thee also.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Wherefore I put thee in remembrance that thou stir up the gift of God, which is in thee by the putting on of my hands.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> For God hath not given us the spirit of fear; but of power, and of love, and of a sound mind.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Be not thou therefore ashamed of the testimony of our Lord, nor of me his prisoner: but be thou partaker of the afflictions of the gospel according to the power of God;</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Who hath saved us, and called us with an holy calling, not according to our works, but according to his own purpose and grace, which was given us in Christ Jesus before the world began,</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> But is now made manifest by the appearing of our Saviour Jesus Christ, who hath abolished death, and hath brought life and immortality to light through the gospel:</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Whereunto I am appointed a preacher, and an apostle, and a teacher of the Gentiles.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> For the which cause I also suffer these things: nevertheless I am not ashamed: for I know whom I have believed, and am persuaded that he is able to keep that which I have committed unto him against that day.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Hold fast the form of sound words, which thou hast heard of me, in faith and love which is in Christ Jesus.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> That good thing which was committed unto thee keep by the Holy Ghost which dwelleth in us.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> This thou knowest, that all they which are in Asia be turned away from me; of whom are Phygellus and Hermogenes.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> The Lord give mercy unto the house of Onesiphorus; for he oft refreshed me, and was not ashamed of my chain:</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> But, when he was in Rome, he sought me out very diligently, and found me.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> The Lord grant unto him that he may find mercy of the Lord in that day: and in how many things he ministered unto me at Ephesus, thou knowest very well.</p>
