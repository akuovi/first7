## Genesis 18

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And the LORD appeared unto him in the plains of Mamre: and he sat in the tent door in the heat of the day;</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And he lift up his eyes and looked, and, lo, three men stood by him: and when he saw them, he ran to meet them from the tent door, and bowed himself toward the ground,</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And said, My Lord, if now I have found favour in thy sight, pass not away, I pray thee, from thy servant:</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Let a little water, I pray you, be fetched, and wash your feet, and rest yourselves under the tree:</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And I will fetch a morsel of bread, and comfort ye your hearts; after that ye shall pass on: for therefore are ye come to your servant. And they said, So do, as thou hast said.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And Abraham hastened into the tent unto Sarah, and said, Make ready quickly three measures of fine meal, knead it, and make cakes upon the hearth.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And Abraham ran unto the herd, and fetcht a calf tender and good, and gave it unto a young man; and he hasted to dress it.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And he took butter, and milk, and the calf which he had dressed, and set it before them; and he stood by them under the tree, and they did eat.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And they said unto him, Where is Sarah thy wife? And he said, Behold, in the tent.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And he said, I will certainly return unto thee according to the time of life; and, lo, Sarah thy wife shall have a son. And Sarah heard it in the tent door, which was behind him.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Now Abraham and Sarah were old and well stricken in age; and it ceased to be with Sarah after the manner of women.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Therefore Sarah laughed within herself, saying, After I am waxed old shall I have pleasure, my lord being old also?</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And the LORD said unto Abraham, Wherefore did Sarah laugh, saying, Shall I of a surety bear a child, which am old?</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Is any thing too hard for the LORD? At the time appointed I will return unto thee, according to the time of life, and Sarah shall have a son.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Then Sarah denied, saying, I laughed not; for she was afraid. And he said, Nay; but thou didst laugh.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And the men rose up from thence, and looked toward Sodom: and Abraham went with them to bring them on the way.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And the LORD said, Shall I hide from Abraham that thing which I do;</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Seeing that Abraham shall surely become a great and mighty nation, and all the nations of the earth shall be blessed in him?</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> For I know him, that he will command his children and his household after him, and they shall keep the way of the LORD, to do justice and judgment; that the LORD may bring upon Abraham that which he hath spoken of him.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And the LORD said, Because the cry of Sodom and Gomorrah is great, and because their sin is very grievous;</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> I will go down now, and see whether they have done altogether according to the cry of it, which is come unto me; and if not, I will know.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And the men turned their faces from thence, and went toward Sodom: but Abraham stood yet before the LORD.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And Abraham drew near, and said, Wilt thou also destroy the righteous with the wicked?</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Peradventure there be fifty righteous within the city: wilt thou also destroy and not spare the place for the fifty righteous that are therein?</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> That be far from thee to do after this manner, to slay the righteous with the wicked: and that the righteous should be as the wicked, that be far from thee: Shall not the Judge of all the earth do right?</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And the LORD said, If I find in Sodom fifty righteous within the city, then I will spare all the place for their sakes.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And Abraham answered and said, Behold now, I have taken upon me to speak unto the Lord, which am but dust and ashes:</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Peradventure there shall lack five of the fifty righteous: wilt thou destroy all the city for lack of five? And he said, If I find there forty and five, I will not destroy it.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And he spake unto him yet again, and said, Peradventure there shall be forty found there. And he said, I will not do it for forty's sake.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And he said unto him, Oh let not the Lord be angry, and I will speak: Peradventure there shall thirty be found there. And he said, I will not do it, if I find thirty there.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> And he said, Behold now, I have taken upon me to speak unto the Lord: Peradventure there shall be twenty found there. And he said, I will not destroy it for twenty's sake.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> And he said, Oh let not the Lord be angry, and I will speak yet but this once: Peradventure ten shall be found there. And he said, I will not destroy it for ten's sake.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> And the LORD went his way, as soon as he had left communing with Abraham: and Abraham returned unto his place.</p>
