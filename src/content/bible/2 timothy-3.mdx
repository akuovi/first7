## 2 Timothy 3

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> This know also, that in the last days perilous times shall come.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> For men shall be lovers of their own selves, covetous, boasters, proud, blasphemers, disobedient to parents, unthankful, unholy,</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Without natural affection, trucebreakers, false accusers, incontinent, fierce, despisers of those that are good,</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Traitors, heady, highminded, lovers of pleasures more than lovers of God;</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Having a form of godliness, but denying the power thereof: from such turn away.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> For of this sort are they which creep into houses, and lead captive silly women laden with sins, led away with divers lusts,</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Ever learning, and never able to come to the knowledge of the truth.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Now as Jannes and Jambres withstood Moses, so do these also resist the truth: men of corrupt minds, reprobate concerning the faith.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> But they shall proceed no further: for their folly shall be manifest unto all men, as their's also was.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> But thou hast fully known my doctrine, manner of life, purpose, faith, longsuffering, charity, patience,</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Persecutions, afflictions, which came unto me at Antioch, at Iconium, at Lystra; what persecutions I endured: but out of them all the Lord delivered me.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Yea, and all that will live godly in Christ Jesus shall suffer persecution.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> But evil men and seducers shall wax worse and worse, deceiving, and being deceived.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> But continue thou in the things which thou hast learned and hast been assured of, knowing of whom thou hast learned them;</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And that from a child thou hast known the holy scriptures, which are able to make thee wise unto salvation through faith which is in Christ Jesus.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> All scripture is given by inspiration of God, and is profitable for doctrine, for reproof, for correction, for instruction in righteousness:</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> That the man of God may be perfect, throughly furnished unto all good works.</p>
