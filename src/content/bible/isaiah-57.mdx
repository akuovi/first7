## Isaiah 57

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> The righteous perisheth, and no man layeth it to heart: and merciful men are taken away, none considering that the righteous is taken away from the evil to come.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> He shall enter into peace: they shall rest in their beds, each one walking in his uprightness.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> But draw near hither, ye sons of the sorceress, the seed of the adulterer and the whore.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Against whom do ye sport yourselves? against whom make ye a wide mouth, and draw out the tongue? are ye not children of transgression, a seed of falsehood,</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Enflaming yourselves with idols under every green tree, slaying the children in the valleys under the clifts of the rocks?</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Among the smooth stones of the stream is thy portion; they, they are thy lot: even to them hast thou poured a drink offering, thou hast offered a meat offering. Should I receive comfort in these?</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Upon a lofty and high mountain hast thou set thy bed: even thither wentest thou up to offer sacrifice.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Behind the doors also and the posts hast thou set up thy remembrance: for thou hast discovered thyself to another than me, and art gone up; thou hast enlarged thy bed, and made thee a covenant with them; thou lovedst their bed where thou sawest it.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And thou wentest to the king with ointment, and didst increase thy perfumes, and didst send thy messengers far off, and didst debase thyself even unto hell.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Thou art wearied in the greatness of thy way; yet saidst thou not, There is no hope: thou hast found the life of thine hand; therefore thou wast not grieved.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And of whom hast thou been afraid or feared, that thou hast lied, and hast not remembered me, nor laid it to thy heart? have not I held my peace even of old, and thou fearest me not?</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> I will declare thy righteousness, and thy works; for they shall not profit thee.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> When thou criest, let thy companies deliver thee; but the wind shall carry them all away; vanity shall take them: but he that putteth his trust in me shall possess the land, and shall inherit my holy mountain;</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And shall say, Cast ye up, cast ye up, prepare the way, take up the stumblingblock out of the way of my people.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> For thus saith the high and lofty One that inhabiteth eternity, whose name is Holy; I dwell in the high and holy place, with him also that is of a contrite and humble spirit, to revive the spirit of the humble, and to revive the heart of the contrite ones.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> For I will not contend for ever, neither will I be always wroth: for the spirit should fail before me, and the souls which I have made.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> For the iniquity of his covetousness was I wroth, and smote him: I hid me, and was wroth, and he went on frowardly in the way of his heart.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> I have seen his ways, and will heal him: I will lead him also, and restore comforts unto him and to his mourners.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> I create the fruit of the lips; Peace, peace to him that is far off, and to him that is near, saith the LORD; and I will heal him.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> But the wicked are like the troubled sea, when it cannot rest, whose waters cast up mire and dirt.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> There is no peace, saith my God, to the wicked.</p>
