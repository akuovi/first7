## Luke 11

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And it came to pass, that, as he was praying in a certain place, when he ceased, one of his disciples said unto him, Lord, teach us to pray, as John also taught his disciples.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And he said unto them, When ye pray, say, Our Father which art in heaven, Hallowed be thy name. Thy kingdom come. Thy will be done, as in heaven, so in earth.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Give us day by day our daily bread.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And forgive us our sins; for we also forgive every one that is indebted to us. And lead us not into temptation; but deliver us from evil.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And he said unto them, Which of you shall have a friend, and shall go unto him at midnight, and say unto him, Friend, lend me three loaves;</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> For a friend of mine in his journey is come to me, and I have nothing to set before him?</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And he from within shall answer and say, Trouble me not: the door is now shut, and my children are with me in bed; I cannot rise and give thee.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> I say unto you, Though he will not rise and give him, because he is his friend, yet because of his importunity he will rise and give him as many as he needeth.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And I say unto you, Ask, and it shall be given you; seek, and ye shall find; knock, and it shall be opened unto you.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> For every one that asketh receiveth; and he that seeketh findeth; and to him that knocketh it shall be opened.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> If a son shall ask bread of any of you that is a father, will he give him a stone? or if he ask a fish, will he for a fish give him a serpent?</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Or if he shall ask an egg, will he offer him a scorpion?</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> If ye then, being evil, know how to give good gifts unto your children: how much more shall your heavenly Father give the Holy Spirit to them that ask him?</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And he was casting out a devil, and it was dumb. And it came to pass, when the devil was gone out, the dumb spake; and the people wondered.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> But some of them said, He casteth out devils through Beelzebub the chief of the devils.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And others, tempting him, sought of him a sign from heaven.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> But he, knowing their thoughts, said unto them, Every kingdom divided against itself is brought to desolation; and a house divided against a house falleth.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> If Satan also be divided against himself, how shall his kingdom stand? because ye say that I cast out devils through Beelzebub.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And if I by Beelzebub cast out devils, by whom do your sons cast them out? therefore shall they be your judges.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> But if I with the finger of God cast out devils, no doubt the kingdom of God is come upon you.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> When a strong man armed keepeth his palace, his goods are in peace:</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> But when a stronger than he shall come upon him, and overcome him, he taketh from him all his armour wherein he trusted, and divideth his spoils.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> He that is not with me is against me: and he that gathereth not with me scattereth.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> When the unclean spirit is gone out of a man, he walketh through dry places, seeking rest; and finding none, he saith, I will return unto my house whence I came out.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And when he cometh, he findeth it swept and garnished.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Then goeth he, and taketh to him seven other spirits more wicked than himself; and they enter in, and dwell there: and the last state of that man is worse than the first.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And it came to pass, as he spake these things, a certain woman of the company lifted up her voice, and said unto him, Blessed is the womb that bare thee, and the paps which thou hast sucked.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> But he said, Yea rather, blessed are they that hear the word of God, and keep it.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And when the people were gathered thick together, he began to say, This is an evil generation: they seek a sign; and there shall no sign be given it, but the sign of Jonas the prophet.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> For as Jonas was a sign unto the Ninevites, so shall also the Son of man be to this generation.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> The queen of the south shall rise up in the judgment with the men of this generation, and condemn them: for she came from the utmost parts of the earth to hear the wisdom of Solomon; and, behold, a greater than Solomon is here.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> The men of Nineve shall rise up in the judgment with this generation, and shall condemn it: for they repented at the preaching of Jonas; and, behold, a greater than Jonas is here.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> No man, when he hath lighted a candle, putteth it in a secret place, neither under a bushel, but on a candlestick, that they which come in may see the light.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> The light of the body is the eye: therefore when thine eye is single, thy whole body also is full of light; but when thine eye is evil, thy body also is full of darkness.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> Take heed therefore that the light which is in thee be not darkness.</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> If thy whole body therefore be full of light, having no part dark, the whole shall be full of light, as when the bright shining of a candle doth give thee light.</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> And as he spake, a certain Pharisee besought him to dine with him: and he went in, and sat down to meat.</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> And when the Pharisee saw it, he marvelled that he had not first washed before dinner.</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> And the Lord said unto him, Now do ye Pharisees make clean the outside of the cup and the platter; but your inward part is full of ravening and wickedness.</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> Ye fools, did not he that made that which is without make that which is within also?</p>

<p id="v41" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">41</span> But rather give alms of such things as ye have; and, behold, all things are clean unto you.</p>

<p id="v42" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">42</span> But woe unto you, Pharisees! for ye tithe mint and rue and all manner of herbs, and pass over judgment and the love of God: these ought ye to have done, and not to leave the other undone.</p>

<p id="v43" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">43</span> Woe unto you, Pharisees! for ye love the uppermost seats in the synagogues, and greetings in the markets.</p>

<p id="v44" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">44</span> Woe unto you, scribes and Pharisees, hypocrites! for ye are as graves which appear not, and the men that walk over them are not aware of them.</p>

<p id="v45" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">45</span> Then answered one of the lawyers, and said unto him, Master, thus saying thou reproachest us also.</p>

<p id="v46" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">46</span> And he said, Woe unto you also, ye lawyers! for ye lade men with burdens grievous to be borne, and ye yourselves touch not the burdens with one of your fingers.</p>

<p id="v47" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">47</span> Woe unto you! for ye build the sepulchres of the prophets, and your fathers killed them.</p>

<p id="v48" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">48</span> Truly ye bear witness that ye allow the deeds of your fathers: for they indeed killed them, and ye build their sepulchres.</p>

<p id="v49" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">49</span> Therefore also said the wisdom of God, I will send them prophets and apostles, and some of them they shall slay and persecute:</p>

<p id="v50" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">50</span> That the blood of all the prophets, which was shed from the foundation of the world, may be required of this generation;</p>

<p id="v51" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">51</span> From the blood of Abel unto the blood of Zacharias, which perished between the altar and the temple: verily I say unto you, It shall be required of this generation.</p>

<p id="v52" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">52</span> Woe unto you, lawyers! for ye have taken away the key of knowledge: ye entered not in yourselves, and them that were entering in ye hindered.</p>

<p id="v53" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">53</span> And as he said these things unto them, the scribes and the Pharisees began to urge him vehemently, and to provoke him to speak of many things:</p>

<p id="v54" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">54</span> Laying wait for him, and seeking to catch something out of his mouth, that they might accuse him.</p>
