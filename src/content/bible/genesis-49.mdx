## Genesis 49

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And Jacob called unto his sons, and said, Gather yourselves together, that I may tell you that which shall befall you in the last days.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Gather yourselves together, and hear, ye sons of Jacob; and hearken unto Israel your father.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Reuben, thou art my firstborn, my might, and the beginning of my strength, the excellency of dignity, and the excellency of power:</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Unstable as water, thou shalt not excel; because thou wentest up to thy father's bed; then defiledst thou it: he went up to my couch.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Simeon and Levi are brethren; instruments of cruelty are in their habitations.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> O my soul, come not thou into their secret; unto their assembly, mine honour, be not thou united: for in their anger they slew a man, and in their selfwill they digged down a wall.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Cursed be their anger, for it was fierce; and their wrath, for it was cruel: I will divide them in Jacob, and scatter them in Israel.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Judah, thou art he whom thy brethren shall praise: thy hand shall be in the neck of thine enemies; thy father's children shall bow down before thee.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Judah is a lion's whelp: from the prey, my son, thou art gone up: he stooped down, he couched as a lion, and as an old lion; who shall rouse him up?</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> The sceptre shall not depart from Judah, nor a lawgiver from between his feet, until Shiloh come; and unto him shall the gathering of the people be.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Binding his foal unto the vine, and his ass's colt unto the choice vine; he washed his garments in wine, and his clothes in the blood of grapes:</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> His eyes shall be red with wine, and his teeth white with milk.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Zebulun shall dwell at the haven of the sea; and he shall be for an haven of ships; and his border shall be unto Zidon.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Issachar is a strong ass couching down between two burdens:</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And he saw that rest was good, and the land that it was pleasant; and bowed his shoulder to bear, and became a servant unto tribute.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Dan shall judge his people, as one of the tribes of Israel.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Dan shall be a serpent by the way, an adder in the path, that biteth the horse heels, so that his rider shall fall backward.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> I have waited for thy salvation, O LORD.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Gad, a troop shall overcome him: but he shall overcome at the last.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Out of Asher his bread shall be fat, and he shall yield royal dainties.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Naphtali is a hind let loose: he giveth goodly words.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Joseph is a fruitful bough, even a fruitful bough by a well; whose branches run over the wall:</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> The archers have sorely grieved him, and shot at him, and hated him:</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> But his bow abode in strength, and the arms of his hands were made strong by the hands of the mighty God of Jacob; (from thence is the shepherd, the stone of Israel:)</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Even by the God of thy father, who shall help thee; and by the Almighty, who shall bless thee with blessings of heaven above, blessings of the deep that lieth under, blessings of the breasts, and of the womb:</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> The blessings of thy father have prevailed above the blessings of my progenitors unto the utmost bound of the everlasting hills: they shall be on the head of Joseph, and on the crown of the head of him that was separate from his brethren.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Benjamin shall ravin as a wolf: in the morning he shall devour the prey, and at night he shall divide the spoil.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> All these are the twelve tribes of Israel: and this is it that their father spake unto them, and blessed them; every one according to his blessing he blessed them.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And he charged them, and said unto them, I am to be gathered unto my people: bury me with my fathers in the cave that is in the field of Ephron the Hittite,</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> In the cave that is in the field of Machpelah, which is before Mamre, in the land of Canaan, which Abraham bought with the field of Ephron the Hittite for a possession of a buryingplace.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> There they buried Abraham and Sarah his wife; there they buried Isaac and Rebekah his wife; and there I buried Leah.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> The purchase of the field and of the cave that is therein was from the children of Heth.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> And when Jacob had made an end of commanding his sons, he gathered up his feet into the bed, and yielded up the ghost, and was gathered unto his people.</p>
