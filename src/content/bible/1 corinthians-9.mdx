## 1 Corinthians 9

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Am I not an apostle? am I not free? have I not seen Jesus Christ our Lord? are not ye my work in the Lord?</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> If I be not an apostle unto others, yet doubtless I am to you: for the seal of mine apostleship are ye in the Lord.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Mine answer to them that do examine me is this,</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Have we not power to eat and to drink?</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Have we not power to lead about a sister, a wife, as well as other apostles, and as the brethren of the Lord, and Cephas?</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Or I only and Barnabas, have not we power to forbear working?</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Who goeth a warfare any time at his own charges? who planteth a vineyard, and eateth not of the fruit thereof? or who feedeth a flock, and eateth not of the milk of the flock?</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Say I these things as a man? or saith not the law the same also?</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> For it is written in the law of Moses, Thou shalt not muzzle the mouth of the ox that treadeth out the corn. Doth God take care for oxen?</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Or saith he it altogether for our sakes? For our sakes, no doubt, this is written: that he that ploweth should plow in hope; and that he that thresheth in hope should be partaker of his hope.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> If we have sown unto you spiritual things, is it a great thing if we shall reap your carnal things?</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> If others be partakers of this power over you, are not we rather? Nevertheless we have not used this power; but suffer all things, lest we should hinder the gospel of Christ.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Do ye not know that they which minister about holy things live of the things of the temple? and they which wait at the altar are partakers with the altar?</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Even so hath the Lord ordained that they which preach the gospel should live of the gospel.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> But I have used none of these things: neither have I written these things, that it should be so done unto me: for it were better for me to die, than that any man should make my glorying void.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> For though I preach the gospel, I have nothing to glory of: for necessity is laid upon me; yea, woe is unto me, if I preach not the gospel!</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> For if I do this thing willingly, I have a reward: but if against my will, a dispensation of the gospel is committed unto me.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> What is my reward then? Verily that, when I preach the gospel, I may make the gospel of Christ without charge, that I abuse not my power in the gospel.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> For though I be free from all men, yet have I made myself servant unto all, that I might gain the more.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And unto the Jews I became as a Jew, that I might gain the Jews; to them that are under the law, as under the law, that I might gain them that are under the law;</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> To them that are without law, as without law, (being not without law to God, but under the law to Christ,) that I might gain them that are without law.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> To the weak became I as weak, that I might gain the weak: I am made all things to all men, that I might by all means save some.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And this I do for the gospel's sake, that I might be partaker thereof with you.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Know ye not that they which run in a race run all, but one receiveth the prize? So run, that ye may obtain.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And every man that striveth for the mastery is temperate in all things. Now they do it to obtain a corruptible crown; but we an incorruptible.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> I therefore so run, not as uncertainly; so fight I, not as one that beateth the air:</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> But I keep under my body, and bring it into subjection: lest that by any means, when I have preached to others, I myself should be a castaway.</p>
