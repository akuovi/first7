## Ezekiel 3

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Moreover he said unto me, Son of man, eat that thou findest; eat this roll, and go speak unto the house of Israel.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> So I opened my mouth, and he caused me to eat that roll.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And he said unto me, Son of man, cause thy belly to eat, and fill thy bowels with this roll that I give thee. Then did I eat it; and it was in my mouth as honey for sweetness.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And he said unto me, Son of man, go, get thee unto the house of Israel, and speak with my words unto them.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> For thou art not sent to a people of a strange speech and of an hard language, but to the house of Israel;</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Not to many people of a strange speech and of an hard language, whose words thou canst not understand. Surely, had I sent thee to them, they would have hearkened unto thee.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> But the house of Israel will not hearken unto thee; for they will not hearken unto me: for all the house of Israel are impudent and hardhearted.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Behold, I have made thy face strong against their faces, and thy forehead strong against their foreheads.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> As an adamant harder than flint have I made thy forehead: fear them not, neither be dismayed at their looks, though they be a rebellious house.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Moreover he said unto me, Son of man, all my words that I shall speak unto thee receive in thine heart, and hear with thine ears.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And go, get thee to them of the captivity, unto the children of thy people, and speak unto them, and tell them, Thus saith the Lord GOD; whether they will hear, or whether they will forbear.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Then the spirit took me up, and I heard behind me a voice of a great rushing, saying, Blessed be the glory of the LORD from his place.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> I heard also the noise of the wings of the living creatures that touched one another, and the noise of the wheels over against them, and a noise of a great rushing.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> So the spirit lifted me up, and took me away, and I went in bitterness, in the heat of my spirit; but the hand of the LORD was strong upon me.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Then I came to them of the captivity at Tel-abib, that dwelt by the river of Chebar, and I sat where they sat, and remained there astonished among them seven days.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And it came to pass at the end of seven days, that the word of the LORD came unto me, saying,</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Son of man, I have made thee a watchman unto the house of Israel: therefore hear the word at my mouth, and give them warning from me.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> When I say unto the wicked, Thou shalt surely die; and thou givest him not warning, nor speakest to warn the wicked from his wicked way, to save his life; the same wicked man shall die in his iniquity; but his blood will I require at thine hand.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Yet if thou warn the wicked, and he turn not from his wickedness, nor from his wicked way, he shall die in his iniquity; but thou hast delivered thy soul.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Again, When a righteous man doth turn from his righteousness, and commit iniquity, and I lay a stumblingblock before him, he shall die: because thou hast not given him warning, he shall die in his sin, and his righteousness which he hath done shall not be remembered; but his blood will I require at thine hand.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Nevertheless if thou warn the righteous man, that the righteous sin not, and he doth not sin, he shall surely live, because he is warned; also thou hast delivered thy soul.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And the hand of the LORD was there upon me; and he said unto me, Arise, go forth into the plain, and I will there talk with thee.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Then I arose, and went forth into the plain: and, behold, the glory of the LORD stood there, as the glory which I saw by the river of Chebar: and I fell on my face.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Then the spirit entered into me, and set me upon my feet, and spake with me, and said unto me, Go, shut thyself within thine house.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> But thou, O son of man, behold, they shall put bands upon thee, and shall bind thee with them, and thou shalt not go out among them:</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And I will make thy tongue cleave to the roof of thy mouth, that thou shalt be dumb, and shalt not be to them a reprover: for they are a rebellious house.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> But when I speak with thee, I will open thy mouth, and thou shalt say unto them, Thus saith the Lord GOD; He that heareth, let him hear; and he that forbeareth, let him forbear: for they are a rebellious house.</p>
