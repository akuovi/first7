## Colossians 3

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> If ye then be risen with Christ, seek those things which are above, where Christ sitteth on the right hand of God.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Set your affection on things above, not on things on the earth.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For ye are dead, and your life is hid with Christ in God.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> When Christ, who is our life, shall appear, then shall ye also appear with him in glory.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Mortify therefore your members which are upon the earth; fornication, uncleanness, inordinate affection, evil concupiscence, and covetousness, which is idolatry:</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> For which things' sake the wrath of God cometh on the children of disobedience:</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> In the which ye also walked some time, when ye lived in them.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> But now ye also put off all these; anger, wrath, malice, blasphemy, filthy communication out of your mouth.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Lie not one to another, seeing that ye have put off the old man with his deeds;</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And have put on the new man, which is renewed in knowledge after the image of him that created him:</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Where there is neither Greek nor Jew, circumcision nor uncircumcision, Barbarian, Scythian, bond nor free: but Christ is all, and in all.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Put on therefore, as the elect of God, holy and beloved, bowels of mercies, kindness, humbleness of mind, meekness, longsuffering;</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Forbearing one another, and forgiving one another, if any man have a quarrel against any: even as Christ forgave you, so also do ye.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And above all these things put on charity, which is the bond of perfectness.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And let the peace of God rule in your hearts, to the which also ye are called in one body; and be ye thankful.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Let the word of Christ dwell in you richly in all wisdom; teaching and admonishing one another in psalms and hymns and spiritual songs, singing with grace in your hearts to the Lord.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And whatsoever ye do in word or deed, do all in the name of the Lord Jesus, giving thanks to God and the Father by him.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Wives, submit yourselves unto your own husbands, as it is fit in the Lord.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Husbands, love your wives, and be not bitter against them.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Children, obey your parents in all things: for this is well pleasing unto the Lord.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Fathers, provoke not your children to anger, lest they be discouraged.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Servants, obey in all things your masters according to the flesh; not with eyeservice, as menpleasers; but in singleness of heart, fearing God:</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And whatsoever ye do, do it heartily, as to the Lord, and not unto men;</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Knowing that of the Lord ye shall receive the reward of the inheritance: for ye serve the Lord Christ.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> But he that doeth wrong shall receive for the wrong which he hath done: and there is no respect of persons.</p>
