## Colossians 4

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Masters, give unto your servants that which is just and equal; knowing that ye also have a Master in heaven.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Continue in prayer, and watch in the same with thanksgiving;</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Withal praying also for us, that God would open unto us a door of utterance, to speak the mystery of Christ, for which I am also in bonds:</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> That I may make it manifest, as I ought to speak.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Walk in wisdom toward them that are without, redeeming the time.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Let your speech be alway with grace, seasoned with salt, that ye may know how ye ought to answer every man.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> All my state shall Tychicus declare unto you, who is a beloved brother, and a faithful minister and fellowservant in the Lord:</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Whom I have sent unto you for the same purpose, that he might know your estate, and comfort your hearts;</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> With Onesimus, a faithful and beloved brother, who is one of you. They shall make known unto you all things which are done here.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Aristarchus my fellowprisoner saluteth you, and Marcus, sister's son to Barnabas, (touching whom ye received commandments: if he come unto you, receive him;)</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And Jesus, which is called Justus, who are of the circumcision. These only are my fellowworkers unto the kingdom of God, which have been a comfort unto me.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Epaphras, who is one of you, a servant of Christ, saluteth you, always labouring fervently for you in prayers, that ye may stand perfect and complete in all the will of God.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> For I bear him record, that he hath a great zeal for you, and them that are in Laodicea, and them in Hierapolis.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Luke, the beloved physician, and Demas, greet you.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Salute the brethren which are in Laodicea, and Nymphas, and the church which is in his house.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And when this epistle is read among you, cause that it be read also in the church of the Laodiceans; and that ye likewise read the epistle from Laodicea.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And say to Archippus, Take heed to the ministry which thou hast received in the Lord, that thou fulfil it.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> The salutation by the hand of me Paul. Remember my bonds. Grace be with you. Amen.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Written from Rome to the Colossians by Tychicus and Onesimus.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> COLOSSIANS</p>
