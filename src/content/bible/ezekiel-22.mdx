## Ezekiel 22

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Moreover the word of the LORD came unto me, saying,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Now, thou son of man, wilt thou judge, wilt thou judge the bloody city? yea, thou shalt shew her all her abominations.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Then say thou, Thus saith the Lord GOD, The city sheddeth blood in the midst of it, that her time may come, and maketh idols against herself to defile herself.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Thou art become guilty in thy blood that thou hast shed; and hast defiled thyself in thine idols which thou hast made; and thou hast caused thy days to draw near, and art come even unto thy years: therefore have I made thee a reproach unto the heathen, and a mocking to all countries.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Those that be near, and those that be far from thee, shall mock thee, which art infamous and much vexed.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Behold, the princes of Israel, every one were in thee to their power to shed blood.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> In thee have they set light by father and mother: in the midst of thee have they dealt by oppression with the stranger: in thee have they vexed the fatherless and the widow.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Thou hast despised mine holy things, and hast profaned my sabbaths.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> In thee are men that carry tales to shed blood: and in thee they eat upon the mountains: in the midst of thee they commit lewdness.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> In thee have they discovered their fathers' nakedness: in thee have they humbled her that was set apart for pollution.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And one hath committed abomination with his neighbour's wife; and another hath lewdly defiled his daughter in law; and another in thee hath humbled his sister, his father's daughter.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> In thee have they taken gifts to shed blood; thou hast taken usury and increase, and thou hast greedily gained of thy neighbours by extortion, and hast forgotten me, saith the Lord GOD.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Behold, therefore I have smitten mine hand at thy dishonest gain which thou hast made, and at thy blood which hath been in the midst of thee.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Can thine heart endure, or can thine hands be strong, in the days that I shall deal with thee? I the LORD have spoken it, and will do it.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And I will scatter thee among the heathen, and disperse thee in the countries, and will consume thy filthiness out of thee.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And thou shalt take thine inheritance in thyself in the sight of the heathen, and thou shalt know that I am the LORD.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And the word of the LORD came unto me, saying,</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Son of man, the house of Israel is to me become dross: all they are brass, and tin, and iron, and lead, in the midst of the furnace; they are even the dross of silver.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Therefore thus saith the Lord GOD; Because ye are all become dross, behold, therefore I will gather you into the midst of Jerusalem.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> As they gather silver, and brass, and iron, and lead, and tin, into the midst of the furnace, to blow the fire upon it, to melt it; so will I gather you in mine anger and in my fury, and I will leave you there, and melt you.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Yea, I will gather you, and blow upon you in the fire of my wrath, and ye shall be melted in the midst thereof.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> As silver is melted in the midst of the furnace, so shall ye be melted in the midst thereof; and ye shall know that I the LORD have poured out my fury upon you.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And the word of the LORD came unto me, saying,</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Son of man, say unto her, Thou art the land that is not cleansed, nor rained upon in the day of indignation.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> There is a conspiracy of her prophets in the midst thereof, like a roaring lion ravening the prey; they have devoured souls; they have taken the treasure and precious things; they have made her many widows in the midst thereof.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Her priests have violated my law, and have profaned mine holy things: they have put no difference between the holy and profane, neither have they shewed difference between the unclean and the clean, and have hid their eyes from my sabbaths, and I am profaned among them.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Her princes in the midst thereof are like wolves ravening the prey, to shed blood, and to destroy souls, to get dishonest gain.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And her prophets have daubed them with untempered morter, seeing vanity, and divining lies unto them, saying, Thus saith the Lord GOD, when the LORD hath not spoken.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> The people of the land have used oppression, and exercised robbery, and have vexed the poor and needy: yea, they have oppressed the stranger wrongfully.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And I sought for a man among them, that should make up the hedge, and stand in the gap before me for the land, that I should not destroy it: but I found none.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Therefore have I poured out mine indignation upon them; I have consumed them with the fire of my wrath: their own way have I recompensed upon their heads, saith the Lord GOD.</p>
