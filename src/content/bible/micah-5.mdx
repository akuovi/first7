## Micah 5

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Now gather thyself in troops, O daughter of troops: he hath laid siege against us: they shall smite the judge of Israel with a rod upon the cheek.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> But thou, Beth-lehem Ephratah, though thou be little among the thousands of Judah, yet out of thee shall he come forth unto me that is to be ruler in Israel; whose goings forth have been from of old, from everlasting.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Therefore will he give them up, until the time that she which travaileth hath brought forth: then the remnant of his brethren shall return unto the children of Israel.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And he shall stand and feed in the strength of the LORD, in the majesty of the name of the LORD his God; and they shall abide: for now shall he be great unto the ends of the earth.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And this man shall be the peace, when the Assyrian shall come into our land: and when he shall tread in our palaces, then shall we raise against him seven shepherds, and eight principal men.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And they shall waste the land of Assyria with the sword, and the land of Nimrod in the entrances thereof: thus shall he deliver us from the Assyrian, when he cometh into our land, and when he treadeth within our borders.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And the remnant of Jacob shall be in the midst of many people as a dew from the LORD, as the showers upon the grass, that tarrieth not for man, nor waiteth for the sons of men.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And the remnant of Jacob shall be among the Gentiles in the midst of many people as a lion among the beasts of the forest, as a young lion among the flocks of sheep: who, if he go through, both treadeth down, and teareth in pieces, and none can deliver.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Thine hand shall be lifted up upon thine adversaries, and all thine enemies shall be cut off.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And it shall come to pass in that day, saith the LORD, that I will cut off thy horses out of the midst of thee, and I will destroy thy chariots:</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And I will cut off the cities of thy land, and throw down all thy strong holds:</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And I will cut off witchcrafts out of thine hand; and thou shalt have no more soothsayers:</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Thy graven images also will I cut off, and thy standing images out of the midst of thee; and thou shalt no more worship the work of thine hands.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And I will pluck up thy groves out of the midst of thee: so will I destroy thy cities.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And I will execute vengeance in anger and fury upon the heathen, such as they have not heard.</p>
