## Proverbs 28

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> The wicked flee when no man pursueth: but the righteous are bold as a lion.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> For the transgression of a land many are the princes thereof: but by a man of understanding and knowledge the state thereof shall be prolonged.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> A poor man that oppresseth the poor is like a sweeping rain which leaveth no food.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> They that forsake the law praise the wicked: but such as keep the law contend with them.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Evil men understand not judgment: but they that seek the LORD understand all things.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Better is the poor that walketh in his uprightness, than he that is perverse in his ways, though he be rich.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Whoso keepeth the law is a wise son: but he that is a companion of riotous men shameth his father.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> He that by usury and unjust gain increaseth his substance, he shall gather it for him that will pity the poor.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> He that turneth away his ear from hearing the law, even his prayer shall be abomination.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Whoso causeth the righteous to go astray in an evil way, he shall fall himself into his own pit: but the upright shall have good things in possession.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> The rich man is wise in his own conceit; but the poor that hath understanding searcheth him out.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> When righteous men do rejoice, there is great glory: but when the wicked rise, a man is hidden.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> He that covereth his sins shall not prosper: but whoso confesseth and forsaketh them shall have mercy.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Happy is the man that feareth alway: but he that hardeneth his heart shall fall into mischief.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> As a roaring lion, and a ranging bear; so is a wicked ruler over the poor people.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> The prince that wanteth understanding is also a great oppressor: but he that hateth covetousness shall prolong his days.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> A man that doeth violence to the blood of any person shall flee to the pit; let no man stay him.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Whoso walketh uprightly shall be saved: but he that is perverse in his ways shall fall at once.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> He that tilleth his land shall have plenty of bread: but he that followeth after vain persons shall have poverty enough.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> A faithful man shall abound with blessings: but he that maketh haste to be rich shall not be innocent.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> To have respect of persons is not good: for for a piece of bread that man will transgress.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> He that hasteth to be rich hath an evil eye, and considereth not that poverty shall come upon him.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> He that rebuketh a man afterwards shall find more favour than he that flattereth with the tongue.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Whoso robbeth his father or his mother, and saith, It is no transgression; the same is the companion of a destroyer.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> He that is of a proud heart stirreth up strife: but he that putteth his trust in the LORD shall be made fat.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> He that trusteth in his own heart is a fool: but whoso walketh wisely, he shall be delivered.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> He that giveth unto the poor shall not lack: but he that hideth his eyes shall have many a curse.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> When the wicked rise, men hide themselves: but when they perish, the righteous increase.</p>
