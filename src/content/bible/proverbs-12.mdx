## Proverbs 12

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Whoso loveth instruction loveth knowledge: but he that hateth reproof is brutish.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> A good man obtaineth favour of the LORD: but a man of wicked devices will he condemn.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> A man shall not be established by wickedness: but the root of the righteous shall not be moved.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> A virtuous woman is a crown to her husband: but she that maketh ashamed is as rottenness in his bones.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> The thoughts of the righteous are right: but the counsels of the wicked are deceit.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> The words of the wicked are to lie in wait for blood: but the mouth of the upright shall deliver them.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> The wicked are overthrown, and are not: but the house of the righteous shall stand.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> A man shall be commended according to his wisdom: but he that is of a perverse heart shall be despised.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> He that is despised, and hath a servant, is better than he that honoureth himself, and lacketh bread.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> A righteous man regardeth the life of his beast: but the tender mercies of the wicked are cruel.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> He that tilleth his land shall be satisfied with bread: but he that followeth vain persons is void of understanding.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> The wicked desireth the net of evil men: but the root of the righteous yieldeth fruit.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> The wicked is snared by the transgression of his lips: but the just shall come out of trouble.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> A man shall be satisfied with good by the fruit of his mouth: and the recompence of a man's hands shall be rendered unto him.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> The way of a fool is right in his own eyes: but he that hearkeneth unto counsel is wise.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> A fool's wrath is presently known: but a prudent man covereth shame.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> He that speaketh truth sheweth forth righteousness: but a false witness deceit.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> There is that speaketh like the piercings of a sword: but the tongue of the wise is health.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> The lip of truth shall be established for ever: but a lying tongue is but for a moment.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Deceit is in the heart of them that imagine evil: but to the counsellors of peace is joy.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> There shall no evil happen to the just: but the wicked shall be filled with mischief.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Lying lips are abomination to the LORD: but they that deal truly are his delight.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> A prudent man concealeth knowledge: but the heart of fools proclaimeth foolishness.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> The hand of the diligent shall bear rule: but the slothful shall be under tribute.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Heaviness in the heart of man maketh it stoop: but a good word maketh it glad.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> The righteous is more excellent than his neighbour: but the way of the wicked seduceth them.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> The slothful man roasteth not that which he took in hunting: but the substance of a diligent man is precious.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> In the way of righteousness is life; and in the pathway thereof there is no death.</p>
