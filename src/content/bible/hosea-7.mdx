## Hosea 7

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> When I would have healed Israel, then the iniquity of Ephraim was discovered, and the wickedness of Samaria: for they commit falsehood; and the thief cometh in, and the troop of robbers spoileth without.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And they consider not in their hearts that I remember all their wickedness: now their own doings have beset them about; they are before my face.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> They make the king glad with their wickedness, and the princes with their lies.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> They are all adulterers, as an oven heated by the baker, who ceaseth from raising after he hath kneaded the dough, until it be leavened.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> In the day of our king the princes have made him sick with bottles of wine; he stretched out his hand with scorners.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> For they have made ready their heart like an oven, whiles they lie in wait: their baker sleepeth all the night; in the morning it burneth as a flaming fire.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> They are all hot as an oven, and have devoured their judges; all their kings are fallen: there is none among them that calleth unto me.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Ephraim, he hath mixed himself among the people; Ephraim is a cake not turned.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Strangers have devoured his strength, and he knoweth it not: yea, gray hairs are here and there upon him, yet he knoweth not.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And the pride of Israel testifieth to his face: and they do not return to the LORD their God, nor seek him for all this.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Ephraim also is like a silly dove without heart: they call to Egypt, they go to Assyria.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> When they shall go, I will spread my net upon them; I will bring them down as the fowls of the heaven; I will chastise them, as their congregation hath heard.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Woe unto them! for they have fled from me: destruction unto them! because they have transgressed against me: though I have redeemed them, yet they have spoken lies against me.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And they have not cried unto me with their heart, when they howled upon their beds: they assemble themselves for corn and wine, and they rebel against me.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Though I have bound and strengthened their arms, yet do they imagine mischief against me.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> They return, but not to the most High: they are like a deceitful bow: their princes shall fall by the sword for the rage of their tongue: this shall be their derision in the land of Egypt.</p>
