## Isaiah 61

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> The Spirit of the Lord GOD is upon me; because the LORD hath anointed me to preach good tidings unto the meek; he hath sent me to bind up the brokenhearted, to proclaim liberty to the captives, and the opening of the prison to them that are bound;</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> To proclaim the acceptable year of the LORD, and the day of vengeance of our God; to comfort all that mourn;</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> To appoint unto them that mourn in Zion, to give unto them beauty for ashes, the oil of joy for mourning, the garment of praise for the spirit of heaviness; that they might be called trees of righteousness, the planting of the LORD, that he might be glorified.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And they shall build the old wastes, they shall raise up the former desolations, and they shall repair the waste cities, the desolations of many generations.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And strangers shall stand and feed your flocks, and the sons of the alien shall be your plowmen and your vinedressers.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> But ye shall be named the Priests of the LORD: men shall call you the Ministers of our God: ye shall eat the riches of the Gentiles, and in their glory shall ye boast yourselves.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> For your shame ye shall have double; and for confusion they shall rejoice in their portion: therefore in their land they shall possess the double: everlasting joy shall be unto them.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> For I the LORD love judgment, I hate robbery for burnt offering; and I will direct their work in truth, and I will make an everlasting covenant with them.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And their seed shall be known among the Gentiles, and their offspring among the people: all that see them shall acknowledge them, that they are the seed which the LORD hath blessed.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> I will greatly rejoice in the LORD, my soul shall be joyful in my God; for he hath clothed me with the garments of salvation, he hath covered me with the robe of righteousness, as a bridegroom decketh himself with ornaments, and as a bride adorneth herself with her jewels.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> For as the earth bringeth forth her bud, and as the garden causeth the things that are sown in it to spring forth; so the Lord GOD will cause righteousness and praise to spring forth before all the nations.</p>
