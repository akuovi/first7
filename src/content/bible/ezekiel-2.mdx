## Ezekiel 2

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And he said unto me, Son of man, stand upon thy feet, and I will speak unto thee.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And the spirit entered into me when he spake unto me, and set me upon my feet, that I heard him that spake unto me.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And he said unto me, Son of man, I send thee to the children of Israel, to a rebellious nation that hath rebelled against me: they and their fathers have transgressed against me, even unto this very day.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> For they are impudent children and stiffhearted. I do send thee unto them; and thou shalt say unto them, Thus saith the Lord GOD.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And they, whether they will hear, or whether they will forbear, (for they are a rebellious house,) yet shall know that there hath been a prophet among them.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And thou, son of man, be not afraid of them, neither be afraid of their words, though briers and thorns be with thee, and thou dost dwell among scorpions: be not afraid of their words, nor be dismayed at their looks, though they be a rebellious house.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And thou shalt speak my words unto them, whether they will hear, or whether they will forbear: for they are most rebellious.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> But thou, son of man, hear what I say unto thee; Be not thou rebellious like that rebellious house: open thy mouth, and eat that I give thee.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And when I looked, behold, an hand was sent unto me; and, lo, a roll of a book was therein;</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And he spread it before me; and it was written within and without: and there was written therein lamentations, and mourning, and woe.</p>
