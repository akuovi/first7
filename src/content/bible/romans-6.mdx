## Romans 6

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> What shall we say then? Shall we continue in sin, that grace may abound?</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> God forbid. How shall we, that are dead to sin, live any longer therein?</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Know ye not, that so many of us as were baptized into Jesus Christ were baptized into his death?</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Therefore we are buried with him by baptism into death: that like as Christ was raised up from the dead by the glory of the Father, even so we also should walk in newness of life.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> For if we have been planted together in the likeness of his death, we shall be also in the likeness of his resurrection:</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Knowing this, that our old man is crucified with him, that the body of sin might be destroyed, that henceforth we should not serve sin.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> For he that is dead is freed from sin.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Now if we be dead with Christ, we believe that we shall also live with him:</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Knowing that Christ being raised from the dead dieth no more; death hath no more dominion over him.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> For in that he died, he died unto sin once: but in that he liveth, he liveth unto God.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Likewise reckon ye also yourselves to be dead indeed unto sin, but alive unto God through Jesus Christ our Lord.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Let not sin therefore reign in your mortal body, that ye should obey it in the lusts thereof.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Neither yield ye your members as instruments of unrighteousness unto sin: but yield yourselves unto God, as those that are alive from the dead, and your members as instruments of righteousness unto God.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> For sin shall not have dominion over you: for ye are not under the law, but under grace.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> What then? shall we sin, because we are not under the law, but under grace? God forbid.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Know ye not, that to whom ye yield yourselves servants to obey, his servants ye are to whom ye obey; whether of sin unto death, or of obedience unto righteousness?</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> But God be thanked, that ye were the servants of sin, but ye have obeyed from the heart that form of doctrine which was delivered you.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Being then made free from sin, ye became the servants of righteousness.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> I speak after the manner of men because of the infirmity of your flesh: for as ye have yielded your members servants to uncleanness and to iniquity unto iniquity; even so now yield your members servants to righteousness unto holiness.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> For when ye were the servants of sin, ye were free from righteousness.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> What fruit had ye then in those things whereof ye are now ashamed? for the end of those things is death.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> But now being made free from sin, and become servants to God, ye have your fruit unto holiness, and the end everlasting life.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> For the wages of sin is death; but the gift of God is eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord.</p>
