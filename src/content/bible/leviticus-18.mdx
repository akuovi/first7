## Leviticus 18

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Speak unto the children of Israel, and say unto them, I am the LORD your God.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> After the doings of the land of Egypt, wherein ye dwelt, shall ye not do: and after the doings of the land of Canaan, whither I bring you, shall ye not do: neither shall ye walk in their ordinances.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Ye shall do my judgments, and keep mine ordinances, to walk therein: I am the LORD your God.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Ye shall therefore keep my statutes, and my judgments: which if a man do, he shall live in them: I am the LORD.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> None of you shall approach to any that is near of kin to him, to uncover their nakedness: I am the LORD.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> The nakedness of thy father, or the nakedness of thy mother, shalt thou not uncover: she is thy mother; thou shalt not uncover her nakedness.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> The nakedness of thy father's wife shalt thou not uncover: it is thy father's nakedness.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> The nakedness of thy sister, the daughter of thy father, or daughter of thy mother, whether she be born at home, or born abroad, even their nakedness thou shalt not uncover.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> The nakedness of thy son's daughter, or of thy daughter's daughter, even their nakedness thou shalt not uncover: for their's is thine own nakedness.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> The nakedness of thy father's wife's daughter, begotten of thy father, she is thy sister, thou shalt not uncover her nakedness.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Thou shalt not uncover the nakedness of thy father's sister: she is thy father's near kinswoman.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Thou shalt not uncover the nakedness of thy mother's sister: for she is thy mother's near kinswoman.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Thou shalt not uncover the nakedness of thy father's brother, thou shalt not approach to his wife: she is thine aunt.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Thou shalt not uncover the nakedness of thy daughter in law: she is thy son's wife; thou shalt not uncover her nakedness.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Thou shalt not uncover the nakedness of thy brother's wife: it is thy brother's nakedness.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Thou shalt not uncover the nakedness of a woman and her daughter, neither shalt thou take her son's daughter, or her daughter's daughter, to uncover her nakedness; for they are her near kinswomen: it is wickedness.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Neither shalt thou take a wife to her sister, to vex her, to uncover her nakedness, beside the other in her life time.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Also thou shalt not approach unto a woman to uncover her nakedness, as long as she is put apart for her uncleanness.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Moreover thou shalt not lie carnally with thy neighbour's wife, to defile thyself with her.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And thou shalt not let any of thy seed pass through the fire to Molech, neither shalt thou profane the name of thy God: I am the LORD.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Thou shalt not lie with mankind, as with womankind: it is abomination.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Neither shalt thou lie with any beast to defile thyself therewith: neither shall any woman stand before a beast to lie down thereto: it is confusion.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Defile not ye yourselves in any of these things: for in all these the nations are defiled which I cast out before you:</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And the land is defiled: therefore I do visit the iniquity thereof upon it, and the land itself vomiteth out her inhabitants.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Ye shall therefore keep my statutes and my judgments, and shall not commit any of these abominations; neither any of your own nation, nor any stranger that sojourneth among you:</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> (For all these abominations have the men of the land done, which were before you, and the land is defiled;)</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> That the land spue not you out also, when ye defile it, as it spued out the nations that were before you.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> For whosoever shall commit any of these abominations, even the souls that commit them shall be cut off from among their people.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Therefore shall ye keep mine ordinance, that ye commit not any one of these abominable customs, which were committed before you, and that ye defile not yourselves therein: I am the LORD your God.</p>
