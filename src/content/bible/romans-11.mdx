## Romans 11

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> I say then, Hath God cast away his people? God forbid. For I also am an Israelite, of the seed of Abraham, of the tribe of Benjamin.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> God hath not cast away his people which he foreknew. Wot ye not what the scripture saith of Elias? how he maketh intercession to God against Israel, saying,</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Lord, they have killed thy prophets, and digged down thine altars; and I am left alone, and they seek my life.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> But what saith the answer of God unto him? I have reserved to myself seven thousand men, who have not bowed the knee to the image of Baal.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Even so then at this present time also there is a remnant according to the election of grace.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And if by grace, then is it no more of works: otherwise grace is no more grace. But if it be of works, then is it no more grace: otherwise work is no more work.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> What then? Israel hath not obtained that which he seeketh for; but the election hath obtained it, and the rest were blinded</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> (According as it is written, God hath given them the spirit of slumber, eyes that they should not see, and ears that they should not hear;) unto this day.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And David saith, Let their table be made a snare, and a trap, and a stumblingblock, and a recompence unto them:</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Let their eyes be darkened, that they may not see, and bow down their back alway.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> I say then, Have they stumbled that they should fall? God forbid: but rather through their fall salvation is come unto the Gentiles, for to provoke them to jealousy.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Now if the fall of them be the riches of the world, and the diminishing of them the riches of the Gentiles; how much more their fulness?</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> For I speak to you Gentiles, inasmuch as I am the apostle of the Gentiles, I magnify mine office:</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> If by any means I may provoke to emulation them which are my flesh, and might save some of them.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> For if the casting away of them be the reconciling of the world, what shall the receiving of them be, but life from the dead?</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> For if the firstfruit be holy, the lump is also holy: and if the root be holy, so are the branches.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And if some of the branches be broken off, and thou, being a wild olive tree, wert graffed in among them, and with them partakest of the root and fatness of the olive tree;</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Boast not against the branches. But if thou boast, thou bearest not the root, but the root thee.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Thou wilt say then, The branches were broken off, that I might be graffed in.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Well; because of unbelief they were broken off, and thou standest by faith. Be not highminded, but fear:</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> For if God spared not the natural branches, take heed lest he also spare not thee.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Behold therefore the goodness and severity of God: on them which fell, severity; but toward thee, goodness, if thou continue in his goodness: otherwise thou also shalt be cut off.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And they also, if they abide not still in unbelief, shall be graffed in: for God is able to graff them in again.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> For if thou wert cut out of the olive tree which is wild by nature, and wert graffed contrary to nature into a good olive tree: how much more shall these, which be the natural branches, be graffed into their own olive tree?</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> For I would not, brethren, that ye should be ignorant of this mystery, lest ye should be wise in your own conceits; that blindness in part is happened to Israel, until the fulness of the Gentiles be come in.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And so all Israel shall be saved: as it is written, There shall come out of Sion the Deliverer, and shall turn away ungodliness from Jacob:</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> For this is my covenant unto them, when I shall take away their sins.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> As concerning the gospel, they are enemies for your sakes: but as touching the election, they are beloved for the fathers' sakes.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> For the gifts and calling of God are without repentance.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> For as ye in times past have not believed God, yet have now obtained mercy through their unbelief:</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Even so have these also now not believed, that through your mercy they also may obtain mercy.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> For God hath concluded them all in unbelief, that he might have mercy upon all.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> O the depth of the riches both of the wisdom and knowledge of God! how unsearchable are his judgments, and his ways past finding out!</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> For who hath known the mind of the Lord? or who hath been his counsellor?</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> Or who hath first given to him, and it shall be recompensed unto him again?</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> For of him, and through him, and to him, are all things: to whom be glory for ever. Amen.</p>
