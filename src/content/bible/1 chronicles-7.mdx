## 1 Chronicles 7

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Now the sons of Issachar were, Tola, and Puah, Jashub, and Shimron, four.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And the sons of Tola; Uzzi, and Rephaiah, and Jeriel, and Jahmai, and Jibsam, and Shemuel, heads of their father's house, to wit, of Tola: they were valiant men of might in their generations; whose number was in the days of David two and twenty thousand and six hundred.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And the sons of Uzzi; Izrahiah: and the sons of Izrahiah; Michael, and Obadiah, and Joel, Ishiah, five: all of them chief men.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And with them, by their generations, after the house of their fathers, were bands of soldiers for war, six and thirty thousand men: for they had many wives and sons.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And their brethren among all the families of Issachar were valiant men of might, reckoned in all by their genealogies fourscore and seven thousand.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> The sons of Benjamin; Bela, and Becher, and Jediael, three.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And the sons of Bela; Ezbon, and Uzzi, and Uzziel, and Jerimoth, and Iri, five; heads of the house of their fathers, mighty men of valour; and were reckoned by their genealogies twenty and two thousand and thirty and four.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And the sons of Becher; Zemira, and Joash, and Eliezer, and Elioenai, and Omri, and Jerimoth, and Abiah, and Anathoth, and Alameth. All these are the sons of Becher.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And the number of them, after their genealogy by their generations, heads of the house of their fathers, mighty men of valour, was twenty thousand and two hundred.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> The sons also of Jediael; Bilhan: and the sons of Bilhan; Jeush, and Benjamin, and Ehud, and Chenaanah, and Zethan, and Tharshish, and Ahishahar.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> All these the sons of Jediael, by the heads of their fathers, mighty men of valour, were seventeen thousand and two hundred soldiers, fit to go out for war and battle.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Shuppim also, and Huppim, the children of Ir, and Hushim, the sons of Aher.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> The sons of Naphtali; Jahziel, and Guni, and Jezer, and Shallum, the sons of Bilhah.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> The sons of Manasseh; Ashriel, whom she bare: (but his concubine the Aramitess bare Machir the father of Gilead:</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And Machir took to wife the sister of Huppim and Shuppim, whose sister's name was Maachah;) and the name of the second was Zelophehad: and Zelophehad had daughters.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And Maachah the wife of Machir bare a son, and she called his name Peresh; and the name of his brother was Sheresh; and his sons were Ulam and Rakem.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And the sons of Ulam; Bedan. These were the sons of Gilead, the son of Machir, the son of Manasseh.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And his sister Hammoleketh bare Ishod, and Abiezer, and Mahalah.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And the sons of Shemidah were, Ahian, and Shechem, and Likhi, and Aniam.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And the sons of Ephraim; Shuthelah, and Bered his son, and Tahath his son, and Eladah his son, and Tahath his son,</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And Zabad his son, and Shuthelah his son, and Ezer, and Elead, whom the men of Gath that were born in that land slew, because they came down to take away their cattle.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And Ephraim their father mourned many days, and his brethren came to comfort him.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And when he went in to his wife, she conceived, and bare a son, and he called his name Beriah, because it went evil with his house.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> (And his daughter was Sherah, who built Beth-horon the nether, and the upper, and Uzzen-sherah.)</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And Rephah was his son, also Resheph, and Telah his son, and Tahan his son,</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Laadan his son, Ammihud his son, Elishama his son,</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Non his son, Jehoshua his son.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And their possessions and habitations were, Beth-el and the towns thereof, and eastward Naaran, and westward Gezer, with the towns thereof; Shechem also and the towns thereof, unto Gaza and the towns thereof:</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And by the borders of the children of Manasseh, Beth-shean and her towns, Taanach and her towns, Megiddo and her towns, Dor and her towns. In these dwelt the children of Joseph the son of Israel.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> The sons of Asher; Imnah, and Isuah, and Ishuai, and Beriah, and Serah their sister.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> And the sons of Beriah; Heber, and Malchiel, who is the father of Birzavith.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> And Heber begat Japhlet, and Shomer, and Hotham, and Shua their sister.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> And the sons of Japhlet; Pasach, and Bimhal, and Ashvath. These are the children of Japhlet.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> And the sons of Shamer; Ahi, and Rohgah, Jehubbah, and Aram.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> And the sons of his brother Helem; Zophah, and Imna, and Shelesh, and Amal.</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> The sons of Zophah; Suah, and Harnepher, and Shual, and Beri, and Imrah,</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> Bezer, and Hod, and Shamma, and Shilshah, and Ithran, and Beera.</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> And the sons of Jether; Jephunneh, and Pispah, and Ara.</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> And the sons of Ulla; Arah, and Haniel, and Rezia.</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> All these were the children of Asher, heads of their father's house, choice and mighty men of valour, chief of the princes. And the number throughout the genealogy of them that were apt to the war and to battle was twenty and six thousand men.</p>
