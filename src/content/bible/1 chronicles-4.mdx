## 1 Chronicles 4

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> The sons of Judah; Pharez, Hezron, and Carmi, and Hur, and Shobal.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And Reaiah the son of Shobal begat Jahath; and Jahath begat Ahumai, and Lahad. These are the families of the Zorathites.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And these were of the father of Etam; Jezreel, and Ishma, and Idbash: and the name of their sister was Hazelelponi:</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And Penuel the father of Gedor, and Ezer the father of Hushah. These are the sons of Hur, the firstborn of Ephratah, the father of Beth-lehem.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And Ashur the father of Tekoa had two wives, Helah and Naarah.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And Naarah bare him Ahuzam, and Hepher, and Temeni, and Haahashtari. These were the sons of Naarah.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And the sons of Helah were, Zereth, and Jezoar, and Ethnan.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And Coz begat Anub, and Zobebah, and the families of Aharhel the son of Harum.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And Jabez was more honourable than his brethren: and his mother called his name Jabez, saying, Because I bare him with sorrow.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And Jabez called on the God of Israel, saying, Oh that thou wouldest bless me indeed, and enlarge my coast, and that thine hand might be with me, and that thou wouldest keep me from evil, that it may not grieve me! And God granted him that which he requested.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And Chelub the brother of Shuah begat Mehir, which was the father of Eshton.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And Eshton begat Beth-rapha, and Paseah, and Tehinnah the father of Ir-nahash. These are the men of Rechah.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And the sons of Kenaz; Othniel, and Seraiah: and the sons of Othniel; Hathath.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And Meonothai begat Ophrah: and Seraiah begat Joab, the father of the valley of Charashim; for they were craftsmen.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And the sons of Caleb the son of Jephunneh; Iru, Elah, and Naam: and the sons of Elah, even Kenaz.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And the sons of Jehaleleel; Ziph, and Ziphah, Tiria, and Asareel.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And the sons of Ezra were, Jether, and Mered, and Epher, and Jalon: and she bare Miriam, and Shammai, and Ishbah the father of Eshtemoa.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And his wife Jehudijah bare Jered the father of Gedor, and Heber the father of Socho, and Jekuthiel the father of Zanoah. And these are the sons of Bithiah the daughter of Pharaoh, which Mered took.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And the sons of his wife Hodiah the sister of Naham, the father of Keilah the Garmite, and Eshtemoa the Maachathite.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And the sons of Shimon were, Amnon, and Rinnah, Ben-hanan, and Tilon. And the sons of Ishi were, Zoheth, and Ben-zoheth.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> The sons of Shelah the son of Judah were, Er the father of Lecah, and Laadah the father of Mareshah, and the families of the house of them that wrought fine linen, of the house of Ashbea,</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And Jokim, and the men of Chozeba, and Joash, and Saraph, who had the dominion in Moab, and Jashubi-lehem. And these are ancient things.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> These were the potters, and those that dwelt among plants and hedges: there they dwelt with the king for his work.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> The sons of Simeon were, Nemuel, and Jamin, Jarib, Zerah, and Shaul:</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Shallum his son, Mibsam his son, Mishma his son.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And the sons of Mishma; Hamuel his son, Zacchur his son, Shimei his son.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And Shimei had sixteen sons and six daughters; but his brethren had not many children, neither did all their family multiply, like to the children of Judah.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And they dwelt at Beer-sheba, and Moladah, and Hazar-shual,</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And at Bilhah, and at Ezem, and at Tolad,</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And at Bethuel, and at Hormah, and at Ziklag,</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> And at Beth-marcaboth, and Hazar-susim, and at Beth-birei, and at Shaaraim. These were their cities unto the reign of David.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> And their villages were, Etam, and Ain, Rimmon, and Tochen, and Ashan, five cities:</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> And all their villages that were round about the same cities, unto Baal. These were their habitations, and their genealogy.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> And Meshobab, and Jamlech, and Joshah the son of Amaziah,</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> And Joel, and Jehu the son of Josibiah, the son of Seraiah, the son of Asiel,</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> And Elioenai, and Jaakobah, and Jeshohaiah, and Asaiah, and Adiel, and Jesimiel, and Benaiah,</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> And Ziza the son of Shiphi, the son of Allon, the son of Jedaiah, the son of Shimri, the son of Shemaiah;</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> These mentioned by their names were princes in their families: and the house of their fathers increased greatly.</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> And they went to the entrance of Gedor, even unto the east side of the valley, to seek pasture for their flocks.</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> And they found fat pasture and good, and the land was wide, and quiet, and peaceable; for they of Ham had dwelt there of old.</p>

<p id="v41" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">41</span> And these written by name came in the days of Hezekiah king of Judah, and smote their tents, and the habitations that were found there, and destroyed them utterly unto this day, and dwelt in their rooms: because there was pasture there for their flocks.</p>

<p id="v42" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">42</span> And some of them, even of the sons of Simeon, five hundred men, went to mount Seir, having for their captains Pelatiah, and Neariah, and Rephaiah, and Uzziel, the sons of Ishi.</p>

<p id="v43" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">43</span> And they smote the rest of the Amalekites that were escaped, and dwelt there unto this day.</p>
