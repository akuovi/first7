## Job 22

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Then Eliphaz the Temanite answered and said,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Can a man be profitable unto God, as he that is wise may be profitable unto himself?</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Is it any pleasure to the Almighty, that thou art righteous? or is it gain to him, that thou makest thy ways perfect?</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Will he reprove thee for fear of thee? will he enter with thee into judgment?</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Is not thy wickedness great? and thine iniquities infinite?</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> For thou hast taken a pledge from thy brother for nought, and stripped the naked of their clothing.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Thou hast not given water to the weary to drink, and thou hast withholden bread from the hungry.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> But as for the mighty man, he had the earth; and the honourable man dwelt in it.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Thou hast sent widows away empty, and the arms of the fatherless have been broken.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Therefore snares are round about thee, and sudden fear troubleth thee;</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Or darkness, that thou canst not see; and abundance of waters cover thee.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Is not God in the height of heaven? and behold the height of the stars, how high they are!</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And thou sayest, How doth God know? can he judge through the dark cloud?</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Thick clouds are a covering to him, that he seeth not; and he walketh in the circuit of heaven.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Hast thou marked the old way which wicked men have trodden?</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Which were cut down out of time, whose foundation was overflown with a flood:</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Which said unto God, Depart from us: and what can the Almighty do for them?</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Yet he filled their houses with good things: but the counsel of the wicked is far from me.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> The righteous see it, and are glad: and the innocent laugh them to scorn.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Whereas our substance is not cut down, but the remnant of them the fire consumeth.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Acquaint now thyself with him, and be at peace: thereby good shall come unto thee.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Receive, I pray thee, the law from his mouth, and lay up his words in thine heart.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> If thou return to the Almighty, thou shalt be built up, thou shalt put away iniquity far from thy tabernacles.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Then shalt thou lay up gold as dust, and the gold of Ophir as the stones of the brooks.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Yea, the Almighty shall be thy defence, and thou shalt have plenty of silver.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> For then shalt thou have thy delight in the Almighty, and shalt lift up thy face unto God.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Thou shalt make thy prayer unto him, and he shall hear thee, and thou shalt pay thy vows.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Thou shalt also decree a thing, and it shall be established unto thee: and the light shall shine upon thy ways.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> When men are cast down, then thou shalt say, There is lifting up; and he shall save the humble person.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> He shall deliver the island of the innocent: and it is delivered by the pureness of thine hands.</p>
