## John 16

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> These things have I spoken unto you, that ye should not be offended.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> They shall put you out of the synagogues: yea, the time cometh, that whosoever killeth you will think that he doeth God service.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And these things will they do unto you, because they have not known the Father, nor me.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> But these things have I told you, that when the time shall come, ye may remember that I told you of them. And these things I said not unto you at the beginning, because I was with you.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> But now I go my way to him that sent me; and none of you asketh me, Whither goest thou?</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> But because I have said these things unto you, sorrow hath filled your heart.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Nevertheless I tell you the truth; It is expedient for you that I go away: for if I go not away, the Comforter will not come unto you; but if I depart, I will send him unto you.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And when he is come, he will reprove the world of sin, and of righteousness, and of judgment:</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Of sin, because they believe not on me;</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Of righteousness, because I go to my Father, and ye see me no more;</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Of judgment, because the prince of this world is judged.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> I have yet many things to say unto you, but ye cannot bear them now.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Howbeit when he, the Spirit of truth, is come, he will guide you into all truth: for he shall not speak of himself; but whatsoever he shall hear, that shall he speak: and he will shew you things to come.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> He shall glorify me: for he shall receive of mine, and shall shew it unto you.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> All things that the Father hath are mine: therefore said I, that he shall take of mine, and shall shew it unto you.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> A little while, and ye shall not see me: and again, a little while, and ye shall see me, because I go to the Father.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Then said some of his disciples among themselves, What is this that he saith unto us, A little while, and ye shall not see me: and again, a little while, and ye shall see me: and, Because I go to the Father?</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> They said therefore, What is this that he saith, A little while? we cannot tell what he saith.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Now Jesus knew that they were desirous to ask him, and said unto them, Do ye enquire among yourselves of that I said, A little while, and ye shall not see me: and again, a little while, and ye shall see me?</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Verily, verily, I say unto you, That ye shall weep and lament, but the world shall rejoice: and ye shall be sorrowful, but your sorrow shall be turned into joy.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> A woman when she is in travail hath sorrow, because her hour is come: but as soon as she is delivered of the child, she remembereth no more the anguish, for joy that a man is born into the world.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And ye now therefore have sorrow: but I will see you again, and your heart shall rejoice, and your joy no man taketh from you.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And in that day ye shall ask me nothing. Verily, verily, I say unto you, Whatsoever ye shall ask the Father in my name, he will give it you.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Hitherto have ye asked nothing in my name: ask, and ye shall receive, that your joy may be full.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> These things have I spoken unto you in proverbs: but the time cometh, when I shall no more speak unto you in proverbs, but I shall shew you plainly of the Father.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> At that day ye shall ask in my name: and I say not unto you, that I will pray the Father for you:</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> For the Father himself loveth you, because ye have loved me, and have believed that I came out from God.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> I came forth from the Father, and am come into the world: again, I leave the world, and go to the Father.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> His disciples said unto him, Lo, now speakest thou plainly, and speakest no proverb.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Now are we sure that thou knowest all things, and needest not that any man should ask thee: by this we believe that thou camest forth from God.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Jesus answered them, Do ye now believe?</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> Behold, the hour cometh, yea, is now come, that ye shall be scattered, every man to his own, and shall leave me alone: and yet I am not alone, because the Father is with me.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> These things I have spoken unto you, that in me ye might have peace. In the world ye shall have tribulation: but be of good cheer; I have overcome the world.</p>
