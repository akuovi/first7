## John 10

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Verily, verily, I say unto you, He that entereth not by the door into the sheepfold, but climbeth up some other way, the same is a thief and a robber.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> But he that entereth in by the door is the shepherd of the sheep.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> To him the porter openeth; and the sheep hear his voice: and he calleth his own sheep by name, and leadeth them out.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And when he putteth forth his own sheep, he goeth before them, and the sheep follow him: for they know his voice.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And a stranger will they not follow, but will flee from him: for they know not the voice of strangers.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> This parable spake Jesus unto them: but they understood not what things they were which he spake unto them.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Then said Jesus unto them again, Verily, verily, I say unto you, I am the door of the sheep.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> All that ever came before me are thieves and robbers: but the sheep did not hear them.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> I am the door: by me if any man enter in, he shall be saved, and shall go in and out, and find pasture.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> The thief cometh not, but for to steal, and to kill, and to destroy: I am come that they might have life, and that they might have it more abundantly.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> I am the good shepherd: the good shepherd giveth his life for the sheep.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> But he that is an hireling, and not the shepherd, whose own the sheep are not, seeth the wolf coming, and leaveth the sheep, and fleeth: and the wolf catcheth them, and scattereth the sheep.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> The hireling fleeth, because he is an hireling, and careth not for the sheep.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> I am the good shepherd, and know my sheep, and am known of mine.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> As the Father knoweth me, even so know I the Father: and I lay down my life for the sheep.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And other sheep I have, which are not of this fold: them also I must bring, and they shall hear my voice; and there shall be one fold, and one shepherd.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Therefore doth my Father love me, because I lay down my life, that I might take it again.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> No man taketh it from me, but I lay it down of myself. I have power to lay it down, and I have power to take it again. This commandment have I received of my Father.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> There was a division therefore again among the Jews for these sayings.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And many of them said, He hath a devil, and is mad; why hear ye him?</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Others said, These are not the words of him that hath a devil. Can a devil open the eyes of the blind?</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And it was at Jerusalem the feast of the dedication, and it was winter.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And Jesus walked in the temple in Solomon's porch.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Then came the Jews round about him, and said unto him, How long dost thou make us to doubt? If thou be the Christ, tell us plainly.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Jesus answered them, I told you, and ye believed not: the works that I do in my Father's name, they bear witness of me.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> But ye believe not, because ye are not of my sheep, as I said unto you.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> My sheep hear my voice, and I know them, and they follow me:</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And I give unto them eternal life; and they shall never perish, neither shall any man pluck them out of my hand.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> My Father, which gave them me, is greater than all; and no man is able to pluck them out of my Father's hand.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> I and my Father are one.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Then the Jews took up stones again to stone him.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> Jesus answered them, Many good works have I shewed you from my Father; for which of those works do ye stone me?</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> The Jews answered him, saying, For a good work we stone thee not; but for blasphemy; and because that thou, being a man, makest thyself God.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> Jesus answered them, Is it not written in your law, I said, Ye are gods?</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> If he called them gods, unto whom the word of God came, and the scripture cannot be broken;</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> Say ye of him, whom the Father hath sanctified, and sent into the world, Thou blasphemest; because I said, I am the Son of God?</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> If I do not the works of my Father, believe me not.</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> But if I do, though ye believe not me, believe the works: that ye may know, and believe, that the Father is in me, and I in him.</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> Therefore they sought again to take him: but he escaped out of their hand,</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> And went away again beyond Jordan into the place where John at first baptized; and there he abode.</p>

<p id="v41" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">41</span> And many resorted unto him, and said, John did no miracle: but all things that John spake of this man were true.</p>

<p id="v42" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">42</span> And many believed on him there.</p>
