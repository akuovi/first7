## Jonah 2

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Then Jonah prayed unto the LORD his God out of the fish's belly,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And said, I cried by reason of mine affliction unto the LORD, and he heard me; out of the belly of hell cried I, and thou heardest my voice.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For thou hadst cast me into the deep, in the midst of the seas; and the floods compassed me about: all thy billows and thy waves passed over me.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Then I said, I am cast out of thy sight; yet I will look again toward thy holy temple.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> The waters compassed me about, even to the soul: the depth closed me round about, the weeds were wrapped about my head.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> I went down to the bottoms of the mountains; the earth with her bars was about me for ever: yet hast thou brought up my life from corruption, O LORD my God.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> When my soul fainted within me I remembered the LORD: and my prayer came in unto thee, into thine holy temple.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> They that observe lying vanities forsake their own mercy.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> But I will sacrifice unto thee with the voice of thanksgiving; I will pay that that I have vowed. Salvation is of the LORD.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And the LORD spake unto the fish, and it vomited out Jonah upon the dry land.</p>
