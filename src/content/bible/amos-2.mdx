## Amos 2

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Thus saith the LORD; For three transgressions of Moab, and for four, I will not turn away the punishment thereof; because he burned the bones of the king of Edom into lime:</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> But I will send a fire upon Moab, and it shall devour the palaces of Kerioth: and Moab shall die with tumult, with shouting, and with the sound of the trumpet:</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And I will cut off the judge from the midst thereof, and will slay all the princes thereof with him, saith the LORD.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Thus saith the LORD; For three transgressions of Judah, and for four, I will not turn away the punishment thereof; because they have despised the law of the LORD, and have not kept his commandments, and their lies caused them to err, after the which their fathers have walked:</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> But I will send a fire upon Judah, and it shall devour the palaces of Jerusalem.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Thus saith the LORD; For three transgressions of Israel, and for four, I will not turn away the punishment thereof; because they sold the righteous for silver, and the poor for a pair of shoes;</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> That pant after the dust of the earth on the head of the poor, and turn aside the way of the meek: and a man and his father will go in unto the same maid, to profane my holy name:</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And they lay themselves down upon clothes laid to pledge by every altar, and they drink the wine of the condemned in the house of their god.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Yet destroyed I the Amorite before them, whose height was like the height of the cedars, and he was strong as the oaks; yet I destroyed his fruit from above, and his roots from beneath.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Also I brought you up from the land of Egypt, and led you forty years through the wilderness, to possess the land of the Amorite.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And I raised up of your sons for prophets, and of your young men for Nazarites. Is it not even thus, O ye children of Israel? saith the LORD.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> But ye gave the Nazarites wine to drink; and commanded the prophets, saying, Prophesy not.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Behold, I am pressed under you, as a cart is pressed that is full of sheaves.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Therefore the flight shall perish from the swift, and the strong shall not strengthen his force, neither shall the mighty deliver himself:</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Neither shall he stand that handleth the bow; and he that is swift of foot shall not deliver himself: neither shall he that rideth the horse deliver himself.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And he that is courageous among the mighty shall flee away naked in that day, saith the LORD.</p>
