## 1 Peter 5

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> The elders which are among you I exhort, who am also an elder, and a witness of the sufferings of Christ, and also a partaker of the glory that shall be revealed:</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Feed the flock of God which is among you, taking the oversight thereof, not by constraint, but willingly; not for filthy lucre, but of a ready mind;</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Neither as being lords over God's heritage, but being ensamples to the flock.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And when the chief Shepherd shall appear, ye shall receive a crown of glory that fadeth not away.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Likewise, ye younger, submit yourselves unto the elder. Yea, all of you be subject one to another, and be clothed with humility: for God resisteth the proud, and giveth grace to the humble.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Humble yourselves therefore under the mighty hand of God, that he may exalt you in due time:</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Casting all your care upon him; for he careth for you.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Be sober, be vigilant; because your adversary the devil, as a roaring lion, walketh about, seeking whom he may devour:</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Whom resist stedfast in the faith, knowing that the same afflictions are accomplished in your brethren that are in the world.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> But the God of all grace, who hath called us unto his eternal glory by Christ Jesus, after that ye have suffered a while, make you perfect, stablish, strengthen, settle you.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> To him be glory and dominion for ever and ever. Amen.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> By Silvanus, a faithful brother unto you, as I suppose, I have written briefly, exhorting, and testifying that this is the true grace of God wherein ye stand.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> The church that is at Babylon, elected together with you, saluteth you; and so doth Marcus my son.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Greet ye one another with a kiss of charity. Peace be with you all that are in Christ Jesus. Amen.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> PETER</p>
