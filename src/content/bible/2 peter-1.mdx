## 2 Peter 1

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Simon Peter, a servant and an apostle of Jesus Christ, to them that have obtained like precious faith with us through the righteousness of God and our Saviour Jesus Christ:</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Grace and peace be multiplied unto you through the knowledge of God, and of Jesus our Lord,</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> According as his divine power hath given unto us all things that pertain unto life and godliness, through the knowledge of him that hath called us to glory and virtue:</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Whereby are given unto us exceeding great and precious promises: that by these ye might be partakers of the divine nature, having escaped the corruption that is in the world through lust.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And beside this, giving all diligence, add to your faith virtue; and to virtue knowledge;</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And to knowledge temperance; and to temperance patience; and to patience godliness;</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And to godliness brotherly kindness; and to brotherly kindness charity.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> For if these things be in you, and abound, they make you that ye shall neither be barren nor unfruitful in the knowledge of our Lord Jesus Christ.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> But he that lacketh these things is blind, and cannot see afar off, and hath forgotten that he was purged from his old sins.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Wherefore the rather, brethren, give diligence to make your calling and election sure: for if ye do these things, ye shall never fall:</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> For so an entrance shall be ministered unto you abundantly into the everlasting kingdom of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Wherefore I will not be negligent to put you always in remembrance of these things, though ye know them, and be established in the present truth.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Yea, I think it meet, as long as I am in this tabernacle, to stir you up by putting you in remembrance;</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Knowing that shortly I must put off this my tabernacle, even as our Lord Jesus Christ hath shewed me.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Moreover I will endeavour that ye may be able after my decease to have these things always in remembrance.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> For we have not followed cunningly devised fables, when we made known unto you the power and coming of our Lord Jesus Christ, but were eyewitnesses of his majesty.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> For he received from God the Father honour and glory, when there came such a voice to him from the excellent glory, This is my beloved Son, in whom I am well pleased.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And this voice which came from heaven we heard, when we were with him in the holy mount.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> We have also a more sure word of prophecy; whereunto ye do well that ye take heed, as unto a light that shineth in a dark place, until the day dawn, and the day star arise in your hearts:</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Knowing this first, that no prophecy of the scripture is of any private interpretation.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> For the prophecy came not in old time by the will of man: but holy men of God spake as they were moved by the Holy Ghost.</p>
