## Genesis 5

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> This is the book of the generations of Adam. In the day that God created man, in the likeness of God made he him;</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Male and female created he them; and blessed them, and called their name Adam, in the day when they were created.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And Adam lived an hundred and thirty years, and begat a son in his own likeness, after his image; and called his name Seth:</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And the days of Adam after he had begotten Seth were eight hundred years: and he begat sons and daughters:</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And all the days that Adam lived were nine hundred and thirty years: and he died.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And Seth lived an hundred and five years, and begat Enos:</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And Seth lived after he begat Enos eight hundred and seven years, and begat sons and daughters:</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And all the days of Seth were nine hundred and twelve years: and he died.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And Enos lived ninety years, and begat Cainan:</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And Enos lived after he begat Cainan eight hundred and fifteen years, and begat sons and daughters:</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And all the days of Enos were nine hundred and five years: and he died.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And Cainan lived seventy years, and begat Mahalaleel:</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And Cainan lived after he begat Mahalaleel eight hundred and forty years, and begat sons and daughters:</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And all the days of Cainan were nine hundred and ten years: and he died.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And Mahalaleel lived sixty and five years, and begat Jared:</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And Mahalaleel lived after he begat Jared eight hundred and thirty years, and begat sons and daughters:</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And all the days of Mahalaleel were eight hundred ninety and five years: and he died.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And Jared lived an hundred sixty and two years, and he begat Enoch:</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And Jared lived after he begat Enoch eight hundred years, and begat sons and daughters:</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And all the days of Jared were nine hundred sixty and two years: and he died.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And Enoch lived sixty and five years, and begat Methuselah:</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And Enoch walked with God after he begat Methuselah three hundred years, and begat sons and daughters:</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And all the days of Enoch were three hundred sixty and five years:</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And Enoch walked with God: and he was not; for God took him.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And Methuselah lived an hundred eighty and seven years, and begat Lamech:</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And Methuselah lived after he begat Lamech seven hundred eighty and two years, and begat sons and daughters:</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And all the days of Methuselah were nine hundred sixty and nine years: and he died.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And Lamech lived an hundred eighty and two years, and begat a son:</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And he called his name Noah, saying, This same shall comfort us concerning our work and toil of our hands, because of the ground which the LORD hath cursed.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And Lamech lived after he begat Noah five hundred ninety and five years, and begat sons and daughters:</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> And all the days of Lamech were seven hundred seventy and seven years: and he died.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> And Noah was five hundred years old: and Noah begat Shem, Ham, and Japheth.</p>
