## John 15

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> I am the true vine, and my Father is the husbandman.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Every branch in me that beareth not fruit he taketh away: and every branch that beareth fruit, he purgeth it, that it may bring forth more fruit.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Now ye are clean through the word which I have spoken unto you.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Abide in me, and I in you. As the branch cannot bear fruit of itself, except it abide in the vine; no more can ye, except ye abide in me.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> I am the vine, ye are the branches: He that abideth in me, and I in him, the same bringeth forth much fruit: for without me ye can do nothing.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> If a man abide not in me, he is cast forth as a branch, and is withered; and men gather them, and cast them into the fire, and they are burned.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> If ye abide in me, and my words abide in you, ye shall ask what ye will, and it shall be done unto you.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Herein is my Father glorified, that ye bear much fruit; so shall ye be my disciples.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> As the Father hath loved me, so have I loved you: continue ye in my love.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> If ye keep my commandments, ye shall abide in my love; even as I have kept my Father's commandments, and abide in his love.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> These things have I spoken unto you, that my joy might remain in you, and that your joy might be full.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> This is my commandment, That ye love one another, as I have loved you.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Greater love hath no man than this, that a man lay down his life for his friends.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Ye are my friends, if ye do whatsoever I command you.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Henceforth I call you not servants; for the servant knoweth not what his lord doeth: but I have called you friends; for all things that I have heard of my Father I have made known unto you.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Ye have not chosen me, but I have chosen you, and ordained you, that ye should go and bring forth fruit, and that your fruit should remain: that whatsoever ye shall ask of the Father in my name, he may give it you.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> These things I command you, that ye love one another.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> If the world hate you, ye know that it hated me before it hated you.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> If ye were of the world, the world would love his own: but because ye are not of the world, but I have chosen you out of the world, therefore the world hateth you.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Remember the word that I said unto you, The servant is not greater than his lord. If they have persecuted me, they will also persecute you; if they have kept my saying, they will keep your's also.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> But all these things will they do unto you for my name's sake, because they know not him that sent me.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> If I had not come and spoken unto them, they had not had sin: but now they have no cloke for their sin.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> He that hateth me hateth my Father also.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> If I had not done among them the works which none other man did, they had not had sin: but now have they both seen and hated both me and my Father.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> But this cometh to pass, that the word might be fulfilled that is written in their law, They hated me without a cause.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> But when the Comforter is come, whom I will send unto you from the Father, even the Spirit of truth, which proceedeth from the Father, he shall testify of me:</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And ye also shall bear witness, because ye have been with me from the beginning.</p>
