## 1 Timothy 1

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Paul, an apostle of Jesus Christ by the commandment of God our Saviour, and Lord Jesus Christ, which is our hope;</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Unto Timothy, my own son in the faith: Grace, mercy, and peace, from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> As I besought thee to abide still at Ephesus, when I went into Macedonia, that thou mightest charge some that they teach no other doctrine,</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Neither give heed to fables and endless genealogies, which minister questions, rather than godly edifying which is in faith: so do.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Now the end of the commandment is charity out of a pure heart, and of a good conscience, and of faith unfeigned:</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> From which some having swerved have turned aside unto vain jangling;</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Desiring to be teachers of the law; understanding neither what they say, nor whereof they affirm.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> But we know that the law is good, if a man use it lawfully;</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Knowing this, that the law is not made for a righteous man, but for the lawless and disobedient, for the ungodly and for sinners, for unholy and profane, for murderers of fathers and murderers of mothers, for manslayers,</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> For whoremongers, for them that defile themselves with mankind, for menstealers, for liars, for perjured persons, and if there be any other thing that is contrary to sound doctrine;</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> According to the glorious gospel of the blessed God, which was committed to my trust.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And I thank Christ Jesus our Lord, who hath enabled me, for that he counted me faithful, putting me into the ministry;</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Who was before a blasphemer, and a persecutor, and injurious: but I obtained mercy, because I did it ignorantly in unbelief.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And the grace of our Lord was exceeding abundant with faith and love which is in Christ Jesus.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> This is a faithful saying, and worthy of all acceptation, that Christ Jesus came into the world to save sinners; of whom I am chief.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Howbeit for this cause I obtained mercy, that in me first Jesus Christ might shew forth all longsuffering, for a pattern to them which should hereafter believe on him to life everlasting.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Now unto the King eternal, immortal, invisible, the only wise God, be honour and glory for ever and ever. Amen.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> This charge I commit unto thee, son Timothy, according to the prophecies which went before on thee, that thou by them mightest war a good warfare;</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Holding faith, and a good conscience; which some having put away concerning faith have made shipwreck:</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Of whom is Hymenaeus and Alexander; whom I have delivered unto Satan, that they may learn not to blaspheme.</p>
