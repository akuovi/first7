## 2 Timothy 4

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> I charge thee therefore before God, and the Lord Jesus Christ, who shall judge the quick and the dead at his appearing and his kingdom;</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Preach the word; be instant in season, out of season; reprove, rebuke, exhort with all longsuffering and doctrine.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For the time will come when they will not endure sound doctrine; but after their own lusts shall they heap to themselves teachers, having itching ears;</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And they shall turn away their ears from the truth, and shall be turned unto fables.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> But watch thou in all things, endure afflictions, do the work of an evangelist, make full proof of thy ministry.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> For I am now ready to be offered, and the time of my departure is at hand.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> I have fought a good fight, I have finished my course, I have kept the faith:</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Henceforth there is laid up for me a crown of righteousness, which the Lord, the righteous judge, shall give me at that day: and not to me only, but unto all them also that love his appearing.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Do thy diligence to come shortly unto me:</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> For Demas hath forsaken me, having loved this present world, and is departed unto Thessalonica; Crescens to Galatia, Titus unto Dalmatia.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Only Luke is with me. Take Mark, and bring him with thee: for he is profitable to me for the ministry.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And Tychicus have I sent to Ephesus.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> The cloke that I left at Troas with Carpus, when thou comest, bring with thee, and the books, but especially the parchments.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Alexander the coppersmith did me much evil: the Lord reward him according to his works:</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Of whom be thou ware also; for he hath greatly withstood our words.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> At my first answer no man stood with me, but all men forsook me: I pray God that it may not be laid to their charge.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Notwithstanding the Lord stood with me, and strengthened me; that by me the preaching might be fully known, and that all the Gentiles might hear: and I was delivered out of the mouth of the lion.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And the Lord shall deliver me from every evil work, and will preserve me unto his heavenly kingdom: to whom be glory for ever and ever. Amen.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Salute Prisca and Aquila, and the household of Onesiphorus.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Erastus abode at Corinth: but Trophimus have I left at Miletum sick.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Do thy diligence to come before winter. Eubulus greeteth thee, and Pudens, and Linus, and Claudia, and all the brethren.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> The Lord Jesus Christ be with thy spirit. Grace be with you. Amen.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> The second epistle unto Timotheus, ordained the first bishop of the church of the Ephesians, was written from Rome, when Paul was brought before Nero the second time.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> TIMOTHY</p>
