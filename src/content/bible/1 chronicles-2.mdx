## 1 Chronicles 2

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> These are the sons of Israel; Reuben, Simeon, Levi, and Judah, Issachar, and Zebulun,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Dan, Joseph, and Benjamin, Naphtali, Gad, and Asher.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> The sons of Judah; Er, and Onan, and Shelah: which three were born unto him of the daughter of Shua the Canaanitess. And Er, the firstborn of Judah, was evil in the sight of the LORD; and he slew him.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And Tamar his daughter in law bare him Pharez and Zerah. All the sons of Judah were five.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> The sons of Pharez; Hezron, and Hamul.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And the sons of Zerah; Zimri, and Ethan, and Heman, and Calcol, and Dara: five of them in all.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And the sons of Carmi; Achar, the troubler of Israel, who transgressed in the thing accursed.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And the sons of Ethan; Azariah.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> The sons also of Hezron, that were born unto him; Jerahmeel, and Ram, and Chelubai.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And Ram begat Amminadab; and Amminadab begat Nahshon, prince of the children of Judah;</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And Nahshon begat Salma, and Salma begat Boaz,</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And Boaz begat Obed, and Obed begat Jesse,</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And Jesse begat his firstborn Eliab, and Abinadab the second, and Shimma the third,</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Nethaneel the fourth, Raddai the fifth,</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Ozem the sixth, David the seventh:</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Whose sisters were Zeruiah, and Abigail. And the sons of Zeruiah; Abishai, and Joab, and Asahel, three.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And Abigail bare Amasa: and the father of Amasa was Jether the Ishmeelite.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And Caleb the son of Hezron begat children of Azubah his wife, and of Jerioth: her sons are these; Jesher, and Shobab, and Ardon.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And when Azubah was dead, Caleb took unto him Ephrath, which bare him Hur.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And Hur begat Uri, and Uri begat Bezaleel.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And afterward Hezron went in to the daughter of Machir the father of Gilead, whom he married when he was threescore years old; and she bare him Segub.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And Segub begat Jair, who had three and twenty cities in the land of Gilead.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And he took Geshur, and Aram, with the towns of Jair, from them, with Kenath, and the towns thereof, even threescore cities. All these belonged to the sons of Machir the father of Gilead.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And after that Hezron was dead in Caleb-ephratah, then Abiah Hezron's wife bare him Ashur the father of Tekoa.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And the sons of Jerahmeel the firstborn of Hezron were, Ram the firstborn, and Bunah, and Oren, and Ozem, and Ahijah.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Jerahmeel had also another wife, whose name was Atarah; she was the mother of Onam.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And the sons of Ram the firstborn of Jerahmeel were, Maaz, and Jamin, and Eker.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And the sons of Onam were, Shammai, and Jada. And the sons of Shammai; Nadab, and Abishur.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And the name of the wife of Abishur was Abihail, and she bare him Ahban, and Molid.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And the sons of Nadab; Seled, and Appaim: but Seled died without children.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> And the sons of Appaim; Ishi. And the sons of Ishi; Sheshan. And the children of Sheshan; Ahlai.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> And the sons of Jada the brother of Shammai; Jether, and Jonathan: and Jether died without children.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> And the sons of Jonathan; Peleth, and Zaza. These were the sons of Jerahmeel.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> Now Sheshan had no sons, but daughters. And Sheshan had a servant, an Egyptian, whose name was Jarha.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> And Sheshan gave his daughter to Jarha his servant to wife; and she bare him Attai.</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> And Attai begat Nathan, and Nathan begat Zabad,</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> And Zabad begat Ephlal, and Ephlal begat Obed,</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> And Obed begat Jehu, and Jehu begat Azariah,</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> And Azariah begat Helez, and Helez begat Eleasah,</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> And Eleasah begat Sisamai, and Sisamai begat Shallum,</p>

<p id="v41" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">41</span> And Shallum begat Jekamiah, and Jekamiah begat Elishama.</p>

<p id="v42" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">42</span> Now the sons of Caleb the brother of Jerahmeel were, Mesha his firstborn, which was the father of Ziph; and the sons of Mareshah the father of Hebron.</p>

<p id="v43" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">43</span> And the sons of Hebron; Korah, and Tappuah, and Rekem, and Shema.</p>

<p id="v44" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">44</span> And Shema begat Raham, the father of Jorkoam: and Rekem begat Shammai.</p>

<p id="v45" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">45</span> And the son of Shammai was Maon: and Maon was the father of Beth-zur.</p>

<p id="v46" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">46</span> And Ephah, Caleb's concubine, bare Haran, and Moza, and Gazez: and Haran begat Gazez.</p>

<p id="v47" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">47</span> And the sons of Jahdai; Regem, and Jotham, and Gesham, and Pelet, and Ephah, and Shaaph.</p>

<p id="v48" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">48</span> Maachah, Caleb's concubine, bare Sheber, and Tirhanah.</p>

<p id="v49" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">49</span> She bare also Shaaph the father of Madmannah, Sheva the father of Machbenah, and the father of Gibea: and the daughter of Caleb was Achsa.</p>

<p id="v50" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">50</span> These were the sons of Caleb the son of Hur, the firstborn of Ephratah; Shobal the father of Kirjath-jearim,</p>

<p id="v51" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">51</span> Salma the father of Beth-lehem, Hareph the father of Beth-gader.</p>

<p id="v52" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">52</span> And Shobal the father of Kirjath-jearim had sons; Haroeh, and half of the Manahethites.</p>

<p id="v53" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">53</span> And the families of Kirjath-jearim; the Ithrites, and the Puhites, and the Shumathites, and the Mishraites; of them came the Zareathites, and the Eshtaulites.</p>

<p id="v54" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">54</span> The sons of Salma; Beth-lehem, and the Netophathites, Ataroth, the house of Joab, and half of the Manahethites, the Zorites.</p>

<p id="v55" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">55</span> And the families of the scribes which dwelt at Jabez; the Tirathites, the Shimeathites, and Suchathites. These are the Kenites that came of Hemath, the father of the house of Rechab.</p>
