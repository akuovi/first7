## Galatians 6

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Brethren, if a man be overtaken in a fault, ye which are spiritual, restore such an one in the spirit of meekness; considering thyself, lest thou also be tempted.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Bear ye one another's burdens, and so fulfil the law of Christ.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For if a man think himself to be something, when he is nothing, he deceiveth himself.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> But let every man prove his own work, and then shall he have rejoicing in himself alone, and not in another.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> For every man shall bear his own burden.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Let him that is taught in the word communicate unto him that teacheth in all good things.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Be not deceived; God is not mocked: for whatsoever a man soweth, that shall he also reap.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> For he that soweth to his flesh shall of the flesh reap corruption; but he that soweth to the Spirit shall of the Spirit reap life everlasting.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And let us not be weary in well doing: for in due season we shall reap, if we faint not.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> As we have therefore opportunity, let us do good unto all men, especially unto them who are of the household of faith.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Ye see how large a letter I have written unto you with mine own hand.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> As many as desire to make a fair shew in the flesh, they constrain you to be circumcised; only lest they should suffer persecution for the cross of Christ.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> For neither they themselves who are circumcised keep the law; but desire to have you circumcised, that they may glory in your flesh.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> But God forbid that I should glory, save in the cross of our Lord Jesus Christ, by whom the world is crucified unto me, and I unto the world.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> For in Christ Jesus neither circumcision availeth any thing, nor uncircumcision, but a new creature.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And as many as walk according to this rule, peace be on them, and mercy, and upon the Israel of God.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> From henceforth let no man trouble me: for I bear in my body the marks of the Lord Jesus.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Brethren, the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with your spirit. Amen.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Unto the Galatians written from Rome.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> GALATIANS</p>
