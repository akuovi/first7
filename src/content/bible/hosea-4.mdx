## Hosea 4

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Hear the word of the LORD, ye children of Israel: for the LORD hath a controversy with the inhabitants of the land, because there is no truth, nor mercy, nor knowledge of God in the land.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> By swearing, and lying, and killing, and stealing, and committing adultery, they break out, and blood toucheth blood.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Therefore shall the land mourn, and every one that dwelleth therein shall languish, with the beasts of the field, and with the fowls of heaven; yea, the fishes of the sea also shall be taken away.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Yet let no man strive, nor reprove another: for thy people are as they that strive with the priest.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Therefore shalt thou fall in the day, and the prophet also shall fall with thee in the night, and I will destroy thy mother.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> My people are destroyed for lack of knowledge: because thou hast rejected knowledge, I will also reject thee, that thou shalt be no priest to me: seeing thou hast forgotten the law of thy God, I will also forget thy children.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> As they were increased, so they sinned against me: therefore will I change their glory into shame.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> They eat up the sin of my people, and they set their heart on their iniquity.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And there shall be, like people, like priest: and I will punish them for their ways, and reward them their doings.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> For they shall eat, and not have enough: they shall commit whoredom, and shall not increase: because they have left off to take heed to the LORD.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Whoredom and wine and new wine take away the heart.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> My people ask counsel at their stocks, and their staff declareth unto them: for the spirit of whoredoms hath caused them to err, and they have gone a whoring from under their God.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> They sacrifice upon the tops of the mountains, and burn incense upon the hills, under oaks and poplars and elms, because the shadow thereof is good: therefore your daughters shall commit whoredom, and your spouses shall commit adultery.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> I will not punish your daughters when they commit whoredom, nor your spouses when they commit adultery: for themselves are separated with whores, and they sacrifice with harlots: therefore the people that doth not understand shall fall.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Though thou, Israel, play the harlot, yet let not Judah offend; and come not ye unto Gilgal, neither go ye up to Beth-aven, nor swear, The LORD liveth.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> For Israel slideth back as a backsliding heifer: now the LORD will feed them as a lamb in a large place.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Ephraim is joined to idols: let him alone.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Their drink is sour: they have committed whoredom continually: her rulers with shame do love, Give ye.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> The wind hath bound her up in her wings, and they shall be ashamed because of their sacrifices.</p>
