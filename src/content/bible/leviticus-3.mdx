## Leviticus 3

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And if his oblation be a sacrifice of peace offering, if he offer it of the herd; whether it be a male or female, he shall offer it without blemish before the LORD.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And he shall lay his hand upon the head of his offering, and kill it at the door of the tabernacle of the congregation: and Aaron's sons the priests shall sprinkle the blood upon the altar round about.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And he shall offer of the sacrifice of the peace offering an offering made by fire unto the LORD; the fat that covereth the inwards, and all the fat that is upon the inwards,</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And the two kidneys, and the fat that is on them, which is by the flanks, and the caul above the liver, with the kidneys, it shall he take away.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And Aaron's sons shall burn it on the altar upon the burnt sacrifice, which is upon the wood that is on the fire: it is an offering made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And if his offering for a sacrifice of peace offering unto the LORD be of the flock; male or female, he shall offer it without blemish.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> If he offer a lamb for his offering, then shall he offer it before the LORD.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And he shall lay his hand upon the head of his offering, and kill it before the tabernacle of the congregation: and Aaron's sons shall sprinkle the blood thereof round about upon the altar.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And he shall offer of the sacrifice of the peace offering an offering made by fire unto the LORD; the fat thereof, and the whole rump, it shall he take off hard by the backbone; and the fat that covereth the inwards, and all the fat that is upon the inwards,</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And the two kidneys, and the fat that is upon them, which is by the flanks, and the caul above the liver, with the kidneys, it shall he take away.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And the priest shall burn it upon the altar: it is the food of the offering made by fire unto the LORD.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And if his offering be a goat, then he shall offer it before the LORD.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And he shall lay his hand upon the head of it, and kill it before the tabernacle of the congregation: and the sons of Aaron shall sprinkle the blood thereof upon the altar round about.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And he shall offer thereof his offering, even an offering made by fire unto the LORD; the fat that covereth the inwards, and all the fat that is upon the inwards,</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And the two kidneys, and the fat that is upon them, which is by the flanks, and the caul above the liver, with the kidneys, it shall he take away.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And the priest shall burn them upon the altar: it is the food of the offering made by fire for a sweet savour: all the fat is the LORD'S.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> It shall be a perpetual statute for your generations throughout all your dwellings, that ye eat neither fat nor blood.</p>
