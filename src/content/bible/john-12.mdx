## John 12

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Then Jesus six days before the passover came to Bethany, where Lazarus was which had been dead, whom he raised from the dead.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> There they made him a supper; and Martha served: but Lazarus was one of them that sat at the table with him.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Then took Mary a pound of ointment of spikenard, very costly, and anointed the feet of Jesus, and wiped his feet with her hair: and the house was filled with the odour of the ointment.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Then saith one of his disciples, Judas Iscariot, Simon's son, which should betray him,</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Why was not this ointment sold for three hundred pence, and given to the poor?</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> This he said, not that he cared for the poor; but because he was a thief, and had the bag, and bare what was put therein.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Then said Jesus, Let her alone: against the day of my burying hath she kept this.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> For the poor always ye have with you; but me ye have not always.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Much people of the Jews therefore knew that he was there: and they came not for Jesus' sake only, but that they might see Lazarus also, whom he had raised from the dead.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> But the chief priests consulted that they might put Lazarus also to death;</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Because that by reason of him many of the Jews went away, and believed on Jesus.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> On the next day much people that were come to the feast, when they heard that Jesus was coming to Jerusalem,</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Took branches of palm trees, and went forth to meet him, and cried, Hosanna: Blessed is the King of Israel that cometh in the name of the Lord.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And Jesus, when he had found a young ass, sat thereon; as it is written,</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Fear not, daughter of Sion: behold, thy King cometh, sitting on an ass's colt.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> These things understood not his disciples at the first: but when Jesus was glorified, then remembered they that these things were written of him, and that they had done these things unto him.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> The people therefore that was with him when he called Lazarus out of his grave, and raised him from the dead, bare record.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> For this cause the people also met him, for that they heard that he had done this miracle.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> The Pharisees therefore said among themselves, Perceive ye how ye prevail nothing? behold, the world is gone after him.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And there were certain Greeks among them that came up to worship at the feast:</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> The same came therefore to Philip, which was of Bethsaida of Galilee, and desired him, saying, Sir, we would see Jesus.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Philip cometh and telleth Andrew: and again Andrew and Philip tell Jesus.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And Jesus answered them, saying, The hour is come, that the Son of man should be glorified.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Verily, verily, I say unto you, Except a corn of wheat fall into the ground and die, it abideth alone: but if it die, it bringeth forth much fruit.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> He that loveth his life shall lose it; and he that hateth his life in this world shall keep it unto life eternal.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> If any man serve me, let him follow me; and where I am, there shall also my servant be: if any man serve me, him will my Father honour.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Now is my soul troubled; and what shall I say? Father, save me from this hour: but for this cause came I unto this hour.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Father, glorify thy name. Then came there a voice from heaven, saying, I have both glorified it, and will glorify it again.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> The people therefore, that stood by, and heard it, said that it thundered: others said, An angel spake to him.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Jesus answered and said, This voice came not because of me, but for your sakes.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Now is the judgment of this world: now shall the prince of this world be cast out.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> And I, if I be lifted up from the earth, will draw all men unto me.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> This he said, signifying what death he should die.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> The people answered him, We have heard out of the law that Christ abideth for ever: and how sayest thou, The Son of man must be lifted up? who is this Son of man?</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> Then Jesus said unto them, Yet a little while is the light with you. Walk while ye have the light, lest darkness come upon you: for he that walketh in darkness knoweth not whither he goeth.</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> While ye have light, believe in the light, that ye may be the children of light. These things spake Jesus, and departed, and did hide himself from them.</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> But though he had done so many miracles before them, yet they believed not on him:</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> That the saying of Esaias the prophet might be fulfilled, which he spake, Lord, who hath believed our report? and to whom hath the arm of the Lord been revealed?</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> Therefore they could not believe, because that Esaias said again,</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> He hath blinded their eyes, and hardened their heart; that they should not see with their eyes, nor understand with their heart, and be converted, and I should heal them.</p>

<p id="v41" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">41</span> These things said Esaias, when he saw his glory, and spake of him.</p>

<p id="v42" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">42</span> Nevertheless among the chief rulers also many believed on him; but because of the Pharisees they did not confess him, lest they should be put out of the synagogue:</p>

<p id="v43" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">43</span> For they loved the praise of men more than the praise of God.</p>

<p id="v44" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">44</span> Jesus cried and said, He that believeth on me, believeth not on me, but on him that sent me.</p>

<p id="v45" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">45</span> And he that seeth me seeth him that sent me.</p>

<p id="v46" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">46</span> I am come a light into the world, that whosoever believeth on me should not abide in darkness.</p>

<p id="v47" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">47</span> And if any man hear my words, and believe not, I judge him not: for I came not to judge the world, but to save the world.</p>

<p id="v48" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">48</span> He that rejecteth me, and receiveth not my words, hath one that judgeth him: the word that I have spoken, the same shall judge him in the last day.</p>

<p id="v49" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">49</span> For I have not spoken of myself; but the Father which sent me, he gave me a commandment, what I should say, and what I should speak.</p>

<p id="v50" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">50</span> And I know that his commandment is life everlasting: whatsoever I speak therefore, even as the Father said unto me, so I speak.</p>
