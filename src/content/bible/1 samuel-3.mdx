## 1 Samuel 3

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And the child Samuel ministered unto the LORD before Eli. And the word of the LORD was precious in those days; there was no open vision.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And it came to pass at that time, when Eli was laid down in his place, and his eyes began to wax dim, that he could not see;</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And ere the lamp of God went out in the temple of the LORD, where the ark of God was, and Samuel was laid down to sleep;</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> That the LORD called Samuel: and he answered, Here am I.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And he ran unto Eli, and said, Here am I; for thou calledst me. And he said, I called not; lie down again. And he went and lay down.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And the LORD called yet again, Samuel. And Samuel arose and went to Eli, and said, Here am I; for thou didst call me. And he answered, I called not, my son; lie down again.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Now Samuel did not yet know the LORD, neither was the word of the LORD yet revealed unto him.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And the LORD called Samuel again the third time. And he arose and went to Eli, and said, Here am I; for thou didst call me. And Eli perceived that the LORD had called the child.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Therefore Eli said unto Samuel, Go, lie down: and it shall be, if he call thee, that thou shalt say, Speak, LORD; for thy servant heareth. So Samuel went and lay down in his place.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And the LORD came, and stood, and called as at other times, Samuel, Samuel. Then Samuel answered, Speak; for thy servant heareth.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And the LORD said to Samuel, Behold, I will do a thing in Israel, at which both the ears of every one that heareth it shall tingle.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> In that day I will perform against Eli all things which I have spoken concerning his house: when I begin, I will also make an end.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> For I have told him that I will judge his house for ever for the iniquity which he knoweth; because his sons made themselves vile, and he restrained them not.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And therefore I have sworn unto the house of Eli, that the iniquity of Eli's house shall not be purged with sacrifice nor offering for ever.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And Samuel lay until the morning, and opened the doors of the house of the LORD. And Samuel feared to shew Eli the vision.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Then Eli called Samuel, and said, Samuel, my son. And he answered, Here am I.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And he said, What is the thing that the LORD hath said unto thee? I pray thee hide it not from me: God do so to thee, and more also, if thou hide any thing from me of all the things that he said unto thee.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And Samuel told him every whit, and hid nothing from him. And he said, It is the LORD: let him do what seemeth him good.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And Samuel grew, and the LORD was with him, and did let none of his words fall to the ground.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And all Israel from Dan even to Beer-sheba knew that Samuel was established to be a prophet of the LORD.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And the LORD appeared again in Shiloh: for the LORD revealed himself to Samuel in Shiloh by the word of the LORD.</p>
