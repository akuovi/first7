## Malachi 1

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> The burden of the word of the LORD to Israel by Malachi.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> I have loved you, saith the LORD. Yet ye say, Wherein hast thou loved us? Was not Esau Jacob's brother? saith the LORD: yet I loved Jacob,</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And I hated Esau, and laid his mountains and his heritage waste for the dragons of the wilderness.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Whereas Edom saith, We are impoverished, but we will return and build the desolate places; thus saith the LORD of hosts, They shall build, but I will throw down; and they shall call them, The border of wickedness, and, The people against whom the LORD hath indignation for ever.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And your eyes shall see, and ye shall say, The LORD will be magnified from the border of Israel.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> A son honoureth his father, and a servant his master: if then I be a father, where is mine honour? and if I be a master, where is my fear? saith the LORD of hosts unto you, O priests, that despise my name. And ye say, Wherein have we despised thy name?</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Ye offer polluted bread upon mine altar; and ye say, Wherein have we polluted thee? In that ye say, The table of the LORD is contemptible.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And if ye offer the blind for sacrifice, is it not evil? and if ye offer the lame and sick, is it not evil? offer it now unto thy governor; will he be pleased with thee, or accept thy person? saith the LORD of hosts.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And now, I pray you, beseech God that he will be gracious unto us: this hath been by your means: will he regard your persons? saith the LORD of hosts.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Who is there even among you that would shut the doors for nought? neither do ye kindle fire on mine altar for nought. I have no pleasure in you, saith the LORD of hosts, neither will I accept an offering at your hand.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> For from the rising of the sun even unto the going down of the same my name shall be great among the Gentiles; and in every place incense shall be offered unto my name, and a pure offering: for my name shall be great among the heathen, saith the LORD of hosts.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> But ye have profaned it, in that ye say, The table of the LORD is polluted; and the fruit thereof, even his meat, is contemptible.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Ye said also, Behold, what a weariness is it! and ye have snuffed at it, saith the LORD of hosts; and ye brought that which was torn, and the lame, and the sick; thus ye brought an offering: should I accept this of your hand? saith the LORD.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> But cursed be the deceiver, which hath in his flock a male, and voweth, and sacrificeth unto the Lord a corrupt thing: for I am a great King, saith the LORD of hosts, and my name is dreadful among the heathen.</p>
