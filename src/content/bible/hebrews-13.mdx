## Hebrews 13

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Let brotherly love continue.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Be not forgetful to entertain strangers: for thereby some have entertained angels unawares.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Remember them that are in bonds, as bound with them; and them which suffer adversity, as being yourselves also in the body.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Marriage is honourable in all, and the bed undefiled: but whoremongers and adulterers God will judge.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Let your conversation be without covetousness; and be content with such things as ye have: for he hath said, I will never leave thee, nor forsake thee.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> So that we may boldly say, The Lord is my helper, and I will not fear what man shall do unto me.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Remember them which have the rule over you, who have spoken unto you the word of God: whose faith follow, considering the end of their conversation.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Jesus Christ the same yesterday, and to day, and for ever.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Be not carried about with divers and strange doctrines. For it is a good thing that the heart be established with grace; not with meats, which have not profited them that have been occupied therein.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> We have an altar, whereof they have no right to eat which serve the tabernacle.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> For the bodies of those beasts, whose blood is brought into the sanctuary by the high priest for sin, are burned without the camp.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Wherefore Jesus also, that he might sanctify the people with his own blood, suffered without the gate.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Let us go forth therefore unto him without the camp, bearing his reproach.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> For here have we no continuing city, but we seek one to come.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> By him therefore let us offer the sacrifice of praise to God continually, that is, the fruit of our lips giving thanks to his name.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> But to do good and to communicate forget not: for with such sacrifices God is well pleased.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Obey them that have the rule over you, and submit yourselves: for they watch for your souls, as they that must give account, that they may do it with joy, and not with grief: for that is unprofitable for you.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Pray for us: for we trust we have a good conscience, in all things willing to live honestly.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> But I beseech you the rather to do this, that I may be restored to you the sooner.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Now the God of peace, that brought again from the dead our Lord Jesus, that great shepherd of the sheep, through the blood of the everlasting covenant,</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Make you perfect in every good work to do his will, working in you that which is wellpleasing in his sight, through Jesus Christ; to whom be glory for ever and ever. Amen.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And I beseech you, brethren, suffer the word of exhortation: for I have written a letter unto you in few words.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Know ye that our brother Timothy is set at liberty; with whom, if he come shortly, I will see you.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Salute all them that have the rule over you, and all the saints. They of Italy salute you.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Grace be with you all. Amen.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Written to the Hebrews from Italy by Timothy.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> HEBREWS</p>
