## Job 25

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Then answered Bildad the Shuhite, and said,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Dominion and fear are with him, he maketh peace in his high places.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Is there any number of his armies? and upon whom doth not his light arise?</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> How then can man be justified with God? or how can he be clean that is born of a woman?</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Behold even to the moon, and it shineth not; yea, the stars are not pure in his sight.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> How much less man, that is a worm? and the son of man, which is a worm?</p>
