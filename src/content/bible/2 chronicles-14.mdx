## 2 Chronicles 14

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> So Abijah slept with his fathers, and they buried him in the city of David: and Asa his son reigned in his stead. In his days the land was quiet ten years.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And Asa did that which was good and right in the eyes of the LORD his God:</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For he took away the altars of the strange gods, and the high places, and brake down the images, and cut down the groves:</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And commanded Judah to seek the LORD God of their fathers, and to do the law and the commandment.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Also he took away out of all the cities of Judah the high places and the images: and the kingdom was quiet before him.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And he built fenced cities in Judah: for the land had rest, and he had no war in those years; because the LORD had given him rest.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Therefore he said unto Judah, Let us build these cities, and make about them walls, and towers, gates, and bars, while the land is yet before us; because we have sought the LORD our God, we have sought him, and he hath given us rest on every side. So they built and prospered.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And Asa had an army of men that bare targets and spears, out of Judah three hundred thousand; and out of Benjamin, that bare shields and drew bows, two hundred and fourscore thousand: all these were mighty men of valour.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And there came out against them Zerah the Ethiopian with an host of a thousand thousand, and three hundred chariots; and came unto Mareshah.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Then Asa went out against him, and they set the battle in array in the valley of Zephathah at Mareshah.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And Asa cried unto the LORD his God, and said, LORD, it is nothing with thee to help, whether with many, or with them that have no power: help us, O LORD our God; for we rest on thee, and in thy name we go against this multitude. O LORD, thou art our God; let not man prevail against thee.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> So the LORD smote the Ethiopians before Asa, and before Judah; and the Ethiopians fled.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And Asa and the people that were with him pursued them unto Gerar: and the Ethiopians were overthrown, that they could not recover themselves; for they were destroyed before the LORD, and before his host; and they carried away very much spoil.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And they smote all the cities round about Gerar; for the fear of the LORD came upon them: and they spoiled all the cities; for there was exceeding much spoil in them.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> They smote also the tents of cattle, and carried away sheep and camels in abundance, and returned to Jerusalem.</p>
