## 2 Samuel 22

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And David spake unto the LORD the words of this song in the day that the LORD had delivered him out of the hand of all his enemies, and out of the hand of Saul:</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And he said, The LORD is my rock, and my fortress, and my deliverer;</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> The God of my rock; in him will I trust: he is my shield, and the horn of my salvation, my high tower, and my refuge, my saviour; thou savest me from violence.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> I will call on the LORD, who is worthy to be praised: so shall I be saved from mine enemies.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> When the waves of death compassed me, the floods of ungodly men made me afraid;</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> The sorrows of hell compassed me about; the snares of death prevented me;</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> In my distress I called upon the LORD, and cried to my God: and he did hear my voice out of his temple, and my cry did enter into his ears.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Then the earth shook and trembled; the foundations of heaven moved and shook, because he was wroth.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> There went up a smoke out of his nostrils, and fire out of his mouth devoured: coals were kindled by it.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> He bowed the heavens also, and came down; and darkness was under his feet.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And he rode upon a cherub, and did fly: and he was seen upon the wings of the wind.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And he made darkness pavilions round about him, dark waters, and thick clouds of the skies.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Through the brightness before him were coals of fire kindled.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> The LORD thundered from heaven, and the most High uttered his voice.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And he sent out arrows, and scattered them; lightning, and discomfited them.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And the channels of the sea appeared, the foundations of the world were discovered, at the rebuking of the LORD, at the blast of the breath of his nostrils.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> He sent from above, he took me; he drew me out of many waters;</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> He delivered me from my strong enemy, and from them that hated me: for they were too strong for me.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> They prevented me in the day of my calamity: but the LORD was my stay.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> He brought me forth also into a large place: he delivered me, because he delighted in me.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> The LORD rewarded me according to my righteousness: according to the cleanness of my hands hath he recompensed me.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> For I have kept the ways of the LORD, and have not wickedly departed from my God.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> For all his judgments were before me: and as for his statutes, I did not depart from them.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> I was also upright before him, and have kept myself from mine iniquity.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Therefore the LORD hath recompensed me according to my righteousness; according to my cleanness in his eye sight.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> With the merciful thou wilt shew thyself merciful, and with the upright man thou wilt shew thyself upright.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> With the pure thou wilt shew thyself pure; and with the froward thou wilt shew thyself unsavoury.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And the afflicted people thou wilt save: but thine eyes are upon the haughty, that thou mayest bring them down.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> For thou art my lamp, O LORD: and the LORD will lighten my darkness.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> For by thee I have run through a troop: by my God have I leaped over a wall.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> As for God, his way is perfect; the word of the LORD is tried: he is a buckler to all them that trust in him.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> For who is God, save the LORD? and who is a rock, save our God?</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> God is my strength and power: and he maketh my way perfect.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> He maketh my feet like hinds' feet: and setteth me upon my high places.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> He teacheth my hands to war; so that a bow of steel is broken by mine arms.</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> Thou hast also given me the shield of thy salvation: and thy gentleness hath made me great.</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> Thou hast enlarged my steps under me; so that my feet did not slip.</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> I have pursued mine enemies, and destroyed them; and turned not again until I had consumed them.</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> And I have consumed them, and wounded them, that they could not arise: yea, they are fallen under my feet.</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> For thou hast girded me with strength to battle: them that rose up against me hast thou subdued under me.</p>

<p id="v41" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">41</span> Thou hast also given me the necks of mine enemies, that I might destroy them that hate me.</p>

<p id="v42" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">42</span> They looked, but there was none to save; even unto the LORD, but he answered them not.</p>

<p id="v43" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">43</span> Then did I beat them as small as the dust of the earth, I did stamp them as the mire of the street, and did spread them abroad.</p>

<p id="v44" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">44</span> Thou also hast delivered me from the strivings of my people, thou hast kept me to be head of the heathen: a people which I knew not shall serve me.</p>

<p id="v45" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">45</span> Strangers shall submit themselves unto me: as soon as they hear, they shall be obedient unto me.</p>

<p id="v46" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">46</span> Strangers shall fade away, and they shall be afraid out of their close places.</p>

<p id="v47" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">47</span> The LORD liveth; and blessed be my rock; and exalted be the God of the rock of my salvation.</p>

<p id="v48" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">48</span> It is God that avengeth me, and that bringeth down the people under me,</p>

<p id="v49" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">49</span> And that bringeth me forth from mine enemies: thou also hast lifted me up on high above them that rose up against me: thou hast delivered me from the violent man.</p>

<p id="v50" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">50</span> Therefore I will give thanks unto thee, O LORD, among the heathen, and I will sing praises unto thy name.</p>

<p id="v51" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">51</span> He is the tower of salvation for his king: and sheweth mercy to his anointed, unto David, and to his seed for evermore.</p>
