## Proverbs 26

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> As snow in summer, and as rain in harvest, so honour is not seemly for a fool.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> As the bird by wandering, as the swallow by flying, so the curse causeless shall not come.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> A whip for the horse, a bridle for the ass, and a rod for the fool's back.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Answer not a fool according to his folly, lest thou also be like unto him.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Answer a fool according to his folly, lest he be wise in his own conceit.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> He that sendeth a message by the hand of a fool cutteth off the feet, and drinketh damage.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> The legs of the lame are not equal: so is a parable in the mouth of fools.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> As he that bindeth a stone in a sling, so is he that giveth honour to a fool.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> As a thorn goeth up into the hand of a drunkard, so is a parable in the mouth of fools.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> The great God that formed all things both rewardeth the fool, and rewardeth transgressors.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> As a dog returneth to his vomit, so a fool returneth to his folly.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Seest thou a man wise in his own conceit? there is more hope of a fool than of him.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> The slothful man saith, There is a lion in the way; a lion is in the streets.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> As the door turneth upon his hinges, so doth the slothful upon his bed.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> The slothful hideth his hand in his bosom; it grieveth him to bring it again to his mouth.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> The sluggard is wiser in his own conceit than seven men that can render a reason.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> He that passeth by, and meddleth with strife belonging not to him, is like one that taketh a dog by the ears.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> As a mad man who casteth firebrands, arrows, and death,</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> So is the man that deceiveth his neighbour, and saith, Am not I in sport?</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Where no wood is, there the fire goeth out: so where there is no talebearer, the strife ceaseth.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> As coals are to burning coals, and wood to fire; so is a contentious man to kindle strife.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> The words of a talebearer are as wounds, and they go down into the innermost parts of the belly.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Burning lips and a wicked heart are like a potsherd covered with silver dross.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> He that hateth dissembleth with his lips, and layeth up deceit within him;</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> When he speaketh fair, believe him not: for there are seven abominations in his heart.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Whose hatred is covered by deceit, his wickedness shall be shewed before the whole congregation.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Whoso diggeth a pit shall fall therein: and he that rolleth a stone, it will return upon him.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> A lying tongue hateth those that are afflicted by it; and a flattering mouth worketh ruin.</p>
