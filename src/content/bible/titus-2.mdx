## Titus 2

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> But speak thou the things which become sound doctrine:</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> That the aged men be sober, grave, temperate, sound in faith, in charity, in patience.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> The aged women likewise, that they be in behaviour as becometh holiness, not false accusers, not given to much wine, teachers of good things;</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> That they may teach the young women to be sober, to love their husbands, to love their children,</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> To be discreet, chaste, keepers at home, good, obedient to their own husbands, that the word of God be not blasphemed.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Young men likewise exhort to be sober minded.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> In all things shewing thyself a pattern of good works: in doctrine shewing uncorruptness, gravity, sincerity,</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Sound speech, that cannot be condemned; that he that is of the contrary part may be ashamed, having no evil thing to say of you.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Exhort servants to be obedient unto their own masters, and to please them well in all things; not answering again;</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Not purloining, but shewing all good fidelity; that they may adorn the doctrine of God our Saviour in all things.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> For the grace of God that bringeth salvation hath appeared to all men,</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Teaching us that, denying ungodliness and worldly lusts, we should live soberly, righteously, and godly, in this present world;</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Looking for that blessed hope, and the glorious appearing of the great God and our Saviour Jesus Christ;</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Who gave himself for us, that he might redeem us from all iniquity, and purify unto himself a peculiar people, zealous of good works.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> These things speak, and exhort, and rebuke with all authority. Let no man despise thee.</p>
