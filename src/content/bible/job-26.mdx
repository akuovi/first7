## Job 26

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> But Job answered and said,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> How hast thou helped him that is without power? how savest thou the arm that hath no strength?</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> How hast thou counselled him that hath no wisdom? and how hast thou plentifully declared the thing as it is?</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> To whom hast thou uttered words? and whose spirit came from thee?</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Dead things are formed from under the waters, and the inhabitants thereof.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Hell is naked before him, and destruction hath no covering.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> He stretcheth out the north over the empty place, and hangeth the earth upon nothing.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> He bindeth up the waters in his thick clouds; and the cloud is not rent under them.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> He holdeth back the face of his throne, and spreadeth his cloud upon it.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> He hath compassed the waters with bounds, until the day and night come to an end.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> The pillars of heaven tremble and are astonished at his reproof.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> He divideth the sea with his power, and by his understanding he smiteth through the proud.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> By his spirit he hath garnished the heavens; his hand hath formed the crooked serpent.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Lo, these are parts of his ways: but how little a portion is heard of him? but the thunder of his power who can understand?</p>
