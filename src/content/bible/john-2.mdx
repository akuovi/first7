## John 2

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And the third day there was a marriage in Cana of Galilee; and the mother of Jesus was there:</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And both Jesus was called, and his disciples, to the marriage.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And when they wanted wine, the mother of Jesus saith unto him, They have no wine.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Jesus saith unto her, Woman, what have I to do with thee? mine hour is not yet come.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> His mother saith unto the servants, Whatsoever he saith unto you, do it.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And there were set there six waterpots of stone, after the manner of the purifying of the Jews, containing two or three firkins apiece.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Jesus saith unto them, Fill the waterpots with water. And they filled them up to the brim.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And he saith unto them, Draw out now, and bear unto the governor of the feast. And they bare it.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> When the ruler of the feast had tasted the water that was made wine, and knew not whence it was: (but the servants which drew the water knew;) the governor of the feast called the bridegroom,</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And saith unto him, Every man at the beginning doth set forth good wine; and when men have well drunk, then that which is worse: but thou hast kept the good wine until now.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> This beginning of miracles did Jesus in Cana of Galilee, and manifested forth his glory; and his disciples believed on him.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> After this he went down to Capernaum, he, and his mother, and his brethren, and his disciples: and they continued there not many days.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And the Jews' passover was at hand, and Jesus went up to Jerusalem,</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And found in the temple those that sold oxen and sheep and doves, and the changers of money sitting:</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And when he had made a scourge of small cords, he drove them all out of the temple, and the sheep, and the oxen; and poured out the changers' money, and overthrew the tables;</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And said unto them that sold doves, Take these things hence; make not my Father's house an house of merchandise.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And his disciples remembered that it was written, The zeal of thine house hath eaten me up.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Then answered the Jews and said unto him, What sign shewest thou unto us, seeing that thou doest these things?</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Jesus answered and said unto them, Destroy this temple, and in three days I will raise it up.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Then said the Jews, Forty and six years was this temple in building, and wilt thou rear it up in three days?</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> But he spake of the temple of his body.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> When therefore he was risen from the dead, his disciples remembered that he had said this unto them; and they believed the scripture, and the word which Jesus had said.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Now when he was in Jerusalem at the passover, in the feast day, many believed in his name, when they saw the miracles which he did.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> But Jesus did not commit himself unto them, because he knew all men,</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And needed not that any should testify of man: for he knew what was in man.</p>
