## 1 Corinthians 5

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> It is reported commonly that there is fornication among you, and such fornication as is not so much as named among the Gentiles, that one should have his father's wife.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And ye are puffed up, and have not rather mourned, that he that hath done this deed might be taken away from among you.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For I verily, as absent in body, but present in spirit, have judged already, as though I were present, concerning him that hath so done this deed,</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> In the name of our Lord Jesus Christ, when ye are gathered together, and my spirit, with the power of our Lord Jesus Christ,</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> To deliver such an one unto Satan for the destruction of the flesh, that the spirit may be saved in the day of the Lord Jesus.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Your glorying is not good. Know ye not that a little leaven leaveneth the whole lump?</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Purge out therefore the old leaven, that ye may be a new lump, as ye are unleavened. For even Christ our passover is sacrificed for us:</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Therefore let us keep the feast, not with old leaven, neither with the leaven of malice and wickedness; but with the unleavened bread of sincerity and truth.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> I wrote unto you in an epistle not to company with fornicators:</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Yet not altogether with the fornicators of this world, or with the covetous, or extortioners, or with idolaters; for then must ye needs go out of the world.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> But now I have written unto you not to keep company, if any man that is called a brother be a fornicator, or covetous, or an idolater, or a railer, or a drunkard, or an extortioner; with such an one no not to eat.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> For what have I to do to judge them also that are without? do not ye judge them that are within?</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> But them that are without God judgeth. Therefore put away from among yourselves that wicked person.</p>
