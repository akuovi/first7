## 1 Corinthians 13

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Though I speak with the tongues of men and of angels, and have not charity, I am become as sounding brass, or a tinkling cymbal.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And though I have the gift of prophecy, and understand all mysteries, and all knowledge; and though I have all faith, so that I could remove mountains, and have not charity, I am nothing.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And though I bestow all my goods to feed the poor, and though I give my body to be burned, and have not charity, it profiteth me nothing.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Charity suffereth long, and is kind; charity envieth not; charity vaunteth not itself, is not puffed up,</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Doth not behave itself unseemly, seeketh not her own, is not easily provoked, thinketh no evil;</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Rejoiceth not in iniquity, but rejoiceth in the truth;</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Beareth all things, believeth all things, hopeth all things, endureth all things.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Charity never faileth: but whether there be prophecies, they shall fail; whether there be tongues, they shall cease; whether there be knowledge, it shall vanish away.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> For we know in part, and we prophesy in part.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> But when that which is perfect is come, then that which is in part shall be done away.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> When I was a child, I spake as a child, I understood as a child, I thought as a child: but when I became a man, I put away childish things.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> For now we see through a glass, darkly; but then face to face: now I know in part; but then shall I know even as also I am known.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And now abideth faith, hope, charity, these three; but the greatest of these is charity.</p>
