## Job 30

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> But now they that are younger than I have me in derision, whose fathers I would have disdained to have set with the dogs of my flock.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Yea, whereto might the strength of their hands profit me, in whom old age was perished?</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For want and famine they were solitary; fleeing into the wilderness in former time desolate and waste.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Who cut up mallows by the bushes, and juniper roots for their meat.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> They were driven forth from among men, (they cried after them as after a thief;)</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> To dwell in the clifts of the valleys, in caves of the earth, and in the rocks.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Among the bushes they brayed; under the nettles they were gathered together.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> They were children of fools, yea, children of base men: they were viler than the earth.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And now am I their song, yea, I am their byword.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> They abhor me, they flee far from me, and spare not to spit in my face.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Because he hath loosed my cord, and afflicted me, they have also let loose the bridle before me.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Upon my right hand rise the youth; they push away my feet, and they raise up against me the ways of their destruction.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> They mar my path, they set forward my calamity, they have no helper.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> They came upon me as a wide breaking in of waters: in the desolation they rolled themselves upon me.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Terrors are turned upon me: they pursue my soul as the wind: and my welfare passeth away as a cloud.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And now my soul is poured out upon me; the days of affliction have taken hold upon me.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> My bones are pierced in me in the night season: and my sinews take no rest.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> By the great force of my disease is my garment changed: it bindeth me about as the collar of my coat.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> He hath cast me into the mire, and I am become like dust and ashes.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> I cry unto thee, and thou dost not hear me: I stand up, and thou regardest me not.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Thou art become cruel to me: with thy strong hand thou opposest thyself against me.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Thou liftest me up to the wind; thou causest me to ride upon it, and dissolvest my substance.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> For I know that thou wilt bring me to death, and to the house appointed for all living.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Howbeit he will not stretch out his hand to the grave, though they cry in his destruction.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Did not I weep for him that was in trouble? was not my soul grieved for the poor?</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> When I looked for good, then evil came unto me: and when I waited for light, there came darkness.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> My bowels boiled, and rested not: the days of affliction prevented me.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> I went mourning without the sun: I stood up, and I cried in the congregation.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> I am a brother to dragons, and a companion to owls.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> My skin is black upon me, and my bones are burned with heat.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> My harp also is turned to mourning, and my organ into the voice of them that weep.</p>
