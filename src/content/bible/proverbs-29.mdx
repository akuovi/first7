## Proverbs 29

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> He, that being often reproved hardeneth his neck, shall suddenly be destroyed, and that without remedy.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> When the righteous are in authority, the people rejoice: but when the wicked beareth rule, the people mourn.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Whoso loveth wisdom rejoiceth his father: but he that keepeth company with harlots spendeth his substance.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> The king by judgment establisheth the land: but he that receiveth gifts overthroweth it.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> A man that flattereth his neighbour spreadeth a net for his feet.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> In the transgression of an evil man there is a snare: but the righteous doth sing and rejoice.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> The righteous considereth the cause of the poor: but the wicked regardeth not to know it.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Scornful men bring a city into a snare: but wise men turn away wrath.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> If a wise man contendeth with a foolish man, whether he rage or laugh, there is no rest.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> The bloodthirsty hate the upright: but the just seek his soul.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> A fool uttereth all his mind: but a wise man keepeth it in till afterwards.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> If a ruler hearken to lies, all his servants are wicked.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> The poor and the deceitful man meet together: the LORD lighteneth both their eyes.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> The king that faithfully judgeth the poor, his throne shall be established for ever.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> The rod and reproof give wisdom: but a child left to himself bringeth his mother to shame.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> When the wicked are multiplied, transgression increaseth: but the righteous shall see their fall.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Correct thy son, and he shall give thee rest; yea, he shall give delight unto thy soul.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Where there is no vision, the people perish: but he that keepeth the law, happy is he.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> A servant will not be corrected by words: for though he understand he will not answer.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Seest thou a man that is hasty in his words? there is more hope of a fool than of him.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> He that delicately bringeth up his servant from a child shall have him become his son at the length.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> An angry man stirreth up strife, and a furious man aboundeth in transgression.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> A man's pride shall bring him low: but honour shall uphold the humble in spirit.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Whoso is partner with a thief hateth his own soul: he heareth cursing, and bewrayeth it not.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> The fear of man bringeth a snare: but whoso putteth his trust in the LORD shall be safe.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Many seek the ruler's favour; but every man's judgment cometh from the LORD.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> An unjust man is an abomination to the just: and he that is upright in the way is abomination to the wicked.</p>
