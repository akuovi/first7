## Matthew 2

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Now when Jesus was born in Bethlehem of Judaea in the days of Herod the king, behold, there came wise men from the east to Jerusalem,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Saying, Where is he that is born King of the Jews? for we have seen his star in the east, and are come to worship him.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> When Herod the king had heard these things, he was troubled, and all Jerusalem with him.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And when he had gathered all the chief priests and scribes of the people together, he demanded of them where Christ should be born.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And they said unto him, In Bethlehem of Judaea: for thus it is written by the prophet,</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> And thou Bethlehem, in the land of Juda, art not the least among the princes of Juda: for out of thee shall come a Governor, that shall rule my people Israel.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Then Herod, when he had privily called the wise men, enquired of them diligently what time the star appeared.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And he sent them to Bethlehem, and said, Go and search diligently for the young child; and when ye have found him, bring me word again, that I may come and worship him also.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> When they had heard the king, they departed; and, lo, the star, which they saw in the east, went before them, till it came and stood over where the young child was.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> When they saw the star, they rejoiced with exceeding great joy.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And when they were come into the house, they saw the young child with Mary his mother, and fell down, and worshipped him: and when they had opened their treasures, they presented unto him gifts; gold, and frankincense, and myrrh.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And being warned of God in a dream that they should not return to Herod, they departed into their own country another way.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And when they were departed, behold, the angel of the Lord appeareth to Joseph in a dream, saying, Arise, and take the young child and his mother, and flee into Egypt, and be thou there until I bring thee word: for Herod will seek the young child to destroy him.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> When he arose, he took the young child and his mother by night, and departed into Egypt:</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And was there until the death of Herod: that it might be fulfilled which was spoken of the Lord by the prophet, saying, Out of Egypt have I called my son.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Then Herod, when he saw that he was mocked of the wise men, was exceeding wroth, and sent forth, and slew all the children that were in Bethlehem, and in all the coasts thereof, from two years old and under, according to the time which he had diligently enquired of the wise men.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Then was fulfilled that which was spoken by Jeremy the prophet, saying,</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> In Rama was there a voice heard, lamentation, and weeping, and great mourning, Rachel weeping for her children, and would not be comforted, because they are not.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> But when Herod was dead, behold, an angel of the Lord appeareth in a dream to Joseph in Egypt,</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Saying, Arise, and take the young child and his mother, and go into the land of Israel: for they are dead which sought the young child's life.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And he arose, and took the young child and his mother, and came into the land of Israel.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> But when he heard that Archelaus did reign in Judaea in the room of his father Herod, he was afraid to go thither: notwithstanding, being warned of God in a dream, he turned aside into the parts of Galilee:</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And he came and dwelt in a city called Nazareth: that it might be fulfilled which was spoken by the prophets, He shall be called a Nazarene.</p>
