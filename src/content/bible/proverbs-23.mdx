## Proverbs 23

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> When thou sittest to eat with a ruler, consider diligently what is before thee:</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And put a knife to thy throat, if thou be a man given to appetite.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Be not desirous of his dainties: for they are deceitful meat.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Labour not to be rich: cease from thine own wisdom.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Wilt thou set thine eyes upon that which is not? for riches certainly make themselves wings; they fly away as an eagle toward heaven.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Eat thou not the bread of him that hath an evil eye, neither desire thou his dainty meats:</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> For as he thinketh in his heart, so is he: Eat and drink, saith he to thee; but his heart is not with thee.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> The morsel which thou hast eaten shalt thou vomit up, and lose thy sweet words.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Speak not in the ears of a fool: for he will despise the wisdom of thy words.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Remove not the old landmark; and enter not into the fields of the fatherless:</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> For their redeemer is mighty; he shall plead their cause with thee.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Apply thine heart unto instruction, and thine ears to the words of knowledge.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Withhold not correction from the child: for if thou beatest him with the rod, he shall not die.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Thou shalt beat him with the rod, and shalt deliver his soul from hell.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> My son, if thine heart be wise, my heart shall rejoice, even mine.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Yea, my reins shall rejoice, when thy lips speak right things.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Let not thine heart envy sinners: but be thou in the fear of the LORD all the day long.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> For surely there is an end; and thine expectation shall not be cut off.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Hear thou, my son, and be wise, and guide thine heart in the way.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Be not among winebibbers; among riotous eaters of flesh:</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> For the drunkard and the glutton shall come to poverty: and drowsiness shall clothe a man with rags.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Hearken unto thy father that begat thee, and despise not thy mother when she is old.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Buy the truth, and sell it not; also wisdom, and instruction, and understanding.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> The father of the righteous shall greatly rejoice: and he that begetteth a wise child shall have joy of him.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Thy father and thy mother shall be glad, and she that bare thee shall rejoice.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> My son, give me thine heart, and let thine eyes observe my ways.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> For a whore is a deep ditch; and a strange woman is a narrow pit.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> She also lieth in wait as for a prey, and increaseth the transgressors among men.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Who hath woe? who hath sorrow? who hath contentions? who hath babbling? who hath wounds without cause? who hath redness of eyes?</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> They that tarry long at the wine; they that go to seek mixed wine.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Look not thou upon the wine when it is red, when it giveth his colour in the cup, when it moveth itself aright.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> At the last it biteth like a serpent, and stingeth like an adder.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> Thine eyes shall behold strange women, and thine heart shall utter perverse things.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> Yea, thou shalt be as he that lieth down in the midst of the sea, or as he that lieth upon the top of a mast.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> They have stricken me, shalt thou say, and I was not sick; they have beaten me, and I felt it not: when shall I awake? I will seek it yet again.</p>
