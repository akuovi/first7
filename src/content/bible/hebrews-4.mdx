## Hebrews 4

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Let us therefore fear, lest, a promise being left us of entering into his rest, any of you should seem to come short of it.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> For unto us was the gospel preached, as well as unto them: but the word preached did not profit them, not being mixed with faith in them that heard it.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For we which have believed do enter into rest, as he said, As I have sworn in my wrath, if they shall enter into my rest: although the works were finished from the foundation of the world.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> For he spake in a certain place of the seventh day on this wise, And God did rest the seventh day from all his works.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And in this place again, If they shall enter into my rest.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Seeing therefore it remaineth that some must enter therein, and they to whom it was first preached entered not in because of unbelief:</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Again, he limiteth a certain day, saying in David, To day, after so long a time; as it is said, To day if ye will hear his voice, harden not your hearts.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> For if Jesus had given them rest, then would he not afterward have spoken of another day.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> There remaineth therefore a rest to the people of God.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> For he that is entered into his rest, he also hath ceased from his own works, as God did from his.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Let us labour therefore to enter into that rest, lest any man fall after the same example of unbelief.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> For the word of God is quick, and powerful, and sharper than any twoedged sword, piercing even to the dividing asunder of soul and spirit, and of the joints and marrow, and is a discerner of the thoughts and intents of the heart.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Neither is there any creature that is not manifest in his sight: but all things are naked and opened unto the eyes of him with whom we have to do.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Seeing then that we have a great high priest, that is passed into the heavens, Jesus the Son of God, let us hold fast our profession.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> For we have not an high priest which cannot be touched with the feeling of our infirmities; but was in all points tempted like as we are, yet without sin.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Let us therefore come boldly unto the throne of grace, that we may obtain mercy, and find grace to help in time of need.</p>
