## Ephesians 1

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Paul, an apostle of Jesus Christ by the will of God, to the saints which are at Ephesus, and to the faithful in Christ Jesus:</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Grace be to you, and peace, from God our Father, and from the Lord Jesus Christ.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Blessed be the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, who hath blessed us with all spiritual blessings in heavenly places in Christ:</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> According as he hath chosen us in him before the foundation of the world, that we should be holy and without blame before him in love:</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Having predestinated us unto the adoption of children by Jesus Christ to himself, according to the good pleasure of his will,</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> To the praise of the glory of his grace, wherein he hath made us accepted in the beloved.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> In whom we have redemption through his blood, the forgiveness of sins, according to the riches of his grace;</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Wherein he hath abounded toward us in all wisdom and prudence;</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Having made known unto us the mystery of his will, according to his good pleasure which he hath purposed in himself:</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> That in the dispensation of the fulness of times he might gather together in one all things in Christ, both which are in heaven, and which are on earth; even in him:</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> In whom also we have obtained an inheritance, being predestinated according to the purpose of him who worketh all things after the counsel of his own will:</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> That we should be to the praise of his glory, who first trusted in Christ.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> In whom ye also trusted, after that ye heard the word of truth, the gospel of your salvation: in whom also after that ye believed, ye were sealed with that holy Spirit of promise,</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Which is the earnest of our inheritance until the redemption of the purchased possession, unto the praise of his glory.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Wherefore I also, after I heard of your faith in the Lord Jesus, and love unto all the saints,</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Cease not to give thanks for you, making mention of you in my prayers;</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> That the God of our Lord Jesus Christ, the Father of glory, may give unto you the spirit of wisdom and revelation in the knowledge of him:</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> The eyes of your understanding being enlightened; that ye may know what is the hope of his calling, and what the riches of the glory of his inheritance in the saints,</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And what is the exceeding greatness of his power to us-ward who believe, according to the working of his mighty power,</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Which he wrought in Christ, when he raised him from the dead, and set him at his own right hand in the heavenly places,</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Far above all principality, and power, and might, and dominion, and every name that is named, not only in this world, but also in that which is to come:</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And hath put all things under his feet, and gave him to be the head over all things to the church,</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Which is his body, the fulness of him that filleth all in all.</p>
