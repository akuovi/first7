## SongofSolomon 1

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> The song of songs, which is Solomon's.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Let him kiss me with the kisses of his mouth: for thy love is better than wine.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Because of the savour of thy good ointments thy name is as ointment poured forth, therefore do the virgins love thee.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Draw me, we will run after thee: the king hath brought me into his chambers: we will be glad and rejoice in thee, we will remember thy love more than wine: the upright love thee.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> I am black, but comely, O ye daughters of Jerusalem, as the tents of Kedar, as the curtains of Solomon.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Look not upon me, because I am black, because the sun hath looked upon me: my mother's children were angry with me; they made me the keeper of the vineyards; but mine own vineyard have I not kept.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Tell me, O thou whom my soul loveth, where thou feedest, where thou makest thy flock to rest at noon: for why should I be as one that turneth aside by the flocks of thy companions?</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> If thou know not, O thou fairest among women, go thy way forth by the footsteps of the flock, and feed thy kids beside the shepherds' tents.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> I have compared thee, O my love, to a company of horses in Pharaoh's chariots.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Thy cheeks are comely with rows of jewels, thy neck with chains of gold.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> We will make thee borders of gold with studs of silver.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> While the king sitteth at his table, my spikenard sendeth forth the smell thereof.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> A bundle of myrrh is my wellbeloved unto me; he shall lie all night betwixt my breasts.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> My beloved is unto me as a cluster of camphire in the vineyards of En-gedi.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Behold, thou art fair, my love; behold, thou art fair; thou hast doves' eyes.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Behold, thou art fair, my beloved, yea, pleasant: also our bed is green.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> The beams of our house are cedar, and our rafters of fir.</p>
