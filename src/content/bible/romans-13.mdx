## Romans 13

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Let every soul be subject unto the higher powers. For there is no power but of God: the powers that be are ordained of God.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Whosoever therefore resisteth the power, resisteth the ordinance of God: and they that resist shall receive to themselves damnation.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For rulers are not a terror to good works, but to the evil. Wilt thou then not be afraid of the power? do that which is good, and thou shalt have praise of the same:</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> For he is the minister of God to thee for good. But if thou do that which is evil, be afraid; for he beareth not the sword in vain: for he is the minister of God, a revenger to execute wrath upon him that doeth evil.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Wherefore ye must needs be subject, not only for wrath, but also for conscience sake.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> For for this cause pay ye tribute also: for they are God's ministers, attending continually upon this very thing.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Render therefore to all their dues: tribute to whom tribute is due; custom to whom custom; fear to whom fear; honour to whom honour.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Owe no man any thing, but to love one another: for he that loveth another hath fulfilled the law.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> For this, Thou shalt not commit adultery, Thou shalt not kill, Thou shalt not steal, Thou shalt not bear false witness, Thou shalt not covet; and if there be any other commandment, it is briefly comprehended in this saying, namely, Thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Love worketh no ill to his neighbour: therefore love is the fulfilling of the law.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And that, knowing the time, that now it is high time to awake out of sleep: for now is our salvation nearer than when we believed.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> The night is far spent, the day is at hand: let us therefore cast off the works of darkness, and let us put on the armour of light.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Let us walk honestly, as in the day; not in rioting and drunkenness, not in chambering and wantonness, not in strife and envying.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> But put ye on the Lord Jesus Christ, and make not provision for the flesh, to fulfil the lusts thereof.</p>
