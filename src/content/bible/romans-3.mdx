## Romans 3

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> What advantage then hath the Jew? or what profit is there of circumcision?</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Much every way: chiefly, because that unto them were committed the oracles of God.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For what if some did not believe? shall their unbelief make the faith of God without effect?</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> God forbid: yea, let God be true, but every man a liar; as it is written, That thou mightest be justified in thy sayings, and mightest overcome when thou art judged.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> But if our unrighteousness commend the righteousness of God, what shall we say? Is God unrighteous who taketh vengeance? (I speak as a man)</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> God forbid: for then how shall God judge the world?</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> For if the truth of God hath more abounded through my lie unto his glory; why yet am I also judged as a sinner?</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And not rather, (as we be slanderously reported, and as some affirm that we say,) Let us do evil, that good may come? whose damnation is just.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> What then? are we better than they? No, in no wise: for we have before proved both Jews and Gentiles, that they are all under sin;</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> As it is written, There is none righteous, no, not one:</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> There is none that understandeth, there is none that seeketh after God.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> They are all gone out of the way, they are together become unprofitable; there is none that doeth good, no, not one.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Their throat is an open sepulchre; with their tongues they have used deceit; the poison of asps is under their lips:</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Whose mouth is full of cursing and bitterness:</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Their feet are swift to shed blood:</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Destruction and misery are in their ways:</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And the way of peace have they not known:</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> There is no fear of God before their eyes.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Now we know that what things soever the law saith, it saith to them who are under the law: that every mouth may be stopped, and all the world may become guilty before God.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Therefore by the deeds of the law there shall no flesh be justified in his sight: for by the law is the knowledge of sin.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> But now the righteousness of God without the law is manifested, being witnessed by the law and the prophets;</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Even the righteousness of God which is by faith of Jesus Christ unto all and upon all them that believe: for there is no difference:</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> For all have sinned, and come short of the glory of God;</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Being justified freely by his grace through the redemption that is in Christ Jesus:</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Whom God hath set forth to be a propitiation through faith in his blood, to declare his righteousness for the remission of sins that are past, through the forbearance of God;</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> To declare, I say, at this time his righteousness: that he might be just, and the justifier of him which believeth in Jesus.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Where is boasting then? It is excluded. By what law? of works? Nay: but by the law of faith.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Therefore we conclude that a man is justified by faith without the deeds of the law.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Is he the God of the Jews only? is he not also of the Gentiles? Yes, of the Gentiles also:</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Seeing it is one God, which shall justify the circumcision by faith, and uncircumcision through faith.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Do we then make void the law through faith? God forbid: yea, we establish the law.</p>
