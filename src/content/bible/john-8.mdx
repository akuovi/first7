## John 8

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Jesus went unto the mount of Olives.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And early in the morning he came again into the temple, and all the people came unto him; and he sat down, and taught them.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And the scribes and Pharisees brought unto him a woman taken in adultery; and when they had set her in the midst,</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> They say unto him, Master, this woman was taken in adultery, in the very act.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Now Moses in the law commanded us, that such should be stoned: but what sayest thou?</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> This they said, tempting him, that they might have to accuse him. But Jesus stooped down, and with his finger wrote on the ground, as though he heard them not.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> So when they continued asking him, he lifted up himself, and said unto them, He that is without sin among you, let him first cast a stone at her.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And again he stooped down, and wrote on the ground.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And they which heard it, being convicted by their own conscience, went out one by one, beginning at the eldest, even unto the last: and Jesus was left alone, and the woman standing in the midst.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> When Jesus had lifted up himself, and saw none but the woman, he said unto her, Woman, where are those thine accusers? hath no man condemned thee?</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> She said, No man, Lord. And Jesus said unto her, Neither do I condemn thee: go, and sin no more.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Then spake Jesus again unto them, saying, I am the light of the world: he that followeth me shall not walk in darkness, but shall have the light of life.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> The Pharisees therefore said unto him, Thou bearest record of thyself; thy record is not true.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Jesus answered and said unto them, Though I bear record of myself, yet my record is true: for I know whence I came, and whither I go; but ye cannot tell whence I come, and whither I go.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Ye judge after the flesh; I judge no man.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And yet if I judge, my judgment is true: for I am not alone, but I and the Father that sent me.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> It is also written in your law, that the testimony of two men is true.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> I am one that bear witness of myself, and the Father that sent me beareth witness of me.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Then said they unto him, Where is thy Father? Jesus answered, Ye neither know me, nor my Father: if ye had known me, ye should have known my Father also.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> These words spake Jesus in the treasury, as he taught in the temple: and no man laid hands on him; for his hour was not yet come.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Then said Jesus again unto them, I go my way, and ye shall seek me, and shall die in your sins: whither I go, ye cannot come.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Then said the Jews, Will he kill himself? because he saith, Whither I go, ye cannot come.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And he said unto them, Ye are from beneath; I am from above: ye are of this world; I am not of this world.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> I said therefore unto you, that ye shall die in your sins: for if ye believe not that I am he, ye shall die in your sins.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Then said they unto him, Who art thou? And Jesus saith unto them, Even the same that I said unto you from the beginning.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> I have many things to say and to judge of you: but he that sent me is true; and I speak to the world those things which I have heard of him.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> They understood not that he spake to them of the Father.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Then said Jesus unto them, When ye have lifted up the Son of man, then shall ye know that I am he, and that I do nothing of myself; but as my Father hath taught me, I speak these things.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And he that sent me is with me: the Father hath not left me alone; for I do always those things that please him.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> As he spake these words, many believed on him.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Then said Jesus to those Jews which believed on him, If ye continue in my word, then are ye my disciples indeed;</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> And ye shall know the truth, and the truth shall make you free.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> They answered him, We be Abraham's seed, and were never in bondage to any man: how sayest thou, Ye shall be made free?</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> Jesus answered them, Verily, verily, I say unto you, Whosoever committeth sin is the servant of sin.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> And the servant abideth not in the house for ever: but the Son abideth ever.</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> If the Son therefore shall make you free, ye shall be free indeed.</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> I know that ye are Abraham's seed; but ye seek to kill me, because my word hath no place in you.</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> I speak that which I have seen with my Father: and ye do that which ye have seen with your father.</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> They answered and said unto him, Abraham is our father. Jesus saith unto them, If ye were Abraham's children, ye would do the works of Abraham.</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> But now ye seek to kill me, a man that hath told you the truth, which I have heard of God: this did not Abraham.</p>

<p id="v41" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">41</span> Ye do the deeds of your father. Then said they to him, We be not born of fornication; we have one Father, even God.</p>

<p id="v42" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">42</span> Jesus said unto them, If God were your Father, ye would love me: for I proceeded forth and came from God; neither came I of myself, but he sent me.</p>

<p id="v43" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">43</span> Why do ye not understand my speech? even because ye cannot hear my word.</p>

<p id="v44" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">44</span> Ye are of your father the devil, and the lusts of your father ye will do. He was a murderer from the beginning, and abode not in the truth, because there is no truth in him. When he speaketh a lie, he speaketh of his own: for he is a liar, and the father of it.</p>

<p id="v45" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">45</span> And because I tell you the truth, ye believe me not.</p>

<p id="v46" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">46</span> Which of you convinceth me of sin? And if I say the truth, why do ye not believe me?</p>

<p id="v47" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">47</span> He that is of God heareth God's words: ye therefore hear them not, because ye are not of God.</p>

<p id="v48" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">48</span> Then answered the Jews, and said unto him, Say we not well that thou art a Samaritan, and hast a devil?</p>

<p id="v49" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">49</span> Jesus answered, I have not a devil; but I honour my Father, and ye do dishonour me.</p>

<p id="v50" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">50</span> And I seek not mine own glory: there is one that seeketh and judgeth.</p>

<p id="v51" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">51</span> Verily, verily, I say unto you, If a man keep my saying, he shall never see death.</p>

<p id="v52" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">52</span> Then said the Jews unto him, Now we know that thou hast a devil. Abraham is dead, and the prophets; and thou sayest, If a man keep my saying, he shall never taste of death.</p>

<p id="v53" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">53</span> Art thou greater than our father Abraham, which is dead? and the prophets are dead: whom makest thou thyself?</p>

<p id="v54" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">54</span> Jesus answered, If I honour myself, my honour is nothing: it is my Father that honoureth me; of whom ye say, that he is your God:</p>

<p id="v55" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">55</span> Yet ye have not known him; but I know him: and if I should say, I know him not, I shall be a liar like unto you: but I know him, and keep his saying.</p>

<p id="v56" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">56</span> Your father Abraham rejoiced to see my day: and he saw it, and was glad.</p>

<p id="v57" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">57</span> Then said the Jews unto him, Thou art not yet fifty years old, and hast thou seen Abraham?</p>

<p id="v58" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">58</span> Jesus said unto them, Verily, verily, I say unto you, Before Abraham was, I am.</p>

<p id="v59" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">59</span> Then took they up stones to cast at him: but Jesus hid himself, and went out of the temple, going through the midst of them, and so passed by.</p>
