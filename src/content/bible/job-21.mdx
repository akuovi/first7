## Job 21

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> But Job answered and said,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Hear diligently my speech, and let this be your consolations.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Suffer me that I may speak; and after that I have spoken, mock on.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> As for me, is my complaint to man? and if it were so, why should not my spirit be troubled?</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Mark me, and be astonished, and lay your hand upon your mouth.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Even when I remember I am afraid, and trembling taketh hold on my flesh.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Wherefore do the wicked live, become old, yea, are mighty in power?</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Their seed is established in their sight with them, and their offspring before their eyes.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Their houses are safe from fear, neither is the rod of God upon them.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Their bull gendereth, and faileth not; their cow calveth, and casteth not her calf.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> They send forth their little ones like a flock, and their children dance.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> They take the timbrel and harp, and rejoice at the sound of the organ.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> They spend their days in wealth, and in a moment go down to the grave.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Therefore they say unto God, Depart from us; for we desire not the knowledge of thy ways.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> What is the Almighty, that we should serve him? and what profit should we have, if we pray unto him?</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Lo, their good is not in their hand: the counsel of the wicked is far from me.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> How oft is the candle of the wicked put out! and how oft cometh their destruction upon them! God distributeth sorrows in his anger.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> They are as stubble before the wind, and as chaff that the storm carrieth away.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> God layeth up his iniquity for his children: he rewardeth him, and he shall know it.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> His eyes shall see his destruction, and he shall drink of the wrath of the Almighty.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> For what pleasure hath he in his house after him, when the number of his months is cut off in the midst?</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Shall any teach God knowledge? seeing he judgeth those that are high.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> One dieth in his full strength, being wholly at ease and quiet.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> His breasts are full of milk, and his bones are moistened with marrow.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And another dieth in the bitterness of his soul, and never eateth with pleasure.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> They shall lie down alike in the dust, and the worms shall cover them.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Behold, I know your thoughts, and the devices which ye wrongfully imagine against me.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> For ye say, Where is the house of the prince? and where are the dwelling places of the wicked?</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Have ye not asked them that go by the way? and do ye not know their tokens,</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> That the wicked is reserved to the day of destruction? they shall be brought forth to the day of wrath.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Who shall declare his way to his face? and who shall repay him what he hath done?</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> Yet shall he be brought to the grave, and shall remain in the tomb.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> The clods of the valley shall be sweet unto him, and every man shall draw after him, as there are innumerable before him.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> How then comfort ye me in vain, seeing in your answers there remaineth falsehood?</p>
