## 2 Samuel 7

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And it came to pass, when the king sat in his house, and the LORD had given him rest round about from all his enemies;</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> That the king said unto Nathan the prophet, See now, I dwell in an house of cedar, but the ark of God dwelleth within curtains.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And Nathan said to the king, Go, do all that is in thine heart; for the LORD is with thee.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And it came to pass that night, that the word of the LORD came unto Nathan, saying,</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Go and tell my servant David, Thus saith the LORD, Shalt thou build me an house for me to dwell in?</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Whereas I have not dwelt in any house since the time that I brought up the children of Israel out of Egypt, even to this day, but have walked in a tent and in a tabernacle.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> In all the places wherein I have walked with all the children of Israel spake I a word with any of the tribes of Israel, whom I commanded to feed my people Israel, saying, Why build ye not me an house of cedar?</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Now therefore so shalt thou say unto my servant David, Thus saith the LORD of hosts, I took thee from the sheepcote, from following the sheep, to be ruler over my people, over Israel:</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And I was with thee whithersoever thou wentest, and have cut off all thine enemies out of thy sight, and have made thee a great name, like unto the name of the great men that are in the earth.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Moreover I will appoint a place for my people Israel, and will plant them, that they may dwell in a place of their own, and move no more; neither shall the children of wickedness afflict them any more, as beforetime,</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And as since the time that I commanded judges to be over my people Israel, and have caused thee to rest from all thine enemies. Also the LORD telleth thee that he will make thee an house.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And when thy days be fulfilled, and thou shalt sleep with thy fathers, I will set up thy seed after thee, which shall proceed out of thy bowels, and I will establish his kingdom.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> He shall build an house for my name, and I will stablish the throne of his kingdom for ever.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> I will be his father, and he shall be my son. If he commit iniquity, I will chasten him with the rod of men, and with the stripes of the children of men:</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> But my mercy shall not depart away from him, as I took it from Saul, whom I put away before thee.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And thine house and thy kingdom shall be established for ever before thee: thy throne shall be established for ever.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> According to all these words, and according to all this vision, so did Nathan speak unto David.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Then went king David in, and sat before the LORD, and he said, Who am I, O Lord GOD? and what is my house, that thou hast brought me hitherto?</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> And this was yet a small thing in thy sight, O Lord GOD; but thou hast spoken also of thy servant's house for a great while to come. And is this the manner of man, O Lord GOD?</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And what can David say more unto thee? for thou, Lord GOD, knowest thy servant.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> For thy word's sake, and according to thine own heart, hast thou done all these great things, to make thy servant know them.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Wherefore thou art great, O LORD God: for there is none like thee, neither is there any God beside thee, according to all that we have heard with our ears.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And what one nation in the earth is like thy people, even like Israel, whom God went to redeem for a people to himself, and to make him a name, and to do for you great things and terrible, for thy land, before thy people, which thou redeemedst to thee from Egypt, from the nations and their gods?</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> For thou hast confirmed to thyself thy people Israel to be a people unto thee for ever: and thou, LORD, art become their God.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And now, O LORD God, the word that thou hast spoken concerning thy servant, and concerning his house, establish it for ever, and do as thou hast said.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And let thy name be magnified for ever, saying, The LORD of hosts is the God over Israel: and let the house of thy servant David be established before thee.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> For thou, O LORD of hosts, God of Israel, hast revealed to thy servant, saying, I will build thee an house: therefore hath thy servant found in his heart to pray this prayer unto thee.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And now, O Lord GOD, thou art that God, and thy words be true, and thou hast promised this goodness unto thy servant:</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Therefore now let it please thee to bless the house of thy servant, that it may continue for ever before thee: for thou, O Lord GOD, hast spoken it: and with thy blessing let the house of thy servant be blessed for ever.</p>
