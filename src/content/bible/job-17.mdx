## Job 17

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> My breath is corrupt, my days are extinct, the graves are ready for me.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Are there not mockers with me? and doth not mine eye continue in their provocation?</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Lay down now, put me in a surety with thee; who is he that will strike hands with me?</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> For thou hast hid their heart from understanding: therefore shalt thou not exalt them.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> He that speaketh flattery to his friends, even the eyes of his children shall fail.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> He hath made me also a byword of the people; and aforetime I was as a tabret.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Mine eye also is dim by reason of sorrow, and all my members are as a shadow.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Upright men shall be astonied at this, and the innocent shall stir up himself against the hypocrite.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> The righteous also shall hold on his way, and he that hath clean hands shall be stronger and stronger.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> But as for you all, do ye return, and come now: for I cannot find one wise man among you.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> My days are past, my purposes are broken off, even the thoughts of my heart.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> They change the night into day: the light is short because of darkness.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> If I wait, the grave is mine house: I have made my bed in the darkness.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> I have said to corruption, Thou art my father: to the worm, Thou art my mother, and my sister.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And where is now my hope? as for my hope, who shall see it?</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> They shall go down to the bars of the pit, when our rest together is in the dust.</p>
