## Proverbs 2

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> My son, if thou wilt receive my words, and hide my commandments with thee;</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> So that thou incline thine ear unto wisdom, and apply thine heart to understanding;</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Yea, if thou criest after knowledge, and liftest up thy voice for understanding;</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> If thou seekest her as silver, and searchest for her as for hid treasures;</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Then shalt thou understand the fear of the LORD, and find the knowledge of God.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> For the LORD giveth wisdom: out of his mouth cometh knowledge and understanding.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> He layeth up sound wisdom for the righteous: he is a buckler to them that walk uprightly.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> He keepeth the paths of judgment, and preserveth the way of his saints.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Then shalt thou understand righteousness, and judgment, and equity; yea, every good path.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> When wisdom entereth into thine heart, and knowledge is pleasant unto thy soul;</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Discretion shall preserve thee, understanding shall keep thee:</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> To deliver thee from the way of the evil man, from the man that speaketh froward things;</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Who leave the paths of uprightness, to walk in the ways of darkness;</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Who rejoice to do evil, and delight in the frowardness of the wicked;</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Whose ways are crooked, and they froward in their paths:</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> To deliver thee from the strange woman, even from the stranger which flattereth with her words;</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Which forsaketh the guide of her youth, and forgetteth the covenant of her God.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> For her house inclineth unto death, and her paths unto the dead.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> None that go unto her return again, neither take they hold of the paths of life.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> That thou mayest walk in the way of good men, and keep the paths of the righteous.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> For the upright shall dwell in the land, and the perfect shall remain in it.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> But the wicked shall be cut off from the earth, and the transgressors shall be rooted out of it.</p>
