## Job 27

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Moreover Job continued his parable, and said,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> As God liveth, who hath taken away my judgment; and the Almighty, who hath vexed my soul;</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> All the while my breath is in me, and the spirit of God is in my nostrils;</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> My lips shall not speak wickedness, nor my tongue utter deceit.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> God forbid that I should justify you: till I die I will not remove mine integrity from me.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> My righteousness I hold fast, and will not let it go: my heart shall not reproach me so long as I live.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Let mine enemy be as the wicked, and he that riseth up against me as the unrighteous.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> For what is the hope of the hypocrite, though he hath gained, when God taketh away his soul?</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Will God hear his cry when trouble cometh upon him?</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Will he delight himself in the Almighty? will he always call upon God?</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> I will teach you by the hand of God: that which is with the Almighty will I not conceal.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Behold, all ye yourselves have seen it; why then are ye thus altogether vain?</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> This is the portion of a wicked man with God, and the heritage of oppressors, which they shall receive of the Almighty.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> If his children be multiplied, it is for the sword: and his offspring shall not be satisfied with bread.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Those that remain of him shall be buried in death: and his widows shall not weep.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Though he heap up silver as the dust, and prepare raiment as the clay;</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> He may prepare it, but the just shall put it on, and the innocent shall divide the silver.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> He buildeth his house as a moth, and as a booth that the keeper maketh.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> The rich man shall lie down, but he shall not be gathered: he openeth his eyes, and he is not.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Terrors take hold on him as waters, a tempest stealeth him away in the night.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> The east wind carrieth him away, and he departeth: and as a storm hurleth him out of his place.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> For God shall cast upon him, and not spare: he would fain flee out of his hand.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Men shall clap their hands at him, and shall hiss him out of his place.</p>
