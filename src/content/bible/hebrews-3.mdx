## Hebrews 3

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Wherefore, holy brethren, partakers of the heavenly calling, consider the Apostle and High Priest of our profession, Christ Jesus;</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Who was faithful to him that appointed him, as also Moses was faithful in all his house.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For this man was counted worthy of more glory than Moses, inasmuch as he who hath builded the house hath more honour than the house.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> For every house is builded by some man; but he that built all things is God.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And Moses verily was faithful in all his house, as a servant, for a testimony of those things which were to be spoken after;</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> But Christ as a son over his own house; whose house are we, if we hold fast the confidence and the rejoicing of the hope firm unto the end.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Wherefore (as the Holy Ghost saith, To day if ye will hear his voice,</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Harden not your hearts, as in the provocation, in the day of temptation in the wilderness:</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> When your fathers tempted me, proved me, and saw my works forty years.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Wherefore I was grieved with that generation, and said, They do alway err in their heart; and they have not known my ways.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> So I sware in my wrath, They shall not enter into my rest.)</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Take heed, brethren, lest there be in any of you an evil heart of unbelief, in departing from the living God.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> But exhort one another daily, while it is called To day; lest any of you be hardened through the deceitfulness of sin.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> For we are made partakers of Christ, if we hold the beginning of our confidence stedfast unto the end;</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> While it is said, To day if ye will hear his voice, harden not your hearts, as in the provocation.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> For some, when they had heard, did provoke: howbeit not all that came out of Egypt by Moses.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> But with whom was he grieved forty years? was it not with them that had sinned, whose carcases fell in the wilderness?</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> And to whom sware he that they should not enter into his rest, but to them that believed not?</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> So we see that they could not enter in because of unbelief.</p>
