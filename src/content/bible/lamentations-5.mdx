## Lamentations 5

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Remember, O LORD, what is come upon us: consider, and behold our reproach.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Our inheritance is turned to strangers, our houses to aliens.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> We are orphans and fatherless, our mothers are as widows.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> We have drunken our water for money; our wood is sold unto us.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Our necks are under persecution: we labour, and have no rest.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> We have given the hand to the Egyptians, and to the Assyrians, to be satisfied with bread.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Our fathers have sinned, and are not; and we have borne their iniquities.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Servants have ruled over us: there is none that doth deliver us out of their hand.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> We gat our bread with the peril of our lives because of the sword of the wilderness.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Our skin was black like an oven because of the terrible famine.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> They ravished the women in Zion, and the maids in the cities of Judah.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Princes are hanged up by their hand: the faces of elders were not honoured.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> They took the young men to grind, and the children fell under the wood.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> The elders have ceased from the gate, the young men from their musick.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> The joy of our heart is ceased; our dance is turned into mourning.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> The crown is fallen from our head: woe unto us, that we have sinned!</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> For this our heart is faint; for these things our eyes are dim.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Because of the mountain of Zion, which is desolate, the foxes walk upon it.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Thou, O LORD, remainest for ever; thy throne from generation to generation.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Wherefore dost thou forget us for ever, and forsake us so long time?</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Turn thou us unto thee, O LORD, and we shall be turned; renew our days as of old.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> But thou hast utterly rejected us; thou art very wroth against us.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> LAMENTATIONS</p>
