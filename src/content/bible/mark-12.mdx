## Mark 12

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And he began to speak unto them by parables. A certain man planted a vineyard, and set an hedge about it, and digged a place for the winefat, and built a tower, and let it out to husbandmen, and went into a far country.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> And at the season he sent to the husbandmen a servant, that he might receive from the husbandmen of the fruit of the vineyard.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And they caught him, and beat him, and sent him away empty.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And again he sent unto them another servant; and at him they cast stones, and wounded him in the head, and sent him away shamefully handled.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And again he sent another; and him they killed, and many others; beating some, and killing some.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Having yet therefore one son, his wellbeloved, he sent him also last unto them, saying, They will reverence my son.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> But those husbandmen said among themselves, This is the heir; come, let us kill him, and the inheritance shall be our's.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And they took him, and killed him, and cast him out of the vineyard.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> What shall therefore the lord of the vineyard do? he will come and destroy the husbandmen, and will give the vineyard unto others.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And have ye not read this scripture; The stone which the builders rejected is become the head of the corner:</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> This was the Lord's doing, and it is marvellous in our eyes?</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And they sought to lay hold on him, but feared the people: for they knew that he had spoken the parable against them: and they left him, and went their way.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And they send unto him certain of the Pharisees and of the Herodians, to catch him in his words.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And when they were come, they say unto him, Master, we know that thou art true, and carest for no man: for thou regardest not the person of men, but teachest the way of God in truth: Is it lawful to give tribute to Caesar, or not?</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Shall we give, or shall we not give? But he, knowing their hypocrisy, said unto them, Why tempt ye me? bring me a penny, that I may see it.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And they brought it. And he saith unto them, Whose is this image and superscription? And they said unto him, Caesar's.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And Jesus answering said unto them, Render to Caesar the things that are Caesar's, and to God the things that are God's. And they marvelled at him.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Then come unto him the Sadducees, which say there is no resurrection; and they asked him, saying,</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Master, Moses wrote unto us, If a man's brother die, and leave his wife behind him, and leave no children, that his brother should take his wife, and raise up seed unto his brother.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Now there were seven brethren: and the first took a wife, and dying left no seed.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> And the second took her, and died, neither left he any seed: and the third likewise.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And the seven had her, and left no seed: last of all the woman died also.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> In the resurrection therefore, when they shall rise, whose wife shall she be of them? for the seven had her to wife.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And Jesus answering said unto them, Do ye not therefore err, because ye know not the scriptures, neither the power of God?</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> For when they shall rise from the dead, they neither marry, nor are given in marriage; but are as the angels which are in heaven.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And as touching the dead, that they rise: have ye not read in the book of Moses, how in the bush God spake unto him, saying, I am the God of Abraham, and the God of Isaac, and the God of Jacob?</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> He is not the God of the dead, but the God of the living: ye therefore do greatly err.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And one of the scribes came, and having heard them reasoning together, and perceiving that he had answered them well, asked him, Which is the first commandment of all?</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> And Jesus answered him, The first of all the commandments is, Hear, O Israel; The Lord our God is one Lord:</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And thou shalt love the Lord thy God with all thy heart, and with all thy soul, and with all thy mind, and with all thy strength: this is the first commandment.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> And the second is like, namely this, Thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself. There is none other commandment greater than these.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> And the scribe said unto him, Well, Master, thou hast said the truth: for there is one God; and there is none other but he:</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> And to love him with all the heart, and with all the understanding, and with all the soul, and with all the strength, and to love his neighbour as himself, is more than all whole burnt offerings and sacrifices.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> And when Jesus saw that he answered discreetly, he said unto him, Thou art not far from the kingdom of God. And no man after that durst ask him any question.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> And Jesus answered and said, while he taught in the temple, How say the scribes that Christ is the Son of David?</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> For David himself said by the Holy Ghost, The LORD said to my Lord, Sit thou on my right hand, till I make thine enemies thy footstool.</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> David therefore himself calleth him Lord; and whence is he then his son? And the common people heard him gladly.</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> And he said unto them in his doctrine, Beware of the scribes, which love to go in long clothing, and love salutations in the marketplaces,</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> And the chief seats in the synagogues, and the uppermost rooms at feasts:</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> Which devour widows' houses, and for a pretence make long prayers: these shall receive greater damnation.</p>

<p id="v41" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">41</span> And Jesus sat over against the treasury, and beheld how the people cast money into the treasury: and many that were rich cast in much.</p>

<p id="v42" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">42</span> And there came a certain poor widow, and she threw in two mites, which make a farthing.</p>

<p id="v43" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">43</span> And he called unto him his disciples, and saith unto them, Verily I say unto you, That this poor widow hath cast more in, than all they which have cast into the treasury:</p>

<p id="v44" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">44</span> For all they did cast in of their abundance; but she of her want did cast in all that she had, even all her living.</p>
