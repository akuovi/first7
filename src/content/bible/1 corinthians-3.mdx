## 1 Corinthians 3

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And I, brethren, could not speak unto you as unto spiritual, but as unto carnal, even as unto babes in Christ.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> I have fed you with milk, and not with meat: for hitherto ye were not able to bear it, neither yet now are ye able.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> For ye are yet carnal: for whereas there is among you envying, and strife, and divisions, are ye not carnal, and walk as men?</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> For while one saith, I am of Paul; and another, I am of Apollos; are ye not carnal?</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Who then is Paul, and who is Apollos, but ministers by whom ye believed, even as the Lord gave to every man?</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> I have planted, Apollos watered; but God gave the increase.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> So then neither is he that planteth any thing, neither he that watereth; but God that giveth the increase.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Now he that planteth and he that watereth are one: and every man shall receive his own reward according to his own labour.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> For we are labourers together with God: ye are God's husbandry, ye are God's building.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> According to the grace of God which is given unto me, as a wise masterbuilder, I have laid the foundation, and another buildeth thereon. But let every man take heed how he buildeth thereupon.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> For other foundation can no man lay than that is laid, which is Jesus Christ.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Now if any man build upon this foundation gold, silver, precious stones, wood, hay, stubble;</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Every man's work shall be made manifest: for the day shall declare it, because it shall be revealed by fire; and the fire shall try every man's work of what sort it is.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> If any man's work abide which he hath built thereupon, he shall receive a reward.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> If any man's work shall be burned, he shall suffer loss: but he himself shall be saved; yet so as by fire.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Know ye not that ye are the temple of God, and that the Spirit of God dwelleth in you?</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> If any man defile the temple of God, him shall God destroy; for the temple of God is holy, which temple ye are.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Let no man deceive himself. If any man among you seemeth to be wise in this world, let him become a fool, that he may be wise.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> For the wisdom of this world is foolishness with God. For it is written, He taketh the wise in their own craftiness.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And again, The Lord knoweth the thoughts of the wise, that they are vain.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Therefore let no man glory in men. For all things are your's;</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Whether Paul, or Apollos, or Cephas, or the world, or life, or death, or things present, or things to come; all are your's;</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And ye are Christ's; and Christ is God's.</p>
