## Proverbs 7

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> My son, keep my words, and lay up my commandments with thee.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Keep my commandments, and live; and my law as the apple of thine eye.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Bind them upon thy fingers, write them upon the table of thine heart.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Say unto wisdom, Thou art my sister; and call understanding thy kinswoman:</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> That they may keep thee from the strange woman, from the stranger which flattereth with her words.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> For at the window of my house I looked through my casement,</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And beheld among the simple ones, I discerned among the youths, a young man void of understanding,</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Passing through the street near her corner; and he went the way to her house,</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> In the twilight, in the evening, in the black and dark night:</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And, behold, there met him a woman with the attire of an harlot, and subtil of heart.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> (She is loud and stubborn; her feet abide not in her house:</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Now is she without, now in the streets, and lieth in wait at every corner.)</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> So she caught him, and kissed him, and with an impudent face said unto him,</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> I have peace offerings with me; this day have I payed my vows.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> Therefore came I forth to meet thee, diligently to seek thy face, and I have found thee.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> I have decked my bed with coverings of tapestry, with carved works, with fine linen of Egypt.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> I have perfumed my bed with myrrh, aloes, and cinnamon.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Come, let us take our fill of love until the morning: let us solace ourselves with loves.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> For the goodman is not at home, he is gone a long journey:</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> He hath taken a bag of money with him, and will come home at the day appointed.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> With her much fair speech she caused him to yield, with the flattering of her lips she forced him.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> He goeth after her straightway, as an ox goeth to the slaughter, or as a fool to the correction of the stocks;</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Till a dart strike through his liver; as a bird hasteth to the snare, and knoweth not that it is for his life.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Hearken unto me now therefore, O ye children, and attend to the words of my mouth.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Let not thine heart decline to her ways, go not astray in her paths.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> For she hath cast down many wounded: yea, many strong men have been slain by her.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Her house is the way to hell, going down to the chambers of death.</p>
