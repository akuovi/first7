## John 7

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> After these things Jesus walked in Galilee: for he would not walk in Jewry, because the Jews sought to kill him.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Now the Jews' feast of tabernacles was at hand.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> His brethren therefore said unto him, Depart hence, and go into Judaea, that thy disciples also may see the works that thou doest.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> For there is no man that doeth any thing in secret, and he himself seeketh to be known openly. If thou do these things, shew thyself to the world.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> For neither did his brethren believe in him.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Then Jesus said unto them, My time is not yet come: but your time is alway ready.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> The world cannot hate you; but me it hateth, because I testify of it, that the works thereof are evil.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Go ye up unto this feast: I go not up yet unto this feast; for my time is not yet full come.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> When he had said these words unto them, he abode still in Galilee.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> But when his brethren were gone up, then went he also up unto the feast, not openly, but as it were in secret.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Then the Jews sought him at the feast, and said, Where is he?</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And there was much murmuring among the people concerning him: for some said, He is a good man: others said, Nay; but he deceiveth the people.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Howbeit no man spake openly of him for fear of the Jews.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Now about the midst of the feast Jesus went up into the temple, and taught.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And the Jews marvelled, saying, How knoweth this man letters, having never learned?</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Jesus answered them, and said, My doctrine is not mine, but his that sent me.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> If any man will do his will, he shall know of the doctrine, whether it be of God, or whether I speak of myself.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> He that speaketh of himself seeketh his own glory: but he that seeketh his glory that sent him, the same is true, and no unrighteousness is in him.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Did not Moses give you the law, and yet none of you keepeth the law? Why go ye about to kill me?</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> The people answered and said, Thou hast a devil: who goeth about to kill thee?</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Jesus answered and said unto them, I have done one work, and ye all marvel.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Moses therefore gave unto you circumcision; (not because it is of Moses, but of the fathers;) and ye on the sabbath day circumcise a man.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> If a man on the sabbath day receive circumcision, that the law of Moses should not be broken; are ye angry at me, because I have made a man every whit whole on the sabbath day?</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Judge not according to the appearance, but judge righteous judgment.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Then said some of them of Jerusalem, Is not this he, whom they seek to kill?</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> But, lo, he speaketh boldly, and they say nothing unto him. Do the rulers know indeed that this is the very Christ?</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Howbeit we know this man whence he is: but when Christ cometh, no man knoweth whence he is.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> Then cried Jesus in the temple as he taught, saying, Ye both know me, and ye know whence I am: and I am not come of myself, but he that sent me is true, whom ye know not.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> But I know him: for I am from him, and he hath sent me.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Then they sought to take him: but no man laid hands on him, because his hour was not yet come.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> And many of the people believed on him, and said, When Christ cometh, will he do more miracles than these which this man hath done?</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> The Pharisees heard that the people murmured such things concerning him; and the Pharisees and the chief priests sent officers to take him.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> Then said Jesus unto them, Yet a little while am I with you, and then I go unto him that sent me.</p>

<p id="v34" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">34</span> Ye shall seek me, and shall not find me: and where I am, thither ye cannot come.</p>

<p id="v35" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">35</span> Then said the Jews among themselves, Whither will he go, that we shall not find him? will he go unto the dispersed among the Gentiles, and teach the Gentiles?</p>

<p id="v36" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">36</span> What manner of saying is this that he said, Ye shall seek me, and shall not find me: and where I am, thither ye cannot come?</p>

<p id="v37" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">37</span> In the last day, that great day of the feast, Jesus stood and cried, saying, If any man thirst, let him come unto me, and drink.</p>

<p id="v38" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">38</span> He that believeth on me, as the scripture hath said, out of his belly shall flow rivers of living water.</p>

<p id="v39" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">39</span> (But this spake he of the Spirit, which they that believe on him should receive: for the Holy Ghost was not yet given; because that Jesus was not yet glorified.)</p>

<p id="v40" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">40</span> Many of the people therefore, when they heard this saying, said, Of a truth this is the Prophet.</p>

<p id="v41" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">41</span> Others said, This is the Christ. But some said, Shall Christ come out of Galilee?</p>

<p id="v42" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">42</span> Hath not the scripture said, That Christ cometh of the seed of David, and out of the town of Bethlehem, where David was?</p>

<p id="v43" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">43</span> So there was a division among the people because of him.</p>

<p id="v44" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">44</span> And some of them would have taken him; but no man laid hands on him.</p>

<p id="v45" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">45</span> Then came the officers to the chief priests and Pharisees; and they said unto them, Why have ye not brought him?</p>

<p id="v46" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">46</span> The officers answered, Never man spake like this man.</p>

<p id="v47" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">47</span> Then answered them the Pharisees, Are ye also deceived?</p>

<p id="v48" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">48</span> Have any of the rulers or of the Pharisees believed on him?</p>

<p id="v49" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">49</span> But this people who knoweth not the law are cursed.</p>

<p id="v50" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">50</span> Nicodemus saith unto them, (he that came to Jesus by night, being one of them,)</p>

<p id="v51" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">51</span> Doth our law judge any man, before it hear him, and know what he doeth?</p>

<p id="v52" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">52</span> They answered and said unto him, Art thou also of Galilee? Search, and look: for out of Galilee ariseth no prophet.</p>

<p id="v53" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">53</span> And every man went unto his own house.</p>
