## Isaiah 1

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> The vision of Isaiah the son of Amoz, which he saw concerning Judah and Jerusalem in the days of Uzziah, Jotham, Ahaz, and Hezekiah, kings of Judah.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Hear, O heavens, and give ear, O earth: for the LORD hath spoken, I have nourished and brought up children, and they have rebelled against me.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> The ox knoweth his owner, and the ass his master's crib: but Israel doth not know, my people doth not consider.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> Ah sinful nation, a people laden with iniquity, a seed of evildoers, children that are corrupters: they have forsaken the LORD, they have provoked the Holy One of Israel unto anger, they are gone away backward.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> Why should ye be stricken any more? ye will revolt more and more: the whole head is sick, and the whole heart faint.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> From the sole of the foot even unto the head there is no soundness in it; but wounds, and bruises, and putrifying sores: they have not been closed, neither bound up, neither mollified with ointment.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Your country is desolate, your cities are burned with fire: your land, strangers devour it in your presence, and it is desolate, as overthrown by strangers.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And the daughter of Zion is left as a cottage in a vineyard, as a lodge in a garden of cucumbers, as a besieged city.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Except the LORD of hosts had left unto us a very small remnant, we should have been as Sodom, and we should have been like unto Gomorrah.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Hear the word of the LORD, ye rulers of Sodom; give ear unto the law of our God, ye people of Gomorrah.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> To what purpose is the multitude of your sacrifices unto me? saith the LORD: I am full of the burnt offerings of rams, and the fat of fed beasts; and I delight not in the blood of bullocks, or of lambs, or of he goats.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> When ye come to appear before me, who hath required this at your hand, to tread my courts?</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Bring no more vain oblations; incense is an abomination unto me; the new moons and sabbaths, the calling of assemblies, I cannot away with; it is iniquity, even the solemn meeting.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> Your new moons and your appointed feasts my soul hateth: they are a trouble unto me; I am weary to bear them.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And when ye spread forth your hands, I will hide mine eyes from you: yea, when ye make many prayers, I will not hear: your hands are full of blood.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Wash you, make you clean; put away the evil of your doings from before mine eyes; cease to do evil;</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Learn to do well; seek judgment, relieve the oppressed, judge the fatherless, plead for the widow.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Come now, and let us reason together, saith the LORD: though your sins be as scarlet, they shall be as white as snow; though they be red like crimson, they shall be as wool.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> If ye be willing and obedient, ye shall eat the good of the land:</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> But if ye refuse and rebel, ye shall be devoured with the sword: for the mouth of the LORD hath spoken it.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> How is the faithful city become an harlot! it was full of judgment; righteousness lodged in it; but now murderers.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Thy silver is become dross, thy wine mixed with water:</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Thy princes are rebellious, and companions of thieves: every one loveth gifts, and followeth after rewards: they judge not the fatherless, neither doth the cause of the widow come unto them.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Therefore saith the Lord, the LORD of hosts, the mighty One of Israel, Ah, I will ease me of mine adversaries, and avenge me of mine enemies:</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And I will turn my hand upon thee, and purely purge away thy dross, and take away all thy tin:</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> And I will restore thy judges as at the first, and thy counsellors as at the beginning: afterward thou shalt be called, The city of righteousness, the faithful city.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Zion shall be redeemed with judgment, and her converts with righteousness.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And the destruction of the transgressors and of the sinners shall be together, and they that forsake the LORD shall be consumed.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> For they shall be ashamed of the oaks which ye have desired, and ye shall be confounded for the gardens that ye have chosen.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> For ye shall be as an oak whose leaf fadeth, and as a garden that hath no water.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> And the strong shall be as tow, and the maker of it as a spark, and they shall both burn together, and none shall quench them.</p>
