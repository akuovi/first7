## Proverbs 15

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> A soft answer turneth away wrath: but grievous words stir up anger.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> The tongue of the wise useth knowledge aright: but the mouth of fools poureth out foolishness.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> The eyes of the LORD are in every place, beholding the evil and the good.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> A wholesome tongue is a tree of life: but perverseness therein is a breach in the spirit.</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> A fool despiseth his father's instruction: but he that regardeth reproof is prudent.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> In the house of the righteous is much treasure: but in the revenues of the wicked is trouble.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> The lips of the wise disperse knowledge: but the heart of the foolish doeth not so.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> The sacrifice of the wicked is an abomination to the LORD: but the prayer of the upright is his delight.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> The way of the wicked is an abomination unto the LORD: but he loveth him that followeth after righteousness.</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Correction is grievous unto him that forsaketh the way: and he that hateth reproof shall die.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Hell and destruction are before the LORD: how much more then the hearts of the children of men?</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> A scorner loveth not one that reproveth him: neither will he go unto the wise.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> A merry heart maketh a cheerful countenance: but by sorrow of the heart the spirit is broken.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> The heart of him that hath understanding seeketh knowledge: but the mouth of fools feedeth on foolishness.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> All the days of the afflicted are evil: but he that is of a merry heart hath a continual feast.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Better is little with the fear of the LORD than great treasure and trouble therewith.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Better is a dinner of herbs where love is, than a stalled ox and hatred therewith.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> A wrathful man stirreth up strife: but he that is slow to anger appeaseth strife.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> The way of the slothful man is as an hedge of thorns: but the way of the righteous is made plain.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> A wise son maketh a glad father: but a foolish man despiseth his mother.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Folly is joy to him that is destitute of wisdom: but a man of understanding walketh uprightly.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Without counsel purposes are disappointed: but in the multitude of counsellors they are established.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> A man hath joy by the answer of his mouth: and a word spoken in due season, how good is it!</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> The way of life is above to the wise, that he may depart from hell beneath.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> The LORD will destroy the house of the proud: but he will establish the border of the widow.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> The thoughts of the wicked are an abomination to the LORD: but the words of the pure are pleasant words.</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> He that is greedy of gain troubleth his own house; but he that hateth gifts shall live.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> The heart of the righteous studieth to answer: but the mouth of the wicked poureth out evil things.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> The LORD is far from the wicked: but he heareth the prayer of the righteous.</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> The light of the eyes rejoiceth the heart: and a good report maketh the bones fat.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> The ear that heareth the reproof of life abideth among the wise.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> He that refuseth instruction despiseth his own soul: but he that heareth reproof getteth understanding.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> The fear of the LORD is the instruction of wisdom; and before honour is humility.</p>
