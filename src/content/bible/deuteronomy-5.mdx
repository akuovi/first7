## Deuteronomy 5

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And Moses called all Israel, and said unto them, Hear, O Israel, the statutes and judgments which I speak in your ears this day, that ye may learn them, and keep, and do them.</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> The LORD our God made a covenant with us in Horeb.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> The LORD made not this covenant with our fathers, but with us, even us, who are all of us here alive this day.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> The LORD talked with you face to face in the mount out of the midst of the fire,</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> (I stood between the LORD and you at that time, to shew you the word of the LORD: for ye were afraid by reason of the fire, and went not up into the mount;) saying,</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> I am the LORD thy God, which brought thee out of the land of Egypt, from the house of bondage.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> Thou shalt have none other gods before me.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> Thou shalt not make thee any graven image, or any likeness of any thing that is in heaven above, or that is in the earth beneath, or that is in the waters beneath the earth:</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> Thou shalt not bow down thyself unto them, nor serve them: for I the LORD thy God am a jealous God, visiting the iniquity of the fathers upon the children unto the third and fourth generation of them that hate me,</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> And shewing mercy unto thousands of them that love me and keep my commandments.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> Thou shalt not take the name of the LORD thy God in vain: for the LORD will not hold him guiltless that taketh his name in vain.</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> Keep the sabbath day to sanctify it, as the LORD thy God hath commanded thee.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Six days thou shalt labour, and do all thy work:</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> But the seventh day is the sabbath of the LORD thy God: in it thou shalt not do any work, thou, nor thy son, nor thy daughter, nor thy manservant, nor thy maidservant, nor thine ox, nor thine ass, nor any of thy cattle, nor thy stranger that is within thy gates; that thy manservant and thy maidservant may rest as well as thou.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And remember that thou wast a servant in the land of Egypt, and that the LORD thy God brought thee out thence through a mighty hand and by a stretched out arm: therefore the LORD thy God commanded thee to keep the sabbath day.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> Honour thy father and thy mother, as the LORD thy God hath commanded thee; that thy days may be prolonged, and that it may go well with thee, in the land which the LORD thy God giveth thee.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> Thou shalt not kill.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> Neither shalt thou commit adultery.</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Neither shalt thou steal.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> Neither shalt thou bear false witness against thy neighbour.</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Neither shalt thou desire thy neighbour's wife, neither shalt thou covet thy neighbour's house, his field, or his manservant, or his maidservant, his ox, or his ass, or any thing that is thy neighbour's.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> These words the LORD spake unto all your assembly in the mount out of the midst of the fire, of the cloud, and of the thick darkness, with a great voice: and he added no more. And he wrote them in two tables of stone, and delivered them unto me.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And it came to pass, when ye heard the voice out of the midst of the darkness, (for the mountain did burn with fire,) that ye came near unto me, even all the heads of your tribes, and your elders;</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> And ye said, Behold, the LORD our God hath shewed us his glory and his greatness, and we have heard his voice out of the midst of the fire: we have seen this day that God doth talk with man, and he liveth.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Now therefore why should we die? for this great fire will consume us: if we hear the voice of the LORD our God any more, then we shall die.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> For who is there of all flesh, that hath heard the voice of the living God speaking out of the midst of the fire, as we have, and lived?</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> Go thou near, and hear all that the LORD our God shall say: and speak thou unto us all that the LORD our God shall speak unto thee; and we will hear it, and do it.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And the LORD heard the voice of your words, when ye spake unto me; and the LORD said unto me, I have heard the voice of the words of this people, which they have spoken unto thee: they have well said all that they have spoken.</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> O that there were such an heart in them, that they would fear me, and keep all my commandments always, that it might be well with them, and with their children for ever!</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Go say to them, Get you into your tents again.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> But as for thee, stand thou here by me, and I will speak unto thee all the commandments, and the statutes, and the judgments, which thou shalt teach them, that they may do them in the land which I give them to possess it.</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> Ye shall observe to do therefore as the LORD your God hath commanded you: ye shall not turn aside to the right hand or to the left.</p>

<p id="v33" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">33</span> Ye shall walk in all the ways which the LORD your God hath commanded you, that ye may live, and that it may be well with you, and that ye may prolong your days in the land which ye shall possess.</p>
