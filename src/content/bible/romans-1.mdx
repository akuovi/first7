## Romans 1

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> Paul, a servant of Jesus Christ, called to be an apostle, separated unto the gospel of God,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> (Which he had promised afore by his prophets in the holy scriptures,)</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> Concerning his Son Jesus Christ our Lord, which was made of the seed of David according to the flesh;</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> And declared to be the Son of God with power, according to the spirit of holiness, by the resurrection from the dead:</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> By whom we have received grace and apostleship, for obedience to the faith among all nations, for his name:</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> Among whom are ye also the called of Jesus Christ:</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> To all that be in Rome, beloved of God, called to be saints: Grace to you and peace from God our Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> First, I thank my God through Jesus Christ for you all, that your faith is spoken of throughout the whole world.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> For God is my witness, whom I serve with my spirit in the gospel of his Son, that without ceasing I make mention of you always in my prayers;</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> Making request, if by any means now at length I might have a prosperous journey by the will of God to come unto you.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> For I long to see you, that I may impart unto you some spiritual gift, to the end ye may be established;</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> That is, that I may be comforted together with you by the mutual faith both of you and me.</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> Now I would not have you ignorant, brethren, that oftentimes I purposed to come unto you, (but was let hitherto,) that I might have some fruit among you also, even as among other Gentiles.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> I am debtor both to the Greeks, and to the Barbarians; both to the wise, and to the unwise.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> So, as much as in me is, I am ready to preach the gospel to you that are at Rome also.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> For I am not ashamed of the gospel of Christ: for it is the power of God unto salvation to every one that believeth; to the Jew first, and also to the Greek.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> For therein is the righteousness of God revealed from faith to faith: as it is written, The just shall live by faith.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> For the wrath of God is revealed from heaven against all ungodliness and unrighteousness of men, who hold the truth in unrighteousness;</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> Because that which may be known of God is manifest in them; for God hath shewed it unto them.</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> For the invisible things of him from the creation of the world are clearly seen, being understood by the things that are made, even his eternal power and Godhead; so that they are without excuse:</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> Because that, when they knew God, they glorified him not as God, neither were thankful; but became vain in their imaginations, and their foolish heart was darkened.</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> Professing themselves to be wise, they became fools,</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> And changed the glory of the uncorruptible God into an image made like to corruptible man, and to birds, and fourfooted beasts, and creeping things.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> Wherefore God also gave them up to uncleanness through the lusts of their own hearts, to dishonour their own bodies between themselves:</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> Who changed the truth of God into a lie, and worshipped and served the creature more than the Creator, who is blessed for ever. Amen.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> For this cause God gave them up unto vile affections: for even their women did change the natural use into that which is against nature:</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> And likewise also the men, leaving the natural use of the woman, burned in their lust one toward another; men with men working that which is unseemly, and receiving in themselves that recompence of their error which was meet.</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And even as they did not like to retain God in their knowledge, God gave them over to a reprobate mind, to do those things which are not convenient;</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> Being filled with all unrighteousness, fornication, wickedness, covetousness, maliciousness; full of envy, murder, debate, deceit, malignity; whisperers,</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> Backbiters, haters of God, despiteful, proud, boasters, inventors of evil things, disobedient to parents,</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Without understanding, covenantbreakers, without natural affection, implacable, unmerciful:</p>

<p id="v32" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">32</span> Who knowing the judgment of God, that they which commit such things are worthy of death, not only do the same, but have pleasure in them that do them.</p>
