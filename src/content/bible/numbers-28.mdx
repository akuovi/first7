## Numbers 28

<p id="v1" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">1</span> And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying,</p>

<p id="v2" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">2</span> Command the children of Israel, and say unto them, My offering, and my bread for my sacrifices made by fire, for a sweet savour unto me, shall ye observe to offer unto me in their due season.</p>

<p id="v3" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">3</span> And thou shalt say unto them, This is the offering made by fire which ye shall offer unto the LORD; two lambs of the first year without spot day by day, for a continual burnt offering.</p>

<p id="v4" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">4</span> The one lamb shalt thou offer in the morning, and the other lamb shalt thou offer at even;</p>

<p id="v5" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">5</span> And a tenth part of an ephah of flour for a meat offering, mingled with the fourth part of an hin of beaten oil.</p>

<p id="v6" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">6</span> It is a continual burnt offering, which was ordained in mount Sinai for a sweet savour, a sacrifice made by fire unto the LORD.</p>

<p id="v7" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">7</span> And the drink offering thereof shall be the fourth part of an hin for the one lamb: in the holy place shalt thou cause the strong wine to be poured unto the LORD for a drink offering.</p>

<p id="v8" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">8</span> And the other lamb shalt thou offer at even: as the meat offering of the morning, and as the drink offering thereof, thou shalt offer it, a sacrifice made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD.</p>

<p id="v9" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">9</span> And on the sabbath day two lambs of the first year without spot, and two tenth deals of flour for a meat offering, mingled with oil, and the drink offering thereof:</p>

<p id="v10" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">10</span> This is the burnt offering of every sabbath, beside the continual burnt offering, and his drink offering.</p>

<p id="v11" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">11</span> And in the beginnings of your months ye shall offer a burnt offering unto the LORD; two young bullocks, and one ram, seven lambs of the first year without spot;</p>

<p id="v12" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">12</span> And three tenth deals of flour for a meat offering, mingled with oil, for one bullock; and two tenth deals of flour for a meat offering, mingled with oil, for one ram;</p>

<p id="v13" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">13</span> And a several tenth deal of flour mingled with oil for a meat offering unto one lamb; for a burnt offering of a sweet savour, a sacrifice made by fire unto the LORD.</p>

<p id="v14" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">14</span> And their drink offerings shall be half an hin of wine unto a bullock, and the third part of an hin unto a ram, and a fourth part of an hin unto a lamb: this is the burnt offering of every month throughout the months of the year.</p>

<p id="v15" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">15</span> And one kid of the goats for a sin offering unto the LORD shall be offered, beside the continual burnt offering, and his drink offering.</p>

<p id="v16" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">16</span> And in the fourteenth day of the first month is the passover of the LORD.</p>

<p id="v17" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">17</span> And in the fifteenth day of this month is the feast: seven days shall unleavened bread be eaten.</p>

<p id="v18" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">18</span> In the first day shall be an holy convocation; ye shall do no manner of servile work therein:</p>

<p id="v19" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">19</span> But ye shall offer a sacrifice made by fire for a burnt offering unto the LORD; two young bullocks, and one ram, and seven lambs of the first year: they shall be unto you without blemish:</p>

<p id="v20" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">20</span> And their meat offering shall be of flour mingled with oil: three tenth deals shall ye offer for a bullock, and two tenth deals for a ram;</p>

<p id="v21" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">21</span> A several tenth deal shalt thou offer for every lamb, throughout the seven lambs:</p>

<p id="v22" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">22</span> And one goat for a sin offering, to make an atonement for you.</p>

<p id="v23" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">23</span> Ye shall offer these beside the burnt offering in the morning, which is for a continual burnt offering.</p>

<p id="v24" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">24</span> After this manner ye shall offer daily, throughout the seven days, the meat of the sacrifice made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD: it shall be offered beside the continual burnt offering, and his drink offering.</p>

<p id="v25" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">25</span> And on the seventh day ye shall have an holy convocation; ye shall do no servile work.</p>

<p id="v26" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">26</span> Also in the day of the firstfruits, when ye bring a new meat offering unto the LORD, after your weeks be out, ye shall have an holy convocation; ye shall do no servile work:</p>

<p id="v27" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">27</span> But ye shall offer the burnt offering for a sweet savour unto the LORD; two young bullocks, one ram, seven lambs of the first year;</p>

<p id="v28" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">28</span> And their meat offering of flour mingled with oil, three tenth deals unto one bullock, two tenth deals unto one ram,</p>

<p id="v29" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">29</span> A several tenth deal unto one lamb, throughout the seven lambs;</p>

<p id="v30" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">30</span> And one kid of the goats, to make an atonement for you.</p>

<p id="v31" class="bible-verse"><span class="verse-number">31</span> Ye shall offer them beside the continual burnt offering, and his meat offering, (they shall be unto you without blemish) and their drink offerings.</p>
